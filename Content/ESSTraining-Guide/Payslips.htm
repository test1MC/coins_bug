Payslips

Viewing Payslips

Navigate to Own Details and click on the ‘Pay History’ tab

To view the payslip of the required date/period click on the underlined ‘Date Paid’ which will open up the payslip

Click on the tab ‘Payslip Style View’

 

Printing / Emailing Payslips

Click on the orange paperclip in the top right hand corner. (when hovering over the paperclip a preview of the paperclip should appear)

Once you have clicked on the paperclip, a pdf version of the payslip should open. From here you can print or download the payslip to email if so required
