ESS User Guide

This guide should be used in conjunction with formal COINS training and together you will have the skills and knowledge to:

    Access the employee self service area

    View / Amend your own details kept in the HR

    Access, Print and Email own payslips

    Record own absences

    Request, amend, cancel holidays

    If permitted, authorise holiday requests and view team calendar
