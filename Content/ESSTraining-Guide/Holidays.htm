Holidays

Viewing current holiday summary

 

Navigate to Holidays > My Holidays

 

This will provide you with a screen showing a summary of holiday entitlement, carried forward, lieu days and any unpaid leave arrangements. The page also identifies the current holiday year and holiday authoriser(s)

 

Requesting a Holiday

Navigate to My Holidays then click on the ‘Holiday Booking’ tab

Click on the Add button

The ‘From’ and ‘To’ dates are inclusive so being the first and last dates of the annual leave required. Either type in the date or click on the calendar icon to enter the dates.

Enter a reason for the leave from the drop down

Selecting whether the holiday request will be payable or not dictates whether the annual leave entitlement is used or the unpaid entitlement

Select ‘Morning ‘ or ‘Afternoon’ from the drop downs to dictate whether only half a day is required.

To save and send the request, click the save button which will automatically email the request to the relevant holiday authoriser(s)

A confirmation will be sent to the requester once the holiday has been authorised or denied

Amending a Holiday

Navigate to My Holidays > Holiday Booking

Highlight the existing holiday request that needs to be amended and press the arrow next to ‘Amend Holiday’ or alternatively use the open folder button

You will then be able to amend the details already entered and press the save button, if the holiday request had already been authorised a new email will be sent to the authorisers with the amendment request

 

Cancelling a Holiday

Navigate to My Holidays > Holiday Booking

Highlight the holiday to be cancelled and press the arrow next to ‘Cancel holiday’ or alternatively press the delete button

A cancellation request will be sent to the holiday authorisers

 

Authorising a Holiday

When a holiday is requested in employee self service there are 2 notifications

An email is sent to the holiday authoriser similar to below

When the authoriser clicks on the hyperlink in the email they are taken directly to the request

Alternatively, on the Home page of Coins a notification will appear in the form of a red dot next to the user’s name if there are outstanding actions as shown below

Click on the dot followed by Activity Workbench when available on the drop down

 

This will open up the Activity Workbench and on the ‘All Actions’ tab it will show any outstanding requests

Click on the hyperlink next to the one you wish to look at

This will open the request and Approve or Deny can be selected

 

 

Team Calendar

Navigate to Holidays > Team Calendar

Team Calendar shows holiday requests, booked holiday, absences and training on a month by month basis for employees that are available to the user


Filters can be used to search for individuals, departments, particular roles, etc

Selecting the help shows the key of various acronyms used
