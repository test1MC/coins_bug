Admissions Module

From first inquiry to matriculation, EMPOWER delivers fast, accurate and complete prospect contact and communications management. Beginning with fully configurable inquiry and admissions applications linked to your website, admissions counselors develop a complete picture of every potential student. Communications and contact process sequencing, automated mail/email, customized data gathering, document coordination and imaging, and automated download of thirdparty information are all provided. Complete documentation and extensive admissions analysis enable professionals to monitor admissions activity for effective goal setting and measurement.
