Plant Control

Plant Control empowers the efficient management of your plant, stock, and fleet assets. It handles the full life cycle of an asset: from purchase, to tracking and charging the hires, to disposal or sale.

Plant Control is fully integrated with other modules such as Fixed Assets, Stock, Sales Ledger, Human Resources and Payroll in order to support the key processes related to plant:

  • Process the hiring of plant (fixed assets and stock).
  • Schedule and track maintenance for each asset.
  • Allocate assets such as company vehicles, mobile phones, fuel cards or laptop computers to individuals.
  • Analyse performance, based on the costs and revenue associated with each asset (plant, stock, or fleet).
  • Manage plant using standard reports and enquiries (for regular business reporting) and configurable reports and enquiries defined to your specific requirements.
  • Dig deeper into enquiry data, from the summary level down to individual transaction details.
