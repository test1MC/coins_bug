Overview

Many  (MK) functions are used in the Project Management (PT) module. However, this caused a problem for clients who do not have an MK . As a temporary fix, MK functions were changed to the System module (SY), which only partially solved the problem.

A more permanent solution (as discussed in this document) has resolved this problem by:

  • Re-assigning some MK functions to proper common modules that do not need an MK .
  • Making "switches" that switch between MK functions and functions of other modules, depending on the existence of an MK .
