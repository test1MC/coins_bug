Implementation and Planning

Companies need to start planning now for the changes VAT business process changes required.

 

Preparation

 

In preparation for moving to coins it is recommended that

  • A recent copy of is available for Testing
  • VAT transactions are reconciled and up to date
  • Review and Document existing VAT business process
  • Activate VAT Workbench

 

Survey and Questionnaire

  • To assist with Implementation a questionnaire will need to be completed to aid understanding of current VAT process and coins set.
