Sales Workbench Tabs

 

By Development

Developments displays a list of existing developments, sorted alpha-numerically by development number. It also displays the number of plots there are in each development, how many of them are unreleased, available for sale, and so on.

 

By Purchaser

Purchasers shows a list of purchasers which can be sorted by name or postcode.

 

 

By Pre-release

Pre-release Reservations shows a list of Pre-release Reservations.
