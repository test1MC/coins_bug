Revenue Tab

The Revenue tab lists all Revenue Heads.

Each Revenue Head line can be opened and a Site Start Budget and Current Forecast can be manually input.  Adjustments can be made for Actual Revenue to Date.

A drilldown hyperlink is available on the Ledger Revenue column to Live Status data
