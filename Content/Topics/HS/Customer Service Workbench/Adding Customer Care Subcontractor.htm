Subcontractor Contacts

 

Subcontractor Contacts are linked to the Subcontractors Ledger Account

 

14.1.1 Adding Subcontractor Contact

Click on the button to the left hand side of the Subcontractor record.

Enter the following information:

 

Field

Content

Comments

Contact:

Free text

The customer services contact at the subcontractor.

Fax Number:

Free text

The customer services fax number for the subcontractor.

Mobile Number:

Free text

The customer services mobile number for the subcontractor.

Email: 

Free text

The email address for the subcontractor.

Send Appointment Confirmation:

Tick

Whether an automatic confirmation text message is sent to the subcontractor when a task appointment is made, amended or cancelled (IF you are set up to send SMS from the System).

Click the button.

Subcontractor Contacts are linked to the Subcontractors Ledger Account. If your Subcontractor is not present in the workbench then contact the Accounts Department who will need to add the record.
