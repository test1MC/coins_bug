Tasks Tab

Customer Care Workbench Tasks lists the Customer Care tasks.  There is a link to the task details, and links to the details of the call or plot to which the task relates.

Select the TasksTab. 

There are also several filter options on the tasks screen. In the simple filters you can choose to show just open, just closed, just overdue or all tasks. You can also filter by task ref., development number, date, completion date, call no. and task reason.
