Contacts Tab

The Contacts tab lists contacts in the House Sales database, including owners, purchasers, and agents.  There is a link to the Customer Care details for the plot on which the contact is recorded; if the contact is recorded on more than one plot, you will first be presented with a list of those plots.

Select the Contacts Tab.

The initial frame in this workbench lists the names, addresses and telephone numbers of all purchasers who have reserved a plot or part-exchange property with you. The list also includes the names and addresses of other contacts associated with a plot, who may not necessarily be the purchaser.

You can select a purchaser from the list to view details of the plot(s) or properties they currently have reserved, or are in the process of purchasing and/or have purchased. You can view, add and amend existing details as required.

You can filter to include/exclude contacts on active or inactive plots by using the Apply Filter button.

The contacts list can be filtered to include/exclude contacts on all or just active or inactive plots. Select the Filter drop-down list and choose Contacts on Active Plots, Contacts on Inactive Plots or Contacts on All Plots from the list and then either press Return or select the Apply Filter button.

To view the plot’s summary details select the surname of one of the contacts attached to the plot.
