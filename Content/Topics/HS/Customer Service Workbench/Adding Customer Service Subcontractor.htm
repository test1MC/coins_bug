14.1 Adding Customer Service Subcontractor Contacts

‘%’  Note – Subcontractor Contacts are linked to the Sub Contractors Ledger Account. 

Navigate to the Customer Service Module.

Select Customer Service Setup Folder.

Select the Subcontractor Contact

14.1.1 Adding Customer Service Subcontractor Contact

Click on the Open button to the left hand side of the Subcontractor record.

 

 

 

 

 

Enter the following information:

Table 12

Field

Content

Comments

Buttons

Contact:

Free text

The customer services contact at the subcontractor.

 

Fax Number:

Free text

The customer services fax number for the subcontractor.

 

Mobile Number:

Free text

The customer services mobile number for the subcontractor.

 

Email: 

Free text

The email address for the subcontractor.

 

Send Appointment Confirmation:

Tick

Whether an automatic confirmation text message is sent to the subcontractor when a task appointment is made, amended or cancelled (IF you are set up to send SMS from the System).

   or 

 

Click the Save button.

 

‘%’  Note – Subcontractor Contacts are linked to the Sub Contractors Ledger Account, if your Subcontractor is not present in the workbench then contact the Accounts Department who will need to add the record.
