Customer Care - Supplier Contacts

Supplier Contact allows you to record a contact name and fax number for each supplier.  This is used when printing documents in Customer Services, instead of the details in .

 

Supplier Contacts are linked to the account. 

 

Navigate to the > Setup > Supplier Contact.

 

Adding Supplier Contact

Click on the Open button to the left hand side of the Supplier record.

Enter the following information:

 

Field

Content

Comments

Contact:

Free text

The contact at the supplier.

Fax Number:

Free text

The fax number for the supplier.

Email: 

Free text

The email address for the supplier.

 

Click the button.

 

Supplier Contacts are linked to the Account. If your Supplier is not present in the workbench then contact the Accounts Department who will need to add the record.
