15.1 Adding Customer Service Supplier Contacts

‘%’  Note – Subcontractor Contacts are linked to the Account. 

Navigate to the Customer Service Module.

Select Customer Service Setup Folder.

Select the Supplier Contact

 

15.1.1 Adding Customer Service Supplier Contact

Click on the Open button to the left hand side of the Supplier record.

 

 

 

 

 

Enter the following information:

Table 13

Field

Content

Comments

Buttons

Contact:

Free text

The customer services contact at the supplier.

 

Fax Number:

Free text

The customer services fax number for the supplier.

 

Email: 

Free text

The email address for the supplier.

 

 

Click the Save button.

 

‘%’  Note – Supplier Contacts are linked to the Account, if your Supplier is not present in the workbench then contact the Accounts Department who will need to add the record.
