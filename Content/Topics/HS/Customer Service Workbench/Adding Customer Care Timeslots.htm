Customer Care Timeslots

 

Timeslots allows you to set up the timeslots that are available for scheduling appointments. For example, you could have a morning and an afternoon slot for appointments, or you could have four slots: early morning, late morning, early afternoon, late afternoon.

 

Navigate Customer Care > Customer Care Setup > Timeslots

 

Adding Customer Care Timeslots

Click .  Enter the following information:

Field

Content

Comments

Time Slot:

Free text

Enter a description of the time slot, which is usually AM or PM

Start Time:

Time HH:MM

Enter the start time of the time slot.

 

End Time:

Time HH:MM

Enter the end time of the time slot.

 

 

Click

The system will prompt you to add more Timeslots, click on the Undo button when you are finished.
