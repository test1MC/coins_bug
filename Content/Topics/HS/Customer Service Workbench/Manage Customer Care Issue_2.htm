Manage Customer Care Issue Status Codes

Issue Status Codes allows you to set up the codes you will use to track the status of customer services issues.

Adding Customer Care Issue Status Codes

Issue Status Codes are also used in System parameters, seek Support Desk help if you are thinking of deleting or adding any further codes.

Navigate to the Customer CareModule.

Select Customer Care SetupFolder.

Select the Issue Status Codes.

Click

Enter the following information:
