4.1 Adding Customer Service Company Holidays

Navigate to the Customer Service Module.

Select Customer Service Setup Folder.

Select the Company Holidays .

4.1.1 Adding a Customer Service Company Holiday

Click on the Add button.  Enter the following information:

Table 2

Field

Content

Comments

Buttons

Date:

Numerical

Enter the date of the company holiday.

Description:

Free text

Enter a description for the company holiday.

 

 

Click the Save button.

 

 

 

 

The system will prompt you to add more Company Holidays, click on the Undo button when you are finished.

‘%’  Note – Company Holidays will show against the Customer Services Scheduler as a reminder to the user that Tasks should not be allocated to an on that day.
