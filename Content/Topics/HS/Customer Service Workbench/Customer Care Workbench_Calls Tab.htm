Calls Tab

 

Customer Care Workbench Calls lists the Customer Care calls.  There is a link from each call to the call details, or to the Customer Care plot details for the plot that the call relates to.

Select the CallsTab. 

There are also several filter options on the call screen. In the simple filters you can choose to show just open, just closed or all calls.

Selecting on the call hyperlink will take you to the summary details for that individual call.
