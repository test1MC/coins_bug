Company Holidays

Company Holidays allows you to setup company holidays in the system.

 

Navigate to the Module.

Select the Setup Folder.

Select the Company Holidays

Adding a Company Holiday

Click . 

Enter the following information:

Field

Content

Comments

Date:

Numerical

Enter the date of the company holiday.

Description:

Free text

Enter a description for the company holiday.

 

Click

The system will prompt you to add more Company Holidays, click when you are finished.

Company Holidays will show against the Customer Services Scheduler as a reminder to the user that Tasks should not be allocated to an on that day.
