Issues Tab

 

Customer Care Workbench Issues lists the Customer Care issues.  There is a link to the issue details, and links to the details of the call or plot to which the issue relates.

Select the IssuesTab. 

There are also several filter options on the issues screen. In the simple filters you can choose to show just open, just closed, just new or all issues.

Selecting on the issue hyperlink will take you to the summary details for that issue:
