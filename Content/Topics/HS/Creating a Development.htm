Details Tab

The Development Maintenance - Details tab allows you to maintain key details relating to the development, such as the name, areas, number of plots and sales classification.

Field

Description
Development

The contract (and phase, if appropriate).

Development Name

The name of the development.
Land Name

The original name of the land before it was purchased by the house builder.
Gross Area The gross area of the development
Net Area The net area of the development.
Area Unit of Measure The unit of measure for the area of this development.
Number of Plots The number of plots in this development. This can be system generated or manually entered based on parameter settings
Number of HA Plots The number of Housing Association units on the development. This can be system generated or manually entered based on parameter settings
Classification The development classification code.
Sales Status The sales status of the development (that is, whether the development is inactive, active or complete).
Development Status The development status code.
Use Replan Workbench Select this option so the adding of plots, deleting of plots and changes to a plots house type must be done through the VAP > Development Maintenance > Replan Workbench

 

Sales Tab

The Development Maintenance - Sales tab allows you to maintain the sales information for the site. Most of this information is for printing on the availability list.

Field

Description
Address The address of the sales office.
Postcode The postcode of the sales office.
Telephone The sales office telephone number.
Fax The sales office fax number.
Sales Manager The sales manager for this site.
Telephone The sales manager telephone number.
Sales Representative The marketeer for this site.
Telephone The marketeer telephone number.
Site Agent The site agent for this site.
Telephone The site agent telephone number.
Email Address The email address of the sales office.
Development URL Development URL.
OS Northing Geographical Northing Number Maximum Length: 6 characters
OS Easting Geographical Easting Number Maximum Length: 6 characters
Longitude The geographical longitude of the development.
Latitude The geographical latitude of the development.
Sales Office Open The sales office opening hours.
Development Opens The date development opens.
First Price Release The date of first price releases for this site.
Price Range Price Range
Unlabelled field Price Range
Pre-release Reservation Are pre-release reservations allowed (Y or N)? This setting is the default for new plots.
Pre-release Reservation Deposit If pre-release reservations are allowed, what is the reservation deposit? This setting is the default for new plots.
Sales Schemes Not Allowed Enter a can-do list of the sales schemes that cannot be used on this development.
Conveyancer The conveyancer's code.
Unlabelled field The conveyancer's name.
Availability Form The availability list form to use on this development.
Availability Document Availability Document
Default Solicitor The default solicitor company name.
Office The default solicitor company office name.
Contact The name of the contact at the solicitor company.
Management Company The management company name.
Office The management company office name.
Contact The name of the contact at the management company.
Year End The year end for the management company on this development; this is used for pro-rating management charges.

 

Information Tab

The Development Maintenance - Information tab allows you to enter instructions on how to get to the development.

Field

Description
Directions Instructions on how to get to this development.
Sales Summary The sales summary for this development.
Other Information Any additional sales information for this development
