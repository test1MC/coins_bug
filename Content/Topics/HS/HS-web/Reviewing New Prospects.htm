Reviewing New Prospects

 

The Prospects Workbench includes a new option to review new prospects received via the HS connection interface.  This allows you the option to check for duplicates and allows the option of merging new details with a current prospect record already in .  The ‘To Review’ tab shows a list of new prospects received see below:

 

 

From here you can either mark them as reviewed using the ‘Choose action’ or select to view the possible duplicates by clicking on the link in the ‘Possible Duplicates’ column.

Clicking on the possible duplicates link will then take you to a screen which shows a list of potential duplicates and details the fields that match. 

If you want to merge the new record with one of these potential duplicates then click on the relevant merge button in the right hand column.  You will then be presented with a screen where you can then choose the fields to merge and also revise the level of interest.  Merging the records will update the current record with the selected new information and then delete the new prospect record.
