Managing Old Data

Over time, users of HB CRM will build up a database of many and person records. To assist with GDPR compliance, the following changes have been made:

  • The option for person records to be deleted automatically when a records is deleted; see New Parameter.
  • A new routine to delete records in bulk; see Deleting (Bulk).
  • A new routine to delete person records in bulk; see Deleting People (Bulk).
  • A new routine to merge or delete duplicate person records; see Removing Duplicate People Records.
