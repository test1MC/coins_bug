Deleting People (Bulk)

The People Information Workbench contains records of people that are associated with other records in , such as contact records or records. If the associated records are deleted, the people records are not necessarily also deleted. Under GDPR you should only retain records where there is a legitimate reason, so you can use Bulk Delete People to delete people records that are not linked to any other record.

  1. Go to Bulk Delete People.Bulk Delete People Screen

  2. Fill in the fields:
    Field
    Description

    Date Created

    Enter a range of dates; only people records that were created in this date range will be selected. If this is left blank then no check on the date created will be done.

    Report Mode
    this box to produce a report showing which records will be deleted, without deleting any records. Clear the box to delete the records.
  3. Click .

The report will show all people matching the selection criteria. Only people where there are no links to any other records in any will be included. If report mode has not been selected then the people records will be deleted.
