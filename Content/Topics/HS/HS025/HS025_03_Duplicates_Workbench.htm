Removing Duplicate People Records

The Duplicate Workbench on the HB CRM Administration menu allows you to identify and merge duplicate people records.

  1. Go to Duplicates Workbench.
  2. From the Options menu, choose Search for Duplicates.
  3. Choose output options for the report and click .

    This will search the People Information Workbench for potential duplicates against people related to the logged-in . This runs as a report, and may take some time if there are large numbers of PIW records.

    When the search has completed, the browse shows any potential duplicate records, with the potential match and the fields that match between the two records. A potential pair of duplicate people records is only shown once in the browse.
