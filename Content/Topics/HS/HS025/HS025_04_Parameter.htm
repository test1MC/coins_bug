New Parameter

A new HS parameter controls whether person records are deleted when a record is deleted from the Workbench.

Parameter Description

HS/PWDELPIW

Whether to delete the associated person record or records when a prospect is deleted from the Prospects Workbench (Y or N).

Y = If the associated person records are not linked to any other record in COINS, they will be deleted when the prospect is deleted.

N = When a prospect is deleted, associated person records are not deleted. They can be deleted manually, or using Bulk Delete People (see Deleting People (Bulk)).
