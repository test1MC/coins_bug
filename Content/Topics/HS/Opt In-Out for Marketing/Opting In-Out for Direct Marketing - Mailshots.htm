Consent for Direct Marketing - Mailshots

If the new configurable consent categories are in use, only people with the relevant consent category set to Yes can be selected for inclusion in the mailshot.

  • If the destination medium is “Word Mail Merge”, the relevant category is the one that is mapped to “Do Not Mail”.
  • If the destination medium is email or Communigator, the relevant category is the one that is mapped to “Do Not Email”.
  • If the destination medium is SMS/text, the relevant category is the one that is mapped to “Do Not SMS”.
