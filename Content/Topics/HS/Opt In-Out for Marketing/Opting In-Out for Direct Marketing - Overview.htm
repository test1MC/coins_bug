Consent for Direct Marketing - Overview

Under the new General Data Protection Regulations which come into force in the UK from May 2018, companies involved in any direct marketing need to be able to provide evidence that individuals have positively opted in to receive such marketing.

New configurable Configurable data protection fields on the Person records in House Sales can record the type of consent and the source of consent for each, along with the ability to record comments and upload documents. Auditing of the Person records has been extended to give a more user-friendly gives a view of the history of changes to these fields.

Data protection rules cannot be overridden when running mailshots, etc.

A data migration routine converts existing consent data to the new format.
