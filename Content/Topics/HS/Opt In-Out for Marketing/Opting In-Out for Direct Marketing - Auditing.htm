Consent for Direct Marketing - Auditing

The People Workbench audit functionality has been enhanced to include a new includes a Consents Audit History tab which shows an audit of the changes to the consent categories.

The standard note flag indicator is used to indicate if any ‘Audit Consent Comments’ were entered by the user. A tooltip on this flag shows the notes or comments entered by the user.

The standard document flag is shown if a document was uploaded when the consent category was updated. Clicking on the icon opens the document.

The person audit functionality is controlled by the House Sales parameter PPAHIST. This needs to be set to “Y” to enable this functionality.

Customer Workbench

The personal details section of the Customer Workbench has been amended to show shows the current person audit history and the new consent category-specific auditing.
