View Campaigns

View Campaigns is a new function that allows you to see details of campaigns sent to Communigator. The initial browse lists the campaigns.

View Campaigns Browse

Clicking the button displays a summary of the campaign, with gauges to show the percentage of emails opened (Opens), the percentage of links clicked (Clicks), and the ratio of Clicks to Opens.

View  Campaigns – Details

A link on the Ref column opens a summary page. The Main tab shows the same details as the Detail button.

Campaign Summary – Main Tab

The Recipients tab lists the recipients of the campaign.

Campaign Summary – Recipients Tab

The Interactions tab shows details of who interacted with the campaign, and how (for example, which emails they opened, which links they clicked).

Campaign Summary – Interactions Tab

The button displays the email.
