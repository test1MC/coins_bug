Sales Staff

 

Sales Staff allows you to maintain the staff lists that are used in the House Sales, Customer Care, Lettings and Inspections modules.

 

From the House Sales Module select Plot Sales > Setup > Sales Staff

Click

 

Field

Description
Setup Details for the sales staff member.
Staff Code The sales staff code.
Name The staff member's name.
Job Title The job title of the sales staff member.
Telephone Number The telephone number of the sales staff member.
Mobile Number The mobile telephone number of the sales staff member.
Fax Number The fax number of the sales staff member.
Email The email address of the sales staff member.
Discount/Incentive Level The maximum level of Discount and Incentive that this user can give without the reservation needing to be authorised.
Sales Manager The Sales Manager of the staff member
Sales Director The Sales Director of the staff member
Start Time Working Day Start Time
End Time Working Day End Time
Dormant Is this sales staff record dormant and should no longer be used?
Roles The roles the staff member performs.
Plot Sales Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the Sales Workbench.
Part Exchange Sales Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the Part Exchange Workbench.
Sales Manager Whether this Sales Staff member is the manager of a sales team.
Sales Director Whether this Sales Staff member is the director of a sales team.
Call/Issue Manager Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the Customer Care call and issue manager dropdowns.
Inspector Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the Inspection Workbench.
Site Agent Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the development maintenance site agent field.
Lettings Advisor Whether this sales staff member is available to select in the Lettings Workbench.
Customer Care Tasks Whether this sales staff member can have Customer Care tasks assigned to them.
Scheduling Operative Whether this person appears as an on the task scheduling screen.
Scheduling TBA Whether this user appears as a "TBA" on the task scheduling screen.
Other Scheduling Whether this person appears on the task scheduling screen as "Other Staff".
