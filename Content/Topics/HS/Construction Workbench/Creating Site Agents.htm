Creating Site Agents

From the House Sales Module select Plot Sales.

Select Setup then select Sales Staff.

Create the Sales Staff Record

Select the Add button.

Enter the following Setup information:

 

Field Content Comments Buttons
Staff Code: Free text The sales staff code.  
Name: Free text The staff member's name.  
Job Title: Free text The staff member’s Job Title.  
Telephone Number: Numerical The telephone number of the sales staff member.  
Fax Number: Numerical The fax number of the sales staff member.  
E Mail: Free text The email address of the sales staff member.  

Click the Save button.

 

Enter the following Roles information:

 

Field Content Comments Buttons
Site Agent: Tick  
Customer Care Tasks: Tick  

 

Click the Save button.

 

Continue to enter all Site Agents.

 

Select the Undo button to return to the Sales Staff workbench

 

‘%’  Note – The Sales staff workbench lists Sales and Customer Care staff as well as Site Agents.

‘%’  Note – Additional selections will need to be made for Site staff that also carry out Inspections or are to be included in the Customer Services Scheduler.
