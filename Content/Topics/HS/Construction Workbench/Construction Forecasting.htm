Construction Forecasting

The Construction Gantt Chart allows the User to Create and maintain the construction forecasts for the Development.

There are four Development Forecasts / Budgets:

  • BD01 Annual Budget Also known as the Land Forecast / Budget
  • BD02 Revised Budget Also known as the Site Start Forecast / Budget
  • FC01 Current Forecast Also known as the Current Forecast / Budget
  • FC02 Revised Forecast Also known as the “what if” Forecast / Budget

This Section will cover creating and managing the Construction Forecast using the Gantt Chart.
