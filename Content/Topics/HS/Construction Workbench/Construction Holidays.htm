Construction Holidays

 

Construction Holidays allows you to maintain a list of construction holiday dates with a description against each.  These dates will then be displayed as holidays on the Gantt chart style construction programme reports.  In calculating construction stage start dates from end dates and durations, these dates will be excluded.

 

Create a Construction Holiday

From the House Sales Module select Plot Sales > Setup > Construction Holidays

Remember you can use the button for holidays with the same description, such as Christmas Holiday.

Are you using a 5 day Construction Week?  If so remember to set Construction Holiday stand down as a 5 day week.

Avoid standard Bank Holidays such as Easter and May Day.  These will push your programme out by a day.  Especially if you prefer a build Stage to end on a Friday or are using a 5 day construction week.
