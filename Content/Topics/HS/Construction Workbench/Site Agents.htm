Site Agents

 

Site Staff can be created for your Company and then assigned to a Development. The Site Agent can be used for reporting purposes and also for scheduling within the Customer Care Workbench.

mSite will also use the Site Agent that has been assigned to the Development.

 

The Sales staff workbench lists Sales and Customer Care staff as well as Site Agents.
Additional selections will need to be made for Site staff that also carry out Inspections or are to be included in the Customer Services Scheduler.
