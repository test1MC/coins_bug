Planned Gaps within the Construction Forecast

Often gaps need to be programmed into the Gantt Chart.  These can be System Generated due to Construction Holidays (covered in Chapter 2), or Planned Gaps in the Plot build such as allowing Groundworks for multiple Plots, where the remainder of the build will start at a much later date.

System Generated Gaps

If you have set up Construction Holidays, the System will automatically plan any gaps into your Construction Programme.

Any Stage that starts or ends within the Construction holiday will automatically be extended to finish after the Holiday but still within the stage time frame.  i.e. a 5 day stage that is due to end during a 5 day Holiday will extend to be 10 days, with the Holiday 5 days being grey indicating no actual build activity.
