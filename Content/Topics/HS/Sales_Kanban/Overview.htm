House Sales Kanban - Overview

A new screen has been introduced that allows you to view plots on a in a 'Kanban' formatA Kanban (看板) board shows stages in a process as columns, with cards in each column representing items of work., with key events along the screen in columns, and plots shown as cards under the relevant column. You can update plots by dragging them from one column to another, and can perform various actions by clicking icons on the cards.
