Bespoke Choices

Bespoke choices are choices that are not part of the pre-defined list of standard choices.

To record a bespoke choice

  1. Click the button.
  2. Complete the information on the Choice Details frame.

  3. Click .

The bespoke choice must then be reviewed by the commercial team. Depending on how is set up, saving the choice may launch a workflow.

When the bespoke choice has been reviewed, the tooltip shows the review status.
