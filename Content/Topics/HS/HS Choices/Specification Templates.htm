Specification Templates

Specification Templates allows you to set up pre-configured combinations of areas, items and choices. This reduces the effort required to configure the specifications and choices available for a development, house type or plot.

When you have created a template record, the Generate Template option allows you to populate the template with selected standard specification areas, items and choices. You can also add or update these manually.

  • When you add a new area, the standard specification items linked to the specification area, and the standard choices associated with each item, are also added.

  • When you add a new item, the standard specification choices linked to the specification item are also added.

The Pricing tab allows you to override specific prices from the standard choice price. It shows a browse of all the choices contained in the template that are marked as Chargeable Choice.

If a choice has a single price, you can update it on the browse. If the choice is priced by number of bedrooms or floor area, the link in the Level column allows you to update the price breakdown.
