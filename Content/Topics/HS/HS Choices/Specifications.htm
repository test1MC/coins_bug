Specifications

A specification is what is being installed in the plot. It can include the standard specification for the plot (which kitchen, bathroom etc), and also defines the available choices for the purchaser. These can be free choices or chargeable upgrades.

The specification is made up of different layers.

  • Specification Areas are the top-level structure. This could be based on location, or a combination of location and choices such as paint or flooring.
  • Specification Items define each of the items within an area where a choice is available.
  • Specification Choices are the list of choices that are available for each item.
