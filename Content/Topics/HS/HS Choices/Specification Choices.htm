Specification Choices

Specification Choices is a new function that allows you to input the detailed choices a customer can make. For example, the choice of kitchen units or worktops.

An add-type selector allows you to choose between adding a standard choice and a bespoke choice.

Details of a specification choice include how the choice can be user (whether it is standard, no cost or chargeable), how it is priced, and when it must be chosen or installed. See the side-frame help for details of individual fields.

Images for Choices

The paperclip button allows you to upload images for each choice. Assign one image to the “thumbnail” document group, and assign others to the “images” document group. The thumbnail shows as a small image against the choice on the Customer Choices screen, and a larger version, along with any other images attached to the choice, can be viewed by clicking the thumbnail. The new HS parameters CHODMGRP and CHODMTN determine the document groups to use for images and thumbnails respectively.

Price Breakdown

Choices can have a single price, or be priced by number of bedrooms or the floor area of the plot. If the Pricing Method is set to Number of Bedrooms or Floor Area, a link on the Choice column opens the Specification Choice Price Breakdown screen, which allows you to enter the price breakdown for the choice.
