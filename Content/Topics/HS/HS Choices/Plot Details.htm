House Sales Choices - Plot Details Changes

If HS Choices are being used, the following changes are made to the Sales Workbench plot details.

Choice Status

A new field, Choice Sales Status, shows how many of the choices that have been selected by the purchaser are at each sales status (for example: 3 Requested, 2 ordered).

A new field, Choice Construction Status, shows how many of the choices that have been selected by the purchaser are at each construction status (for example: 2 To be processed, 1 Fitted).

The Sales Extras price field is replaced with a new Chargeable Choices field which shows the total value of all the choices to be charged to the purchaser.

Choices Summary Tab

The choices summary tab uses the new choices functionality, and also shows the Choice Sales Status and Choice Construction Status fields. The amount fields show the total values of all the choices to be charged to the purchaser.

Choices Tab

The extras and options tabs are replaced with a single new Choices tab. This is a browse of the choices to be installed on the plot, including the standard specification and the selected customer choices.

The new Choices tab is also available on the Workbench.
