House Sales Customer Choices

When updating a plot, you can record customer choices.

To record customer choices for a plot

  1. On the plot summary record, click the Choices link.

    This opens a new window that lists the choices available for the plot.

    The left-hand side of the screen shows the areas, the number of items already chosen in each area, and the total price for the choices in each area. The right-hand side shows the items (the things that the customer can choose) for the area you select.

  2. Click an item heading to show the choices available.

    If there is a standard choice for the item, it shows a green , to indicate that it is selected by default. The other choices available are listed, along with their prices.

    NOTE

    Prices

    The price shown is the total price to be charged for the choice, including . If the choice is priced based on quantity, and has not been selected, the unit price and unit of measure are shown. If the choice has been selected, the total price is shown, along with the quantity and unit of measure.

    If there is a thumbnail image, you can click this to show a larger view of the image, and any other images that have been set up against the choice.

    If the description of the choice is highlighted as a link, this opens a web page (usually a page on the supplier’s website) that gives more details about the choice.

  3. If the purchaser chooses one of the choices, click the button next to the choice.

  4. Complete the information on the Choice Details frame and click .

    The Price column shows a green to indicate that the choice has been selected, and the Choice column shows the sales and construction statuses.

  5. To update the details of a selected choice, click the button next to it.
  6. To cancel a selected choice, click the button next to the choice and click OK at the prompt. This unselects the choice, and if there is a standard choice for the item, selects that instead.
