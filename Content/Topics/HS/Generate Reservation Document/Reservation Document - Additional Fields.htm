Reservation Document - Additional Fields

The following additional RO fields are available for use on configurable reservation documents.

Plot Fields

Field

Returns
RO_vwb_ConStageAtRes The actual construction stage as at the reservation date.
RO_vwb_DIList This existing field has been enhanced to allow two new options so it can return a list of discount and incentives in the same format as the sales mail merge data file fields <Cash_Discs_and_Incs> and <Non_Cash_Discs_and_Incs>.
RO_vwb_purSolDX

The DX number of the purchaser’s solicitor.

This and similar fields are array fields with an array of 1 to 4 (1 being the main purchaser, 2 the second joint purchaser, and so on), so RO_vwb_purSolDX__1 is the DX number of the main purchaser’s solicitor.
RO_vwb_purSolFax The fax number of the contact at the purchaser’s solicitor. If no contact fax number is available then the fax number for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_purSolContact The name of the contact at the purchaser’s solicitor.
RO_vwb_purSolEmail The email address of the contact at the purchaser’s solicitor. If no email is available then the email for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_purMorDX The DX number of the purchaser’s mortgage lender.
RO_vwb_purMorFax The fax number of the contact at the purchaser’s mortgage lender. If no contact fax number is available then the fax number for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_purMorContact The name of the contact at the purchaser’s mortgage lender.
RO_vwb_purMorEmail The email address of the contact at the purchaser’s mortgage lender. If no email is available then the email for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_purFinFax The fax number of the contact at the purchaser’s financial advisor. If no contact fax number is available then the fax number for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_purFinContact The name of the contact at the purchaser’s financial advisor.
RO_vwb_purFinEmail The email address of the contact at the purchaser’s financial advisor. If no email is available then the email for the office is returned.
RO_vwb_ResDeposit The reservation deposit amount.
RO_vwb_ResComments The comments from the reservation event.
RO_vwb_SalesRep This existing field has been extended to also allow the telephone number and email to be returned.
RO_vwb_ResExchDeposit The exchange deposit required at reservation.

Part Exchange Fields

Field

Returns
RO_hpx_leaseyr The number of full years remaining on the lease (fractions of a year are ignored). For freehold properties this returns blank.
RO_hpx_ManCompany The company name of the management company.
RO_hpx_ManOffice The office name of the management company.
RO_hpx_ManContact The name of the contact at the management company.
RO_hpx_ManPhone The telephone number of the contact at the management company. If no contact telephone is available then the phone number for the office is returned.
RO_hpx_ManAdd[1-4] The address of the management company.
RO_hpx_ManPCode The of the management company.
RO_hpx_ManEmail The email of the contact at the management company.

Prospect Fields

Field

Returns
RO_hvi_EstCompany The company name of the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstOffice The office name of the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstContact The name of the contact at the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstPhone The telephone number of the contact at the estate agent. If no contact telephone is available then the phone number for the office is returned.
RO_hvi_EstAdd[1-4] The address of the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstPCode The post code of the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstEmail The email of the contact at the estate agent.
RO_hvi_EstDXNum The DX number the estate agent.
