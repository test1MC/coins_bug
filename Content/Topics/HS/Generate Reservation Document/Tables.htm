Generate Reservation Document - Tables

The following tables are available for the reservation document:

  • vp_wbsdef – Plot
  • vp_unitdef – House Type
  • co_config – company details
  • jc_job - Contract
  • vp_site - Development
  • hs_staff – Sales staff details for reservation sales advisor
  • hs_patex – Part Exchange
  • hs_parkspace – Parking space 1
  • hs_parkspace2 – Parking space 2
  • hs_purchaser – Purchaser 1
  • hs_purchaser2 – Purchaser 2
  • pp_person – PIW details for purchaser 1
  • pp_person2 – PIW details for purchaser 2
  • hs_visitor – Associated prospect record (for the main contact)
  • hs_extra – Extras details, this is available in the extras body section
  • hs_trans – Incentive details, this is available in the discount and incentive body sections.

NOTE

To use hs_parkspace2, hs_purchaser2 and pp_person2 you need to prefix the table and field with the text TABLE, for example TABLE.pp_person2.ppp_sname would give the second purchasers surname.
