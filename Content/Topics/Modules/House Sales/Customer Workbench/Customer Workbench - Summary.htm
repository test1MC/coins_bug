Customer Workbench - Summary

The main customer summary page shows an overview of the customer journey. This page is split into accordion style sections.

Customer Summary

The Customer Summary section shows an overview of where the customer is in the journey from being a through to customer care.

Customer Journey

From here you can go to the relevant workbench by clicking any of the links. The warranty column will be coloured red when the plot is out of warranty. Details for all companies are shown regardless of company security so you can see an overview of the person’s relationship with their company. If you do not have access to the company then no links will be available on those records.

The main headings for the customer journey (Prospect, Sales, Customer Care and Warranty) are greyed out if the person has not reached that stage of the journey. In the example below the person is only a prospect.

Customer Journey - Prospect

Below the Customer Journey display is a layout that shows the customer details.

Customer Details

Buttons allow you to generate a mail merge letter, or send an email or text. You will be prompted for the record to which it relates (for example, prospect, plot, or customer care call).

Open Actions

The Open Actions section shows a browse of the open and overdue actions that are related to the customer. This includes general actions, call and issue actions that exist against the following records in House Sales and Customer Care:

  • Prospect Records
  • Pre-release Reservations
  • Plot Sales
  • Part Exchanges
  • Cancellations
  • Customer Care

The Linked To column shows details of the record to which the action belongs. A link on this column opens the relevant workbench page in a new desktop frame. An update option allows the action to be updated.

History

The History section shows a browse of the history records that are related to the customer. This includes general history, call and issue history records that exist against the following records in House Sales and Customer Care:

  • Prospect Records
  • Pre-release Reservations
  • Plot Sales
  • Part Exchanges
  • Cancellations
  • Customer Care

The Linked To column shows details of the record to which the history reocrd belongs. A link on this column opens the relevant workbench page in a new desktop frame.

Customer Journey

The Customer Journey section shows details of the progression for a selected plot. This shows the same information as the existing plot progression page.

As the customer could be purchasing multiple plots, a drop down allows you to select the plot to show. By default the last plot reserved will be shown.

Customer Calls

The Customer Calls section shows a browse of the call records that are related to the customer. This shows the same information as the Customer Care Workbench Calls tab.

Customer Issues

The Customer Issues section shows a browse of the issue records that are related to the customer. This shows the same information as the Customer Care Workbench Issues tab.

Customer Tasks

The Customer Tasks section shows a browse of the task records that are related to the customer. This shows the same information as the Customer Care Workbench Tasks tab.

Documents

The documents section shows a browse of the documents in Document Manager that are related to the customer. This includes call, issue and task documents that are related the following areas in House Sales and Customer Care:

  • Prospect Records
  • Pre-release Reservations
  • Plot Sales
  • Part Exchanges
  • Cancellations
  • Customer Care

The Linked To column shows details of the record to which the document belongs. A link on this column opens the relevant workbench page in a new desktop frame.

Personal Details

The personal details shows and allows update of the personal details for a person. This is the same details as in the People Information Workbench.

Related People

The related people section shows a browse of the people that are related to the customer, for example as a joint prospect, joint purchaser or customer care contact. This includes people that are recorded in the following areas in House Sales and Customer Care:

  • Prospect Records
  • Pre-release Reservations
  • Plot Sales
  • Part Exchanges
  • Cancellations
  • Customer Care

The Linked To column shows details of the record to which the person belongs. A link on this column opens the relevant workbench page in a new desktop frame.

Links

The links section shows a browse of the records that are related to the customer. This shows similar information to the existing ‘Links’ options in House Sales and Customer Care but also includes links to a Part Exchange property when the seller of the property is the same as the purchaser of the plot.

Desktop Search

When searching for people in the desktop search, the link against the name now takes you to the new Customer Workbench.
