Customer Workbench - Overview

A new Customer Workbench provides a quick way of seeing an overview of a customer across all parts of the customer journey (from prospect through to customer service). This is divided into the following sections using accordion-style tabs:

  • Customer Summary - an overview of where the customer is in the journey from being a through to customer care, with links to the appropriate workbenches
  • Open Actions
  • History
  • Customer Journey
  • Customer Calls
  • Customer Issues
  • Customer Tasks
  • Documents
  • Personal Details
  • Related People
  • Links

The new Customer Workbench is also available from both the ‘’ and ‘Sales’ sections of the site sales desktop.
