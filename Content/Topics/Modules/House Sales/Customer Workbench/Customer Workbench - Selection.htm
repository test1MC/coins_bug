Customer Workbench - Selection

The first screen of the Customer Workbench allows you to select the customer. You can either search for the customer or select them from a standard OA browse.

Search Tab

The Search tab allows you to search for a customer using their name, address, , email address or telephone numbers.

Fields

Field
Description

Search Criteria

A search field in which you can type part of the customer’s name, address, , email address or telephone numbers.

Current Company

A box. When ed, the search is restricted to people associated with the current company. This box is only shown if you have access to the database view.

As you type your search criteria, a list of possible results is shown in the search results box.

Click the link on the Surname field to go to the customer summary.

By Customer Tab

The By Customer tab allows you to select the customer from a standard browse. Click the link on the Surname field to go to the customer summary.
