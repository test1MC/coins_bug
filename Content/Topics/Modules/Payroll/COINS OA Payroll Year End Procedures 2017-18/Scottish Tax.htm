Scottish Tax

Any employee who lives in Scotland is subject to income tax in Scotland. We advise all employers to set up the Scottish tax rates/bands in case they get employees with a Scottish tax code (that is, prefixed with ‘S’).

From April 2018 Scotland will increase their tax banding from 3 to 5 tax bands. Tax band 1 will be a new starter rate band, and tax band 2 will be the basic rate band (remaining at 20%) at which pension providers will apply tax relief on employee pension contributions.
