Amend the Tax Tables, National Insurance and SSP/SMP/SAP/SPP Tables

The Tax and NI tables have been combined into one function in current versions of .

NOTE

The annual details can only be viewed in OA.

  1. Go to Payroll > Global Setup > Annual Details for Tax Year.
  2. Ensure the new NI, Tax and Statutory Payment rates are as shown in the example screen shot below:


    The tax year is now indicated at the top left hand corner.

  3. Enter the new rates for NI and Tax by clicking Open and completing the relevant fields.

    uses the date in the Payroll Configuration screen assigned to the pay frequency to derive the year applicable for tax rates, and thus refers to the appropriate year’s Tax NI, SSP and SMP/SPP/SAP table at the point of payroll calculation.

  4. Once the rates have been entered click Save to accept the details.
