P60 Forms

HMRC rules state that Employers may use substitute forms of proprietary make or of their own design provided that:

  • The employer bears the whole cost
  • The content and design meets the stringent requirements for box sizes and layout, ink, and paper-type
  • The design is approved by HMRC each year before being brought into use

Designs of proprietary forms are negotiated between suppliers producing the forms and HMRC. Anyone producing a manual substitute P60 for - must obtain HMRC approval before the forms are brought into use.
