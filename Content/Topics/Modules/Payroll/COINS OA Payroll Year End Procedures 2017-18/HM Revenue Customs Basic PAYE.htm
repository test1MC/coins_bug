HM Revenue & Customs Basic PAYE Tools

The CD-ROM and Employer’s Pack has now been replaced with the downloadable Basic PAYE Tools.

You can download the Tools straight to your computer from www.businesslink.gov.uk/basicpayetools.

Once downloaded you will be able to transfer the information from your Employer CD-ROM database and complete the year end - and start -.
