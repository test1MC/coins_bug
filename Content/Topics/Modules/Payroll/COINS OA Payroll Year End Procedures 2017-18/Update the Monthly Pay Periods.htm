Update the Monthly Pay Periods

Update the monthly pay periods with the Periods Maintenance procedure using the following example as a guide.

NOTE

Weekly periods will be automatically created once the payroll year-end clearing routine has been run, thus you do not need to manually update as in Monthly.

  1. Payroll menu > Monthly Payroll > Setup > Periods Maintenance.

    Any General Ledger periods not set up yet will not be available to the payroll.

  2. Go to Payroll > Monthly Payroll > Monthly Setup > Periods Maintenance.

    The following screen is displayed to allow you to update the monthly pay periods.

    Any General Ledger periods not set up yet will not be available to the payroll.

  3. Enter the dates for all 12 periods.

    You can add the G/L Dates later when these are available.

  4. Press F1 to accept and save the changes.

You have now completed the year end routine and may run your normal payroll activities in the first period.
