RTI Submissions – FPS and EPS

NOTE

This can only be done in OA.

  1. On Payroll > Global Functions > HMRC Workbench > RTI tab, create an FPS, and the Final Submission for Year box. 

  2. Submit the FPS as normal.

  3. Create an EPS, and the Final Submission for Year box.

  4. Complete the required fields on the Recoverable Amounts Year to Date tab.

  5. Then submit the EPS.

You have now completed your End of Year submission.
