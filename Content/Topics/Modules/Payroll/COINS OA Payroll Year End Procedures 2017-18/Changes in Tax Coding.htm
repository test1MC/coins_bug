Changes in Tax Coding

There will be a general uplift of tax codes with suffix ‘L’.

Full instructions will be issued to employers on form P9X () ‘Tax codes to use from 6 April ’.

As a general rule, unless an amended code notification has been received on a form P9(T) (or an internet or Electronic Data Interchange equivalent) employers should amend - codes as follows:

  • ‘L’ suffix codes – increase by 35; code 1150L becomes 1185L.
  • The PAYE annual allowance with effect from 6 April is raised to £228 per week (£988 per month).
  • The code for emergency use with effect from 6 April is 1185L W1 or 1185L M1 or 1185L X.
