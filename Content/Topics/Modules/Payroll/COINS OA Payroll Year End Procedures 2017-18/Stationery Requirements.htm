Stationery Requirements

The P60 Form format as supported by is the P60 (Single Sheet -) Certificate of Pay. This is a single sheet laser form and you will require a laser printer.

OA will print the approved P60 form fully formatted and so without the need for HMRC stationery to be ordered. You will need a laser printer.

NOTE

HMRC have not yet released details of the new format for the P60 so please refer to their website for any updated details.

For more information, see: http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/ebu/p60snglsheet.pdf

P14/P35 forms no longer need to be printed as these will be submitted online and retained within for future reference.
