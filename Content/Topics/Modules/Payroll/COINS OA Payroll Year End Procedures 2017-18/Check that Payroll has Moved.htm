Check that Payroll has Moved Successfully into the New Tax Year

Once the Start new pay period has finished you should check the Year end routine has completed correctly and that the Payroll has moved successfully into the new tax year.

  1. Check employee details by checking the YTD’s in the Sequence Items Tab.
  2. Go to Payroll > Period Payroll > Company Setup > Payroll Configuration.

  3. Check the details.
