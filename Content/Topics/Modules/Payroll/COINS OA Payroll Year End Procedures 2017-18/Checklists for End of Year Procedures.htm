Checklists for End of Year Procedures

Throughout this document when reference is made to performing procedures within ALL payrolls, this refers to the weekly, fortnightly, four weekly and monthly payrolls.

The Payroll Year-End Routine must be run for each payroll frequency (for example, weekly and monthly), and for each company on the system.

The document has been produced based on the Monthly payroll but has reference to variable procedures between Monthly and Weekly pay frequencies.
