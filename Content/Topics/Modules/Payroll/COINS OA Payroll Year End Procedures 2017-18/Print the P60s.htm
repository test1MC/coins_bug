Print the P60s

Once reconciled to your satisfaction, print the P60s on the new HM Revenue & Customs Stationery.

Follow the instructions outlined in section 0.0.1 (Print Sample P60s – Alignment Test), this time print a P60 for ALL employees. A summary P60 is printed at the end showing the run totals for reconciliation, and is not intended for submission to HMRC.

For details of how to set up the P60 configurable document, see P60 Setup.
