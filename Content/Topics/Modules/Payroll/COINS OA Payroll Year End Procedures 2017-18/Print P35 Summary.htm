Print P35 Summary

Print the P35 summary for your records.

  1. Go to Monthly Payroll > Reports > P35 Report.

    Because the P35 is submitted electronically, you do not need to include leavers.

  2. Go to Payroll > Period Payroll > Period Administration > P35 Print.

    Because P35 is submitted electronically, you do not need to include leavers.
