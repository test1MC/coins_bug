Serious Ill Health Lump Sum

From 2017 a new box is available on Employee Maintenance to report when a serious ill health lump sum payment is made to someone who is over 75.

Employee Maintenance Profile Tab

Field
Description

Serious Ill Health Lump Sum

If ed, this indicates that a serious ill health lump sum payment has been made to the employee. This will then be reported in the FPS and the RTI Report.

You will only be allowed to this box if the employee is or will be 75 or older on the current period’s pay date for this employee.

An equivalent change has been made in COINSplus.
