Employee Auto-Numbering - Transfer Employees

Transfer Employees is the non-US/Australian/Canadian payroll menu option for transferring from one pay frequency to another within the same company. Previously this simply added a prefix to the existing employee number. The new numbers will now be set using the format defined in PR/EMPCODEF.

When auto-generating an employee number or checking its validity (whether auto-generated or manually entered), the utility will check for any clash with either another Payroll employee number or another HR personnel ID.
