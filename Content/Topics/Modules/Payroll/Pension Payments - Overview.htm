FPS Reporting Changes

Start Date Reporting

For a pension type employee where the Pension Death Benefit flag is ed and neither the Flexibly Accessing Pension flag nor the new Serious Ill Health Lump Sum (see 1, Serious Ill Health Lump Sum) flags are ed, there is zero annual pension and there is no taxable pay component (in either the overall taxable pay or the pension taxable pay field), then no start date is included on the first FPS. This change applies already in 2016.

Pay Frequency Reporting

For a pension type employee where the Flexibly Accessing Pension flag, the Pension Death Benefit flag, or the new Serious Ill Health Lump Sum flag is ed, the correct pay frequency to report can be one-off, irregular or annual instead of the normal pay frequency for the payroll the employee is on. In this situation, a new field is available on the Real Time Information layout in Employee Maintenance.

Employee Maintenance Screen

Field
Description

Pay Frequency

The pay frequency to report. The options are:

  • Normal – to be used when part of the pension fund is withdrawn and payments are made each period (this is the default).
  • One-Off – to be used when the whole pension fund is withdrawn in a single payment.
  • Irregular – to be used when part of the pension fund is withdrawn and payments are irregular.
  • Annual – to be used when part of the pension fund is withdrawn and payments are annual.

An equivalent change has been made in COINSplus.
