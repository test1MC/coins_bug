Apprenticeship Levy - Annual Details for Tax Year

Two new fields have been added to the annual details screen.

Annual Details for Tax Year Screen

Field
Description

Rate

The apprenticeship levy rate. This is a percentage of the annual pay bill and is used to calculate the levy liability.

Allowance

The annual levy allowance; this offsets the levy liability (so if the levy liability is less than the allowance, no levy is payable).

When you create the 2017 record, the rate (0.5%) and the allowance (£15,000) are defaulted in.

In future years, the values will be copied from the previous year; if the rate or allowance changes, you will need to update the fields.
