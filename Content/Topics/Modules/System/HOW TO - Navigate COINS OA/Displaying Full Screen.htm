Displaying Full Screen

You may need to look at the full screen when referring to information in an or browse screen. It will hide the toolbars and taskbar at the top of your screen and also the taskbar at the bottom of the screen.

  1. Select the F11 key on your keyboard to remove the panel at the top of the screen. This will now display the full screen view. The taskbar will also be hidden.

  2. To return the panel, select the F11 key again.
  3. Whilst in Full-screen mode, to show the Toolbar panel occasionally, move the cursor to the top of the screen and the panel will dropdown and is displayed. It will hide again as soon as you move your cursor away.
