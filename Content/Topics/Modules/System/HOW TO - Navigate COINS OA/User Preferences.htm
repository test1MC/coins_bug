User Preferences

User Preferences will enable you, as a User, to specify your preferences, for example your password, your filter types and your favourites. By changing your favourites, it will override the company settings. Some of the options we mention here may have been omitted from your User Profile, the settings associated with your User Id, so you may not access to them.

  1. Select the User Preferences icon from the Global Shortcuts iconsUser Menu.

  2. This will display the User Preferences screen.
