Dim Sheet Input View

The Dim sheet input view field enable estimators and surveyors to build up the quantities for material based on the dimensions entered. For example if you are digging a trench for the foundations, the amount of material excavated would be calculated on the depth, width and length of trench. Dim sheets work like a spreadsheet and can calculate the total quantity excavated.

There are two options available for you to select and this will determine how you view your dim sheet. This is specific to you and your preference.

Traditional

This shows the dimension details going down the list, i.e. Length, width then depth. The total is displayed and you can add additional notes.

Modern

This is a Browse screen view with the dimension detail on the same line under separate columns.
