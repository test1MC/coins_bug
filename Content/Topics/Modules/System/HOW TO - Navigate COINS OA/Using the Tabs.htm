Using the Tabs

Many of the Browse screens in have tabs available. These are used to pre-filter the information for you. For example, list all the Material Purchase Orders (Material Orders Tab) or the Material Purchase Orders for a specific (By ).

These tabs are available to enable you to look at the same information in a different sort order, or restricting the data by adding a pre-filter, depending on the tab title. The tabs are named in relation to the sort or pre-filter.

The tab you are currently viewing will be highlighted, the remaining tabs will be show the background only. It is essential to note this, especially if there are more than two or three tabs, as is the case with the Hire Desk Browse screen in the Plant Control Module.
