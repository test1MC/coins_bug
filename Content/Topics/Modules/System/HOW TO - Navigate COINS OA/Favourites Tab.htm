Favourites Tab

The ites tab is a list of screens you will be using on a regular basis. These are listed on your home page and can be viewed in any module. It will enable you to select specific screens without navigating through the menu to locate the specific option.

The ites tab shows ites you have set up in earlier versions of . They are not really used any more; because you can add functions to your Desktop. However, your existing ites are still available on module home pages.

When selecting the ite it will be opened in a new window.

You can maintain the list of ites from here if required.
