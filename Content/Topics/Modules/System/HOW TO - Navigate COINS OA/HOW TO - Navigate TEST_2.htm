The Title Bar

The title bar on the browse screen will show exactly where you are within the system, like a breadcrumb trail. Subject to the configuration of within your own organisation, it may show the Module you are in, the Function you are running and your User ID.It shows the current company, the function you are running, and the module it belongs to.
