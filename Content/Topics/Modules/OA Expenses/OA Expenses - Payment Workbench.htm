OA Expenses - Payment Workbench

The Payments Workbench allows you to process the Expenses payment batches.

Payment Workbench

This screen provides standard payment batch functionality, with actions to freeze (or unfreeze), validate , and post a payment batch.

Payment Workbench Actions

Buttons on the rows allow you to print and create files.

A link on the batch number opens a read-only view of the payments in the batch.

When the payment batch is posted:

  • For payments made via Expenses, the control account will be debited and the paying bank's GL account will be credited.

  • For payments made via Payroll, the payment postings are part of the payroll cost batch.
