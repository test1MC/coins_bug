Customer Care Scheduler - Filtering

You can filter both the unscheduled tasks and the .

  1. Click the filter button above the To Schedule column.

  2. In the Filter, fill in the details of how you want to filter.

    Filter

    This includes the following options:

    • Development Number (with a lookup on developments)
    • Development Name (with a lookup on developments)
    • Plots (no lookup)
    • (with a lookup on staff).
    • Staff - This includes options to show only, other staff, TBA staff or all staff.
    • Unscheduled Tasks SLA - This includes the following options: All, Outside SLA, Out of SLA in 3 days, Out of SLA in 7 days.

    In the Development Number, Development Name, Plot and fields, you can use standard can-do lists.

  3. Click the Filter On button, then click OK.

The filter button shows a yellow icon to indicate that the filter is active.
