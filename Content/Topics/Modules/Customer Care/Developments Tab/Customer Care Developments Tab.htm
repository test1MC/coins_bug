Customer Care Developments Tab

The Customer Care Workbench Developments Tab has been updated to show information that is more relevant to Customer Care users, such as total open and closed tasks, calls and issues.

Customer Care Workbench Developments Tab

Column Sets

Column sets are now available to allow the user more flexibility over the information to show. The following fields are shown by default:

Field
Description

Development Number

Existing field.

A link from here takes the user to a browse of open subcontractor tasks for the development.

Open Internal Tasks

A link from here takes the user to a browse of open internal staff tasks for the development.

Issues Outside SLA

A link from here takes the user to a browse of open issues that are outside of SLA.

Legally Complete

Existing field.

Legal Completions Next 7 Days

This is a count of plots not yet legally complete where the expected legal completion date is in the next 7 days.

A link from here takes the user to a browse of plots not yet legally complete where the expected legal completion date is in the next 7 days.
