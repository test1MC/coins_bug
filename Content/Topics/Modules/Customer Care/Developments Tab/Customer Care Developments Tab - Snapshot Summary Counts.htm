Customer Care Developments Tab - Snapshot Summary Counts

In addition to the Customer Care Developments Tab - Live Summary Counts, a number of statistics need a snapshot to be run once a day to re-evaluate the counts etc. This applies particularly to time-dependent information like Issues outside of SLA, where an issue within SLA today may well be out of date tomorrow. In addition as information changes in during the day these counts will need to be updated to ensure the information they show is up to date.

As with the live summary counts, link records are also created for some of the counts to ensure the browse behind the count performs acceptably.

New reports are available to update the summary counts; see Customer Care Developments Tab - Customer Care Daily Snapshot.
