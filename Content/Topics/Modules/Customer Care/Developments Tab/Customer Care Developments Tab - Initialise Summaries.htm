Customer Care Developments Tab - Initiale Summaries

To perform an initial population of the new summary counts. a new report Initiale Customer Care Summaries is available. Initialation is not done automatically as part of an upgrade due to the potential time this may take to run.

Initiale Customer Care Summaries

Company selection is available so this can be run for individual companies, and inactive companies can be excluded. The selection is a list of company numbers (wildcards do not work).

In addition you can select whether to update call, issue, task and/or housing association plot counts.
