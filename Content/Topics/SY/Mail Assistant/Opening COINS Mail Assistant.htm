Opening Mail Assistant

The way to open Mail Assistant is different depending on whether you are using Outlook or accessing mail using a web browser, and on whether you are composing an email or reading a received email.

Note: the Mail Assistant button may have a different name, as it can be configured by your system administrator.

  1. If you are composing an email in Outlook, click the  Mail Assistant button in the message's ribbon:

  2. If you are composing an email using a web browser, the Mail Assistant button appears at the bottom right of the new email message. Click it to open Mail Assistant.

  3. If you are reading an email in Outlook, click the  Mail Assistant button in the body of the message (shown as " Staging" in this example):

    Mail Assistant opens in a frame within the message.

  4. If you are reading an email in a web browser, in the email message, click Mail Assistant.

    Mail Assistant opens in a frame within the message.

  5. If you have not logged in to , log in to your account, using your normal ID and password.
