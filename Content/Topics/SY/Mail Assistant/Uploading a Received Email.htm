Mail Assistant - Uploading a Received Email

When you receive an email, you can upload it to .

  1. Open Mail Assistant.
  2. Find the record you want to upload the message to.

    Finding a record from a contact

    1. If Mail Assistant detects that the email is from a person in the database, it will display the contact card for that person.

    2. Click the card.
    3. Click the tab for the workbench you want to upload the message to.

    Searching for a record

    1. Click the Search tab.
    2. Enter the details of the record you want to upload the message to.
    3. Click Search.

      Mail Assistant displays any matching records.

  3. In the record you want to upload the email to, click the Upload button .
  4. Click Save.

The Regarding button turns green to remind you that the message has been saved.
