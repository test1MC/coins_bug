Mail Assistant - Using a Email Template

If you have email templates set up in , you can use these when composing an email in Outlook or Office 365.

  1. Open Mail Assistant.
  2. Find the record that the email relates to.

    Finding a record that relates to someone you are sending to

    1. In any of the address fields (To:, Cc:, or Bcc:) type the email addresses of the people you want to send the email to.
    2. Click the Refresh button at the bottom of the Mail Assistant.

      Mail Assistant displays a list of contact cards for people from the database that match the email addresses you entered.

    3. Click a contact card.

    4. This shows Details of the person record in .

      The other tabs list the workbenches that are available. These show any records in those workbenches that relate to the person.

    5. Click the tab for the appropriate workbench and find the record that the email relates to.

    Searching for a record

    1. Click the Search tab.
    2. Enter the details of the record you want to upload the message to.
    3. Click Search.

      Mail Assistant displays any matching records.

  3. Click the Templates button.

  4. Select the template you want to use and click Insert.

    The email message is filled in with the content from the template, with any tokens filled in from the details on the record. The email addresses are populated from the contact details on the record.
