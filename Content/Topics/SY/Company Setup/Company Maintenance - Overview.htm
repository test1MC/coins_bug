Company Setup

The subjects covered by these notes are those that are not considered everyday tasks, but are ones that we are asked to do time and again and require a degree of forward planning and a considerations of the approach, method and tools to be used.

These subjects (in no particular order) cover:

  • Adding a new Company (kco)
  • Deleting a company
  • Merging companies
  • Sectorised companies vs separate companies
  • Sale and breakout of a company
  • Data load techniques
  • GL/JC structures
