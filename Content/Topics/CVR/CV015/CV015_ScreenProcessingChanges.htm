Configuration

A new CV parameter lets you decide whether you will allow for a page section for Risks & Opportunities.

Parameter

Description

CV/RSKUDFRM

Online Update of Data

Each time you add or change a Risk/Opportunity record, dependent Items are recalculated online.

When you delete a Risk/Opportunity record, all record-specific details are set to 0. Dependent Items on summaration levels must be recalculated.

Add, Copy, Generate New

When a new is added, and Models is configured to use the Risks & Opportunities Workbench, Risks & Opportunities records are copied to the new . Source records are used as follows:

  • A previous 's Risks & Opportunities are copied to a new on Copy or Add if it is not flagged as Latest and Copy Previous Period Adjustments is activated.
  • Otherwise, Risks & Opportunities are copied from .

Processing runs as part of the standard copying process of previous adjustments. Records are linked to the new by a reference number.

Attached documents and linked to the source R & O records are not linked to the newly created Risks/Opportunities. Links are only handled in the Latest where source records are joined to copied ones.

Update Items

New methods are now in place for Risk & Opportunity Items. Common methods (such as Summary levels recalculation, purge zero values, count records, etc.) have been modified to handle new Items.

SV Risks & Opportunities

If CV parameter USEBPFXT = Y, and the Latest is incomplete, the Risks & Opportunities is locked (that is, no Add/Update/Delete or Link/Unlink are allowed).

SV

If CV parameter USEBPFXT = Y, and the Latest is incomplete, any change in linked to R & O records updates the Latest ’s Risks & Opportunities Workbench. This involves the SV update and online recalculation of dependent Items (if configured for the R & O Workbench).

MK Project Risks

If CV parameter USEBPFXT = Y, and the Latest is incomplete, the Copy to Risks and Opportunities action creates records in the Latest ’s Risks & Opportunities Workbench. This creates appropriate records in .

When records are copied, Update Items is automatically run for Items defined as dependent on the Model Risk & Opportunities Workbench View.
