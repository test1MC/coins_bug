Overview

During a Period, a can go through a number of iterations and a number of statuses, for which there was previously no audit in  OA. COINS OA therefore was changed to hold and provide on screen a history of the various numbers and events that have occurred throughout the Period. You can now record history, and a new screen gives you a non-editable history of the life of the s throughout the period selected.

Changes made in the module to accommodate this functionality are discussed in this document.
