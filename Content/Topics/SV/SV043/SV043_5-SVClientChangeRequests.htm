SV Client

If an SC is created/linked from/to a Client with options for creating Items and Resources, any changes to pricing on the Client cause the recalculation of and values of the SC in SC Currency.

SC Created from Client

If an SC is created with Items and Resources from a Client , the and values on the SC and its relevant Items and Resources are calculated in Order Currency with the effective date as the SC entry date (if an SC is not approved) or the date on which the SC was first approved.

Client Value Updates

If a Client is linked to an SC , any and value updates on the Client and its relevant Items and Resources cause the recalculation of these values, Items, and Resources in Order Currency. The effective date used for finding an exchange rate is the SC entry date (if an SC is not approved) or the date on which the SC was first approved.

Linked SC Browse

If a Client is linked to one or many SC , the Linked SC tab is shown in the Client summary screen, as shown in Figure 1: Linked SC [%=Variables.Sv Variations caps%] Tab. A Currency column is shown after the Approved column. The Show Amounts in field (not shown below) is also available at the bottom of the screen on the Linked SC browse.
