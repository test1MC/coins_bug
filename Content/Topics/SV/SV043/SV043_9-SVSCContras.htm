SV SC Contras

Contras (Figure 1: Contras) can be created from Client or SC . When Contras are created from a Client , the currency conversion must be performed to get the default values. Since values are in Currency, all values used for creating a Contra from a must be converted from Currency to Order Currency.

Contras

Default values of Cost Claimed, Cost Agreed, and Cost C/Charged fields come from the Client . These values must be converted from Currency to Order Currency. The entry date of the Contra is used for finding exchange rates.

Cost distribution records are created from Contra . If a Contra originates from a , all values used for creating the cost distribution records are converted from Currency to Order Currency. The SC Certificate Date is used for finding exchange rates.
