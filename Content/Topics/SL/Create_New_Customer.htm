Creating a New Customer

After you have added an office to a company in Central Repository, you can add a new customer by allocating an account code to the office.

  1. Go to > Customers > Customer Maintenance.
  2. Click Add Customer

  3. Click the drop-down menu button.Company/Office Drop Down

  4. Click against the desired location.Select desired location

  5. Click .Next

  6. This will populate the Customer Maintenance page. The remainder of the process involves making your way through the various fields and populating the appropriate information in each field.Customer Maintenance

  7. Click when finished populating the Customer Maintenance fields.Save Customer Maintenance

  8. The previously created branch will now appear on the Customer Maintenance Workbench.Updated Customer Maintenance Workbench
