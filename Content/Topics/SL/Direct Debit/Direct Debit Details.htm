Direct Debit Details Screen

A link on the field of the Direct Debit Approval Workbench opens the Direct Debit Details screen. This shows the Direct Debit details that were created during the Statement Print process.

Fields

Field
Description

Customer Code

The account code for the customer.

Customer Name

The customer’s name.

Int Ref

The internal reference of the invoice to be paid.

Invoice Date

The date of the invoice.

To Pay Amount

The amount to be paid in this transaction. If the total amount of the invoices is greater than the customer’s Direct Debit limit, this may not be the full amount of the invoice.

Discount Amount

The amount of any discount applied.

Bank

The bank account that will be credited by this payment.

Status

The status of the detail record.

  • To Email - The extract has not been generated (or emailed).
  • To Approve - The extract has been created and is awaiting approval (which also creates a cash batch).
  • Incomplete - An unposted cash batch has been created.
  • Posted - The payment has been posted.
  • To Cancel - Posted Cash has been used to the corresponding payment but the batch has not yet been posted.
  • Cancelled - The batch has been posted and the Reset Status action can be run.
