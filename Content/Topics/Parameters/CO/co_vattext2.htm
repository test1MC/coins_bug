CO/VATTEXT2 - VAT Doc Text2

Alternative text that can be printed on a non-reverse-charge subcontract certificate in place of "RCTEXT2".

Use the field rctext2 (in ) or RO_rctext__2 (Subcontract Ledger in ) to print this.
