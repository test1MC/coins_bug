CO/BDACCT - VAT Bad Debt Control Account

The $P$AT|VAT$P$ Bad Debt Control Account.

The GL Account Code for $P$AT|VAT$P$ bad debts (part of $P$AT|VAT$P$ Bad Debt Relief). Transactions flagged as Bad Debts have their $P$AT|VAT$P$ transactions reversed and the amount transferred from the normal $P$AT|VAT$P$ account to this account.

If this is not set then any codes that have been identified as Bad Debt will need valid and GL accounts assigned to them (in Codes).

The other side of the entry is controlled by the BDACCT parameter in the relevant ledger.
