CO/TAXTOL% - Tolerance limit for use tax (percent)

The tolerance percentage for US use tax (in SC and PL Sales Tax Processing), expressed as a decimal (for example, 2.5% would be entered as 0.025). If the calculated use tax is less than this tolerance level, no use tax will be accrued. This prevents spurious use tax amounts of 1c due to rounding in the tax calculation.

The CO/TAXTOL$ parameter also specifies a tolerance value; if both TAXTOL% and TAXTOL$ are set, the lower of the two values is used.,
