CO/SECTBS - Sectorised Balance Sheet

Whether to produce a sectored balance sheet.

Y = The control accounts for , Subcontract Ledger, etc. are split into the different sectors (based on the sector allocated to the /Department in the Invoice header, for example).

This is an optional feature of sectors.

NOTE

Certain ledgers (House Sales, and ) have their own parameters which will override the CO/SECTBS parameter setting for that ledger. This allows the sales control accounts to be split into the separate sectors, while one sector (head office) runs , etc. on behalf of all the other sectors.
