CO/CURRTAXP - Currency conversion at payment (ledgers)

This parameter specifies modules (SC,CS or SC or CS) in which, for those transactions which are set for the tax point to be at the point of payment, the value of the will be converted to the base currency using the rate at the point of payment (as opposed to the rate at the invoice/ certificate date).

This only applies to self-billing and authored receipt transactions in Subcontract Ledger, and to certificates.
