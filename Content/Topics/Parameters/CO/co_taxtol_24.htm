CO/TAXTOL$ - Tolerance limit for use tax (value)

The tolerance for US use tax (in SC and PL Sales Tax Processing), expressed as a dollar value (for example, 0.01 for a 1 cent tolerance). If the calculated use tax is less than this tolerance level, no use tax will be accrued. This prevents spurious use tax amounts of 1c due to rounding in the tax calculation.

The CO/TAXTOL% parameter also specifies the tolerance percentage; if both TAXTOL% and TAXTOL$ are set, the lower of the two values is used.
