CO/PRINCIP - No VAT Charge (to Principal) Codes

A list of codes which if used in will not generate transactions (used for Irish Reverse Charge VAT processing).

These are codes for which the customer (that is, the Principal Contractor) will be accounting for on a reverse charge basis.

There is no problem with a code being both in the VATEX list (which is already used for Charging where no is appropriate) and in the PRINCIP list. The distinction between the two is the ledger in which the parameter makes a difference (VATEX for Charging or PRINCIP for ).
