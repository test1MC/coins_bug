CO/PRHR - Payroll Employee to HR

The optional fields in Human Resources that are updated when the Payroll record changes.

NOTE

In Human Resources, there are three fields for forename, middle name and ; in Payroll there are two fields for forenames and , and in Expenses there is a single field for (which is sometimes used to hold the full name). If you choose to update or forenames in Human Resources from Payroll, COINS separates the names into the appropriate fields according to where the spaces are. So if the fields in Payroll were updated as follows: Forename - Peter Timothy John, - Smith; the fields in Human Resources would be updated as follows: Forename - Peter, Middle Name - Timothy John, - Smith.
