CS/REPCLMCOL - List of Certificate Item IDs used for reporting CS APP/CERT details

A comma separated list of Item IDs to be included in Revenue History.

The first entry is always the Item ID for original works. The second entry is the Item ID for approved and the third entry is the Item ID for unapproved . They can be left blank but they have to appear in the order. Subsequent entries of Item IDs also will be shown on the report. Values will be pulled from certificate definition records.
