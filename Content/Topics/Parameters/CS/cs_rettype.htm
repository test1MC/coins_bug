CS/RETTYPE - Retention Ageing Type

The type to use as a default for the CS Outstanding Retention Reports.

To have five periods based on a specified number of days from the retention due date, you would enter n,n,n,n,n.

Alternatively, to have five periods based on a fixed date in a month, you would enter, for example, 1,2,3,4,5. This would give you a fixed date in 1 month's time, 2 month's time, 3 month's time, 4 month's time, and 5 month's time. See RETAGE.

If RETTYPE is blank, the defaults are taken from CS Configuration.
