CS/REFFORM - Int Ref format

The PROGRESS display format (such as 9999) of the numeric part of transaction reference numbers (reference numbers consist of an optional base plus a format).

See also REFBASE. For example, a REFBASE of 9,10,5,6 and a REFFORM of 9999 produce a transaction reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 03050001.
