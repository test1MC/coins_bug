CS/RETAGE - Retention Ageing Breaks

The number of days or months to age by in the CS Outstanding Retention Reports, such as 30 days or 1st of the month. These are the default settings; users can change them when they run the report.

For example, if you have configured the RETTYPE parameter to age by a specific number of days, and you entered 30,60,90,120,150 in RETAGE, this would result in columns based on 30, 60, 90, 120, and 150 days from the reporting date.

Alternatively, if you are by a fixed date in the month and enter, for example, 28,28,28,28,28, this would give you an period that was on the 28th of the corresponding month specified in the RETTYPE parameter.
