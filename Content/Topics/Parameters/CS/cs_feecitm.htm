CS/FEECITM - Comma separated list of ITEMID's related to Certificate definition for cost category

A comma-separated list of Item IDs related to the certificate definition for each of the cost categories.

This determines which lines must be populated on the certificate definition when FEE applies to cost category values. The fee percentage is entered against the with the option to enter a Fee % at the cost category level and to indicate whether it is a % or Value.
