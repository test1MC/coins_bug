SV/VARSTAT - Manage Variation status at variation level

Whether to allow status at item level (Y or N). If this parameter is blank then it is equal to Y.

N = You can override the status at the item level. For example, you can mark individual items as approved while the whole is not yet approved.

If you change the status of the whole , you will have the option to keep the status of the items out of sync, except at the final (approval) stage, when COINS copies the status of the whole to each item.

Y = status is only managed at the header level.
