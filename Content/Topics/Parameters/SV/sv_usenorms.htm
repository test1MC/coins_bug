SV/USENORMS - Use Norm Constants

Whether to use norm constants for material resources (Y or N).

Norm constants are used to define the time required to fit a material item.

For example, an electrical socket may have a quantity of 5 and a norm constant of .5, so the hours to fit five sockets is (5 * .5) = 2.5 hrs. The 2.5 hours are auto filled in when the resource within the item is a -related resource and materials on the item have been entered.

So if a user creates a Item to install electrical , this will likely include a number of different electrical materials. Each will have a norm constant and the er (who in this case would be an electrician) will know how many hours it will take so they can be charged out.
