SV/SCVALMOS - Validate SC material on/off site records when paid when paid is enabled

Whether to validate Subcontract materials on/off site against linked Client transactions (Y or N).

If set to Y and Pay When Paid functionality is enabled, quantity of material on/off site record will be validated while creating an unposted batch for SC Certificate. If quantity of material certified by Client is less than the quantity of material on SC Certificate, then error message will be displayed.
