HR/USINGACCRUEDHRS - Timesheet entry of usage hours - allow/disallow/warn if not enough accrued hours available

Whether to allow, disallow, or give a warning If the hours entered on a timesheet for usage, such as sick or time, exceed the current accrual balance.

The options are:

  • Allow - Allows the hours to exceed the balance.
  • Disallow - Does not allow the hours to exceed the balance.
  • Warn - Gives a warning message if the hours will exceed the balance.
