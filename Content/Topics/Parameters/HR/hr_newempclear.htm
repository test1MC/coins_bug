HR/NEWEMPCLEAR - Personnel fields and child records to clear when a new PR employee links to an existing HR leaver

Comma separated list of personnel fields and child records to clear when a is made current again due to being updated by a linked current Payroll employee.

Captions can be set to their defaults by including RW_por_caption^<caption code>. All captions can be covered by simply including just RW_por_caption.

The child records that can be removed are Contacts, Beneficiaries, and Correspondence.
