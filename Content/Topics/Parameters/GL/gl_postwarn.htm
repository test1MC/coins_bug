GL/POSTWARN - Modules to give warning when not posting to GL

A can-do list of modules (any or all of CS, PL, SC, SL, ST). If this is not blank, when posting from a module that is included in POSTWARN and which has the Post to GL field uned in its configuration file, a warning message will appear that the batch will not post to GL.

Batches Not Posted to GL Report allows you to identify batches that have not posted to the General Ledger.
