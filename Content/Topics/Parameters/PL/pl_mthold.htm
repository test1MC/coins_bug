PL/MTHOLD - Auto Hold Invoices with Missing Tickets

Y = Invoices with missing tickets are put on hold automatically.

During invoice matching, users can (if allowed) enter missing tickets. Putting invoices with missing tickets on hold automatically prevents the invoice being paid. If Missing Ticket Workbench is being used, this allows site personnel to corroborate that the goods were actually received, and takes the invoice off hold if there are no other problems.
