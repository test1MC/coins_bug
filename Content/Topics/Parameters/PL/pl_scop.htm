PL/SCOP - Subcontractors (eg Plant Op's) Thru' PL

Determines whether handles entry of subcontract invoices.

Y = During P/L Transaction Entry, if the S/C field on the record is Y and a subcontractor with the same account number exists, COINS prompts for the Content of the invoice. If an invoice contains a subcontract element, COINS carries out statutory processing (for example, Construction Industry Scheme vouchers, authenticated receipts and year end returns).

N = does not handle entry of subcontract invoices.
