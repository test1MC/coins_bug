PL/USERFLD - User's Hidden Fields in Supplier Maint.

A comma-separated list of users and a list of field names that the user(s) specified are not allowed to update in Details. Separate the two lists with a vertical bar (pipe). For example:

Steve,fred,joe|avm_credlim,avm_credrat

See also MAINTFLD.

In , this parameter is superseded by the functionality in Field Access Maintenance.
