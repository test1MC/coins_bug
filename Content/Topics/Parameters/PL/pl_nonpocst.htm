PL/NONPOCST - Enter Costing on Non-PO eTransactions

Whether to allow eInvoices that are converted to PINNs (purchase invoices with no purchase order) to be costed within the Electronic Invoice Workbench.

Y = The Electronic Invoice Workbench will allow transactions to be costed in the workbench. The default costing is taken from information in the <BuyersOrderNumber> tag in the message.

N = Any PO number referenced by the eInvoice is assumed to be a valid order number.

See How To Change eInvoice PINVs to PINNs.
