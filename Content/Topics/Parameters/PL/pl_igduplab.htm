PL/IGDUPLAB - Ignore Labour only duplicate supplier reference check

Whether to ignore the Supplier of Subcontract setting on a when validating duplicate references [Default=N].

Y = If configured to check for duplicate references, COINS checks regardless of whether the is marked as Supplier of Subcontract .

N = If configured to check for duplicate references, COINS only checks if the is marked as a Supplier of Subcontract .

This depends on the settings of the other relevant parameters.
