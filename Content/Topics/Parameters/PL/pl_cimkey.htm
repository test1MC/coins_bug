PL/CIMKEY - CIM Keys - (Field|Key|..)

The CIMKEY enables you to cross-reference between the field names within COINS (tables ap_invoice & ap_vendor) and the KEY fields as set up in CIM.
Enter a list of field | key pairs. For example: ain_inv | Internal Reference | avm_name | Supplier Name | ohd_num | PO Number | ain_supref | SupplierRef | job_num | Contract
