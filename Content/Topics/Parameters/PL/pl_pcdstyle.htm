PL/PCDSTYLE - Style of PCard Detail screen

Whether PCard details are displayed using the "Old" or "New" Style of costing Breakdown. When set to "New", the PCard detail screen appears with the Breakdown section at the bottom of the page.

New = the PCard detail screen appears with the Breakdown section at the bottom of the page.

Old = the PCard detail screen appears with the Breakdown section on the right side of the page.
