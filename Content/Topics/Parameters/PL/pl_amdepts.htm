PL/AMDEPTS - Specific Automatching for Departments

Whether to allow Automatic Invoice Matching to be applied selectively against Overhead Departments.

Enter a can-do list in the form deptmask|x,deptmask|x,... where deptmask matches against department codes and x is either 0 (zero) (invoices for departments matching the mask will not be auto-matched) or 2 (invoices for departments matching the mask will be 2-way matched if possible). For example, 1*|2,9*|2 will attempt to match for invoices on that belong to departments with codes beginning 1 or 9.

If the parameter is blank or a department does not match any mask then the auto-matching rule from the PL/AUTOINVM parameter applies.
