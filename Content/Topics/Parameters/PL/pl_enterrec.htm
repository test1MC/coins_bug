PL/ENTERREC - Enter Received Date for Purchase Ledger Invoices

Whether to enter the Invoice Received Date.

If this parameter is enabled, it is possible to record the date on which an invoice was physically (or electronically) received.

Y = Invoice Received Date is required.

O = Override – Invoice Received Date is set as TODAY however this can be overwritten.

N = Invoice Received Date is not required.

A warning will be displayed if the Invoice Received Date is more than 30 days old or if the Invoice Received Date is in the future.
