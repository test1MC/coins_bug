PL/MULTAPCD - Allow PCard transactions to be allocated to multiple costing entities

Whether to allow "Analysis Type" and "Analysis" columns to be utilised in the PCards workbench. The "Analysis Type" combo-box contains "", "Department", "Asset" and "Workshop".

Y = the line items details section of the Breakdown pane contains 2 additional columns, "Analysis Type" and "Analysis".

N = the line items details section of the Breakdown pane do not contain "Analysis Type" and "Analysis" columns.
