PL/AUTOPSTE - Automatic Post eInvoice Batches

Whether to automatically post invoices loaded by the Electronic Invoice Workbench.

Y = Invoices loaded by the Electronic Invoice Workbench are placed on the invoice register.

N = Electronic Invoice Workbench creates an unposted batch of invoices, which must be posted manually.

A blank value in this parameter means that the value of the PL/AUTOPOST parameter will be used.
