PL/DEFLABOU - Default Labour From Gross minus VAT

The rules to use for the relationship between the amount and the for suppliers of subcontract .

blank - The field will default to zero unless operating within the AUS taxrules in which case it will default to the amount. This setting is not recommended since this relies on users remembering to include invoices in the scope of subcontract tax.

E = The will default to the amount, and may not be changed. Use this setting if the whole of every invoice for suppliers of subcontract is always within the scope of subcontract tax.

W =The will default to the amount, but may be changed. Use this setting to reduce the risk of not including invoices within the scope of subcontract tax which should be (where some do need to be excluded).
