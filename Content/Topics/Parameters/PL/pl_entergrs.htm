PL/ENTERGRS - Enter Gross on Invoice for Check

In Enter Invoices, this parameter determines whether the net and/or the gross amounts are to be entered by the user.

Y = Users must enter the gross amount on invoice entry, as a check. COINS checks that the gross amount equals the net plus amounts.

N = User must enter the net amount and COINS calculates the gross amount automatically.

This parameter is ignored if PL/GRSFIRST = Y.
