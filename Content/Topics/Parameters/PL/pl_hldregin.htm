PL/HLDREGIN - Auto Hold Registered Invoices

Whether to put registered invoices on hold automatically.

Y = Registered invoices are put on hold automatically.

N = Registered invoices are not put on hold automatically.

To prevent users approving registered invoices during invoice entry, set HLDREGIN to Y and APPRREG to N. The invoices then have to be approved using one of the "approve invoices" functions.

If HLDREGIN = Y and APPREG = Y then the user entering the invoice WILL have the ability to approve the invoice there and then.
