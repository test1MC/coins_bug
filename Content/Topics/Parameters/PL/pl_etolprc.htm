PL/ETOLPRC - Tolerance for eInvoice Price Matching

The price tolerance for matching eInvoices to Purchase Orders.

This allows a tolerance on prices; for example, if set to 0.10, prices on the invoice line will potentially match with PO line prices + or - 10 GBP pence (or USD cents or EUR cents); if set to 1% the invoice line price will potentially match with PO line prices + or - 1% of the PO line price.
