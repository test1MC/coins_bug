PL/DISTTYPE - Valid Distribution Types (RPC)

A list of valid distribution types for invoices. Enter the letters, without spaces, for example: CRP. The first letter that is entered in the list is offered as the default type.

Possible types are:

C = Costed - the costing distribution of the invoice is known and the invoice can be costed immediately.

R = Registered - the costing distribution for the invoice is not known at the time of invoice entry; the invoice will have to be costed at a later time.

P = Provisional - the invoice has been provisionally costed, but the distribution will have to be confirmed at a later time.
