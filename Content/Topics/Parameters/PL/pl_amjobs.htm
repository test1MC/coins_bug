PL/AMJOBS - Specific Automatching for Contracts

Whether to allow Automatic Invoice Matching to be applied selectively against .

Enter a can-do list in the form jobmask|x,jobmask|x,... where jobmask matches against numbers and x is either 0 (zero) (invoices on matching the mask will not be auto-matched) or 2 (invoices on matching the mask will be two-way matched if possible). For example: A*|2,B*|2,C*|0.

If the parameter is blank or a does not match any mask then the auto-matching rule from the PL/AUTOINVM parameter applies.
