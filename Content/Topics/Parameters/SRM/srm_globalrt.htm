SRM/GLOBALRT - Send e-mail alerts on global codes

Whether to send email alerts when the status of a company is changed to one of the status codes specified in the GLOBCDS "parameter".

Emails using the text in the GLOBTXT parameter will be sent to every user that has a "view" of the company in the Company Information Workbench. The email address of each user must have been set up on the user's record.
