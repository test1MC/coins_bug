SRM/GLOBTXT - Text for e-mail alert on global codes

The text that is to form the body of the alert emails sent out when the status of a company is changed to one of the codes specified in the GLOBCDS "parameter".

These are 3 special placeholders that you can insert in this text:

[COMPANY] - The company's name

[OLD-STATUS] - The previous status code

[NEW-STATUS] - The newly allocated status code

These placeholders will be replaced with company specific values at the time of generating the email.
