SRM/TASCOPLB - Trading Agreement Scope Label (Region/Branch)

Labels for the two levels of trading agreements between company and .

You can have trading agreements at group, company and level, and two levels in between company and . Enter the labels for the two additional levels, separated by a comma; for example: Region,Branch.

This is only implemented for orders (not overhead orders). In order to use this feature, JC/JOBREG and JC/JOBBR must be set, and they must refer to entities that correspond to region and branch.
