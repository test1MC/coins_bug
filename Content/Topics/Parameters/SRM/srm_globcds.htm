SRM/GLOBCDS - Status Codes which apply globally

A list of status codes that will be applied "globally" to the company.

You may have decided to allocate a special status (for example, HSI meaning Health & Safety Incident) to a subcontractor in order to make the rest of your organation aware of an exceptional circumstance that may have arisen. Typically these codes would be marked "inactive".

If a user sets the status code of a subcontractor or to one of the codes listed here, COINS applies that code to all views of that company.
