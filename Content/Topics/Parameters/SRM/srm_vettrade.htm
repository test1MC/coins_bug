SRM/VETTRADE - Company Vetting Level for Subcontractors (OA only)

( only) What type of subcontractor to use. is a way of establishing an approval level for a subcontractor with reference to their "primary trade(s)".

BYVIEW = Simple subcontractor by view. You can subcontractors at the level specified by SRM/STATVIEW. For example, one of your offices might a subcontractor for roofing and another might them for brickwork.

DETAIL = You need to set up in Subcontract Attributes a list of attributes you can associate with an office and a trade. On the search tab, you can choose from a list of all possible attributes.

NOVET = You are not using subcontractor . This probably implies that you only record against a subcontractor the trades you specifically consider the subcontractor to be proficient in.

SIMPLE = You can a subcontractor against specific trades (this means the status is awarded with reference to those trades). On the Search tab, you can restrict the view to " only".
