SRM/IPRTFLDS - List of fields to Monitor

A comma-separated list of fields that the iPortal Monitor will monitor.

These are fields that relate to the record (as defined in the Database entry for "ci_company"). If any of the fields in this list are amended by COINS users, then the will appear on the "Amended Companies" tab of the iPortal Monitor.

Fields from the following COINS tables can be included in this list:

  • ci_company (the principal CIW record for the vendor)
  • ci_office (head office record)
  • ci_address (main head office address record)
  • sc_subins (Insurance records in Financials)
