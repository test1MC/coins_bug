p0/MAILFROM - From Email Address [ALL]

The From Email Address to be used for emails that contain the weekly payslips that are sent to the employees.

This parameter may be used in the future by other functions generating secure emails from Payroll.

For detailed information on how to be able to email secure PR payslips to employees, see the PDF How To Guide entitled Email Secure PR Payslips to Employees in OA found in Payroll How To Guides.
