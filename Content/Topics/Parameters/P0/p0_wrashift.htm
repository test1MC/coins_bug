p0/WRASHIFT - WRA can use time based shift rates

When Shifts are allowed, you can specify a number of shifts (for example DAY & NIGHT). Time is then allocated as early, normal, overtime 1 or overtime 2, based on time slots. So DAY shift might pay overtime 2 after 8pm. This feature is only used in External Payments, such as those from FM, where the start and end times are specified.
