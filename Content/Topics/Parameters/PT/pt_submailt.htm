PT/SUBMAILT - 'To' email address when emailing Submittal

This parameter is used to default the "To" email recipients when emailing documents.

Options are as follows:

  • From - use email address from the "Source" person on the .
  • Resp – use email address from the "Responsible Party" person on the .
  • To – use email address from the "Submitted To" person on the .
  • User – use the email address from the current COINS user.

Multiple options can be selected.
