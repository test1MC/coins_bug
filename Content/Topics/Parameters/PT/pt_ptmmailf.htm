PT/PTMMAILF - 'From' email address when emailing Meetings

This parameter is used to determine the ?From? email address when emailing Meetings documents.

Options are as follows:

  • From ? use the email address from the ?Co-ordinator? person on the Meeting.
  • User ? use the email address from the current COINS user.

The ?Co-ordinator? person on the Meetings can only be used under these conditions:

  • It is linked to a Personnel record, not an external contact
  • Personnel record has an email address
