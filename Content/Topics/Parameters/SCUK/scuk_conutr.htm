SCUK/CONUTR - CIS 2007 Contractor UTR

The contractor's UTR.

You will find this on the CIS333 "List of subcontractors you do not need to verify" (both on the paper and electronic format). This is a ten-digit reference and is the same as the first ten digits of your contractor's certificate number from the previous CIS scheme. Please remember not to input the last three digits from the old number, and do not include any spaces when entering it on the screen.
