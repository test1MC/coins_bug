SCUK/CONTXOFF - CIS 2007 Contractor Tax Office

The Contractor\'s Tax Office Reference.

Note that this is not the same as the "tax reference" held in the SCUK/TAXREF parameter (as used for the CIS 1999 scheme).

You will find this on the yellow PAYE Payslip booklet supplied by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC). Please do not include any spaces when entering this number.

  • The letters in the Tax Office Reference are case sensitive; please make sure you have these in upper or lower case exactly as they appear on HMRC documentation such as the paying-in slip booklet.
