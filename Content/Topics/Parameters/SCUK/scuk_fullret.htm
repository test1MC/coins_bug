SCUK/FULLRET - CIS 2007 Full return of all P/L payments

Whether to include in tax returns all payments made to PL subcontractors or only payments with a value.

When making payments to PL Subcontractors, details of all payments made should be included in tax returns. This is to include non only payments. It is possible that some PL Subcontractors may have individual agreements with HMRC regarding including non only payments.

Y = All payments made to PL subcontractors regardless of element or not will be included in tax returns. If at the time of making a payment or scheduling a payment and the subcontractor is not verified, then an error will be given and no payments (regardless of value) will be allowed to be made or scheduled for payment.

N = Payments made to PL Subcontractors which have a value will be included in tax returns.
