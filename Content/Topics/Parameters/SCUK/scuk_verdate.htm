SCUK/VERDATE - CIS 2007 Verification Date Range

The number of days before and after today's date during which users may enter a verification date.

Enter the number of days before today and after today, separated by a comma. For example, 10,30 would allow verification dates up to 10 days before today and up to 30 days after today.

This allows you to apply validation to the verification date, to avoid mis-typed dates. HMRC notification of a change in a subcontractor's status will typically allow a number of days to implement the change. Consequently the date entered could be the date on the letter itself (in the past) or a date which may allow the subcontractor's current payment to go through with the current status (a date in the future). A mis-typed date, say for a different year, could result in a change in status not coming into effect appropriately.
