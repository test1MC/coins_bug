SCUK/CONAOREF - CIS 2007 Contractor AO Reference

The contractor's Accounts Office Reference.

You will find this on the yellow PAYE Payslip booklet supplied by the HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC), usually in the format of 123PX12345.

  • Some references have two or three zeros after the letters which should be included, for example, 123PX00012345. Please do not include any spaces when entering it on the screen.
  • The fourth character of the AO Ref must always be an Upper Case 'P': (submission rejected if in lower case).
  • The fifth character in the AO Ref is case-sensitive; please make sure you have this in upper or lower case exactly as it appears on HMRC documentation such as the paying-in slip booklet.
