FA/POSTTOCH - Cost Head Classes Used for Flows

A comma-separated list of flow heads (asset ) to which asset acquisitions, disposals, transfers, revaluations and adjustments can be posted.

If this is populated, the full life-cycle of an asset is subject to asset analysis, and the configuration options to make this possible are visible on other screens.

If this is blank, acquisitions, disposals, transfers, revaluations and adjustments are always posted directly to the General Ledger.
