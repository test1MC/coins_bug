FA/DEPNCOST - Depreciation Costing

How depreciation should be costed.

Options are:

  • GL/Plant
    Asset analysis will be used if the group is flagged to use costs, otherwise costs go the GL.
  • JC-Manual
    You can set the to cost to for each asset. Assets without a will cost as per GL/Plant.
  • DEPT-DEPOT
    Automatically pro-rated to Departments from dispatches.
  • JC-AUTO
    Costed to s taken from the dispatches.
