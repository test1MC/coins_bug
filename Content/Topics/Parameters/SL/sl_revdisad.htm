SL/REVDISAD - Summarise Revenue Dist by Inv Line Analysis and Desc

Whether to summare the revenue distribution, on a invoice with multiple lines, by the analysis and description entered on the invoice lines.

Y = The revenue distribution is summared by the analysis and description entered on the invoice lines. This allows individual revenue distribution lines to be generated for each invoice line entered if required.

N = The revenue distribution is summared by the analysis entered on the invoice lines.
