SL/INVFORM - Invoice No Format

If INVAUTO is set to Y, this is the format of the sequential number part of invoice numbers.

Invoice numbers are made up of an optional base plus a sequential number.

The format is a PROGRESS display format, such as 9999.

For example, an INVBASE of 9,10,5,6 and an INVFORM of 9999 produces an invoice reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 95050001.
