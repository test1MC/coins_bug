SL/CUSTACCT - Control Accounts by Customer

Whether you can override the GL accounts for an individual customer (Y or N).

Y = COINS displays the G/L Codes tab in Maintenance and prompts for the Sales , SL Control, SL Retention, SL Discounts taken and SL Unallocated Cash accounts. These accounts are used when creating and paying an invoice.

N = COINS does not give the option to change the accounts for a customer; the accounts set up in SL Configuration are used.
