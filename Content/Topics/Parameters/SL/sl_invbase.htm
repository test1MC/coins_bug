SL/INVBASE - Invoice No Base (PPYYMMYYYY)

If INVAUTO is set to Y, this is the format of the base part of invoice numbers.

Invoice numbers are made up of an optional base plus a format.

The base is a combination of digits taken from the batch General Ledger period and financial date. INVBASE is a list of the positions of the required digits from the following sequence PPYYMMYYYY (where PP = ; YY = period year; MM = month; YYYY = year).

For example, an INVBASE of 9,10,5,6 and an INVFORM of 9999 produces an invoice reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 95050001.
