SL/SHOWRET - Show Customer Retention in Dig Deeper

Whether to show customer's retention as part of the main details in Dig Deeper Enquiry.

This allows you to choose whether or not to show the customer's retention as part of the main details. This is because COINS must read all of the customer's records to accumulate the retention, which may result in the main screen taking too long to display; therefore, you can choose not to show the retention by setting SHOWRET to N.
