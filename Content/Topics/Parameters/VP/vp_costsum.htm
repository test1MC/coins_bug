VP/COSTSUM - Cost Distribution Summary

How do you want COINS to post the cost distribution of the certificate to Status (Y or N)?

Y = Distribute costs to the one or two codes specified on the subcontract.

N = Produce a cost distribution based on the details of what was paid in the matrix (that is, the sum of all plots for each of the costs heads paid).

Usually this will be N; the Y setting is for compatibility with earlier versions.
