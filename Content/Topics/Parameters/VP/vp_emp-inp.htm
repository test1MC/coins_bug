VP/EMP-INP - VP/PR timesheet input payment validation

The level of validation to be applied when entering VAP/Payroll timesheets.

When entering payments using VAP/Payroll Payment Input, COINS checks to ensure that you have not exceeded the balance to pay and that the plot is at the correct construction stage for the payment to be made.

E - Error = Prevent payment proceeding until validation issues are resolved.

W - Warning = Display validation messages but allow payment to proceed. This is the default.
