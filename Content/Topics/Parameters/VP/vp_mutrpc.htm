VP/MUTRPC - Multi order trade by percentage (OA)

Do you want the multi order trade validation to work on the percentage of each order paid (Y or N)?

Y = The validation will be based on the percentage of each individual order paid. If the total percentage is greater than 100%, COINS issues an error or warning (determined by VAP parameter CHKOPAY). This will reduce the risk of overpayment when the order values for each subcontractor are different.

N =The validation on the payment amount will be based on the total amount paid across all orders against the VAP budget amount.
