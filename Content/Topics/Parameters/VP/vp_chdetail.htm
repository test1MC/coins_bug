VP/CHDETAIL - Cost Head Detail setup Unit/Plot

Do you want to allow one or more standard payment stages on a (Y or N)?

Usually this will be Y; the N setting is for compatibility with earlier versions.

Payment stages allows you to have a more detailed breakdown of your VAP budget, valuations and payments. For example, if you had a for Brickwork, you could set up payment stages for 1st lift, 2nd lift, etc.
