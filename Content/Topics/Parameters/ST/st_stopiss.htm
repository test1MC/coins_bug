ST/STOPISS - Warn/Error if insufficient Stock to issue

Whether COINS issues a warning or an error if you enter a quantity in a issue that exceeds the quantity of available.

E = Error. COINS does not allow you to enter the quantity.

W = Warning. COINS warns you but allows you to enter the quantity.

WN = COINSplus Only: Warn with OK/Cancel defaulting to Cancel. In OA WN is the same as W.

Blank = No message.

Note that when you complete a transaction to issue more than is available, COINS asks you to confirm that you want to enter the transaction.
