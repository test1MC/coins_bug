PI/PILJOBS1 - List of Pilot Projects subject to Procurement Planning Controls

A comma-separated list of and numbers of projects that are subject to planning control.

If this parameter is populated then only projects appearing in this list are subject to the controls implemented by the following parameters:

  • PSINUSE
  • USEPLNMA
  • USEPLNPL
  • USEPLNSC
  • USETLAMA
  • USETLAPL
  • USETLASC
  • PROJCOPY

If this parameter is blank then all projects are subject to the controls implemented by the above list of parameters.
