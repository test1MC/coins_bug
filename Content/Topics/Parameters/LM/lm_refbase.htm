LM/REFBASE - Invoice internal reference base (PPYYMMYYYY)

The format of the optional base part of internal reference numbers.

The base is a combination of digits taken from the current General Ledger period and . REFBASE is a list of the positions of the required digits from the following sequence PPYYMMYYYY (where PP = ; YY = period year; MM = month; YYYY = year).

For example, a REFBASE of 9,10,5,6 and a REFFORM of 9999 produce an invoice reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 95050001.
