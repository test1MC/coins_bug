CI/CSW-PREF - Company Status: Series|List of Preferred Status Codes

The QA series and a list of company status codes to use for "preferred" in Export Company Data to Causeway Prestige.

The format is a number for the series, followed by a pipe, then a list of company status codes. For example:

1|PART,PRF

In this example, if the company status is PART or PRF, the series to be exported is 1.
