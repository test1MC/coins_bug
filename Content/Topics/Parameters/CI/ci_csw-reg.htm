CI/CSW-REG - Company Status: Series|List of Registered Status Codes

The QA series and a list of company status codes to use for "registered" in Export Company Data to Causeway Prestige.

The format is a number for the series, followed by a pipe, then a list of company status codes. For example:

3|REG

In this example, if the company status is REG, the series to be exported is 3.
