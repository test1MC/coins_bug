CI/DUPFLDIG - A comma separated list of fields to ignore in the Potential Duplicate Companies check

A comma-separated list of fields to ignore in the Potential Duplicate Companies check.

The duplicate check uses the following fields when attempting to find potential duplicates:

  • ADD1 - Address line 1
  • ADD2 - Address line 2
  • ADD3 - Address line 3
  • ADD4 - Address line 4
  • POSTCODE -
  • PHONE - Phone number
  • WEBSITE - Website
  • COMPREGNO - Company registration no.
  • VATREGNO - registration no.
  • EMAIL - Email

If it is not appropriate to compare some of these fields (such as the postcode (zip code) in the US), enter their field names as a comma-separated list.

For example: POSTCODE,VATREGNO would mean that the and registration number would not be used to check for duplicates.
