CI/AUTOLEN - Length of Account Number Base

The length of the base in auto-generated account numbers.

This is the number of characters from the company short name that are used at the beginning of the account number. If you are using , the value of AUTOLEN plus the length of the format in AUTOFORM must be 8 or less.

The parameters AUTOBASE, AUTOFORM, AUTOLEN, and AUTONUM control the way COINS generates account numbers for , customers, and subcontractors.
