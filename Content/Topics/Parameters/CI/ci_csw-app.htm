CI/CSW-APP - Company Status: Series|List of Approved Status Codes

The QA series and a list of company status codes to use for "approved" in Export Company Data to Causeway Prestige.

The format is a number for the series, followed by a pipe, then a list of company status codes. For example:

2|APP

In this example, if the company status is APP, the series to be exported is 2.
