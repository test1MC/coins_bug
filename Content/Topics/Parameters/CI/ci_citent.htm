CI/CITENT - Office entity denoting COINS company interest

This allows you to use an analysis set to restrict which offices are downloaded to the CIT C21 estimating product, for example, to download only information about the offices of a national builders merchant that are local to your .

blank = COINS downloads all offices of all qualifying .

OTHER1 = The 'Other 1' analysis is used to identify offices that are to be downloaded.

OTHER2 = The 'Other 2' analysis is used to identify offices that are to be downloaded.

If OTHER1 or OTHER2 is selected, you can tag an individual office of a company by entering, in the appropriate analysis field ('Other 1' or 'Other 2'), the three-digit number (or numbers) of the COINS (or ) that is interested in that office. When downloading data for that COINS , Download COINS to CIT will download only tagged offices if any office of the company is tagged. If no office is tagged, all offices of the company will be downloaded.

Parameters beginning with CIT control the of the Download COINS to CIT program (ci930). This program is used to export data that relates to and offices in the Company Information Workbench, for the purpose of loading into the CIT C21 third-party estimating product database. Download COINS to CIT can be run interactively (in which case the CIT parameters supply default values), or as a background task.
