CI/PERFBASE - Performance Base

The scoring range used in and subcontractor assessment:

5 = range is 1 to 5.

10 = range is 1 to 10.

COINS automatically updates each rating score when the rating range switches between 5 and 10 (for example, if you change PERFBASE from 5 to 10, then a rating of 3/5 would change to 6/10).
