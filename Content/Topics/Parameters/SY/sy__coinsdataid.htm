SY/_COINSDATAID - Identify COINS specific data

Used in conjunction with logical _GITSAFE parameter, this parameter is used to identify COINS specific data and protect changes to the data on site. Alongside the usual protection of standard data, data identified by this parameter is secured to SuperUserAccess.

Examples: %WBI* to identify all data beginning with %WBI

or %WBI*,WDEV*,WCU* or WCU* etc

If _GITSAFE parameter is TRUE, the data identified by this parameter will in turn be dumped and loaded to and from disk. This data can then be further secured in git.
