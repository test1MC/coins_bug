SY/DATEXCV - Date fields with extended century validation

A can-do list of field names where extended century validation is required. This will prevent the century warning message if the date entered is earlier than the Progress century parameter (-yy) less an additional 50 years.

Note you will need to trim the webspeed agents after changing this parameter.

This may be required, for example, on date of birth fields if you are dealing with a large number of people born before your current setting of the century parameter.
