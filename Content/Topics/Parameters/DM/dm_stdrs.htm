DM/STDRS - Comma separated list of Standard Rubber Stamps to use

A comma separated list of the standard Rubber Stamps which it is possible to use when Annotating a document. Enter * to enable all of these.

The possible values are:

1 - Approved

2 - Assigned

3 - Checked

4 - Copy

5 - Draft

6 - Extended

7 - Fax

8 - Faxed

9 - Important

10 - Invoice

11 - Notice

12 - Official

13 - On File

14 - Paid

15 - Passed

16 - Pending

17 - Processed

18 - Received

19 - Rejected

20 - Release

21 - Sent

22 - Shipped

23 - Top Secret

24 - Urgent

25 - Void

26 - Secret

27 - Confidential

28 - Client Attorney Priviledge
