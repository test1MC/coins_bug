SC/AGEAUTH - Authenticated Receipt Age Limit

The number of days that Authenticated Receipts can remain outstanding before payments are stopped.

If the receipts remain outstanding for longer than allowed, then depending on the setting of the SC parameter STOPATR, COINS either:

  • prevents payments to the subcontractor, or
  • prevents automatic payment selection using Select/Deselect Certificates (Batch), and issues a warning when using Select/Deselect Certificates (Manual) or Manual Payment Entry.
