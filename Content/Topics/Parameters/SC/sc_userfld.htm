SC/USERFLD - User's Hidden Fields in Subcontractor Maint

A comma-separated list of users and a list of field names that the user(s) specified are not allowed to update in Subcontractor Details. Separate the two lists with a vertical bar (pipe). For example:

Steve, fred, joe| sbm_method, sbm_holdpay

In , this parameter is superseded by the functionality in Field Access Maintenance.
