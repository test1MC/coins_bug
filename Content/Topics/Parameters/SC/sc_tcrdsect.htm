SC/TCRDSECT - Sectorise Timecard Postings

Whether postings from timecard entry are sectored (Y or N).

The working of timecard sectoration in standard certificate input and the specialist timecard entry is different:

  • In standard certificate entry, there is a check imposed to prevent entry of from different sectors.
  • In the specialist timecard entry program, there is no restriction at the time of data entry. Instead, when the timecard batch program is run, separate certificates for a subcontractor are created for each sector.

If TCRDSECT is no, these sectoration changes will not take effect.
