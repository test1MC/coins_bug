SC/DEFS-CST - Certificate definitions - Costed on Invoice Register

A list of the standard certificate definitions to be used for certificates generated from costed invoice register transactions.

Only subcontracts linked to a certificate definition listed here allow transactions in the subcontract invoice register with a distibution type of C-Costed.

These certificate definitions need to be configured not to post cost entries as the costing will have been fully handled on the posting of the invoice register transaction.
