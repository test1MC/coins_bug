SC/DEFTAXSC - Default Subcontractor Tax Scheme

The default "Tax Scheme" entry for new subcontractors:

E = exempt;

T = taxable;

U = unknown;

V = valid.

Under the UK Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) and the Irish Relevant Contracts Tax (RCT) scheme, you must not set this to T. We recommend you set this to V (valid) unless there is good reason to do otherwise.
