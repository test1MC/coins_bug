SC/VATNONRG - Disallow VAT on non-reg'd supplier

Whether to prevent being entered on SC registered invoices if the subcontractor is not registered for .

Y = When a value has been entered on an item on the SC Invoice Register and the subcontractor does not have a Registration Number, COINS displays an error and does not allow to be entered on the transaction.

N = COINS allows to be entered on the transaction even if there is no Registration Number.
