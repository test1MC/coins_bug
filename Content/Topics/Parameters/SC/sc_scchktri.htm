SC/SCCHKTRI - SC Orders - Check Tri-Party Agreement exists

When raising a subcontract order, whether to check if factored subcontractors have a tri-party agreement set up.

Blank = Do not check.

E = Check and issue an error message if the subcontractor is factored but the Tri Party Agreement field is N.

N = Check and issue a warning message if the subcontractor is factored but the Tri Party Agreement field is N.
