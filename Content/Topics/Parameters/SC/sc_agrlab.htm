SC/AGRLAB - Agreement Maintenance Event Labels

A comma-separated list of field labels to override the standard labels in Subcontractor Agreement Event Administration in Procurement.

This is a list of five labels, as follows:

  1. Label for Sent to Subcontractor
  2. Label for Received from Subcontractor
  3. Label for Accepted Agreement
  4. Label for Rejected by Head Office
  5. Label for Counterpart Posted
