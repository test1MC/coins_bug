SC/STOPCERT - Tax validation error on entry

Whether, and under what circumstances, COINS disallows entry of a subcontractor certificate if the subcontractor is not verified.

Blank = Only a warning is shown when entering a certificate for a subcontractor that is not verified.

TODAY = An error (no further processing) is shown if the subcontractor is not verified.

DUEDATE = COINS checks the verification status based on the due date entered, rather than 'today', and issues an error if the check fails.
