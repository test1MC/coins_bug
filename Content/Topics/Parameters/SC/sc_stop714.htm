SC/STOP714 - Stop Payment if Tax Cert has expired

Whether to stop payment if the tax exemption certificate has expired (Y or N).

Y = If a subcontractor's tax exemption certificate has expired, they cannot be paid. Under the UK Construction Industry Scheme, you must set this to Y.

N = If the tax exemption certificate has expired, COINS issues a warning, but allows the subcontractor to be selected for payment manually.
