SC/DEFHMODE - Default Hold Mode (HOLD HOLDF or HOLDC)

If APPRENT = Y, which hold codes can be updated?

HOLD = When certificates are entered, they can be marked as on hold for both commercial and financial reasons.

HOLDC = When certificates are entered, they can be marked as on hold for commercial reasons.

HOLDF = When certificates are entered, they can be marked as on hold for financial reasons.
