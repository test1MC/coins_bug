SC/TAXATPAY - Recalculate Tax at Payment

This controls how COINS deducts subcontractor tax.

Under UK tax rules, we strongly recommend you set this to Y.

Y = COINS calculates tax when the certificate is entered, then recalculates at time of payment. This takes account of any changes in the tax status of the subcontractor between the certificate being entered and paid.

N = COINS calculates tax when the certificate is entered, and issues a warning at time of payment if the subcontractor's tax status has changed.

See also TAXSTAGE.
