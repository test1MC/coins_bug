EC/UNSPSC34 - Lowest UNSPSC code level

The lowest level for UNSPSC codes.

3 = The UNSPSC codes will be loaded at the third level as NN.NN.NN.

4 = UNSPSC codes will be loaded at the fourth level as NN.NN.NNNN.

UNSPSC (United Nations Standard Products and Services Code) codes are 8-digit codes; the first six digits identify a group of commodities, and the final two identify more specific products or services within that group. For example:

30131600 Bricks
30131602 Ceramic bricks
30131603 Concrete bricks
30131604 Stone bricks
30141513 Thermal Insulating bricks
