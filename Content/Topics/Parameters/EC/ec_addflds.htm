EC/ADDFLDS - Additional eCommerce Fields

A list of additional eCommerce fields to be displayed on eCommerce Workbenches and PL/PO records.

Available fields:

  • VEHREG - Vehicle Reg Number, will be displayed on a PO Header and PL Invoice level
  • EMPID - Employee ID, will be displayed on a PO Header and PL Invoice level
  • WRKPO - Works Order Number, will be displayed on PO Line and e-Line level
