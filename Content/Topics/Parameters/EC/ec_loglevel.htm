EC/LOGLEVEL - Background process logging level

The level of detail to record in the logs of background processes.

The background processes ecu002.p, ecu003.p and ecu004.p all write to log files (sender.log, receiver.log and router.log respectively) in the directory pointed to by the EC/ECDIR parameter. LOGLEVEL determines the amount of detail.

TERSE = Use this during normal operation; the log provides enough information to trace messages.

VERBOSE = Use this to produce more detailed logs when setting up a new installation or environment.
