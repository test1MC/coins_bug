EC/LOVALHLD - Hold invoices when loading

When loading electronic invoices using the Electronic Invoice Workbench, do you want to prevent low-value or high-value invoices from being moved automatically to

HIGH = Invoices with a value higher than the cutoff will be held automatically. For example, you might want to authore high-value invoices manually.

LOW = Invoices with a value lower than the cutoff will be held automatically. For example, you might want to ensure that high-value invoices can be batched immediately.

In both cases the cutoff is specified by EC/LOVALINV. Held invoices can be released manually using the Electronic Invoice Workbench
