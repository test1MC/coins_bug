EC/FTPRETRY - Retry getting attachments

The number and frequency of attempts to receive eInvoice attachments from the COINS ETC Hub.

Specify this using the format NUM|DELAY, where NUM is the maximum number of retries, and DELAY is the delay (in minutes) between retries. If the parameter is empty, then three retries with 15 minutes' delay is the default.

If the attachment is not received during any of the retries, the XML message is processed as attachment-free.
