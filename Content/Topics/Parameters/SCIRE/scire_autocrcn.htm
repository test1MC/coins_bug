SCIRE/AUTOCRCN - RCT Auto create contract notification

Whether to auto-create records in Contract Notification Workbench when purchase orders are committed for payment through Subcontract Ledger and/or .

If this is set to N, notification records are not created when orders are committed. In this case:

  • For orders paid through , the contract notification needs to be done outside of COINS, and the details recorded manually on the order or the first invoice.
  • For orders paid through Subcontract Ledger, the notification record can be created later from the subcontract record.
