HS/PIWLINKC - PIW COINS companies to link

Allows you to specify groups of COINS which should operate their own People Information Workbench. By default, COINS operates with one combined People Information Workbench for all your COINS .

For example, if you set this parameter as follows:

1,2,3|4,5|6

This would result in 1, 2 and 3 operating under a single combined PIW. 4 and 5 would also operate under a single PIW, but this would be separate from 1, 2, 3 and 6. 6 would operate with its own PIW.
