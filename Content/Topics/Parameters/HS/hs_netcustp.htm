HS/NETCUSTP - Rule for calculating Customer Net Price

The rule for calculating the net customer sale price (that is the net price from the customer's viewpoint).

This would normally ignore non-cash incentives, etc.

Select one of the following options:

1 = Gross - Discount - PX Allocation (default)

2 = Gross - Discount - PX Allocation +

3 = Gross - Discount +

4 = Gross - Discount

5 = Gross - Discount - Allocation + + Parking

6 = Gross - Discount + + Parking

7 = Gross - Cash Discount - Allocation

8 = Gross - Cash Discount
