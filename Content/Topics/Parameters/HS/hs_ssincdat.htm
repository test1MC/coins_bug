HS/SSINCDAT - Sales event date to use for staff sales incentive calculation

The event whose date is used for staff sales incentive calculation (based on sales recorded on the Sales Workbench). (For example, RESERVE for the reservation date, COMPLETE for the completion date.)

Incentive Periods allows you to define the incentive periods for calculating sales staff commissions. For each period you can then define the monthly and period target for each member of staff.
