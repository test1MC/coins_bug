HS/NWNOCANC - Events to exclude from sales cancellation

A list of events to exclude from the plot sales cancellation.

When cancelling the sale of a plot, events entered in this field will be excluded from the cancellation and will not be transferred to the Cancellations Workbench. Use this parameter to enter events that belong to the plot, NOT the current sale.

NOTE

Regardless of this parameter, the events CSTAGE, CANCEL and RELEASE will be excluded from the cancellation, as they are specific to the plot, NOT the sale.
