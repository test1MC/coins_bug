HS/CCNMOBW - Warn when allocating task to a non mCare user? (OA)

Whether COINS shows a warning when assigning a in to an internal member of staff who is not an mCare user (Y or N).

Y = When adding or maintaining a in , if the is allocated to an internal member of staff then a warning is shown.

N = When adding or maintaining a in , if the is allocated to an internal member of staff then no warning is shown.
