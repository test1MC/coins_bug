HS/EVPFIELD - Price field used for sales event forecast

The price fields that are used to update the actual prices in the Forecast Matrix.

The position of items in this list correspond to the positions of the event codes specified in HS/EVPFCAST. The fields available are:

  • vwb_netrelprice - Net release price.
  • vwb_grossrelprice - Gross release price.
  • vwb_netsaleprice - Net sale price.
  • vwb_grosssaleprice - Gross sale price.
  • vwb_netsaleprice_exch - Net sale price (only happen once a plot has exchanged).
  • vwb_grosssaleprice_exch - Gross sale price (only happen once a plot has exchanged).
