HS/SCLSWARR - Use sales class level warranty periods

Determines whether COINS looks at the setup on the plot sales classification table to determine the basis and length of the warranty period.

Yes = Sales Classification allows you to specify the basis and length of the warranty period for each sales classification. This means you can have different warranty periods for different types of sale (such as social and private).

No = The basis and length of the warranty period are determined by the HS parameters WARRDATE and WARRPER.
