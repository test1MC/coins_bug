HS/JRNLCVRS - CVR to use for Journal CVRITEM lines

Which to use as the basis for CVRITEM Journal lines.

  • Latest CVR - The latest will be used.
  • Most recent Authorised and Complete CVR marked as Current - The most recent (determined by date) that is flagged as both authored and complete will be used.
  • Most recent Complete CVR marked as Current - The most recent (determined by date) that is flagged as complete will be used (regardless of whether or not it has been authored).
