HS/SRCCOMBO - Select source using combo boxes in OA

When entering the source of , do you wish to enter by selecting the category first, then the area and then the activity (Y or N)?

Y = The user is presented with three drop-down boxes to first select the source of category, then the area and finally the activities associated with the category and area.

N = The user is presented with a list of all activities from which they can pick the source of activity.
