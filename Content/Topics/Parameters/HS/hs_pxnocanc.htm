HS/PXNOCANC - Events to exclude from PX cancellation

A list of events to exclude from the cancellation of a part-exchange sale.

When cancelling the sale of a part-exchange, events entered in this field are excluded from the cancellation. Enter events that belong to the property, NOT the current sale.

NOTE

Regardless of this parameter, the events BUYIN, SURVEY, CANCEL, and METBUY are excluded from the cancellation.
