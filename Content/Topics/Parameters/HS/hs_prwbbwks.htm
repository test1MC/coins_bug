HS/PRWBBWKS - Brickwork construction stages (OA)

A comma-separated list of construction stages that represent brickwork stages. This is then used to calculate a brickwork complete percentage based on the number of completed brickwork stages divided by the total number of brickwork stages. This can be reported in the Construction Gantt Chart.

This is useful for timber frame construction where the brickwork stages are not on the critical path and therefore have no duration in the construction .
