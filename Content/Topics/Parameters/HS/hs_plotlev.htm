HS/PLOTLEV - Number of discount levels for plots

The number of discount and incentive levels available for plots (see also DI1UPD, DI1VIEW, DI2UPD, DI2VIEW, DI3UPD and DI3VIEW that control which users can access each level).

Select one of the following options:

<blank> = Do not use discount & incentive levels (default).

1 = Use 1 level of discount & incentive.

2 = Use 2 levels of discount & incentive.

3 = Use 3 levels of discount & incentive.
