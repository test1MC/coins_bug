CB/CHQREMSC - Show SC Cert Details on Cheque Remittance

Whether to show details on the remittance of any SC certificates paid by the (Y or N).

Use the following fields in the body section of the remittance:

  • sbp_ref (internal reference)
  • sbp_subref ()
  • sbp_pdate (payment date)
  • sbp_payment (payment amount in document currency)
