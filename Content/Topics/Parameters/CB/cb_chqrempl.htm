CB/CHQREMPL - Show PL Invoice Details on Cheque Remittance

Whether to show details on the remittance of any PL invoices paid by the (Y or N).

Use the following fields in the body section of the remittance:

  • ain_inv (internal reference)
  • ain_su ()
  • ain_idate (invoice date)
  • ain_famount (invoice amount in document currency)
