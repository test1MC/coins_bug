CB/LVCCPACB - Leave any Customer Cash that has been partially allocated in Cash Book

Y = When Customer Cash is distributed to either the or Ledger and the amount entered does not get fully allocated, then the portion that is left will post to the as Customer Cash. I.e. The unallocated amount will stay in .

N = When Customer Cash is distributed to either the or Ledger and the amount entered does not get fully allocated, then the portion that is left will post to either the or Ledger as unallocated cash.

When the parameter is set to Y, then if any Customer Cash is selected but cannot be fully distributed then there is no need to enter a 'CC' analysis line, as during posting the Customer Cash line will be generated automatically. This is designed to enable faster allocation of Customer Cash.
