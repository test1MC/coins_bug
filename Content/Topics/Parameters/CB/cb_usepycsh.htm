CB/USEPYCSH - Allow Bank Account to be identified as a Site Petty Cash Account Only

Whether to enable the petty cash account functionality (Y or N); this allows bank accounts to be identified as Petty Cash Accounts.

Petty cash accounts are restricted to a single or to a set of General Ledger account codes. You can restrict who can enter transactions against the account. Users responsible for petty cash are only able to input, and report on the petty cash bank account for which they are responsible.
