CB/CBLEDGER - Ledgers (G/C/PL/SL/SC/CS/V/FA/E/HS/CC/PR/BK/LM/W)

A list of codes, used when entering transactions, to represent the ledgers which the can post to.

The codes must be in the following order:

  1. G - General Ledger,
  2. C - Status,
  3. PL - ,
  4. SL - ,
  5. SC - Subcontract Ledger,
  6. CS - Ledger,
  7. V - ,
  8. FA - Fixed Assets,
  9. E - Expenses,
  10. HS - House Sales,
  11. CC - Unallocated Cash,
  12. PR - Payroll,
  13. BK - Bank
  14. LM - Lettings.
  15. W - Workshop

For example, if you are not using House Sales, Payroll or Expenses, you could enter:

G,C,P,SL,SC,CS,V,FA,,,CC,,BK,,W.
