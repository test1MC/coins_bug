JC/PROGBASE - Calculate progress % based on ...

How COINS calculates the progress percentage on the Commercial Summary screen.

Select an option from 1 to 4 (or 1 to 6 if you are not showing the GP Adjustment field; see FOHIDE) to determine which "To Date" Cost line (1, 2, 3 or 4) to divide by the final forecast costs.

For example, to indicate progress as a proportion of the final material costs, select the line that represents material costs on the Commercial Summary.
