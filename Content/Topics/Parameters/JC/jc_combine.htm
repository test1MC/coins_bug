JC/COMBINE - Combined Forecasting and Business Plan ?

Allows you to combine some of the functionality of Business Plan into Forecasting. More specifically this means that you can spread the final forecast during the Monthly Review session.

If this parameter is set to Y, you will no longer be able to access the Business Plan screens.

If this is set no N, COINS will not require you to spread the final forecast, and will not show the < highlights against lines that have not been fully spread.
