JC/TSUNIT - Minimum Timesheet Unit

(COINSplus only) The minimum unit of time you can enter when using Quick Timesheet Entry. For example, to require timesheet entry in units of quarter of an hour, enter a minimum unit of 0.25; to require timesheet entry in units of half an hour, enter a minimum unit of 0.5.

Quick Timesheet Entry is intended to be run with TSHOURS set to N.
