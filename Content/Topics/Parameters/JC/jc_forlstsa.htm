JC/FORLSTSA - List of Report Stores: Sales

Controls how the Net Selling Value (Sales To Date) is entered on the Commercial Summary screen.

Enter a comma-separated list of Report Stores - COINS retrieves the net selling value from the sum of the values in these report stores. The report stores can be populated manually or using configurable reports.

Leave blank to calculate the Net Selling Value by multiplying the Prime Cost To Date by the Sales Forecast Divided by the Prime Cost Forecast, or as an adjustment to the "Applied For" value (see FORMIFLD and FORMIVAL).
