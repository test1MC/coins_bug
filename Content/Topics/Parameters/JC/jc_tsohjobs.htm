JC/TSOHJOBS - Overhead contract groups (not costed)

A can-do list of groups that are treated as overhead .

If a timesheet line is entered for a whose group is in this list AND the employee is an overhead employee (that is, the Non-costed field in Employees is ed), no cost transaction is created for that timesheet line when the timesheets are posted.

However, if overhead category staff are allocated to a mainstream , or normal costed staff are allocated to an overhead , the costing to the takes place.
