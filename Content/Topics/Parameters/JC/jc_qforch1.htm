JC/QFORCH1 - Quick Forecast Cost Heads & Cats - Costs

A comma-separated list of "dummy" and categories to be used when entering the forecast to complete Cost figures on the Monthly Review screen. For example: LX999.LA,MX999.MA,SX999.SC,PX999.PL

The must be valid standard , but you must prevent costs being posted to them (for example, by leaving the G/L code blank or giving them a class that is not allowed on ).

COINS uses the descriptions on these as the descriptions for the lines on the Monthly Review screen. You can have as many as necessary; however, only the first three (or five if you are not displaying the GP Adjustment field; see FOHIDE) will be shown on the Commercial Summary screen - the remaining lines will be summared under the fourth (or sixth) heading.

On the Monthly Review screen, actual costs come from the cost codes that are linked to these "dummy" codes; see FORQCON.
