JC/ACCMETH - Mtrls Accrual Method: (G O Z X or combn.)

The default setting for the Accrual Method field in Maintenance.

G = Accruals are calculated from GRNs on all .

O = Accruals are calculated from Purchase Orders on all .

Z = Accruals are calculated when the invoice is received.

X = Accruals are calculated when the invoice is received and GRNs are entered.

blank = The user selects the accrual method (no default).

G,O = The user selects the accrual method, and the default is G (default is O if the letters are reversed).
