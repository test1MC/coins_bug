JC/FOFLDLAB - Forecasting - Field Labels

This allows you to override some of the standard labels in the Commercial Summary screen. Enter a pipe-separated list of field names and the labels to use.

You can change the following field labels:

  • Totl Cost: enter cTotlCostLab=XXXX
  • Accruals: enter cAccLab=XXXX
  • Prme Cost: enter cPrmeCostLab=XXXX
  • Sales: enter cSalesLab=XXXX
  • Formul GP: enter cFormgpLab=XXXX
  • Adj'd GP: enter cAdjgpLab=XXXX
  • GP:XX%: enter cGp%Lab=XXXX
