JC/ONREASST - On reassign to notification

Whether the is sent an email notification when someone else's is assigned to the , and what type of email is sent.

blank = No email notification sent.

N = Email notification with link to Workbench but no attachment.

X = Email notification with link to Workbench and the generated by COINS attached.

Z = Email notification with link to Workbench and the generated by COINS attached in compressed form.

This parameter can be overridden at a personal level via OA -> Setup -> Notification.
