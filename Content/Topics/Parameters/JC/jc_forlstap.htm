JC/FORLSTAP - List of Report Stores: Applied For

Controls how COINS calculates the Applied For figure on the Commercial Summary screen.

Enter a comma-separated list of Report Stores - the Applied For figure will be retrieved from the sum of the values in these report stores. The report stores can be populated manually or using configurable reports.

Leave blank to calculate the Applied For figure from the sum of the net applications and invoices in and .
