JC/FOHIDE - Forecasting - 'Hide' Tokens

Enter a list of tokens representing certain parts that you wish to hide on the Commercial Summary screen.

  • HIDEGPADJ - Hides the GP Adj figure and gives two extra cost lines in the upper costs section.
  • HIDERENG - Hides the Re-engineered column.
  • HIDEENH - Hides the Enhancement column.
  • HIDEWKS - Hides the Wks Gone and Wks Rem fields.
