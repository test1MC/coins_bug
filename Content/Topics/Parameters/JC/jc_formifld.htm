JC/FORMIFLD - Forecasting - Manual Input of Sales field

The field on the that identifies the as one where the Sales Value on the forecast is to be manually adjusted (rather than calculated automatically).

Normally, the Net Selling Value is calculated by multiplying the Prime Cost To Date by the Sales Forecast divided by the Prime Cost Forecast.
