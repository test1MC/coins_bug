PC/DATEFIXD - Confirm Cannot Change Date Group Can-do list (*=all)

A can-do list of asset groups for which you want the asset movement messages to update the on or offhire dates.

When using RFID tag readers to record movements, asset movement messages for these asset groups will update the on or offhire dates to confirm the on / . Note: can only be changed to be later than expected (to allow early delivery), and can only be changed to earlier (to allow late collection).
