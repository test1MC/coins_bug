PC/CRD-CTRL - Control Credit Notes & record reasons

Whether to enable features that allow you to control and the reasons for credit notes raised in (Y or N).

Control is applied at invoice level, analysis is at line level.

When this feature is enabled, a reason for each credit can be entered at line level. Set up credit reason codes using Credit Note Reason.
