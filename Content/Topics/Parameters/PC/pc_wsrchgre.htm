PC/WSRCHGRE - WS Recharge FA Revenue Suspense Account

If you have workshop profit posting enabled (using FA Configuration), revenue and cost of sales will be posted twice. You can direct double postings to a suspense account using these parameters:

WSRCHGCO = cost suspense account mask
WSRCHGPR = profit/loss suspense account mask
WSRCHGRE = revenue suspense account mask
