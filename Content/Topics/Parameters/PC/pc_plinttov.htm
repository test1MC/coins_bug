PC/PLINTTOV - Internal Posting Override CUST1=Y|CUST2=N

This allows you to override, for specific customers, whether postings to another COINS will contain the costing.

Posting to another COINS uses the PC parameter INTCOST to determine if the invoice will be posted in PL as costed. The receiving PL parameter INTPCOST can prevent the costing. This is a list of specific customers, and whether the costing is posted to the PL. The list is in the form: A1234=Y|B5678=N|C9090=Y.
