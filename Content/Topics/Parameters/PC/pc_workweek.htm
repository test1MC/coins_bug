PC/WORKWEEK - Basis of Working Week

The number of days in the work week (used by the ).

The ONDAYS parameter specifies number of days to add to today to determine the start date. WORKWEEK controls what to do if the initial date is at the weekend.

5 = The default date will advance to the Monday date.

6 = Sunday will be advanced to Monday.

7 = The default date will not be altered.

This only affects the first line of the requisition - later lines default the date from the previous line.
