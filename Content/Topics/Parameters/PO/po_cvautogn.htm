PO/CVAUTOGN - Auto-generate CVO Numbers ?

Whether COINS generates CVO numbers automatically (Y or N).

Y = COINS generates CVO numbers automatically.

N = Users must enter CVO numbers manually.

If both CVALLWMN and CVAUTOGN are set to Y, users may enter their own purchase requisition numbers, but if they leave the field blank, COINS generates a number automatically. Do not set both parameters to N!
