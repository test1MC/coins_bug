PO/PRAUTOGN - Auto-generate P/Req Nums ?

Whether COINS generates purchase requisition numbers automatically.

Y = COINS generates purchase requisition numbers automatically.

N = Users must enter purchase requisition numbers manually.

If both PRALLWMN and PRAUTOGN are set to Y, users may enter their own purchase requisition numbers, but if they leave the field blank, COINS generates a number automatically. Do not set both parameters to N!
