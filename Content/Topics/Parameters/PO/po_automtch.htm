PO/AUTOMTCH - Match invoice to GRNs/Orders automatically

Should COINS attempt to match GRNs automatically during invoice matching?

Y = COINS attempts to match GRNs by assuming that a GRN is being invoiced if the order number is the same and the delivery date is on or before the date of the invoice. If the order does not expect GRNs to be entered then the whole outstanding quantity expected is assumed to be invoiced.

N = COINS does not automatically match GRNs/Orders.
