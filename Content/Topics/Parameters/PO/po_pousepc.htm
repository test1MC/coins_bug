PO/POUSEPC - Plant POs with related dispatch use PC Free Days

Whether the Order Accrual calculation routine consults the Free Days calendar (Y or N).

Y = The Order Accrual calculation routine consults the Free Days calendar.

N = The Order Accrual calculation routine consults the Procurement calendar.

The recommended setting is Y. This ensures that when orders are raised via the (and automatically linked to the dispatch), the accrual routine consults the correct set of free days.
