PO/PORQTXT - Copy text lines from Reqs to POs

Set this to Y if you want to copy text lines from material requisitions to orders.

If PORQTXT is set to Y:

  • The O/S Requisition Items screen will have an "Items and Text" filter that will also show text lines from requisitions that have not been brought onto a purchase order.
  • If you convert a requisition to an order, text lines on the requisition will be copied across to the order.
