PO/PLEXPAMT - OA Only - Plant Non-Allocation Invoices: Accrue up to

( only) Which expected amount is shown when entering invoices for maintenance orders

INVOICE TODATE = The expected amount shown will be the Accrual up to the "To Date" entered on the invoice covering period.

ACCRUAL = The expected amount shown will be based on the outstanding accrual.

The Accrual shown elsewhere in COINS will not be affected.
