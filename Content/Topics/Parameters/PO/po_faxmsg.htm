PO/FAXMSG - Fax Policy Warning Message

A warning message that COINS displays when someone selects to fax a purchase order to the .

This allows you to issue a warning (such as "Only low values/low risk orders should be faxed to the ") before orders are faxed. COINS displays the warning and asks the user to confirm that they want to proceed.

If you do not want COINS to issue a warning, leave this parameter blank.
