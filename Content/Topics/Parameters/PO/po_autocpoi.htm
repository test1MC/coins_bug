PO/AUTOCPOI - OA Only - Auto close PO items if invoiced amount and invoiced quantity are exceeded

(COINS OA only) Whether to allow the automatic closure of orders if the quantity invoiced matches the order and the where the invoice value exceeds the order value (Y or N).

Order lines will also be closed if the Total Invoiced Value is greater than the Total Order Value AND the Invoiced Quantity is equal to or greater than the Ordered Quantity.
