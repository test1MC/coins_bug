PO/COMMETH - Commit PO Method (P or M)

How COINS commits orders:

M = Committing and printing orders are two separate processes.
(You can allow specific users to commit orders when they are printed by giving them access to the function %WPO1990FPOHS1.)

P = COINS commits orders automatically when they are printed.

(COINS will not let a user print and commit an order that is over their authoration limit.)
