PO/AUTOCLSL - Auto Close Line when full Amount Invoiced

Whether COINS automatically closes order lines during invoice matching when the full amount has been invoiced (Y or N).

N = COINS will not close the line if the full amount is invoiced, but not the full quantity (for example if the order line is for 10 items @ 10, and the invoice line is for 5 items @ 20, COINS does not close the line).
