PO/HBSPRHSE - HB Subcon Price Chk Completing House Scheds

When completing standard house type schedules, what action do you want COINS to take if some of the schedule lines do not have prices?

E = Error. COINS prevents you from completing the schedule.

W = Warning. COINS warns you that not all lines have prices, and asks if you want to complete the schedule.

N = No error. COINS does not check for lines with no price.
