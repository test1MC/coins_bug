PO/PRFRM - Purchase Requisition Form

(COINSplus only) The name of the form used for printing purchase requisitions.

If you use purchase requisitions with different first and continuation pages, enter the two form names, separated by a comma. The name for the second form must be the first name followed by "2"; for example, if your form name is PRA, the second form must be called PRA2.

Forms are set up using Form Maintenance in the Technical Information System.
