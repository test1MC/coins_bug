PO/NTFY-EXT - Email Template for PO Notifications to Suppliers and Subcontractors

The email template for external notifications (to or subcontractors) in Send PO Notifications.

If this parameter is blank or set to an invalid email template, no external notifications will be sent.

See $O$Topics/PO/Notifications/Notifications.htm$O$.

Email templates are set up using Email Templates, type PONOTIFY-EXT.

You can also configure PO notifications (including the email template) for individual suppliers/subcontractors (at office and/or company level) using Organisation Setup (PONOTIFY record).
