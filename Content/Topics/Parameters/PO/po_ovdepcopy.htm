PO/OVDEPCOPY - Whether to copy department from header or line when copying overhead order

Where to get the department from when copying an overhead order; the header or the line of the order being copied.

Line = If the department of the copied line is not empty then COINS copies the department value from the copied line. If the department of the copied line is not set (empty) COINS copies the department value from the header.

Header = COINS copies the department value from the header.
