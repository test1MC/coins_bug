PO/GRNDDATE - The maximum number of days into the future the GRN Delivery Date is valid

A comma-separated list of the maximum number of days into the future GRN delivery date is valid.

The first entry determines how many days into the future automatically created GRNs can be dated. This applies when a for an order is designated to have GRNs created automatically when ordering hourly-rated items.

The second entry determines how many days into the future manually entered GRNs can be dated. If no second entry is present, then zero days into the future is assumed.

For example, this parameter might be set to “30,10”. This means that automatically created GRNs can be entered with dates 30 days into the future and manually entered GRNs can be dated 10 days in the future.
