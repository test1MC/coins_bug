PO/OPENCLSE - Allow clauses on open orders

Whether to allow clauses on open orders (Y or N)?

This allows you to send electronic orders that contain specific clauses detailing the basis of the order ? for example how call-offs will be managed.

This affects orders listed in the OPENTYPE, OPAGTYPE, EPLTYPEM and EPLTYPE parameters.

After entering the order header details for an electronic order you are taken to the order lines browse. If OPENCLSE = Y, you will at this point be able to add and update clause lines only. After finishing adding clauses to the order, when you click you will be redirected to the selection tabs for any standard clauses for the company and project (should they exist).
