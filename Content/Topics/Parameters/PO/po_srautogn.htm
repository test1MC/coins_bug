PO/SRAUTOGN - Auto-generate SC/Req Nums ?

Whether COINS generates subcontract requisition numbers automatically.

Y = COINS generates subcontract requisition numbers in sequence automatically.

N = Users must enter subcontract requisition numbers manually.

If both SRALLWMN and SRAUTOGN are set to Y, users may enter their own purchase requisition numbers, but if they leave the field blank, COINS generates a number automatically. Do not set both parameters to N!
