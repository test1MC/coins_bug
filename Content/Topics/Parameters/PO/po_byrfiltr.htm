PO/BYRFILTR - Enable Buyer Filter on Order W/B

Whether the buyer filter on the eCommerce Order Confirmation Workbench is active.

When the filter is activated, only those orders pertaining to the current buyer or those without a buyer (that is, with a status of error) are shown. In addition, if the buyer filter has been enabled, buyers may only update orders that are linked to their own buyer code, and may only load other buyers' orders if their user ID is included in the PO parameter SUPUSERS.
