PO/REM1DAYS - S/C Agree. 1st Reminder Sent after Days

The number of days after the date a subcontract agreement is sent that the first reminder is due to be sent.

In SC Agreements Workbench, for agreements that have not been returned from the subcontractor, and were sent more than this number of days ago (but less than the number in REM2DAYS), the Date Received column shows as amber.

Also, for agreements that have not been agreed, and were returned from the subcontractor more than this number of days ago (but less than the number in REM2DAYS), the Date Agreed column shows as amber.

The "Not Yet Received" filters also take account of this.
