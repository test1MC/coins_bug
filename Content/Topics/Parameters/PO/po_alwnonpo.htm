PO/ALWNONPO - Allow non-ordered items on Inv

Do you want to prevent users from entering additional charges in invoice matching? This applies to orders that do not accrue based on GRNs.

N = Users cannot enter additional charges.

Y = Users can use F9 to enter additional charges.

(Note that for orders that accrue based on GRNs, F9 allows you to enter missing tickets; ALWNONPO does not affect this. To disallow entry of missing tickets, use ALWMSTK.)
