PO/HLDINTOL - Auto-hold invoices within tolerance

In invoice matching, do you want to automatically hold invoices that have invoice lines that are within tolerance?

N = Invoices are not placed on hold if they have lines that exceed the order line value but are within tolerance.

Y = Invoices that have lines that do exceed the order line value but are within tolerance are put on hold using the hold code specified by the "parameter"s DEFHCODI or DEFCODC.

The parameters TOL% and TOLAMT or PTOL% and PTOLAMT specify the tolerances. See also ALOUTTOL.
