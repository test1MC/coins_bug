PO/SCWBS-VA - SC Orders(VA) - WBS on (H)eader or (L)ine

How do you want to enter WBS codes on subcontract orders for that are linked to ?

H = Enter WBS code on the header frame.

L = Enter WBS codes for each order line.

For example, you may want to enter the WBS at header level for ordinary subcontract orders (SCWBS = H), but enter them at item level for linked to (by setting SCWBS-VA to L).
