PO/REM2DAYS - S/C Agree. 2nd Reminder Sent after Days

The number of days after the date a subcontract agreement is sent that the second reminder is due to be sent.

In SC Agreements Workbench, for agreements that have not been returned from the subcontractor, and were sent more than this number of days ago, the Date Received column shows as red.

Also, for agreements that have not been agreed, and were returned from the subcontractor more than this number of days ago, the Date Received column shows as red.

The "Not Yet Received" filters also take account of this.
