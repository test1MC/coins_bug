PO/LCUTVAL - Warn or Error if Utilized LC value is greater than Current LC value

Whether COINS issues a warning or an error if the utiled value of a Letter of Credit (that is, the sum of all the acceptance amounts) is greater than the current value.

E = Error.

W = Warning.

N = No error or warning.

The error or warning appears either if the Current LC value of a letter of credit is changed, or if its acceptance is added or changed on Letter of Credit Workbench.
