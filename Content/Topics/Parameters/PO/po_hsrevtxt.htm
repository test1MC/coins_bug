PO/HSREVTXT - The text of the label of H&S information box

The text for the label of the "H&S reviewed" field on the requisition header.

This box is used to stop the person issuing the requisition if they have not confirmed that they have reviewed the Health and Safety information.

If HSREVTXT is not set, the label defaults to "You have received and reviewed the H&S information for the works in this requisition".
