PO/POAUTOGN - Auto-generate PO Nums ?

Whether COINS generates purchase order numbers automatically.

Y = COINS generates purchase order numbers in sequence automatically.

N = Users must enter purchase order numbers manually.

If both POALLWMN and POAUTOGN are set to Y, users may enter their own purchase requisition numbers, but if they leave the field blank, COINS generates a number automatically. Do not set both parameters to N!
