PO/RQDEFPIW - OA-Default PIW SM and Tel onto Requisition

( only) In material and requisitions, and subcontract instructions: whether COINS fills in the Requisitioned By and Information fields from the site manager of the project (Y or N).

Y = COINS fills in the Requisitioned By field with the name of the site manager of the corresponding Project, and enters his/her telephone number into the Information field.

N = COINS fills in the Requisitioned By field with the user ID of the user who enters the requisition; the Information field is not displayed.
