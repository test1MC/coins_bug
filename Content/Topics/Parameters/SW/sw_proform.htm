SW/PROFORM - Proforma internal ref format

The format of the sequential number part of reference numbers.

Reference numbers are made up of an optional base plus a sequential number.

The format is a PROGRESS display format, such as 9999.

For example, a PROBASE of 9,10,5,6 and a PROFORM of 9999 produces a reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 07050001.
