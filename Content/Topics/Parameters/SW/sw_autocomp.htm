SW/AUTOCOMP - Auto Complete Jobs when Task is completed

Whether to automatically mark an FM as Complete and/or Ready for Invoicing when the task is completed.

When an FM task is completed, either from a COINS Mobile device or from , if there are no other outstanding tasks on the and the AUTOCOMP parameter is not set to None, COINS will update the status of the depending on the parameter setting.

Complete = The will be marked as Complete. (Operationally Complete and Pricing Complete).

Ready for Invoicing = The (and all associated SOR Items) will be marked as Ready for Invoicing.

Complete and Ready for Invoicing = Both of the above will happen.

The relevant stage(s) will be marked as having occurred, and a record will be written into the history.
