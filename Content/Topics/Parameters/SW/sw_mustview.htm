SW/MUSTVIEW - Must Preview prior to printing

Whether invoices must be viewed before they can be printed (Y or N).

Y = Invoices must be previewed before they can be printed. Also, the ready to print filter on Invoices shows only those invoices that have been previewed but not printed.

N = Invoices can be printed if they have not been previewed. The ready to print filter shows all unprinted invoices - including those that have not been previewed.
