SW/PROBASE - Proforma internal ref base (PPYYMMYYYY)

The format of the base part of reference numbers.

Reference numbers are made up of an optional base plus a format.

The base is a combination of digits taken from the batch General Ledger period and financial date. PROBASE is a list of the positions of the required digits from the following sequence PPYYMMYYYY (where PP = ; YY = period year; MM = month; YYYY = year).

For example, a PROBASE of 9,10,5,6 and a PROFORM of 9999 produces a reference number made up of the last two digits in the year, the two digits of the month, and four digits, such as 07050001.
