SW/FMPAYPIE - Pay items used for piece-work (1st: monetary 2nd: hours)

Two payroll pay items that hold the piece-work values (comma-separated). The 1st pay item holds monetary values and the 2nd holds hours.

These are used when entering monetary values in the FM commercial review screen. If the pay item selection matches the 1st entry in this parameter, it is classed as piece-work, and the user is prompted to enter hours also.
