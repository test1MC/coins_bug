SW/WSALLNAS - Allow worksheet against Operative not assigned to it

Whether worksheet entry is allowed against that a is not assigned to.

The options are:

Y = Worksheet entry is allowed with a warning. This means you can record the fact that a different did the work.

X = Worksheet entry is allowed with no warning. This means you can record the fact that a different did the work.

N = An error message is displayed during worksheet entry. This means you cannot record the fact that a different did the work.
