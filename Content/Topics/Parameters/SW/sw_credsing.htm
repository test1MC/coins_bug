SW/CREDSING - Credit notes print in Single style

Whether to print FM credit notes in single style (Y or N). This means that you get a separate credit note for each FM and the invoice lines are at item level.

Y = There is a credit notes menu under the FM invoicing menu, containing a credit note workbench, generate report, and process and printing utilities. The Credit Note Generation process takes all SOR items and cost transactions that are negative and have a credit type of 'Credit Note' and creates separate credit notes per FM from them. The standard generate invoices process therefore only deals with positive values and items with negative values which have a credit type of 'invoice'.

The print uses whatever style (consolidated, major category, cross ) is set on the FM .

N = The Invoice Generation process deals with both negative and positive valued items and includes them on the same invoice. It is only classed as a credit note where the total invoice amount is negative.
