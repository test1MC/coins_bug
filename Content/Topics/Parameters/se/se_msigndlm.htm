SE/MSIGNDLM - Mobile Customer Signature Disclaimer

Enter the disclaimer text to be seen above the Signature on the Complete user interface (UI).

The text can use Markdown formatting syntax to provide clickable links.

Example:

This Service Report is subject to the Terms and Conditions located at: [www.thecompany.com/terms.html](http://www.thecompany/terms.html). By signing below, Customer Acknowledges that it is read and agrees to these Terms and Conditions.
