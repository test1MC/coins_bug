PR/PAYWKAHO - Pay One Normal Week After Holiday Pay

How do you process pay?

= Payroll processes a normal week's pay after a week that includes pay. (This implies that you process the employee's pay one week before they go on . It allows for a one week lag in paying timesheets.)

= Payroll pays pay in the period before the employee goes on . (This implies that you do not process timesheets while the employee is on .)
