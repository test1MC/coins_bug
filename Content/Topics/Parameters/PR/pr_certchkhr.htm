PR/CERTCHKHR - If both Contract and Employee are Certified, verify that the Timecard Hours match the Certified Hours Distribution on the Timecard.

If both the and Employee are certified, check that the Timecard Hours match the Certified Hours distribution on the timecard.

The options are:

None = No check occurs.

Warning = Displays a warning if the Timecard Hours do not match the Certified Hours.

Error = Displays an error if the Timecard Hours do not match the Certified Hours.
