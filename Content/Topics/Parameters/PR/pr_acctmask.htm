PR/ACCTMASK - Payitem/Employee Account masking priority

The priority of masking when assigning General Ledger accounts from the defaults on employee and pay item records.

Employee = Default account on employee record has priority (this is the normal setting).

Item = Default account on Pay item has priority (this setting is for compatibility with earlier versions).

If the account on the pay item is complete (no underscores), it will always be used. ACCTMASK controls the priority when the pay item account has underscores.
