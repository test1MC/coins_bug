PR/PR-PL-DEPT - Dept used in creditors invoice creation from payroll

This value is a Procurement Overhead Department (specifically for payroll).

For payroll disbursements that have a , this department goes on the invoice, allowing for easy control of invoice selection during payment runs.

For US only: For more information on this parameter and auto invoicing, see the PDF How To Guide entitled Use Auto Invoicing in OA Payroll (US Only) found in Payroll How To Guides.
