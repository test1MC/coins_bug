PR/AGEBANDS - Schemes with Age Banding (rather than monetary)

If you are using pensions schemes with age banding, enter a comma-separated list of scheme numbers with differing rates by age.

For example, in the PENSBASE parameter, if you have 99=>LEL|9=209, then scheme 99 will be based on earnings above the LEL and scheme 9 will be based on the value of item 209.
