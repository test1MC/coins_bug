PR/PENTAXED - Taxable employee pension items

A list of items giving employee pension contributions not paid under net pay arrangements (those included in the taxable pay).

Where a payment is made to an item in this list via a pension scheme set up as being tax deductible that will override this setting and allocate the payment to the pension paid under a net pay arrangement. It may be necessary to include the item in this list rather than the PENNOTAX list as other pension schemes which are not set up as being tax deductible may also use the same item.
