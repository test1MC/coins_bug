MK/DELETECNTRL - Use Marketing Deletion Controls functionality

Controls the switch on/off functionality for delete restrictions in .

When this parameter = Y, and a Company, , Contact, or record is associated/linked to other records (such as Companies, Contacts, , Sub , , , or Notes), a warning appears when you attempt to delete this record, and the record will not be deleted. The warning describes the types of associated records.

You should remove all linked records before removing the current one.
