Timesheet Entry (Regular and Quick Adds)

This section describes how Timesheet Input Groups are now used during Regular and Quick Add Timesheet Entry.

Regular Timesheet Entry

A new Group field is found on the browse screen of Timesheets, as shown in Figure 1: Timesheet Input Screen. It is available for entry when in Add mode and can only be changed using the Merge function. The lookup lists the owner’s own group and subordinate groups.

  • Payroll can be under multiple groups at the same time, which means that a record might be shown on the screen several times (one instance per each group). When this occurs, the Multiple Groups flag is set to let the user know this situation exists. Additional Filters are available at the bottom of the Timesheets browse screen that allows the user to filter on Timesheets in Multiple Groups, as shown in Figure 2: Group Filters.
  • Once a group is entered for a , the group can no longer be updated on this screen. However, you can enter the same under another group, which means a new is not created, and a new group is added to the comma-separated Check TS Input Group List.

Note:

The time cards for an employee may be under multiple Timesheet Input Groups during Timesheet Entry. Before Calculations are run, these time cards MUST reside in one Timesheet Input Group. The PR record has the Multiple Groups field, which is ed if the time cards for the are in multiple groups.

Group Body Details Screen

The Group Body Details screen (shown above in Figure 1: Timesheet Input Screen) displays when you select the Detail button on a Timesheet Input record. Since groups cannot be entered for recurring , the Group field for those is disabled and remains blank. However, if Timesheet Input Groups are in use in the Company, non-recurring (Regular, Adjusting, and Future) cannot be entered without groups, and the Body Details section only contains group and owner information.

  • A cannot be entered under a frozen group. Time cards and Overrides may not be entered, updated, or deleted under a frozen group.
  • Once you enter a under a group not associated with the PR Period and Year, the group becomes associated with the PR Period and Year of that .
  • If you delete all under a group, the Group PR Period and Year are zeroed out, and the group can be used for the same or another period.
  • During a Timesheet entry session, the group entered defaults to the next , along with the Period.
  • Only under your own groups, subordinate groups, and blank groups (recurring ) are available for you on this screen.
  • You can enter non-recurring under your own groups or under subordinate groups.
  • You can only delete recurring or under your own unfrozen groups that are not under multiple groups.
  • You can enter or update only recurring or under your own or subordinate unfrozen groups that are not under multiple groups.
  • When entering a non-recurring , the group should exist for the PR Frequency, Period, and Year, the group should be unfrozen, and you should have permission to enter under the entered group.
  • If TS Input Groups are in use for the company, the following items appear on the screen:
  • Filters: All Groups/My Groups/All Non-Blank Groups/Blank Group/Multiple Groups/Frozen Groups/Unfrozen Groups (Figure 2: Group Filters)

Time Cards and Overrides Tabs

When you click the Employee link on the Timesheet Input screen, a Time Cards screen appears with the following two tabs: Time Cards (Figure 5: Time Cards Tab) and Overrides (Figure 6: Overrides Tab). These tabs can be used to enter time cards and/or overrides. Upon entry, a group is assigned to all time cards/overrides that are entered.

  • Groups and Current Group have been added to the top section of the Time Cards and Overrides tabs.
  • Timesheet Input Group has been added as a read-only field to the Time Card Details layout of the Body Detail section on the Time Cards tab.
  • Only time cards and overrides for the current group can be present or entered on the Time Cards and Overrides tabs.
  • You can enter or update under your own or subordinate unfrozen groups. The under frozen groups cannot be entered or updated.
  • When you enter time cards/overrides for a non-recurring : (1) the group should exist for the PR Frequency, Period, and Year; (2) the group should be unfrozen; and (3) you should have permission to enter /time cards/overrides under the current group.

Quick Add Timesheets

The Quick Add Timesheets functions (Timesheet – by Job, Timesheet – by Employee, and Timesheet – by Multiple Employee) now allow for TS Input Groups.

Screen 1

Screen 1 of each Quick Add Timesheet function (see Timesheet - by Job ( Timesheet - by Job (Screen 1)), Timesheet - by Employee ( Timesheet - by Employee (Screen 1)), and Timesheet - by Multiple Employee ( Timesheet - by Multiple Employee (Screen 1))) now includes the following:

  • A Group column
  • Only records under your own group and subordinate groups
  • A new Group Browse filter
  • A read-only Group field in the Body Detail section
