Pension Increase

The minimum contribution required for auto-enrolment pensions increases in April 2018 as shown below in the extract from the Pension Regulator. It is the employer’s responsibility to check they are using the correct contribution percentages.

Amending existing contribution levels

1. Pension contribution is set on the pension

2. Pension contributions are set on the individual employees

Pension contribution is set on the pension

To amend the contribution on the pension scheme used for auto-enrolment follow the steps below before entering period 1 timesheet data:

  1. Navigate to Payroll > Global Setup > Pensions.
  2. Open the pension scheme used for auto-enrolment.

  3. Amend the contribution amounts and click (Save) on the pension scheme.

  4. Navigate to Payroll, then the relevant frequency that the auto-enrolment is used in (Weekly, Monthly, 2-Weekly or 4-Weekly).
  5. Go to Setup then Parameters within that Frequency payroll.
  6. Update parameters AEPCNTCO, AEPCNTEE and AEPCNTER as highlighted below with the relevant contribution amounts.

Pension contributions are set on the individual employees

To amend individual pension scheme contributions for auto-enrolment follow the steps below before entering period 1 timesheet data:

  1. Navigate to Payroll > Employee Maintenance.
  2. Click into the employee record then the Pensions tab.

  3. Open the relevant auto-enrolment pension, amend the contribution percentages and save the pension record.
  4. Navigate to Payroll then the relevant frequency that the auto-enrolment is used in (Weekly, Monthly, 2-Weekly or 4-Weekly).
  5. Go to Setup then Parameters within that Frequency payroll.
  6. Update parameters AEPCNTCO, AEPCNTEE and AEPCNTER as highlighted below with the relevant contribution amounts.
