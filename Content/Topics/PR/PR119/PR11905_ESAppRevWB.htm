ES Approve & Review Workbench

A new The Approve & Review Workbench is found in Employee Self Service > ESS Portal, as shown in Figure 1: Approve & Review Workbench Screen. It allows you to view and maintain timesheet approvals, employee timesheets, and missing timesheet reports.

Approve & Review Workbench Screen

The three tabs on this screen (Timesheet Approval, Employee Timesheets, Missing TS Reports) are discussed in the sections below.

Timesheet Approval Tab

The Timesheet Approval tab (Figure 2: Timesheet Approval Tab) displays all Employees who have begun reporting and need approval and review of their timesheets.

Timesheet Approval Tab

  • 1st Named Filter (By Period): Active, Dormant, All
  • 2nd Named Filter (By Approver): My Timesheets, All
  • 3rd Named Filter (By Date): This Month, This Year, All
  • 4th Named Filter (By Employee Status): All, Active, Terminated
  • 5th Named Filter (By Summary Record Status): Waiting for Approval, Work in Progress, Approved, Transferred, All

The following actions are found on this screen:

  • Approve Reporting - Sets all Submitted Timesheets to Approved Status. Timesheets become available for transfer.
  • Reject Reporting – Deletes all Submitted Timesheets, removes the E-Signature, and sets reporting records to Rejected status. Note: Approved Timesheets cannot be rejected and must be unapproved before they can be rejected.
  • Unapprove Reporting – Sets all Approved Timesheets to Submitted status.

Employee Timesheets Tab

The Employee Timesheets tab (Figure 3: Employee Timesheets Tab) displays Employee Payroll Timesheets for review/approval. Any submitted time can be redistributed. Once redistribution begins, update and imbalance lines are displayed as ed, as shown in Figure 3: Employee Timesheets Tab.

Missing TS Reports Tab

The Missing TS Reports tab (Figure 4: Missing Reports Tab) allows you to generate a list of Employees who have not begun reporting. You cannot input values, but you can export a list.
