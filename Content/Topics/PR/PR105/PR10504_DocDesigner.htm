Document Designer Document

A new W2 Form – Employee 4 Part Full Format document named %PRW2EMP-4PFull-v2.1 is available in COINS Document Designer. It lets you generate a 4 part W-2 Form with all boxes, labels, and W-2 data in a format similar to a pre-printed form. Although this 4 part W-2 Full Format Form closely duplicates the existing pre-printed form, you have more flexibility with this output because the forms are not scanned for reading by the government.

Previous forms have a Standard (STD) Version and a Down 1.25mm (D125) Version; however, the new 4 Part W-2 Full Format Form (highlighted in Available W-2 Forms ) does not need two versions since a full form is generated.
