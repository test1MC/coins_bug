Employee Maintenance

A new W2 Default Print Method field is available on the Secure Document Email Information layout in Payroll > Employee Maintenance > Employee Details tab > Other tab, as shown in Employee Maintenance Screen. This field allows you to choose one of the following options as the default W-2 delivery method for the employee: EMAIL, HAND-DELIVER, or POSTAL.

The W-2 Form is emailed as a password-protected PDF document. The Email Address and Password used are determined by the Email and Password for Secure Documents on this screen.
