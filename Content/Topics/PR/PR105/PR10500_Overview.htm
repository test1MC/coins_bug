Overview

This document describes changes that were made in COINS OA v11.07 US Payroll for emailing W-2 Forms and printing full W-2 Forms on blank paper. These changes include the following:

COINS OA v11.07 US Payroll allows for emailing W-2 Forms and printing full W-2 Forms on blank paper. Functionality for this includes the following:

  • To eliminate alignment errors that occurred because the boxes on the W-2 Form are small, you can now produce a 4-part W-2 for distribution to employees. It is printed on special blank paper with three perforations between each part, and W-2 related text is pre-printed on the back side of the form.
  • You can now email a W-2 Form as a password-protected PDF document. This new W-2 email process works in the same way as emailing Payroll Payslips and 1095-C documents.
  • Payroll departments now have the choice to save a W-2 against an employee record, which makes the document available in the Employee Self Service Portal.
