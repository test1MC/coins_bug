Employee Auto-Numbering - Overview

Payroll and Human Resources had different methods for defining the format and auto-generating the format of employee numbers and HR IDs. This PDR provides a single, consistent method of defining the numbering format and handling auto-numbering in both modules, allowing users to create a record in either module without causing numbering problems with records in the other module.

The initial changes are in Payroll only; phase 2 will include changes in HR and further changes in Payroll.
