Employee Leaver Status

The Employee Leaver Status option has been removed as it caused problems when choosing to create a new employee record based on a (due to being unable to set mandatory fields). It was also unnecessary as amending of employee leaver statuses can easily be done in Employee Maintenance. Copying a in Employee Maintenance is the quickest way to create a new employee for someone who has left, which crucially gives you access to the new starter fields which cannot simply be copied.
