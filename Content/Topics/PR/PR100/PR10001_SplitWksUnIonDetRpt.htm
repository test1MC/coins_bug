Split Weeks in Union Detail Report

The Union Detail Report in Payroll can split out wage rates when a change occurs within a Payroll period. Union will renew on the first of a month, which could fall within any day during the payroll period. Union rates for employees usually change when renewals take effect during the Payroll period, and COINS OA users wanted the Union Detail Report to break out rates based on the dates of the wages before and after the went into effect.

To satisfy this request, the The PR Union Detail Report can now split Union Calculations by Weeks for all records into two sections as a result of the addition of a new Summary Date field:

  • First section - goes up to the Summary Date, and
  • Second section - summares the information on and after the Summary Date.

This enhancement report functionality is applicable for the US, Canadian, and Australian markets.

Union Detail Report Selection Screen

A new Summary Date option is available on the Union Detail Report selection screen, as shown in Report Selection Screen. When you input a Summary Date in this field, it is used to split all records into two sections, with the first section going up to the Summary Date, and the second section that summares the information on and after the Summary Date.

NOTE:

When a Summary Date is entered, you will not be able to use the Select the Weeks option.

Union Detail Report Samples

Based on whether the Summary Date is entered, the Union Detail Report generates as follows:

  • If the Summary Date field is entered (not blank), Union Calculations by Weeks for all records of the Union Detail Report are split into 2 sections, as shown in Union Detail Report (split into 2 sections). The first section goes up to the Summary Date, and the second section summares the information on and after the Summary Date.

  • If the Summary Date field is not entered (blank), Union Calculations by Weeks for all records of the Union Detail Report are not split into sections, as shown in Union Detail Report (with no split by Summary Date).

Include Amount Based Earnings

If the Include Amount Based Earnings field is ed, it now also includes the following sections for timecards with amount based Earning Categories (see the Union Calculations example in Union Calculations Example):

  • Regular Amount
  • Overtime Amount
  • Double Time Amount
