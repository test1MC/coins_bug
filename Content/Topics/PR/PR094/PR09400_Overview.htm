Overview

In COINS OA, users could auto-create AP (PL) invoices for Payroll calculations such as child support, etc., but the files did not support this setup. The ability to auto-create Invoices based on Union Calculations is now available in Payroll for both Fringes Not in Pay and Deductions. This alleviates the burden of users having to manually key these invoices into individually.

This enhancement is for the USA region only.
