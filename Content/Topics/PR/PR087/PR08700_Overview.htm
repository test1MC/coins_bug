Overview

Payroll Allocations in COINS OA were based solely upon Employee Types and Earnings Categories. Although this worked well for general construction , Allocations for Service Management related work required more options. To accommodate these additional options, Payroll Allocations have been enhanced so that they can now also be based on Costing Companies, Cost Code Types, Service Departments, Service Order Groups, and Service Order Types.
