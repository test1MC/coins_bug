Gender Pay Reporting - Overview

From 2017, any organisation in the UK that has 250 or more employees must publish and report specific figures about their gender pay gap.

Reporting is based on a snapshot date which for businesses is 5 April each year. Organisations must publish within a year of the snapshot date. So businesses must publish by 4 April each year. The first reports must be published by 4 April 2018 based on a snapshot date of 5 April 2017.

Three new reports have been created to help employers with their gender pay gap reporting obligations.
