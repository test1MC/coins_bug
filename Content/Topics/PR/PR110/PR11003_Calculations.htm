Job Calculations

A new LCP Sort Group field is found on the Job Calculations tab in Payroll > Global Setup > US Specifics > Jobs, as shown in Figure 1: Job Calculations Tab. The options listed for this field are generated based on information in the LCP Tracker.

Also, a new Payment in Lieu of Fringes field is found on the Main tab of the Job Calculation Summary screen, as shown in Figure 2: Job Calculation Summary Screen. It can be used to indicate the preference for cash in lieu of fringes.
