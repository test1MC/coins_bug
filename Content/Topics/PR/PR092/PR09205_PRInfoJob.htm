PR Info for and Section

A new Prevailing Wage Schedule field is available on the General Info layout of the certain screens. This field cannot be entered for the or the section if the has a non-dormant Calculation or a non-dormant Rate.

A warning appears if the Prevailing Wage Schedule field is not blank and the Certified option is not ed.

Listed below are the screens where the Prevailing Wage Schedule field is found.

  • Payroll Info collapsible panel of the SV and JC Details screen, as shown below.

  • Payroll Info tab of the SV and JC Details (Tab View), as shown below.

  • Main tab of PR US Specifics > > Summary, as shown below.

  • Payroll Info tab of SV and JC Section Summary (for with sections), as shown below.
