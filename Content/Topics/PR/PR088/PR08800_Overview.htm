Overview

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, also known as the Affordable Care Act (ACA), is the nation’s health care reform law that was enacted in 2010. The law mandates that all Americans will have access to affordable health insurance options.

Employers and the plans they sponsor are subject to many of the requirements of the ACA.

To ease the burden on our users who are subject to ACA and to improve processing speed, enhancements have been made to the ACA functions as outlined in this document.
