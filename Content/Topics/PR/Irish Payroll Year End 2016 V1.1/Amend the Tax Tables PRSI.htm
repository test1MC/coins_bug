In COINSplus:

  1. Go to Global Parameters > Irish Details > Irish Tax Tables.

COINSplus Irish Tax Tables Screen

  1. Press Return within the function to activate the fields that require updates.
  2. Enter the new Tax and PRSI rates for 2016.
  3. Once the rates have been entered press F1 to accept.

In OA:

  1. Go to Global Setup > Annual Details for Tax Year. The data represented below may not be the correctly yearly values. Please ensure you gather the correct values from the ROS website.

OA Irish Tax Tables Screen

  1. Click Open ().
  2. Enter the new Tax and PRSI rates for 2016.
  3. Click Save ().
