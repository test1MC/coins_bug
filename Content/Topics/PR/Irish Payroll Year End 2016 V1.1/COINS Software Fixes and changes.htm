Software Fixes and changes to the software

The Irish Revenue has just made available an online system for employees to provide their own new start of employment details. A consequence of this is that employers have been told to no longer submit a P46 for such employees. A new field has been added to the employee record: Employee self Registered Employment with Revenue.

Theses updates are included in the October Service pack; individual fixes are listed below. Both the service pack and the individual fixes can be downloaded from the client area on the website. http://www.coins-global.com/client-area/8/

  • Omitting Employee P46s when employee has self-registered with effect from 12th Sept 2016
  • P45 XML changes with effect from 26th November 2016

These are in the Oct 2016 Service Packs or the individual fixes listed below:

  • v11.0510001, 10346 and 10431
  • v11.0410213, 10347 and 10432
  • v11.0310214, 10364 and 10433
  • v11.0210215, 10365 and 10434
  • v11.0110216, 10366 and 10435

A new P60 and XML P35 for 2016 year end. These are in the Oct 2016 Service Packs or the individual fixes listed below:

  • v11.058889
  • v11.0410319
  • v11.0310320
  • v11.0210321
  • v11.01 This version is not supported for 2016 year-end

For Cloud customers: we will install the fixes on the copy of live environment for testing; support will let you know when this has been done. You should then inform us when you are ready for us to install them on the live environment. 

Please note: The individual fixes must be installed together prior to attempting P35 XML creation (via the EOY workbench) and printing P60 Certificates.
