Print P35 Report

For the purpose of the reconciliation, you can print the P35 to establish the column totals

To print the P35 in COINSplus, go to either the Weekly (or Monthly) Payroll > Weekly (or Monthly) Administration > P35 Print.

Print the P35 Summary in TEXT format and use Print Method of Text Preview (view to screen) or foreground - print (print to device).

Once the listing is produced you can use the column totals for TAX, PRSI and USC to reconcile back to the week-on-week and month-on-month updates in the relevant General Ledger control accounts.
