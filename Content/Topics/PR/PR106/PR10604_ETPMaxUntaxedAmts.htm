ETP Max and ETP Max Untaxed Amounts

The ETP process now validates that the ETP Max Untaxed Amount has not been exceeded. Based on the PR-ETP-MAXUNTAXED parameter setting, a warning or error is displayed if this occurs.

Also, the Total ETP amount given to an employee should not exceed the ETP Maximum Amount allowed as set against the corresponding parameter.

  • For the HR Personnel record of the employee, the following are checked: all previous links of the employee number, all previous employment periods of this employee, and all companies.
  • The Total ETP paid to this employee is calculated over the year and compared with the value against the ETP Maximum Amount (set up in the parameter). If necessary, an appropriate warning or error is displayed.
