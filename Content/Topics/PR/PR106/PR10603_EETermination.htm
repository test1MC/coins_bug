Employee Termination

Entitlements Layout

When the employee Termination Type code of Blank (Non ETP) is selected, only the Leave fields are allowed to be updated in the Entitlements layout in Payroll > Employee Administration > Employee Termination > Termination Details tab. All of the ETP columns ( Entitlements Layout) are disabled here (ETP Concessional, ETP Untaxed, ETP Taxed, and Total).

Entitlements Layout

Details Layout

An Under Preservation Age field is now found on the Details layout in Payroll > Employee Administration > Employee Termination > Termination Details tab, as shown in Details Layout. If selected on termination, the age of the person is considered to be ‘under the preservation age’ at the end of the income year that the payment is received. The employee ETP payment is taxed at 32% up to the capped amount. If unselected, the employee is considered to be ‘of preservation age or over’ at the end of the income year, and the ETP payment is taxed at 17% up to the capped amount.
