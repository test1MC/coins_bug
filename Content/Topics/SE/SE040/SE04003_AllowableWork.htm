Allowable Work

A new tab labelled G/L Accounts is available in Allowable Work records for entering/viewing G/L Accounts. It has been added is found in the following areas:

  • Allowable Work Setup
  • Allowable Work
  • Nonagreement (Location/Customer) Allowable Work
  • Allowable Work

Allowable Work Setup

The G/L Accounts tab for setup is found in Service Management > Setup > Company Setup > Allowable Work, as shown in Figure 1: Setup - G/L Accounts.

Allowable Work

When adding an Allowable Work, the G/L Account Masking is combined in the Type, Service Department, and Allowable Work Setup. The results are placed in the Allowable Work in this order of preference: Type, Service Department, Allowable Work Setup.

All generated for the inherit their G/L Account Masking from the final results entered on the Allowable Work.

When adding an Allowable Work to the , its G/L Account mask is combined from the related settings only if this Allowable Work is PM ( Maintenance). If the added Allowable Work is non-PM, its G/L Accounts remain empty.

Nonagreement Allowable Work

When adding a Nonagreement (Figure 2: Allowable Work - Nonagreement (Location/Customer)), the G/L Account Masking is combined in the Service Department and Location/Customer Allowable Work, placing the results in the ’s Allowable Work.

The Department’s G/L Account Masking has priority over the Location/Customer Allowable Work.
