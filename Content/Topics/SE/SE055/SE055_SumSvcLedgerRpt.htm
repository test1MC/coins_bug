Summary Service Ledger Report

The new SE Summary Service Ledger report only displays the GL Account information for cost transactions. It does not refer to beginning balances or any other data that did not post to General Ledger.

This document describes the Summary Service Ledger report in detail.

Menu Item

A new Summary Service Ledger option has been added to is found on the Service Management > Reports menu, as shown in Figure 1: SE Reports Menu.

SE Reports Menu

Selection Screen

When you select Summary Service Ledger from the SE Reports menu, a selection screen appears, as shown in Figure 2: Report Selection Screen.

Report Selection Screen

Field Definitions

Include Detail

Indicate whether you wish to include the Detail on the report.

Show To Date

Indicate whether you wish to include To Date information on the report.

Show To Date Values For Accounts Printed

Indicate whether you wish to include To Date values for accounts printed on the report. Note: These may not be the total To Date values if the GL Accounts used on the are changed prior to the start of the .
