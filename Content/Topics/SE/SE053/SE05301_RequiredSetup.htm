To allow a Detail Line to be billed to a different Customer, the Allowable Work for the Primary Customer must reference the second Customer as the Bill To Customer in Service Management > Locations > Nonagreement Allowable Work Details Tab > Main subtab, as shown in Figure 2: Bill To Information. The value entered in the Bill To Customer field becomes the default Customer assigned to the Detail Line, and the Detail Line’s Customer is then changed to the ’s Customer.
