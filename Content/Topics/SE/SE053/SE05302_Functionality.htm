Functionality

Detail Lines

A new Bill To field is found on the Detail Line, as shown in Figure 2: Bill To Information above. For example, you can use this field to create Detail Lines for both the home owner and the home warranty company. Some notes about this field are as follows:

  • It defaults the Bill To Customer from the Allowable Work setup as shown in Figure 2: Bill To Information above.
  • Users are able to select the appropriate Customer using the Customer lookup, which will be limited to the ’s Customer and the Bill To Customer.
  • It is only enabled when it equals C-Customer and the ’s Customer is not equal to the Bill To Customer.

Allowable Work

If an On-Site Payment exists, the Bill To field is now disabled in the Allowable Work record in Service Management > Locations > Nonagreement Allowable Work Details Tab > Main subtab, as shown in Figure 1: Disabled Bill To Field. This means that you are unable to switch from C-Customer to J-Job or N-Nonbillable.

Disabled Bill To Field

On-Site Payments

A Customer field is now available on the On-Site Payments screen, as shown in Figure 2: On-Site Payments Screen. You can use this field to designate which Customer the payment was from.

On-Site Payments Screen

Some notes about this field are as follows:

  • It defaults from the Customer (such as the home owner). For example, payments received via the mobile device would default as being from the home owner.
  • It is only enabled when Allowable Work Bill To equals C-Customer and the ’s Customer is not equal to the Bill To Customer.
  • It provides Customer lookup, but only the ’s Customer and the Bill To Customer are shown.
  • Validation now only accepts the ’s Customer or the Bill To Customer.

Post On-Site Payments

The posting routine now recognes the Customer on the On-Site Payment record; it does not automatically use the Order’s Customer.

Comments

Any Comment that is set up to be a Invoice Comment is associated with the Bill To Customer Invoice, as shown in Figure 8. No changes have been made in this area.

Comments

Documents to Include in Next Invoice

All documents selected to Include in Next Invoice (Figure 4: Documents to Include in Next Invoice) are now associated with the Bill To Customer Invoice. No changes have been made in this area.

Documents to Include in Next Invoice

Generate Invoice

The Generate Invoice action in s now produces a separate invoice for each Customer referenced on the Detail Lines.

  • The On-Site Payments are applied to the proper invoice.
  • Invoice Comments and Documents to Include in Next Invoice are assigned to the Bill To Customer Invoice.
  • Both invoices receive one additional Comment showing the value of the Invoice Amount and Payment Amount to the other party.

Post Processing

Payroll Post Processing now assigns all Bill To Detail Lines (“SO” only) to equal the ’s Bill To. In addition, the Customer field in all On-Site Payment records is equal to the Customer.
