Overview

The COINS OA Service Management module now allows you to bill two Customers from a single . This includes the ability to collect an on-site payment from the Customer who owns that . For example, residential customers with a home warranty require the home owner to pay a deductible, with the balance billed to the home warranty company. (This does not include the storage of the deductible amount or have a price schedule established with the warranty company.)

This document explains the changes that have been made so that billing two Customers can work correctly in COINS OA.
