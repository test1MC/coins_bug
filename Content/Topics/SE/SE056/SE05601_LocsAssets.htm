Locations -

When an Type is set up to contain refrigerants by Circuit, the Refrigerant information for a Location is maintained in the record in Service Management > Locations > .

Since having the ability to define Capacity in two ways (single Refrigerant Capacity and Refrigerant Capacity by Circuit) did not support the ability to provide Leak Rate calculations, the single Refrigerant Capacity field was has been removed from Locations > . As a result, the screen now only contains the Refrigerant Capacity by Circuit fields, as shown in Figure 1: Locations – [%=Variables.Se Assets caps%] Screen.
