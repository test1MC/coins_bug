Overview

COINS OA users requested a report providing a quick overview of all invoices in a batch that includes Gross Margin values. This type of report has been added to is found in OA and is referred to as the Pre-Posting Invoice Summary Report. It allows management to review the invoices before printing/posting.

A new An Invoice Summary Report is also available in COINS OA. This report lists invoices posted in a range, including the option to print by Invoice Date range. Emphasis is on invoice value, with Gross Margin included for reference.

Both of these Invoice Summary reports are discussed in more detail in this document.
