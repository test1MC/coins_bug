Location , Hazards, Skills Fields

The Locations function of Service Management can now be company (kco) specific for , Hazards, and Skills fields, as described below.

NOTE:
Corresponding functionality for this feature is also found in Mobile Tech versions 1.9 and above.

Location

A Do Not Allow Company List field is now available in Service Management > Locations > tab on a new Do Not Allow Company List tab, as shown in Figure 1: [%=Variables.Se Assets caps%] Do Not Allow Company List.

Do Not Allow Company List

  • The Available list only lists the Companies with the Location in view. It does not display a full list of Companies in the database.
  • When adding an , the Do Not Allow Company List is automatically set to all other Companies in view for the Location.
  • You may use the arrows on this screen to add or delete a current Company from the lists. An cannot be deleted if it is referenced on any , , or parent .

Location Hazards

A Do Not Allow Company List field is now available in Service Management > Locations > Hazards tab, as shown in Figure 2: [%=Variables.Se Assets caps%] Do Not Allow Company List.

Do Not Allow Company List

  • Since the Location RO field that returns the Number of Hazards (RO_slc_NumOfHaz) counts all Hazards, its label was changed to Number of Hazards – All Companies.
  • An A new RO field Number of Hazards – This Company (RO_slc_NumOfHazKco) counts the number of Hazards for the current company. It is found on all browses that reference the original RO field.

Location Skills

A Do Not Allow Company List field is now available in Service Management > Locations > Skills tab, as shown in Figure 3: [%=Variables.Se Technician caps%] Skills Do Not Allow Company List.
