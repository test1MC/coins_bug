Improvements have been made to the Renewal functionality in COINS OA Service Management to increase the efficiency of the process. These improvements include the ability to:

The Renewal functionality in COINS OA Service Management increases the efficiency of the process. It has the ability to:

  • Update the Renewal data in the browse using Concurrent Update.
  • Mass select for Renewal via a selection screen that runs as a report/process.
  • Create a schedule of Amounts for future renewal periods.
  • Store the total Term of Duration and the Amount for that are managed in 12-month renewal periods. Note: The storage of the total Term of Duration, Amount, and Purchase Order values uses a new Term function, as discussed in detail in Section 2.1.2.
  • Reduce the amount of time to manage Purchase Order values.

This document discusses the improvements to this process in more detail.
