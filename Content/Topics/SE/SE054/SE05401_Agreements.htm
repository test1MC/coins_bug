Term Browse

COINS OA users who choose to manage in 12-month renewal reporting periods can now use the new Term browse (Figure 2: Term Browse) to store the ’s contractual obligation, such as total Duration and Amount.

  • When renewing the , the system looks for the Term that applies to the Renewal’s Start Date and copies the PO value.
  • The PO fields on this browse assist with an Renewal only. The system continues to use the PO value within the referenced Renewal record as the default to the .
  • The Total Amount field is the amount for all locations of the . The Term Total Amount is not automatically updated when changes are saved on the Amount browse. You can maintain this field manually when changes to the are made.
