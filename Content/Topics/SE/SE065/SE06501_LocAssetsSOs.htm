The original Quoted Price is not broken down by , and Quoted Price Detail Lines do not allow reference to a particular . As a result, changes were made so that the The value of the Quoted Price Billings (not including tax or add-ons) is now proportionally distributed to each serviced based on Detail Total Hours Worked.

For example, if the Total Hours Worked is 10 and two were serviced for 3 and 7 hours, the first receives 30% of the Quoted Price and the second receives 70% of the Quoted Price.

The column set for the browse in Locations > Location now provides you with the ability to display the Detail Line Billing Amounts and Detail Hours Charged/Worked for a particular . The screen below (Figure 1: Location [%=Variables.Se Assets caps%] - [%=Variables.Se Service Orders caps%]) highlights the columns that are now available in the standard column set to allow for this.

Note: Tax, add-ons, etc. are not broken down by .
