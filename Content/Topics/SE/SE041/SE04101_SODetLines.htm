Detail Lines

You can access the Ready for Invoicing flag (Figure 1: [%=Variables.Se Service Order caps%] Detail Lines) in both Add and Update mode on Detail Lines (unless the Billing Method is NB-Nonbillable). This flag can be manually ed (set to Y), even when the Subtotal Amount is $0.

Detail Lines
NOTE:
When saving a record for a Detail Line referencing the NB-Nonbillable Billing Method, the process behaves as previously by setting the Ready for Invoicing flag to uned and not displaying it on the screen.

The process now does the following:

  • If you add a Detall Line and have a non-zero Subtotal Amount, the Ready for Invoicing box is ed.
  • If you add a Detail Line and have a zero Subtotal Amount, the Ready for Invoicing box may be ed.
  • If you update a Detail Line that has a zero Subtotal Amount by changing the Billing Method to NB-Nonbillable, the Ready for Invoicing box is reset to uned and does not appear on the screen when saved.
