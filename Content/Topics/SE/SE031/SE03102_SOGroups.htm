Groups

The Group record previously had a Minimum Scheduled Hours field meant to serve as the default for a ’s Scheduled Hours. Since the value for this field was a default, "Minimum" was not a good term to use. In addition, this one field served as a default for both PM and Non-PM , which was not practical. To remedy this, the following changes were made in the Groups function and are shown in Figure 2: [%=Variables.Se Service Order caps%] Groups:

  • Minimum Scheduled Hours was relabelled as Default Non-PM Scheduled Hours.
  • A new Default PM Scheduled Hours field was added.
