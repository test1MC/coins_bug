Overview

COINS OA allows users in Service Management to define the Service Report Email Subject, which includes Service Invoice data. Enhancements have been made to extend this capability to also define the Email Subject in the following areas:

  • Service Report printed from a Mobile device; and
  • Service Report printed from either the browse action (Consolidated) or the browse action (Single or Consolidated)

Details on these enhancements are provided in this document.
