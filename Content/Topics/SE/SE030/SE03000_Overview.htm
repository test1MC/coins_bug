Overview

Previously, Revenue in the OA Service Management module could be calculated based on Billings, Sum of Detail Lines, or Percent Complete Method. However, none of these methods was adequate for incomplete without budgets, which were either billed multiple times or billed in a different from when a cost was incurred. To provide for the ability to handle these incomplete Service Orders, a new C (Cost Multiplier) Revenue Recognition Method (RRM) is available in Service Management.

This Cost Multiplier RRM is applicable for Service Orders only and is discussed in more detail in this document.
