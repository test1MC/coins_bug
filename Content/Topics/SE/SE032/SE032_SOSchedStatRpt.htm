Schedule Status Report

A new Schedule Status Report has been added to is available in the Service Management module. It allows you to see the current schedule status of and can also include the schedule history.

Menu Option

The new Schedule Status Report is found as a menu option on the Service Management > Reports menu, as shown in Figure 1: Reports Menu .

Reports Menu

Report Selection Screen

When you select Schedule Status Report from the menu, the report selection screen appears, as shown in Figure 2: Report Selection Screen.

Report Selection Screen

Field Definitions (selected fields)

Sort By

You can indicate how you wish to sort the report by selecting any of the following options:

  • /Location/Call Date
  • Customer/Location/Call Date
  • Preferred /Customer/Location/Call Date
  • Primary /Customer/Location/Call Date

New Page Per

Whether to create a new Page for each . This is enabled only when Sort By is either Preferred or Primary .

Show Schedule History

Whether to show the Schedule History of the on the report.

Note: When Show Schedule History is selected (Y) and details are printed, the Costs data for the 5th and 6th Cost Categories are merged into one column labelled Other.

Scheduling Complete

Indicate the scheduling information that you want to include on the report: All, Not Scheduling Complete, Scheduling Complete.

From/To Call Date

The Call Date or range of Call Dates that you wish to report on for the .

From/To Promised Date

The Promised Date or range of Promised Dates that you wish to report on for the .
