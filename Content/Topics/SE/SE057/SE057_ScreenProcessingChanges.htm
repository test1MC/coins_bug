Conditions When Utility Cannot Be Used

The Transfer utility has a number of conditions under which it cannot be used. These include the following:

  • The is not Schedule Complete.
  • The is Billing Complete.
  • The of the is Billing Complete. Review of the original may be required to determine if any over billing exists.
  • If the references a timesheet, the timesheet must be in Signed status. No changes can be pending on the mobile device.
  • cannot be transferred to PR. The cost analysis on the PR time card will be incorrect.
  • Detail cannot have a generated Detail Line. The billing on the original cannot be started.
  • Detail cannot have a generated transaction. The costing analysis on the transaction will be incorrect.
  • The cannot have any processed On-Site Payments. The utility will not move the payment to a new order.
  • The cannot have any PO Items with non-zero quantities. Purchase Order data follows the Number; therefore, no PO data should be involved. It is the user’s responsibility to determine the state of the PO Lines on the new . Manual processing of returns and other transfers is required. The system removes any PO Item records when moving the to the new .
  • The cannot have any Task List Items marked as DONE. This will be a manual process of updating the Task List for the new .

A must be Schedule Complete and not Billing Complete in order to qualify for transfer to a different . If the meets these two conditions and therefore can be transferred, the following is also considered:

  • If the references Serviced , or any of the details reference an , the Location of the old and new must be the same.
  • The new must have one Type of Work record, which is used as the new value to assign to the Detail. All of the and Groups referenced within Detail must be on file for the new .
