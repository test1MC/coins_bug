Transfer Action

A Transfer action is found at the bottom of the Transfer browse screen, as shown in Figure: Transfer [%=Variables.Se Visit caps%] Action. This action lets you select and submit a for transfer to a different .

Transfer Action

A must be Schedule Complete and not Billing Complete in order to qualify for transfer to a different . If the meets these two conditions and therefore can be transferred, the following is also considered:

  • If the references Serviced , or any of the details reference an , the Location of the old and new must be the same.
  • The new must have one Type of Work record, which is used as the new value to assign to the Detail. All of the and Groups referenced within Detail must be on file for the new .

Once all conditions are met, you can select a from the browse screen and apply the Transfer action. You are then directed to a Transfer screen, as shown in Figure: Transfer [%=Variables.Se Visit caps%] Screen. Here you can transfer the to the that is entered in the Transfer to field.
