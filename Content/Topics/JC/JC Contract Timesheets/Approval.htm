Approval

There are various methods of approval of timesheets, for those users who are set as a timesheet approver.

If you are designated as an Approver and open Employee Department Timesheet Weekly Approval, you are presented with a list of employees whose time you are allowed to approve.

When employees are added in Timesheets > Employees, certain employees are designated to be approvers of other employees timesheets.

Contract Status parameter TSAPPROC must be set to Y to use the review and approval functionality.

Associated Parameters

JC/TSAPPROC

Whether to batch only approved timesheets (Y or N).
