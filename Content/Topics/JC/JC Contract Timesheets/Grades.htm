Grades

Employee Grades contains a list of activities associated with a grade.

Employees are assigned grades in Contract Status.

The association of a grade to an employee allows you to provide a pre-defined list of activities that an employee can use during Contract Timesheet Entry.

The rates are applicable to any employee who belongs to the grade.

Drill on Grade to add activities to a grade.

Employee Grade Rates allows you to add contract specific rates, as well as date specific rates, to a grade

Activity
