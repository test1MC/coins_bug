Screen and Processing Changes

JC Daily Reports - Review and Approval

Deliveries Tab

A new Deliveries tab was added in Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval, as shown in Figure 1. The browse shows all the Material GRN records for the selected and Date.

JC Daily Reports Deliveries Tab

Browse Actions

The actions at the bottom of the Deliveries browse are as follows:

  • Accept Whole Quantity - The entire quantity of the delivery record is marked as accepted.
  • None Delivered - The delivery record is marked as having no deliveries made.
  • Close - The delivery record is closed.
  • Open - The delivery record is opened.
