Audit Workbenches

An audit workbench is created automatically for each H&S Audit Mobile Form that is flagged as Live.

Audit Workbench

When audits are submitted from a mobile device they are completed automatically.  Audits added through OA are incomplete until the user chooses to complete them (using the Complete action). 

Score columns appear when that field is set in the completion calculation.

There is an action to print the document, which uses the default Document Designer form specified for the audit.

On adding a new form you get to update the . For H&S audits the field is always present and validated, but you can choose to leave it blank if the Audit is company level. Then you are taken to the Audit Form summary.

The summary has tabs for update/view of the form and the ancillary data.
