Screen

Once you have made your selections in the Companies Summary screen, the Next button at the bottom of the screen can be clicked. You are then directed to the Companies Summary screen, as shown in Companies Summary [%=Variables.Enquiries caps%] Screen - All Tabs. This screen is divided into four separate tabs:

  • By Company (see Section 1.1 By Company Tab for details)
  • By Company / Office (see Section 1.2 By Company / Office Tab for details)
  • Touch (see Section 1.3 Touch Tab for details)
  • Companies Summary Filter (see Section 1.4 Companies Summary [%=Variables.Enquiries caps%] Filter Tab for details)

Service

Companies Summary provides you with the possibility to check information about Service data of the Company using Column Sets.

The Service column can contain the subcolumns listed below. Each # subcolumn allows you to drill down to the screen with details of corresponding data (see Sections 1.1.3 Agreements of Company Screen and 1.1.4 Agreement Svc Orders of Company Screen for more details)

Additional fields available in Column Sets are:

  • Agreements # (drill-down) - The number of all Agreements of the Company.
  • Agreements $ - The sum of Revenue To Date of all Agreements of the Company.
  • Agreement Svc Orders # (drill-down) - The number of all Agreement Related Service Orders.
  • Agreement Svc Orders $ - The sum of Revenue To Date of all Agreement Related Service Orders.
  • Svc Orders # (drill-down) - The number of all Non-Agreement Service Orders of the Company.
  • Svc Orders $ - The sum of Revenue To Date of all Non-Agreement Service Orders.

Touch Tab

The Touch tab provides you with a screen (Figure 10) that includes information regarding contact with the company.

Touch Tab

Field Definitions

Company

The name of the company.

Last Updated

The date that this record was last updated.

# Offices

The number of offices for this company. For more information about an office, you can drill-down on this column to reach the Company > Offices tab.

# Contacts

The number of contacts for this company. For more information about a contact, you can drill-down on this column to reach the Company > Contacts tab.

Last Note

The most recent note on record for this company. For more information about a note, you can drill-down on this column to reach the Company > Notes tab.

Last Appointment

The most recent appointment on record for this company. For more information about an appointment, you can drill-down on this column to reach the Company > Activities tab.

Last Tasks

The most recent tasks on record for this company. For more information about a task, you can drill-down on this column to reach the Company > Activities tab.

Create Date

The date and user ID for the creation of this record for the company or office.
