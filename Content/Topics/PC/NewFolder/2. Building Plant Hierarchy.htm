Building Plant Hierarchy

Once you have decided on the Plant Hierarchy, you can begin to build it using the below file format in Excel.

 

 

We would advise that the codes are purely alphanumeric with no spaces or special characters in them because when they're exported/typed into Excel some characters are taken as a calculation e.g. "/" is divide.
