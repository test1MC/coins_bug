Creating Shifts

Create Personnel Shifts is a new report process that creates empty shift records for personnel who are working on a shift pattern, and where they have an active status (as specificed by HR/STATACT). You specify on the report how many days in to the future to create shifts for. This report could be scheduled to run overnight on a weekly basis.

These shift records define who is available to an operator or shift requirement.

  • If someone is a qualified operator, their shifts will be tied to the depot they work for. (A qualified operator is someone qualified to operate one or more groups of equipment, and who has been flagged as such using the Qualified Operator Report process.)
  • If they are not a qualified operator, and their job title is linked to a Code that is in the web (in their company), then shift records will be created and they can a requirement.
