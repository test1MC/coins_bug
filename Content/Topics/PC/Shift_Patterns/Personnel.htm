Personnel – Shift Patterns

For personnel, you can say which shift pattern they are working.

To assign a shift pattern

  1. Go to Personnel (on the Central Repository menu).
  2. Click the link in the Shift column.
  3. This displays the Shift Patterns screen for the employee in a new tab or new window.

  4. Enter the From Date (when the employee starts the shift) and select the Shift Pattern.
  5. Optionally, you can enter an Offset if the employee does not start at the beginning of the shift pattern. So if you have a 14 day pattern of night and day shifts they may start on nights or days, or part way through either.

    If the shift pattern starts on a specified day of the week, the offset identifies which shift of the start day. If the pattern has no specific start day, the offset specifies any shift of the pattern.
