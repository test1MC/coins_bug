Entering Workshop GRNs

 

In order to enter goods received against a Workshop Order, the order must first have been committed. creates accruals when the goods received note is entered.

 

Navigate to Procurement > More GRNs > Workshop GRNs

To enter a new GRN, click

Complete the screen with the details below.

Field

Description
Ticket No The Suppliers Delivery note reference.
Delivery Date The date the goods were delivered
PO Number Enter the Workshop Order Number, or use the Lookup to locate it.

 

Add any notes in the Notes field if required and then click .

The lines of the Order will be displayed with the quantities ordered

If the full quantity of an order line has been delivered, select the line and set the Action Drop-down to Accept Whole Quantity and click the action button. will automatically update the Delivered column with the ordered quantity.

Alternatively, if only some of the goods have been delivered, you can record the partial delivery by opening the line and specifying the actual quantity delivered.

Once the GRN has been entered, the invoices may be processed for the goods delivered.

Invoicing of Goods is covered in the relevant Learning Resources and is not documented further here.

Once the invoice is posted, the costs for the goods will appear on the Job Card Costs tab against the Job Card in

Plant Control > Workshop > Job Cards
