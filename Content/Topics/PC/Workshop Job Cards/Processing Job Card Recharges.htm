Processing Job Card Recharges

 

Navigate to Plant > Workshop > Job Cards

Click the Hyperlink on the Job Card Number to view the Job Card details.

Select the Workshop Recharges Tab.

This tab will only be available if the option to recharge the costs was ticked on the Job Card Detail Tab.

This tab displays all the costs incurred by the Job Card. These costs are defaulted into the Recharge column.

To manually override the recharge values, click against the line to amend.

You may also manually add additional lines for recharging by clicking

You must add the line by way of a workshop cost head. You will then be able to enter a description and a recharge value. There will be no cost directly associated with this entry.

Having entered all the recharges (remember that you may be reducing or zeroing the recharge values), you can complete the Job Card, you can complete the Job Card.
