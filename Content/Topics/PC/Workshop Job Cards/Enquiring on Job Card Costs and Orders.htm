Enquiring on Job Card Costs and Orders

 

To see the effect of the Order on the Job Card, navigate to Plant Control > Workshop > Job Cards.

The job Card Costs Screen is displayed, showing the cost transactions entered to date.

The cost of the order you have just raised is not yet shown here. The value of the order is not show as it is not yet a true cost. It will only become a true cost once the order has been invoiced.

 

Select the Orders for Job Card tab.

This tab shows details of all orders placed against the individual Job Card. Your order should now show here.
