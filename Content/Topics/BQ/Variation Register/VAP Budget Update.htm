Variations and VAP Budget Update

When running Update VAP Budget from , detailed items linked to live variations will always be included.

If Update VAP Budget with Items is ed on the status of the variation and at least one item is linked to the variation, the revised items will be included in the budget update.

The budget can also include an allowance for variations which have not been approved, or which have been approved but for which the detailed items have not yet been input. The allowance is included on the budget against a fixed payment stage/plot combination, which is specified on the variation reason code (see 1 , Variation Reason Codes).

This creates an allowance based on the variation’s approved cost for the value of such items in the budget and thus in the . The status at which the approved costs get included in the VAP Budget is determined by the Update VAP Budget with Approved Cost option on the associated status code.
