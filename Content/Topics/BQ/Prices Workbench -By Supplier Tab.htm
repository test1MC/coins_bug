Prices Workbench - By Supplier Tab

By Supplier allows you to view a list of all suppliers.

 

 

Field Description
Name The name of the company. The value of the Search By selector determines which name is shown.
Office The name of the office.
Type A code for the office type
Co T/A's If there is a in this column, your company has a trading agreement with this company. This could be either a memo agreement, or an SRM trading agreement.
Group T/A's If there is a in this column, your group of companies has a trading agreement with this company.
CDM

The CDM approval status of this Supplier.

= You have approved the company from a CDM perspective. The tooltip shows the expiry date.

= The subcontractor's approval is due to expire within the next 15 days. The tooltip shows the expiry date.

= The subcontractor has not been approved or the approval has expired.

blank = The company is not a subcontractor (CDM does not apply
Co Status The status your company has given to this company.
Group Status

The group-wide status code for a company.

Status codes allow you to keep track of the status of information about a company in the Central Repository. Group status codes are visible to all your companies. For example, you might change the group-wide status code if a company ceases trading or goes into liquidation.
Co Score The performance score that your company has given the supplier's office.
Group Score The performance score that your group has given the supplier's office.
O/E

Order = Your company has placed an order with this company.

Enquiry = Your company has sent an enquiry to this company but has not placed an order.

blank = Your company has not dealt with this company.
Unlabelled Field This field displays an "*", if Notes have been entered against this Company

Unlabelled Field

Whether notes have been entered against this company.

If notes have been entered against the company, this field shows a flag icon .

If at least one note is marked by a Red Flag, the flag will appear here as a Red Flag .

To see the notes, click the flag icon.

 

When you click on a supplier link, a screen appears showing a list of prices for that supplier only. Any new prices added here default to the selected supplier.
