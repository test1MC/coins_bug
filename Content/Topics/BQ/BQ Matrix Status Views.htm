BQ Matrix Status Views

The status views show:

  • The item pricing status - whether the items have been priced.
  • The ready to use (from a suveyor's point of view) indicator or, on Package Matrices, the passed to procurement (from a buyer's point of view) indicator - whether the items have been marked as "ready to use".
  • The order status (on the plot workbenches Surveyors Plot Workbench and Buyers Workbench).
Ready to Use /

Passed to Procurement
NRU / NPS =

Not ready/not passed - none of the associated bill items are marked as "ready to use".
PRU / PPS =

Part ready/part passed - some of the items are ready to use.
FRU / PAS =

Ready/fully passed - all associated bill items are ready to use. This means the bill items have the correct quantities and are ready to be priced and ordered.
Item pricing status
UPR =

Unpriced - bill items exist and are all unpriced.
PPR =

Part - bill items exist but are part-priced.
FPR =

Full - bill lines exist and are all fully priced.

A * next to the status shows that there has been a change from the standard house type.

And on the plot workbenches:
Order Status
UOR =

Unordered - there are no orders for any associated bill item.
POR =

Part ordered - some of the bill items have been ordered.
ORD =

Fully ordered - all associated bill items are ordered.
VAR =

required - the details of an order that has already been placed for at least one associated bill item have been changed, so a is required.

The cells may also show:

  • N/A - The element is not applicable.
  • N/R - The element is not required.
  • No Items - There are no bill items associated with the element
