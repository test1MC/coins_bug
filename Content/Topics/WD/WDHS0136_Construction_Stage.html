Updating the Construction Stage

As the construction of a property progresses, you can update the construction stage in the Sales Workbench to reflect this. 

To update the construction stage:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "construction stage".
  3. Select the construction stage.
  4. Click .

Construction stage - configuration

You need to set up an CSTAGE event; see Sales Events.

Alternatively, the construction stage can be automatically updated from the sheet in VAP.
