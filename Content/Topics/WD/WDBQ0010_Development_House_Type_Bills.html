Updating VAP House Type Budget

You can update the House Type Budget in VAP with figures calculated from the House Type Bill.  (When the VAP budget is updated from the module then a copy of the relevant bill records will be created with this field populated with the VAP budget that was updated.  This allows a snapshot of the bill to be taken to support the updated VAP budget values.)

There are different ways to update the VAP Budget:

  • Using an option on the Option menu (this runs a background report process)
  • From the Payment Stage Matrix, by selecting payment stages, or payment stages on specific house types, and applying an update action.
