Report Writer Administrator Training

For Report Writer to be an effective tool for the end-users and the individual needs of your specific business, a trained Report Writer Administrator must be available to assist the end-users who will be writing reports.  Administrator candidates must be experienced in, or capable of acquiring, knowledge of:

  • UNIX
  • 4GL Query writing
  • Relational database structure
  • Modules and table structure

The Administrator training is held at the offices in Slough periodically.  It covers all aspects of administering the RW Toolset.

  • Defining, and creating queries and report selection criteria to provide a normalised view of the data.
  • Accessing queries and data sets from the coins BI Library.
  • Security, access and general system administration
