Making Payments Automatically

allows you to pay invoices automatically by selecting outstanding transactions for a . will automatically create a payment for the total amount. There are a number of procedures that need to be performed in order to produce automatic payments.

To pay automatically:

  1. Select the invoices or batches to be paid (see Selecting Invoices for Payment).
  2. Print a report of the invoices or batches you have selected (see Selected/Not Selected Report).
  3. Freeze the payment batch (see Freezing the Payment Batch).
  4. Process the payments:
    • Process the payments (see Payments).
    • Print the in the payment batch, and the remittances (see Printing and Remittance Advices).
  5. Post the payments (see Posting a Batch).

The following sections cover each of the above steps. Steps 1, 3, 4 and 5 must be done in order. If the steps are not followed as shown, issues an error message and will not let you continue.
