Advanced Filter

Many browse screens have an advanced filter that gives you more powerful filtering options.

For example, the filter above on the Subcontractor Maintenance browse would show subcontractors with account codes between 000 and 500, in the RG area, with "roof" in their name.

To use the advanced filter

  1. To display the advanced filter, click or press Ctrl+Shift+F.

    The Advanced Filter frame opens on top of the browse.

  2. To filter on a range of values, enter the values in the From and To fields.
  3. To filter on records that have fields that match a particular value, enter the value in the Matches field.  You can also use additional search characters.
  4. Click (or press Ctrl+Shift+A).

To clear the advanced filter

  • Click .

    This sets all the filter fields to blank and re-applies the filter.

To hide the advanced filter frame

  • Press Ctrl+Shift+F or Esc.

    This hides the advanced filter frame, but keeps the filter settings.
