Recording a New Part Exchange

If the purchaser's property is being taken in part exchange, once you have entered the reservation, you can enter details of the part exchange. 

To record a new part exchange:

    1. Go to the Plot Details screen in Sales Workbench.
    2. On the PX tab (which is on the Reservation tab), click .
    3. If the part-exchange property is the current address of one of the purchasers, you can copy the address from the purchaser's information: in the Copy From field, select the purchaser.

      If the part-exchange property has a different address, leave the Copy From field set to <Select Details to Copy>.  

    4. Enter the remaining details of the part-exchange property (refer to the side-frame help).
    5. Click .

When you save the part-exchange, generates a reference number.  The part-exchange property will now be visible on Part Exchange Workbench.
