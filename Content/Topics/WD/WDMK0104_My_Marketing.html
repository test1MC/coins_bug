My

My gives you a customed view of the database that includes only the companies, , contacts and events you are interested in. These are either records that you entered as new, or records that you have added to your view. You can add records from the main workbenches to your My view.

Records in your "My " view will still be visible to other users, and other users can have the same records in their "My " view.
