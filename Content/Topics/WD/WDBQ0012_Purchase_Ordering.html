Purchase Ordering in

The Buyers Workbench will be primarily used by buyers, who are responsible for the ordering of the materials, subcontract and specified in the .  The main preparation of the is expected to be done by the estimator/surveyor via the House Type Workbench and Surveyors Plot Workbench. 

The main focus of the Buyers Workbench is to allow you to generate orders in the Procurement module from plot items.  Options allow you to nominate , apply/update prices and to view/order items by their associated material, or subcontract package.  When raising orders you have the option to format the orders either in terms of bulk quantities or in terms of plot sets.  You also have the option of adding lines (text or commodity) to the order, but you will not be able to amend lines sourced from a item.

Nominating

On the Package Matrix, Resource Matrix and Estimating Sections matrix screens in various workbenches, you can nominate a for selected plots/packages from the Companies.

The workbench also provides facilities to generate for items which have been amended since ordering.  may be required for quantity changes, price changes, new lines added, lines cancelled, or replacement of one resource with another. 

In addition there are options to cancel the balance of orders and re-order replacements for lost, damaged or stolen items.

Orders Requiring Variation shows a list of orders placed for the , on which one or more items have been changed. You can then raise a , or mark the changed item as not required.

Variation Matrix shows plots and packages.  It shows whether quantities, prices, or items have been changed from what has been ordered.  It also indicates if re-orders have been placed, or if there are any unordered items.  You can raise or flag changes as not requiring an order.

Items Basket

The Items Basket shows items you have selected for ordering.  You can then raise procurement orders for these items (as long as the items are ready to use, have a nominated, and have an agreed price or are flagged as free of charge).

You can raise orders for all items in their basket, or for selected items only.

An order browse allows you to commit and print orders, and to create or cancellation orders.
