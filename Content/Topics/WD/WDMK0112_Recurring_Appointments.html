Recurring Appointments

A recurring appointment is one that occurs at regular intervals, at the same time of day. You can set up appointments in that recur weekly (on the same day of the week), monthly (on the same date in each month) or yearly (on the same date each year). 

When you save a recurring appointment, creates separate entries for the appointment in the calendar.  If you change one of the appointment entries later, the changes will not affect other entries of the appointment. 

If you are using Outlook integration, the appointment entries are copied to Outlook as separate appointments; they are not linked in the way recurring appointments in Outlook are.

To create a recurring appointment:

  1. On one of the browse screens, click .
  2. Select a Type that corresponds to an appointment.
  3. Fill in the details of the appointment.
  4. the Recurrence box.
  5. Use the Recurrence Type selector to specify how often you want the task or appointment to recur.
  6. Either:
    • in the Recur Until field, enter the date up to which you want the activity to recur, or
    • in the enter number of occurrences field, enter the number of entries to create. 
  7. Save the appointment record.
