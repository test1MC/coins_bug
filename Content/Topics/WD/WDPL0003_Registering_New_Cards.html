Registering New PCards

A newly-issued PCard will usually have been used before has any knowledge of it. When transactions belonging to a new card arrive, creates an unregistered PCard record.  You must register the card before the associated transactions can be processed.

To register a new PCard:

  1. Go to Unregistered PCards Workbench.
  2. Open the unregistered PCard record. Unregistered PCard Workbench

  3. Fill in the details of the card scheme and who is responsible for administering the card (the card holder or central administration).
  4. Fill in details of the card holder.
  5. Click .

NOTE

A PCard belongs to a single administration . The administration determines the initial default that the card holder normally works for. This default can be changed on an individual card. Note that if the PCard is moved to a different administration then the default held against the card will only be changed to the default held on the new administration if the previous default on the card was the same as the held on the previous administration .

You can use PCard Maintenance to amend details of the PCard in future, or to add cards manually.
