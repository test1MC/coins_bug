Conditional Exchange

If there are conditions to the exchange of contracts, you can record these as a conditional exchange event in the Sales Workbench.  When a condition has been met, you record the date in the sales history; when all conditions have been met, automatically records an unconditional exchange.

The plot must be reserved before you can record exchange of contracts.

To record a conditional exchange:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "conditional exchange of contracts".
  3. Enter the details of the exchange.
  4. Click .
  5. On the Condition tab, fill in the details of the conditions using the standard line maintenance options.

To record that a condition has been discharged:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. Click the history tab.
  3. Click the link in the Date column of the Conditional Exchange event.
  4. Click the Condition tab.
  5. Click next to the condition that has been met.
  6. Enter the date in the Discharged column.
  7. Click .
  8. Click .

    When you have entered Discharged dates for all conditions; flags the plot as "exchanged".

Conditional exchange - configuration

You need to set up an EXCHANGE event, set the Conditional flag to Y, and enter the corresponding unconditional exchange event (which runs automatically when the exchange conditions are discharged); see Sales Events.
