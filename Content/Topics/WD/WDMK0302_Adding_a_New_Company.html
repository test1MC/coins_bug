Adding a New Company

Whenever you add a new company, checks the database for existing companies that might be the same as the one you want to add. This avoids duplication and helps to ensure your database is consistent.

When you add a new company, automatically creates a head office record and a postal address for the company.

To add a new company:

  1. Go to Companies.
  2. Click the New Company tab.
  3. Enter the basic details of the new company and click .

    If there are companies that are similar to the one you want to enter, displays a list.

  4. Either:
    • If one of the companies on the list is the same as the one you want to enter, click the link.

      opens the company details which you can then update.

      If the company you have opened is not the one you want, click the browser’s Back button to return to the list.

    • If none of the companies are the same as the one you want to enter, click .

      opens a new record with the details you entered already filled in. You can enter details of the company and a single office here, and add further offices after you save the company record.

  5. Fill in any additional details and click .
