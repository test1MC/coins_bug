Comments on Wiki Topics

You can add comments to a Wiki topic.  For example, you may want to take part in an on-line discussion about the content of a topic, or add notes that do not merit creating a whole new topic.

A list of any comments on a topic appears at the bottom of the topic. 

To add comments to a topic:

  1. Click at the bottom of the topic you want to add the comment to.
  2. Enter a title for the comment.
  3. Enter the comment text.
  4. Click .
