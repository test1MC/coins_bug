Format H (Bank of Ireland - Format 2)

Fields in Bank of Ireland (format 2) statement files are standard CSV format (comma-separated, quote delimited).

  • 1st field -
  • 2nd field - Account number
  • 4th field - Currency
  • 5th field - Transaction date
  • 7th field - Description
  • 8th field - Dr amount
  • 9th field - Cr amount
