SOR items

RO_swj_wipvalue This figure gives a value for the amount of work in Returns the total percent complete of all sor items not yet marked as Ready for Invoicing (value * % complete).

RO_swj_unbillablecost The unbillable cost total is the value of all the cost transactions that have been marked as unbillable. The 'Billable' flag can be set/unset on the 'Costs' tab.
