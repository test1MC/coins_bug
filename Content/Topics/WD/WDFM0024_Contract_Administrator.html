Administrator

The Administrator will create and maintain:

  • Direct and subcontract ' details, including:
    1. Trades.
    2. Geographical areas of work and where they are based.
    3. Availability ( and absences).
  • types, including:
    1. Standard tasks, trades and estimated durations.
    2. Standard schedule items.
  • areas and travel time.
  • categories.
  • Response codes and expected times.
  • Locations, including:
    1. Asset register.
    2. Location hazards.
    3. Access details.
