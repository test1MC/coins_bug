Type Enter the certificate type:

R - Registration Card or Temporary Registration Card (CIS4)

C - Construction Gross Payment Certificate (CIS5)

S - Subcontractors Tax Certificate (CIS6)

Certificate   Enter the number on the subcontractor's certificate.
NI/ Enter the subcontractor's or , as appropriate. For a partnership, leave this blank - will prompt you to confirm that the subcontractor is a CIS(5) partner.
From, To If appropriate, enter the validity of the certificate. You cannot enter a CIS certificate that starts before the CIS start date.
Name Enter the "Authored User" or "Issued To" name as it appears on the certificate.
