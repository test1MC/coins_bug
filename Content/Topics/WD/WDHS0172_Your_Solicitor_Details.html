Your Solicitor Details

The Sales tab on the Sales Workbench and the Part Exchange Workbench shows the details of the solicitor who is acting for you on the sale of a plot or part-exchange property.

To enter or update your solicitor details:

  1. Open the plot summary.
  2. On the Sales tab, click .

  3. In the Solicitor field, type the first few letters of the solicitor's company name; displays an inline lookup of matching companies.  Select the solicitor. 

    Alternatively, click next to the Solicitor field to use the lookup browse.

  4. If the company has more than one office, or to select the contact at the solicitor's company, repeat the above step for the Office and Contact fields.
  5. Click .
