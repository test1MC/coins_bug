Mail Shots

Mail shots allow you to send letters, emails or SMS text messages to House Sales contacts.  You can choose who to send the mail shot to based on a wide range of selection criteria.  Mail shots are available for:

  • Purchasers during the sales process
  • Purchasers you are billing for ground rent, service or other charges

Each mail shot is based on a template which you can edit before you send, and can include a variety of relevant information.

To produce a mail shot:

  1. Go to New Mail Shot, New Sales Mail Shot or New Charge Mail Shot.
  2. Select the Destination Media (whether to produce the mail shot as a Word document, email or SMS text message), and choose the template to use for the message.
