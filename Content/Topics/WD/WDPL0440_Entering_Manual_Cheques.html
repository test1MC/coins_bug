Manual Payments

On occasion, it may be necessary for (that cannot wait to be included in the next run) to be written out manually. For example, a rush payment for a in order to clear outstanding items. This procedure enables you to record the details of manually written out and allocate the amounts to outstanding invoices.

To enter a manual :

  1. Go to Input -Manual Payments.

  2. From the side-menu, select > Payments > Manual Payments

  3. Click

    Field

    Details

    Batch and Reference:

    This will Auto Populate once the batch has been created
    :

    This will default to the current financial period, but may be changed to any currently open period if required.

    Transaction Date:

    The date for transactions in the batch

    Description:

    Free format text, used to help identify the batch.

  4. When you have entered the details above, click to move onto Payment Entry

  5. Click

    Field

    Details

    Supplier Account

    The supplier account to which this payment is being made. Lookup is available.
    Pay Reference A generated unique internal reference number for this Payment

    Cheque Number

    If this payment refers to a manual cheque payment, enter the cheque number here. This may be left blank.

    Bank

    Specify the bank account from which this payment will be made

    Pay Date

    Specify the payment date of this transaction

    Amount

    Specify the amount of the payment

    Currency

    For multi-currency systems, specify the currency this payment is being made in

    To prevent "over payment" to a , displays a warning message if the payment will result in a debit balance on the account.

  6. Either:
    • Allocate the cash to invoices
    • Leave the cash unallocated
  7. Click to return to the list of batches, and post the batch.
