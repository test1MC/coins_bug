– Your Basket

Your basket shows the products you are preparing to request.  Each user only has one basket at a time.  If you log out of , the products remain in your basket.

Whenever you add an item to your basket, shows the current contents of the basket.  You can also display this by clicking .  The number in square brackets on the View Basket tab is the number of items in the basket.

NOTE

If you have requested associated items they are listed immediately below the product they are associated with.  Their descriptions begin with ...

You can add items to the basket, change existing items, or remove items from the basket. 

To add another product:

  • Search for the product you want to add: in the Search field, enter the words to search for.  This takes you to the Search screen (see Search).

 

To remove items from the basket:

  • Click next to the item you want to delete.  If you delete a product that has associated items, removes the associated items too.
  • To remove all items from the basket, click Empty Basket.

 

To change an item (for example, to change the quantity or the expected date):

  • Click the Code or the image.  opens the Update Basket screen (this is similar to the Add to Basket screen; see Adding Items to the Basket).  Update the details and click OK.

 

To submit your request:

  • Click Checkout.
