Matched Subcontractor's Tax Treatment Changes

If the tax treatment of a matched subcontractor changes, you will receive from HRMC a CIS 316.  In this case, create a new verification record for the subcontractor, and enter the Verification Status as "Matched", and the tax treatment, and verification number as indicated on the CIS 316.  the HMRC Notified field.  You do not need to re-verify the subcontractor.
