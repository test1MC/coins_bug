Changing the Subcontractor Record -Trade Codes

If the subcontractor works in more than one trade, use Subcontractor Trades to enter the codes for the trades they work in.

To enter subcontractor trade codes

  1. Go to Subcontractor Maintenance.
  2. Open the subcontractor record.
  3. Select the Trades tab.
  4. Add a record for each trade the subcontractor is engaged in.
