Authoring Sales Events

Sales events can be set up so that one person runs the event but someone else has to authore the event before it takes effect.   

To authore a sales event:

  1. Go to Authore Sales Event.

    2. displays a list of events that are waiting for you to authore them.

  2. Either:
    1. Click .
    2. the Auth? box.
    3. Click .

    or:

    1. the events you want to authore.
    2. Click .
    3. the Authored? box.
    4. Click .

You can also use Sales Events to Authore to print a list of events that need to be authored, then get a manager to sign them off on the report, so you know which events can be authored.
