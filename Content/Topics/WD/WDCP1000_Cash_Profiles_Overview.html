Cash Profiles Overview

The Cash Profiles module lets you re information about cash movements held in Status, so that instead of being shown in conventional accrual accounting terms, it is presented and dated when the actual physical cash associated with the transaction moves in or out. In this way, you can for each , by period, whether it is cash positive or cash negative. This module is not a cash forecasting system.

When you turn Cash Profiles on (see CP Configuration), starts to generate cash profile information automatically. Once Cash Profiles is running, takes into account the following transactions when creating a cash profile:

  • Tracking of all cash movements for both revenues and costs
  • Current cash position for each
  • Actual cash flow and reporting by
  • Optional notional interest calculation
  • Fully integrated with all other modules
