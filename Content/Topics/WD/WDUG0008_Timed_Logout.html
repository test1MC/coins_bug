Timed Logout

 

is set up to log you out automatically after a period of inactivity (the default is sixty minutes). All you need to do is log in again as normal. If the timeout period has passed, still displays the screen that you were last using, but the next time you try to do anything (for example, select a new menu item or open a record), you must log in, and will take you back to the last screen you were at.
