Permissions

You may find that other people using have menus that are different from yours. You may also find that procedures or functions described in the manuals are not available to you.

This is because uses menus to control which procedures you are allowed to use. If an option does not appear on your menu, you do not have the permissions to use it.

For example, in General Ledger, the System Administrator may be the only user allowed to use the Administration and Setup functions, so these options would not appear on other users' menus.

Your System Administrator can change the options that are available to you, and can set up new menus to suit your work needs.
