Reviewing PCard Transactions (Card Holder)

PCards Workbench - Card Holder Mode allows you to review your PCard purchase transactions and complete any missing information.  Marking a transaction as reviewed allows the card administrator to include the transaction in a batch.  The browse shows transactions for any PCards for which you are registered in as the user.

To review PCard transactions:

  1. Go to PCards Workbench - Card Holder Mode.
    • If a transaction contains errors, the Status column shows Error. Errors might occur because of incomplete or mis-typed information coming from the merchant. You must correct any errors before you can mark the transaction as "Reviewed". You can see a detailed explanation of the error or errors by pointing to the error indicator.
    • If there are warnings that relate to a transaction, the column to the right of the Status column shows a red exclamation mark. You can see the warning text by pointing to this warning indicator. Warnings are for your information; the administrator may need to deal with them.
    • You can see an image of the transaction receipt by clicking the button in the View Receipt column (or by clicking the card network logo from the transaction detail screen). Transaction Receipt Image
  2. To fill in any missing transaction details or correct errors, click .
  3. You may need to do the following (depending on your access rights):
    • Select or confirm the for which the purchase was made.
    • Confirm the analysis or enter the correct , overhead department, asset or workshop code.
    • If the uses WBS codes, confirm or enter a valid code. This code will be defaulted into the WBS column against any line item detail line where no WBS code is already present.
    • Enter the amount (Level 1 only).
    • Confirm that you have a receipt for the transaction. (Level 1 only or if the Gross Amount of the transactions exceeds a specified value. The specified value defaults to 5000 but is determined by a parameter.)
  4. Fill in the detail lines:
    • Ensure that the total of the lines is equal to the Net Amount.
    • Transactions from level 1 and level 2 merchants will not show the details of what was purchased. In this case (depending on your company policy) enter lines that include at least a description of the items and the prices.
  5. You can also enter a note for the administrator (for example, to explain the purchase).
  6. Click to save your changes.

    When you have corrected any errors, you can mark the transactions as "reviewed".

    NOTE

    If you are unable to correct errors on a transaction, the administrator will be able to see these transactions and correct them. You may need to let the administrator know that there are transactions that need their attention.
