Competitor Analysis

The Competitor Analysis workbench shows you a subset of selected companies from the Companies workbench.  You can add companies that you want to include in the competitor analysis.  Competitor Analysis shows extra details about the companies, that are not visible in the Companies workbench.  This allows you to effectively competitors and understand their strengths and weaknesses.

Reports are available to help you view and competitor information, and a Composite View allows you to print a summary of the information held about an individual competitor.

Competitor analysis - configuration

There are various lists you need to set up before using Competitor Analysis.

  • Competitor Types allows you to maintain a list of codes you can use to categore companies in Competitor Analysis.
  • Competitor Categories allows you to maintain a list of codes you can use to categore competitor companies.
  • Association Types allows you to maintain codes that you can use to identify different types of associated companies on the Associated screen (in Details). 
  • allows you to maintain a list of the areas of business expertise you can record against a company.

    You can see and update the in the Companies workbench as well as the Competitor Analysis workbench.

  • allows you to maintain a list of the potential you can record against a company.

    You can see and update the in the Companies workbench as well as the Competitor Analysis workbench.

  • Asset Types allows you to maintain a list of types of asset you can record against a competitor.  The Asset Sheet Report groups assets by asset type.
  • Asset Models allows you to maintain a list of the asset models you can record against a competitor.
  • Contact Roles allows you to maintain a list of organational roles that you can assign to a competitor contact.  (This is different from the list of roles set up in Job Group.)
  • Types allows you to maintain codes to identify different types of entry in the Competitor Analysis , and whether they appear in the graphical timeline.

  • Other Types allows you to maintain a list of codes you can use to identify different types of on the Other screen in Competitor Analysis.
