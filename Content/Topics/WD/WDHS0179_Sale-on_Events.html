Sale-on Events

There are a number of things that happen during the sale-on process that you may want to record.  You can record the following using events:

  • Release to market of the part-exchange property
  • Change of sale price
  • Change of discounts and incentives
  • Reservation of the property
  • Exchange of contracts
  • Completion of sale
  • Meter readings at sale-on
  • Occupation
  • Derelease of a property

    • NOTE

    These events may have different names on your system.

Sale-on events configuration

Use Part Exchange Workbench Events to configure buy-in events.  Use these system event codes:

  • RELEASE for release to market of the part-exchange property
  • CHGPRICE for change of sale price
  • CHGDISC for change of discounts and incentives
  • RESERVE for reservation of the property
  • EXCHANGE for exchange of contracts
  • COMPLETE for completion of sale
  • METSALE for meter readings at sale-on
  • OCCUPY for occupation
  • DEREL for derelease of a property
