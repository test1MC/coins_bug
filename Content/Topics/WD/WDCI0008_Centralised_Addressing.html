Centraled Addressing

Centraled addressing ensures that address information for ledger accounts is kept in sync across all in . 

For example, any change to the controlled fields on a record in one is replicated up to the CI Office Address record and also to the corresponding record in all the other .  Additionally, any change to the name field is replicated across the records in each though not to the CI Office record.

As long as the ledgers are linked to the Company Information Workbench, this always happens.  The CI parameter CENT-ADD allows you to choose whether the addresses can be updated on the ledger accounts or on the office record.

All other fields on the account record can be maintained separately in each as required.
