Delivery Register

The Delivery Register allows you to record detailed information about deliveries of concrete and reinforcing steel (for example, the site location of the delivery, extra information related to the type of delivery, quality test requirements and the results of those tests). You enter the information as part of the GRN entry, and can update it and report on it later. You can record different types of detail for different commodities.

NOTE

Currently the Delivery Register can only be used for concrete and reinforcing steel, but could be extended for other commodities. Contact for details.

To enter registry information

You update the delivery register as part of GRN entry, either when you first enter the GRN, or afterwards.

  1. In GRN entry, enter and save the GRN line.
  2. Click the Delivery Register link.
  3. On the Location tab, enter one or more lines to record where the delivery was used. The totals of the Quantity fields must not exceed the quantity delivered (as recorded on the GRN line).
  4. The Fields tab shows a list of pre-defined questions about the delivery. Update each line in turn to enter the information requested.
  5. The Test tab shows a list of pre-defined questions about quality tests.Update each line in turn to enter the information requested.
  6. When you have finished, click to go back to the GRN lines screen.

To report on the delivery register

The following reports provide information from the delivery register:

  • Concrete Register Report allows you to report on the location information recorded for deliveries of concrete.
  • Reinforcement Register Report allows you to report on the location information recorded for deliveries of reinforcing steel.
  • Concrete Test Register Report allows you to report on the results of tests on deliveries of concrete.
