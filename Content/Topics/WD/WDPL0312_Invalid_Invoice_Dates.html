An invoice has an invalid date if:

  • The cost effective period is not open for the .

If the date wrong, tries to suggest a date:

  • If there is a current week ending date on the , suggests this as the default cost-effective date.
  • If not, uses the first open cost-effective period date after the invoice date as the default.
  • If is unable to suggest a date, the field is blank.

You must enter a valid date in order to proceed.
