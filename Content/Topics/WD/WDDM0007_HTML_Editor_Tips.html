HTML Editor - Lists

To create indented bullets within a numbered list, like this:

  1. Item one.
  2. Item two.
    • Bullet two a.
    • Bullet two b.
  3. Item three.  

you would enter the whole list as a numbered list:

  1. Item one.
  2. Item two.
  3. Bullet two a. 
  4. Bullet two b.
  5. Item three. 

then highlight the third and fourth items, click , then click .

 

To create a non-numbered (continuation) paragraph within a numbered list or bulleted list, like this:

  1. Item one.
  2. Item two.

    New paragraph.

  3. Item three.  

one way is to type the paragraph as part of the one before it, then highlight the text you want to use as a continuation paragraph:

From the Styles drop down, select Paragraph.  This puts the selected text in a new paragraph (within the list item):
