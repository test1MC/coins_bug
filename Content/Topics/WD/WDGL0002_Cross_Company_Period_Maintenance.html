Each financial ledger has a maintenance function that allows you to open and close (see ).  In addition, Global Periods Maintenance allows you to maintain the periods for all the ledgers.  These functions only affect the ledger periods in the current .

To maintain periods for several :

  1. Go to GL Periods (Cross-).

  3. To open or close in all ledgers, or set them to current, for several :

    1. Click the View tab.
    2. the for which you want to add the new period.
