Sales Chain

You can record details of the sales chain that affects a reserved plot, including details of the estate agents, the buyers, the sellers, and their financial advisors, mortgage lenders and solicitors.

To record sales chain details:

  1. Go to Sales Workbench.
  2. Find the and plot, and open the Plot Details screen.
  3. Click the Reservation> tab.
  4. Click the Chain tab.
  5. To add a chain, click .
  6. Select which link you are entering the information about (for the first link, this will be the plot).

  7. Enter details of the property that forms the next link in the chain.  You can:
    • Copy the address details from the purchaser, or
    • Use the lookup.

  8. Click .

    displays the Link Details.

  9. You can also use the Notes, Buyer Details> and Seller Details> tabs to enter any additional information about the link.
  10. When you have finishd, either:
    • To go back to the Chain tab, click .
    • To add another link, select Add Another Link from the Options menu.
