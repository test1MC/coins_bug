SC Ledger Basics - Referring to a Subcontract

Whenever you are asked to select a subcontract, there are two ways to do so:

  • By order number.
  • By , phase (if used), subcontractor and sequence.

To select a subcontract by order number:

  • In the Order Number field, enter the order number.

To select a subcontract by and subcontractor:

  1. Leave the Order Number field blank.
  2. In the , Phase, Subcontractor and Sequence fields, enter the relevant codes for this subcontract.

    fills in the order number and description for the subcontract.

To select a timecard subcontract by subcontractor :

  1. Leave the Order Number and fields blank.
  2. In the Subcontractor and Sequence fields, enter the relevant codes for the subcontract.
