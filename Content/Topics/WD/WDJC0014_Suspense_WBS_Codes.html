Suspense WBS Codes

WBS codes are used when raising requisitions and orders to indicate where and how the items being ordered will be used.  Sometimes the person who raises the order or the requisition will not know this; in this case they can assign the items to a suspense WBS code, or leave the WBS code blank and will fill in a suspense code.

When entering GRNs, users can reassign the WBS codes, or split the delivery across several WBS codes.  You can configure so that when users enter a GRN, they are forced to reassign any suspense WBS codes that are carried through from the order, and enter valid WBS codes.
