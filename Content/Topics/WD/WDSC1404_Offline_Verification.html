Offline Verification

Under this method, you communicate with HMRC independently (by telephone or Internet) and then record the verification details in retrospectively.  This is the only method available in COINSplus, and is available as an option in COINS OA.

You can verify a subcontractor with HMRC:

  • By telephone (0845 366 7899).  You can verify up to 10 subcontractors per phone call.
  • Over the Internet (www.hmrc.gov.uk/new-cis).  The free software supplied by HMRC is designed for contractors who regularly pay up to 50 subcontractors.

To verify subcontractors offline

  1. Go to Subcontractor Maintenance.
  2. Click the link in the Subcontractor Account column.
  3. Click the Verification tab.
  4. Click to update the verification record.
  5. Fill in or amend the following fields according to the information from HMRC:
    a. Verification Status
    b. Tax Treatment
    c. Verification Date
    d. Verification Number
  6. Click to save the record.
