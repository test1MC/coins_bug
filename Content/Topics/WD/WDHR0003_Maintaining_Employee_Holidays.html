Maintaining Employee

Within Personnel Workbench, Annual Leave allows you to record the employee's annual leave. Annual leave includes any time taken off by the employee where that time is paid for by the employer.

The Annual Leave tab shows the Status of any requests, and you can update the dates on existing . You can update any of the main details, but for a booked you will only be able to view any amendment details.

You can:

  • Approve new requests by filling in the Authored By field.
  • Approve cancellation requests by deleting the .
  • Approve amendment requests by altering the main record to match the request.

All of these will cause to send out notification emails. If you click the record will always be deleted (that is, this will not simply cancel a cancellation or amendment request).
