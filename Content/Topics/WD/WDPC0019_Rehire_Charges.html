Each depot must have its own overhead , which will normally be configured to accrue based on the due date of the committed order (The Accrual Method field in Details will be set to Order) so there is no need to enter GRNs or do full weekly allocation.

However, for hourly-rated (which is typically operated ), you can configure to expect a GRN and full allocation.  The Timesheets tab shows hourly-rated items and allows timesheets to be entered against them. 

For an hourly-rated order, you need to "visit" the screen and select a charge calendar before you create the dispatch.

The charging run will charge the site, and when posted, the Procurement allocation sheets are updated, keeping the order accrual mechanism in line.
