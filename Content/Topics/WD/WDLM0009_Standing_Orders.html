Standing Orders

The standing order functionality allows you to set up details of the standing orders for a tenancy.  Auto Bank Reconciliation can then match these against your bank statement items. If a match is found, a lettings cash transaction will then be created.

In addition, if this cash transaction can then be matched to an outstanding lettings? charge/adjustment, will then auto allocate the cash to the charge.
