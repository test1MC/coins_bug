Searching

There are various ways to find information in :

  • Search is a quick way to search for companies, or contacts, by entering all or part of their name.  displays any records whose name contains the word you enter.
  • My shows you lists of the companies, , contacts or events you are interested in; you can filter the lists.
  • Each of the workbenches has a main tab that is sorted alphabetically by name, plus other tabs that are sorted according to different fields. You can filter the lists according to the and the records belong to, and various other fields.

    NOTE

    The simple filter on the workbenches looks for names or descriptions that begin with the text you enter, whereas Search looks for names that contain the text you enter.
  • When you are looking at the details of a record, you can follow links to other related records. For example, when you are looking at a contact record, you can see the that contact has worked on, and click a link to open the details. From the details you can see the companies that were associated with the , and click a link to open the company details.
