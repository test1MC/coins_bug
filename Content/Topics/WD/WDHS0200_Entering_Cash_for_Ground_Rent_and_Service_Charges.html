Entering Cash for Ground Rent and Service Charges

To enter cash:

Input the Cash either through the (using HS/ to code to House Sales) or through House SalesCash Entry. When using a Rent or Service Charge Transaction Category, the system will give the option to allocate the Cash to Charge Transactions or to leave it unallocated.

If the Cash is left unallocated on a Rent or Service Charge Transaction Category within a plot, it can be allocated later using Allocate Cash to Charges.


To on ground rent/service charges:

  • To on the transaction for a particular plot, use the Financial Details within the Sales Workbench.
  • Batch Status are available as in other ledgers.

Four reporting options are available:

  • Charge Report.  This will list charges generated in detail.
  • Plot Balances Report.  As for all House Sales Financials Transaction Categories, this will list the balances by and Plot.  The report can be run for one or more Transaction Categories.
  • Category Reconciliation Report.  As for all House Sales Financials Transaction Categories, this will reconcile the detailed balances to the related General Ledger Control Accounts.
  • Sales Configurable Report.  Reports can be configured to show movements and balances using the CASH and JOURNAL fields.  The annual   Rent and Service Charge figures are available in fields: vwb_ rent and vwb_maintchg.  Individual transaction amounts are not available.
