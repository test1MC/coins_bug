Personnel

allows you to record and maintain personnel information about your employees, such as the members of your department; for example:

  • Skills, qualifications and experiences.    Use the Skills/Quals tab and the Experience tab to record details of skills, qualifications and experiences.
  • they have been involved in.    The tab shows details of the employee has been involved in.
  • Contacts they know.    Use the Contacts tab to record contacts the employee knows.

personnel records can either be linked to Human Resources records (in which case when you update details in , they are also updated in Human Resources), or kept separate.
