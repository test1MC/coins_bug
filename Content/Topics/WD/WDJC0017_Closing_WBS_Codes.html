Closing WBS Codes

Open/Close WBS Codes allows you close WBS codes so as to prevent the coding of costs to codes that should no longer be available for costing since the work has been completed.

WBS codes can be closed for a , or within a for specific application areas. The application areas are:

  • RQ - Requisition entry
  • PO - Purchase entry
  • VO - entry - addendum lines
  • GRN - GRN entry and maintenance (Change GRN)
  • PLT - allocation
  • PL - Manual costing of invoices (excluding Invoice Matching)
  • SC - Costing distribution of certificates
  • ST - issues
  • PR - Payroll
  • JNL - GL journals, costing journals and
  • EX - Expenses costing distribution
  • TS - timesheets

NOTE

The extra validation will not be implemented on the "" side.  All of the application areas addressed deal with "cost" transactions.
