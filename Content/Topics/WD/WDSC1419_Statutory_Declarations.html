Statutory Declarations

The person who submits the monthly return (whether by post or electronically) must make the following declarations:

  • That “the information [they] have given on this return is correct and complete to the best of [their] knowledge and belief.”
  • That “the employment status of each individual included on this return, and any continuation sheets, has been considered and payments have not been made under contracts of employment.”
  • That “every subcontractor included on this return, and any continuation sheets, has either been verified with HM Revenue & Customs, or has been included in previous CIS returns in this, or the previous two tax years.”
