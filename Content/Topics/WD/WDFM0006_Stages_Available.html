Stages Available

The following different types of stages are available:

    Stage 

    Button (if used) 

    Description 
    Add Auto-created stage.
    Add Notes Lets you add notes to a . The notes are then visible in the history and on the History report.
    Add/Amend SOR Items Lets you add schedule items to a . If you are using Simple SOR invoicing, you must assign items using Allocate Task and Update Task Progress.
    Allocate Tasks Lets you add tasks to a . Use Update Task when the task is complete.
    Approve/Reject Variation Stage linked to Raise Variation (if not auto-approve).
    Close Lets you indicate that the is closed. This closes the WBS and prevents any more costs/revenues being posted.
    Completion Certificate Received Lets you record that you have received the completion certificate from the client.
    Confirm All Tasks Completed Lets you record that all tasks on the have been completed. This does not auto-complete open tasks.
    Confirm All Tasks Planned Lets you record that all tasks you intend to add to the have been added.
    Confirm Non-Chargeable Lets you record that you will not be producing an invoice for the . (For complex SOR and transaction based invoice types, the stage logic needs to be set up to disallow the Ready for Invoicing and Percentage Complete stages; for other invoicing types, it needs to disallow the Complete and/or the Operationally Complete and Pricing Compete stages. Alternatively, it could be set up to Confirm All Tasks Completed, Stop , or Close instead.)
    Confirm Value to be Invoiced Used with Simple Measured invoicing to confirm, cancel or modify the invoice amount - must use stage after Generate pro forma and before Generate invoice.
    Contact Client Lets you record contact that you have initiated with a client. You can record the type of contact and the outcome, and additional notes. Compare "Record Contact from Client".
    Create Follow on Lets you create a follow-on . Use this if the worker of the identifies additional work that needs to be done. This carries forward details from the original .
    Edit Lets you change some details of the .
    Edit (supervisor) Lets you change most details of the .
    Enter Application Value Used with Simple Measured Invoicing only to enter the value for the pro-forma invoice - before generating the pro-forma.
    Escalate Lets you mark the as one that needs further attention by a supervisor.
    Form 10 Issued Lets you record (date and memo text) that paperwork has been completed. 
    Health and Safety File Lets you record (date and memo text) that paperwork has been completed.
    Health and Safety Plan Lets you record (date and memo text) that paperwork has been completed.
    Hold Lets you put the on hold.
    [Memo unless to setup logic using other stages]
    Complete Lets you mark the as complete; use instead of Operationally Complete and Pricing Complete.
    Log No Access Attempts Lets you record that the operator has tried and been unable to access the property, and whether they left a card.
    Method Statement Lets you record that a method statement has been filled in.
    Open Lets you reopen a closed . This reverses the Close stage and reopens the WBS.
    Operationally Complete Lets you indicate that a is complete. All invoicing types apart from Complex measured and Complex SOR invoicing require this stage, unless you are using Complete stage instead. See also Pricing Complete.
    Partial Repudiation Lets you partially repudiate the (similar to Hold ).
    Percent complete Lets you enter the percentage complete for scheduled items or the whole . Used for reporting achievement (% * SOR value) in JC horizontal reports.
    Pricing Complete The stage before Operationally Complete; this lets you indicate that the financial aspects of the are complete. You can view costs and values. All invoicing apart from Complex measured and Complex SOR requires this stage, unless you are using Complete instead. See also Operationally Complete.
    Print Letter Lets you print a letter. This uses a configurable document.
    Raise Lets you raise a (for example, to change the price, change dates, or add SOR items). [This is linked to the types of Maintenance setup and can use the auto approve. Or use the approve/reject stage. Can also add SOR items; must approve for them to appear on tasks.]
    Reactivate If Stop stage, use this to reactivate, and RESTORE to go back two stages.
    Ready for Invoicing Only used for Complex SOR invoicing.
    Record Contact from Client Memo
    Repudiate Memo Insurance Term - similar to Stop .
    Risk Assessment Memo
    Stop Used to cancel .
    Update Task Progress Confirm task complete start and complete date and time or the task is cancelled.
    Validate Memo
    Works Survey Questionnaire defined in maintenance(2).

    NOTE

    If Restore is used after Stop , it will go back two stages:
    1. The Stop stage.
    2. The stage before Stop .
