Documents Tab

You can add documents (that is, files of any format, including pictures or spreadsheets, as well as word-processing documents) to any item using the  button; any documents that have been added to an item are shown on that item's Documents tab. 

You can also add documents that relate to the whole project. 

Each item's Documents tab shows documents that are associated with that item.

The Files > Documents tab shows documents that are associated with the project, and with any of the project items.

Note that drawings are treated separately (see Drawings and Drawing Sets).

Letters generated from within Project Management are also shown separately (see Options and Actions).
