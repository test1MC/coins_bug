Example of a Certificate Definition - Income Tax Line

Income tax is calculated from the Gross, less any Retention, Discount or Materials costs.

The income tax line looks like this:

Note that:

  • The Rate field on a tax line is ignored; set it to 0. The Income Tax percentage is taken from the parameter PAYE%.

  • The Calc Cumulativefield is uned, since Income Tax is calculated on this payment.

  • The user cannot override the tax paid, so the Update field is uned. This is because recommends calculating the amount of tax again at point of payment so any changes made here would be lost.

This will calculate the amount of income tax to be deducted. You have to subtract this in the Subtotalsfield of the payment line.

What if the Income Tax rate changes?

If the Income Tax rate changes, on the day the rate changes you must change the value on the PAYE%parameter.
