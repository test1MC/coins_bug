Service Fees

Service fees are automatically applied when creating a if they are set up to apply under mark-up configuration (see Mark-Ups).

A SOR item will be created from the service fee details held on the relevant mark-up record.

Service fees - configuration

Service fees are set up in Markup Rates which can be found under FM Setup - 'Invoicing Setup' tab group.

The SOR item will be created on the under the 'Current' SOR type if it exists as a standard item. If not, a 'custom' SOR item will be created. The service fee will be re-evaluated if there are major category, minor category, or customer changes on the once created.
