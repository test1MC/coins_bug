SC Records - Subcontractor Papers

Within Subcontractor Maintenance you can enter details of the subcontractor's and income tax status.

You can change these details later by selecting Papers from the pop up menu.

  1. If you enter V for Valid the system then allows you to enter CIS Tax Exemption Certificates.

Subcontractor papers configuration

Do not set up a subcontractor as exempt from the scheme unless you are certain they are, otherwise the Inland Revenue may require you to pay tax for them.
