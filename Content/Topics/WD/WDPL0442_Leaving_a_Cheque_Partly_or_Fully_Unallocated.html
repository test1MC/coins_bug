Leaving a Partly or Fully Unallocated

When entering manual , you may not want to allocate the full amount to an invoice straight away. If you do decide to leave a amount unallocated, it will need to be allocated at a later time (see Allocating After They Have Been Entered).

If you leave cash unallocated and your system is set to run sector processing, you must assign the unallocated cash to either a or an overhead department.

If you are not running sector processing, you can leave cash unallocated by simply clicking .  

To leave a partly or fully unallocated:

  1. When you have entered the manual , select the tab.  

  2. Click to open the header.
  3. Enter either a contract or an overhead department to assign the cash to.
  4. Click to save the and click to go to the list.
