Printing the Amount in Words

You can either print the words for each digit in the amount, or print the amount in "full text".

  • To print a line with the following format:

    ONE ZERO NINE SEVEN FIVE THREE 64

    add a line containing the field RO_acs_digitword__nwhere n is the position (1 = units, 2 = tens, 3 = hundreds, and so on). Repeat this field for each position. Use RO_acs_pence for the pence/cents amount:

  • To print the payee and the amount in "full text"; for example:

    Anderson & Little ******************
    ONE HUNDRED AND TWENTY THREE POUNDS 99p ONLY ****************
    *************************************************************  

    add consecutive lines containing the fields RO_avm_payeen*, RO_acs_famountWords__1 and RO_acs_famountWords__2.

  • To print the "Pay text" (the payee name and the amount in figures, with no spaces), for example:

    ANDERSON&LITTLE123.99ONLY******************

    use the field RO_acs_payText.

  • To print the amount in words on one line, using the USA format, for example:

    ONE HUNDRED AND TWENTY THREE DOLLARS 99CENTS

    use the field RO_SpelledAmount.
