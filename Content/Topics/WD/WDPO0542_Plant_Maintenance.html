Maintenance

Maintenance (No Allocation) allows you to or transfer for where detailed allocation is not being used.The accrual method on the determines whether allocation is being used.

For where detailed allocation is not being used, calculates the accrual based on the time between the date and either the date or today's date. If you are using GRNs, takes the date from the GRN; if you are not using GRNs, takes the date from the due date of the order.
