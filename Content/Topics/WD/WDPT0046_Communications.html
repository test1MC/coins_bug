Communications

The Communications tab has two different types of communication items: journals and letters.

Journals

Journals are a record of project communications.  They include:

  • Any emails sent from Project Management.
  • Any emails sent to the project email address (if Exchange Integration is enabled).

You can also add journals manually to record other communications such as phone calls and personal conversations.

Letters

Whenever you generate a letter that relates to any of the Project Management items (see Options and Actions), it is shown in this list.
