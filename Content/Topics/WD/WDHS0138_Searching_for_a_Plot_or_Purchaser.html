Searching for a Plot or Purchaser

To find a plot or purchaser in the Sales Workbench, you can:

  • Use the tab to find the , then click one of the links to show the list of plots.
  • Use the By Purchaser tab to find the purchaser, then click the link to show the plot (or plots) they are purchasing.
  • Use the By Prerelease tab to find the prerelease reservation, then click the link to show details of the reservation.
  • Use the Advanced Search tab.

To use the Advanced Search:

  1. In the Sales Workbench, click the Advanced Search tab.
  2. Enter the details you know about the or the purchaser.
  3. Click the button.

Examples:

If you know: Do this:
The purchaser's phone number

On the Advanced Search tab, enter the phone number in the Telephone field. 

You can enter the purchaser's home, work or mobile number, and will find any purchase records that involve that purchaser (provided the telephone number is on the purchaser's personal details).
Part of the name

Either:

  • On the Advanced Search tab, use wildcards and enter the part of the name you know in the Name field (for example: *lake*) OR
  • On the By tab, set the Search selector to Name and enter wildcards and the part of the name you know.
