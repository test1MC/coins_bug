Sending a Letter

Throughout House Sales you can generate standard letters using Word.  Your system administrator can configure the letters, and they are addressed to the appropriate person or company.  The letters can include information from the database such as significant dates or prices.

To send a letter:

  1. From the relevant Summary page, click Options and select the letter from the drop-down menu.

    2. generates the letter as a Word document and displays it in a new window. You can then save, edit or print the document.
