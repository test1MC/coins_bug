eRCT - Certificate Entry

To minime issues with Payment Notifications and RCT Returns, the Payment Notification should be done as close as possible to the point of payment, but legally must be before payment (unless prevented from doing so by failure of Revenue systems or other cause outside of the Principal's control).  Since Payment Certificates may remain unpaid on for some time, these will be input and posted before Payment Notification which will be processed following selection for payment.

There is no significant change to the process of entering subcontract certificates, but there are various new warning/error messages:

  • Depending on the setting of the SCIRE/STOPUNRG parameter, will warn or prevent you entering a certificate for a subcontract that has not been notified.
  • Depending on the setting of the SCIRE/ STOPEXP parameter, if the contract end date (the Finish Date of the subcontract) is more than six months ago, will warn or prevent you entering a certificate.
  • If the contract end date is in the past but is within the last six months, will warn you that the payment needs to be made within six months of the contract end date.
