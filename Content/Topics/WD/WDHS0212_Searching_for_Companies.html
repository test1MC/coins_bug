Searching for and Adding Companies, Offices or Contacts

There are many places in House Sales and where you need to record the details of a company; for example:

  • When adding a solicitor or management company for a .
  • When adding a solicitor, mortgage lender or financial advisor to a purchaser record.
  • When adding an estate agent on a chain link record.

A Search feature allows you to search the Company Information Workbench for companies or people, and to create new records if they don't already exist.  This feature is launched using the Search button next to the Company fields, and is explained below in more detail.

To search for a company:

  1. On the screen where you add or update the company, click the Search button.  (You do not need to type anything in the Company, Office or Contact fields.) 

    displays the Company Information Workbench Search and Add screen.
  2. Type in the details you know for the company or person.

    As you type, searches the database for potential matches, and displays them below the search screen.  

    The list of possible matches is sorted by their score; this is based on how many of the fields you entered match the information on the Company Information Workbench records.

    By default, the Match? field is set to Not a Match. This indicates that the company is not the one you were searching for.  If none of the potential matches have an action selected, then, when you click the Process button at the bottom of the screen (step 4), will create a new company record (and, depending on the details you entered, will also create new office, address and contact records).

  3. If one of the lines in the Potential Duplicates list shows the information you are searching for, update the Match? field as follows:

    • Company Only - only the company is correct.

      When you click the Process button (step 4) the following will take place:
      • If you entered the first line of the address and a then a new office and address record will be created for the selected company.
      • If you entered a then a new contact record will also be created against the new office record.

    • Company and Office - the entry is for the correct company and office, but not the correct contact person.

      When you click the Process button (step 4) the following will take place:
      • If you entered a then a new contact record will be created against the office record.

    • Company, Office and Contact - the entry is for the correct company, office and contact.

    NOTE

    Do not change the Match? field on more than one line.
  4. Click the button.

  5. may warn you that you are about to create a new company.  If so, click OK.  

    populates the Company, Office and Contact fields on the original screen with the appropriate information, and makes any necessary changes to the Company Information Workbench.

NOTES

If creates a new company record, the country is set to the value of the CI parameter DEFCNTRY.

If creates an office for a new company, the office type is set to the value of the CI parameter HOTYPE.  If the office is for an existing company, the office type is set to the value of the HS parameter REGOTYPE.
