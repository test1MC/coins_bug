Confirming Returns

The first part of the process is to create unconfirmed returns.  When the items have been returned, you can confirm the returns.  (For , you may want to confirm the returns immediately, if you are not expecting to be notified that the items have been collected.)

Unconfirmed Returns shows a list of items on returns that have not yet been confirmed, and allows you to confirm them. You can also update the collection dates and print returns.

NOTE

In , the date is the date after which does not accrue charges.  The date only applies when you have confirmed the return.

The collection date applies to all items on a return, but you can amend the date for individual items on a return.

If some items on a return were not returned, you could "Confirm " for the items that were returned, then amend the collection date for the remaining items.  (Note, though, that the Return Note will still show all the items, including those that have been returned.)
