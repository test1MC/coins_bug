Inspection Reporting

You can use the following reports to report on inspection information:

  • The Monthly Status Report shows the plots forecast for inspection, the number of inspections carried out, and the date if the plot has passed the inspection.
  • The Forecast Top Sheet gives a summary of your position now compared with what you thought you were going to do when you bought the land.
  • The Forecast Back Sheet gives details of the current site forecast as shown on the Forecast Top Sheet.
  • Inspections Booked Report allows you to produce a report that shows the inspections (of a selected type) that are booked for each .  You can report on a range of dates.
  • Results Today Report allows you to produce a report that shows the results of inspections of a particular type.  You can report on a range of inspection dates, though the default range is today's date only.
  • Details Report shows details of the inspections (of a selected inspection type) that have taken place over the last seven days.  The details include the plot number, the forecast date, the inspection date and the inspection status.
