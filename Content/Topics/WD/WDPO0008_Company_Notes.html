Company Notes

Notes allow you to add memorandum information to a company record. You can then search the notes according to the index words you enter with the note.  You can mark notes as "red flag notes" to indicate that they are important, for example, to highlight a health and safety issue. 

You can also prevent users entering purchase orders for companies that have notes, or red flag notes, against them.

To add a note to a company:

  1. Go to Companies (or SRM Workbench).
  2. Click the link in the Name column for the company you want to add the note to.

    displays the Company Summary.

  3. Select the Other tab on the top layer of tabs, and then the Notes tab on the 2nd layer.

  4. Click to add a new note.

    automatically assigns a new page number, and defaults in today's date.

  5. You can select an existing Index Word using the lookup, or can enter a new one.

  6. To make the note a "red flag" note, the Red Flag box.

To read the notes against a company:

Either:

  • Open the Company Summary and display the Notes tab.
  • From the Company browse, click the flag image on the company line.
