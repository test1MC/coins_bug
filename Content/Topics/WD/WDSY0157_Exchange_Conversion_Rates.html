Exchange Rates and Conversion Rates

  • Currency Exchange Rates are used for processing foreign currency transactions, unrealed and realed variances.
  • Currency Conversion Rates are used for alternative base currency reporting in the General Ledger.

When you have set up the currencies you are using, enter the exchange rates between your base currency and the other currencies.  You need to update these on a regular basis.  

Determine what your procedures will be for setting your exchange rates. These can be varied at intervals ranging from every day, to annual. Different periods can be used for different rates.

Determine what your source will be for the rate. Typical sources are:

  • As advised by head office.
  • Taken from the daily newspaper.
  • Taken from a website such as www.oanda.com.
