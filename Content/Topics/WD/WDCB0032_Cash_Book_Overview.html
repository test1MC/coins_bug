Overview

is an independent module which records in detail all cash movements on each bank account, either as direct manual entries or automatically from the other modules of , and allows reconciliation of the transactions recorded with the bank statement, either by manual entry or by computer link with the bank.

Features:

  • Automatically updated with full details of payment runs and other cashbook transactions from other modules.
  • Input of requisitions and other direct cashbook transactions.
  • Printing of and remittance advices designed to your requirements.
  • Bank reconciliation using data downloaded from the on-line banking systems of the major clearing banks.
  • Flexible facility allowing transaction tracing by payee name, value, number, etc.
  • Fully integrated with all other modules.
