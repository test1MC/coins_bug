Subcontractor Certificates - Putting a Certificate on Hold

When you have finished entering the certificate (and the costing distribution) there is a further tab to allow you to enter Hold details.

Putting a certificate on hold prevents the certificate from being automatically selected for payment. You can put a certificate on hold for either commercial or financial reasons. 

In addition the Hold code you can also input reasons for the hold.

To release a held certificate for payment, use one of the menu options as appropriate.
