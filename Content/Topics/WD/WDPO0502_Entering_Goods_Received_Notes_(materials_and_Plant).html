Entering Goods Received Notes (Materials and )

The procedure for entering Goods Received (material orders) and Advice ( orders) is similar, except that if the order is order, you also fill in the on Site frame.

To enter GRNs:

  1. Go to Procurement > Material GRNs > Material GRNs or Procurement > GRNs.

    You cannot enter goods against an uncommitted order.

  2. Enter the ticket number (the supplier's delivery note reference).
  3. Enter the number of the order for which you are entering the goods.

If the order is a Order, prompts for the Details for each line on the order.

To record that the full quantity of goods was delivered
  1. Select all the lines on which the full remaining quantity of goods was delivered.
  2. Choose Accept Whole Quantity from the Choose Action list.
  3. fills in the quantities delivered based on the quantities expected, and marks the lines as closed.
 To record that not all expected goods were delivered
  1. Open the line (click ).
  2. Enter the quantity of goods delivered.
  3. Save the line (click ).
 To record the delivery of goods not ordered
  1. Add a new line (click ).
  2. Enter the details of the unordered goods.
  3. Save the line (click ).

You can only add unordered goods if you have permission to do so. There may be a limit on the value of unordered goods you can enter.

 To reject delivered goods
  1. Enter the full quantity of goods delivered.
  2. Enter the quantity of goods that were rejected.
  3. Select an exception code to show why the goods were rejected.
  4. If necessary, enter a note to explain further.
  5. If the line refers to a plant item, displays the Details frame.
 To reassign the costing
  1. To assign the goods to a different WBS or cost code, open the line and enter the new code.
  2. To assign goods to more than one WBS or , open the line, click Multi Cost Head or Multi WBS, then enter the quantities to be costed to each code. The quantities on the individual WBS or lines must add up to the total quantity delivered.
 GRNs - changing units, opening and closing lines
  • If the goods were delivered in units that were different from the units ordered, enter the units delivered.
  • To close a line (if no further goods are required for this order line), select the line and choose Close from the Choose Action list. For example, you might override the quantity delivered, then close the line because no further delivery is needed.
  • To open a closed line, select the line and choose Open from the Choose Action list.

When you have finished entering Goods Received, click NEXT .

processes the GRN, and removes any lines that have no quantity delivered. These will appear on subsequent GRNs.
