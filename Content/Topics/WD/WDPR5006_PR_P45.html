Configurable P45 Document

The document type P45 (PR) is used to print the UK P45.

Use Print P45s, etc. to print the P45s.

To configure P45 forms:

  1. Go to Document Designer in the System Module.
  2. Use the P45 (PR) document type.
  3. See Configurable Documents for information on how to define configurable documents.
  4. The P45 document does not use a BODY form; set the Body Rows and Continuation Body Rows fields to 0, and set up all the fields in the HEAD form.
  5. Fields from the Employee table are available. For UK you can also use fields from the sy_table for the tax reference.
  6. Additionally there are a number of special fields:
    • RO_P45Title - the employee title, e.g. MR or MS.
    • RO_P45GenderIs -RO_P45GenderIs^Male returns an 'X' for a man and blank for a woman. RO_P45GenderIs^Female returns an 'X' for a woman and blank for a man.
    • RO_P45WeekLeft - the week the employee left for a weekly, or employee. Blank for a monthly employee.
    • RO_P45MonthLeft - the month the employee left for a monthly employee. Blank for a weekly, or employee.
    • RO_P45PayYTD - the year to date pay when taxed on a cumulative basis. Blank when taxed on a week 1 basis.
    • RO_P45TaxYTD - the year to date tax when taxed on a cumulative basis. Blank when taxed on a week 1 basis.
    • RO_P45PayThisEmployment - the pay in this employment where this is not given by RO_P45PayYTD. Blank if this is the same as RO_P45PayYTD.
    • RO_P45TaxThisEmployment - the tax in this employment where RO_P45PayThisEmployment is not blank. Blank if RO_P45PayThisEmployment is blank.
    • RO_P45TaxCode - the tax code at the time of leaving.
    • RO_P45Week1 - true if taxed on a week 1 basis at the time of leaving. False if taxed on a cumulative basis.
    • RO_DatePart - the day, month or year of a date.
      Parameter is:
         the name of the field of the employee that holds the date or "TODAY"
         D, M or Y
         Optional format
      For example RO_DatePart^d-o-b|Y|9999
