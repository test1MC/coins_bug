Interrelated Data

One of the strengths of is that information is interrelated, so that for example you can look at a client record and find out what you have already worked on for that client. You can then link to one of those to find out which staff were involved. By looking at an individual's record you can see what skills and experience they have, or what useful contacts they have.

This also means that as you gain more information about clients,  and contacts, you can build on the data already stored in .  That new information will then be available in other ways and to other users.
