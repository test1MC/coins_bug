Part Exchange Against Part Exchange

If you have a buyer for a part-exchange property who wants you to take their current property in part-exchange, you can record a PX against the PX.  This is very similar to recording a PX against a plot.

Recording a PX against a PX

  1. Go to Part Exchange Workbench.
  2. Open the record for the part-exchange property you are selling.
  3. Click the More tab.
  4. Click the PX Against PX tab.
  5. Click .
  6. Fill in the details of the property that the buyer wants you to take in part exchange.  Refer to the side frame help.
  7. Click .

    When you save the part-exchange, generates a reference number.
