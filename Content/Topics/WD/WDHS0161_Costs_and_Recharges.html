Costs and Recharges

If you incur costs as a result of a issue, and need to recoup them from a subcontractor, you can record the details in the Workbench, and produce a report of these costs and recharges (using Costs and Recharges Report).  This information does not update the Subcontract Ledger.

To enter costs and recharges:

  1. From the plot summary or the call summary, click the Cost/Recharges tab (on the plot summary, the Cost/Recharges tab is on the More > tab).

  1. Click to add a record in the normal way.
