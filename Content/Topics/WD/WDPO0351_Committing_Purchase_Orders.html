Committing Purchase Orders

Committing a purchase order makes it available for receipt of goods and makes the cost information available to the accounting system. 

You must be an authored buyer to be able to commit orders.

You can commit several purchase orders together, for example, to commit a day's worth of orders.

To commit purchase orders:

Either:

  1. Go to the order entry screen.
  2. the order or orders you want to commit.
  3. From the Choose Action list, select Commit Purchase Orders

or:

  1. Go to Commit PO's (Batch).
  2. Fill in the selection fields as follows to identify the orders you want to commit, and select the options for the report.
  3. Click [Forward].

produces a report that lists the orders that have been committed.
