Housekeeping - Deleting Previous Years Transactions

SC Delete Previous Years' Transactions is an optional procedure that deletes all Subcontract Ledger transactions up to the end of the you specify. Once deleted they cannot be restored or d upon.

The procedure can only be run after a has been completed and GL Year End has been run. You cannot delete transactions in open periods, or unposted transactions.

The procedure does not delete the account balance information for previous years ? account balance information is held indefinitely.
