eRCT - Reporting and on Payments and Deductions

RCT Payment and Deduction Summary provides an on the records for payments within the scope of eRCT.  You can also use this to help with manual reconciliation of the RCT Return (as an alternative to using Return Reconciliation Workbench - see RCT Return). 

To or report on payments and deductions:

  1. Go to RCT Payment and Deduction Summary.
  2. Click to open the advanced filter.
  3. Enter the range of payment dates to or report on.
  4. Click to apply the filter.

  5. Check the details on screen.

    Negative transactions are highlighted in red, because these are likely to require adjustment in the Return.
