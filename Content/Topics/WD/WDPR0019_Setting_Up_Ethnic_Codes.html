Setting Up Ethnic Codes

 

  ***NOTE: THIS IS A US SPECIFIC TOPIC.***  

In OA, Ethnic Codes are Lookup Codes fully definable by the user. In order to use ethnicity correctly for US Payroll and HR Reporting (EEO-1, CC-257), it is IMPERATIVE that the codes be set up as shown below. Any diversion from this setup may cause reports to print incorrectly.

To maintain consistency, TI must also be in sync with these codes.

How to set up ethnic codes:

In Lookup Codes, add the codes as indicated below.

Code Description Dormant
A Asian no
B Black or African American no
H Hispanic or Latino no
I American Indian or Alaskan Native no
P Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander no
T Two or More Races no
U Unknown no
W White no
