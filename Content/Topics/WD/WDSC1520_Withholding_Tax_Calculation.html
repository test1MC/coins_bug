Withholding Tax Calculation

SC Certificate Input

The tax calculation and validations are dependent on the tax scheme of the subcontractor as follows:

Tax Scheme Other Conditions Result

X

None

The tax amount is zero.

U

None

No certificates may be input.

T

There is a tax rate set on the subcontract.

The tax amount is calculated using the percentage for that tax rate.

T

There is no tax rate set on the subcontract.

No certificates may be input.

V

There is a tax card which is valid on today's date.

The tax amount is zero.

V

There is no tax card or it has expired or not yet started.

No certificates may be input.

 

SC Payment Run

If parameter SC/TAXATPAY is set to N then the tax amount at the time of input will be used. 

If parameter SC/TAXATPAY is set to Y then the tax amount will be recalculated as follows:

Tax Scheme

Other Conditions

Result

X

None

The tax amount is recalculated as zero.

U

None

The certificate may not be paid.

T

There is a tax rate set on the subcontract.

The tax amount is recalculated using the percentage for that tax rate.

T

There is no tax rate set on the subcontract.

The certificate may not be paid.

V

There is a tax card which is valid on today's date.

The tax amount is recalculated as zero.

V

There is no tax card or it has expired or not yet started.

The certificate may not be paid.

PL Invoice Entry

The tax calculation and validations are dependent on the tax scheme of the as held on the linked subcontractor account as follows:

Tax Scheme

Other Conditions

Result

X

None

The tax amount is zero.

U

None

No invoices may be input.

T

None

The tax amount is calculated using the percentage for the tax rate selected for this invoice.

V

There is a tax card which is valid on today's date.

The tax amount is zero.

V

There is no tax card or it has expired or not yet started.

No invoices may be input.

The rules for calculation of the default value in the field follow the standard application of parameter SC/DEFTAXSC.
