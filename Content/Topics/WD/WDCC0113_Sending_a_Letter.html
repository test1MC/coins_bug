Sending a Letter

Throughout you can generate standard letters using Microsoft Word; these can be letters that relate to specific plots, calls, issues, or tasks. 

Your system administrator can configure the letters, and they are addressed to the appropriate person or company. The letters can include information from the database such as significant dates or prices.

To send a letter:

  1. Open the relevant Summary page. For example, to send a letter about a specific call, open the call summary.
  2. Click Options and select the letter from the drop-down menu.

generates the letter as a Word document and displays it in a new window. You can then save, edit, or print the document.
