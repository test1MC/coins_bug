Microsoft Exchange Connector

Project Management can be integrated with Microsoft Exchange, so that:

  • Emails put into a public folder (by moving, copying, drag and drop, or by including the public folder's email address in the To or Cc address list) are imported into as journals and can be viewed in Project Management.
  • events and journals created in Activity Workbench are automatically copied to Exchange. Any updates made in Exchange to these items are also copied back to .
