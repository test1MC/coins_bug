Reserving a Plot

You can only reserve plots that have been released for sale. You do not have to enter all the information about a reservation at the same time; you can come back later to add more information.  But until you mark the reservation as complete, will not treat the plot as reserved. 

To reserve a plot:

  1. Go to Sales Workbench.
  2. Find the plot and open the plot summary.

    NOTE

    You can only reserve plots that are available.
  3. Click the Reserve this plot link at the top right of the screen. (The wording of this link may be different, depending on how your system is set up.)

  4. Choose which type of reservation it is.

  5. If the customer reserving the plot already has a pre-release reservation, you can copy the details from the pre-release: select the customer's name.  If the customer reserving the plot does not have a pre-release reservation, select None.
  6. Click .

    fills in today's date as the reservation date, and calculates the expected exchange, expected completion and expected occupation dates from parameters set in the system; however, you can change these dates if required.

  7. Fill in the details of the reservation. You do not have to fill in all the details of the reservation at once.  You can come back to add more information later.
  8. To add details of the customer:
    1. Click the Customer Details tab.
    2. Enter the customer's names, then use the button to find the customer in the database or add the customer to the database.
    3. Fill in the remaining details on the Customer Details tab.
  9. When you have entered the reservation details, click .

    10. may ask whether you want to enter details of a part-exchange property or details of discounts and incentives.

  10. When you save the reservation, marks the reservation as INCOMPLETE.  To add more details, go to the Reservation tab.
  11. To complete the reservation, when you have added all the necessary information, click the Reservation Entry Complete link on the plot summary. (The wording of this link may be different, depending on how your system is set up.)

Reserving a plot - configuration

You need to set up at least one reservation event, using Sales Workbench Events.
