Valuations

Before making an offer for the part-exchange property, you will normally have the property valued.  You can record details of several valuations, and can use these as part of the input to the offer calculation.

To record valuations for a part-exchange property:

  1. Go to Part Exchange Workbench.
  2. Click the More > tab then the Valuations tab.
  3. Click to record a new valuation.
  4. Fill in the details of the valuation and the agent who provided it.
