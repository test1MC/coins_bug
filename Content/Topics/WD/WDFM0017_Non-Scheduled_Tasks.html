Non-Scheduled Tasks

You can assign a task to a specific without scheduling a time for the task. You would do this if the is managing their own schedule.

To assign a non-scheduled task:

  1. Open the Workbench.
  2. Click to open the task record.
  3. On the Task Details tab, click next to the field.

    displays a lookup of appropriate to the location and the trade on the task.

    NOTE

    You can display the 's from this lookup.
  4. Click to select the you want to assign the task to.
  5. Click to save the record.
