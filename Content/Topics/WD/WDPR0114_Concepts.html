Auto- Concepts

Automatic pension is a requirement for UK employers.  determines whether employees should be enrolled into a pension scheme, and if so s them automatically, and automatically generates the notifications which are required at various stages of the process.

Each employee is categored as one of the following:

  • Eligible Jobholder - a worker who normally works in the UK, is aged between 22 and the state pension age (SPA) and has qualifying earnings above the earnings trigger point for auto-. will automatically these employees into a pension scheme if they are not in one already, though they can choose to opt out.
  • Non-eligible Jobholder ? a worker who normally works in the UK, is aged between 16 and 74, has qualifying earnings and does not meet the criteria to be an eligible jobholder. will not automatically these employees, but they can choose to opt in to a qualifying pension scheme.
  • Entitled Worker ? a worker who normally works in the UK, is aged between 16 and 74 and does not have any qualifying earnings.  will not automatically them, but they can choose to join a pension scheme (which does not need to meet the conditions to be a qualifying pension scheme).
  • Non-entitled Worker ? an employee who does not match any of the above categories. There is nothing to do for these employees.

A qualifying pension scheme is one which meets the minimum contribution levels required by law.
