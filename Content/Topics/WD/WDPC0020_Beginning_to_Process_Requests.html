Beginning to Process Requests

Typically you will process all the lines on a request together, although you can process individual items separately.

To process a request:

  1. Go to .
  2. Click the Pending tab.
  3. Either:
    • If you have not begun to process the request:
      1. the request (or the lines on the request) you want to process.
      2. From the Choose Action menu, select:
        • Process Entire Requisition/Order to process all remaining lines on any of the requests you selected.
        • Process Selected Lines & Associated Items to process the lines you selected, plus any associated items that are associated with any of the selected lines.
        • Process Selected Lines only to only process the lines you selected.
      3. Click .

        assigns a transaction number to the request and displays the Process Pending screen. 

    • If you have already begun to process the request, click the link in the Txn No column.

From the Process Pending screen, you can:

  • Fulfil the request lines - specify where you will get each item from.
  • Change the details of item lines.
