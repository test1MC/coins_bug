Subcontract Certificate Breakdown: Overview

The aim of this document is to detail the steps required to configure and use Subcontract Certificate Breakdown functionality.

This functionality allows you to enter more information against a certificate line, including both claimed and certified amounts (cumulative, previous, and this payment), as well as text fields to explain the reasons why they differ.

The breakdown can be automatically generated from a purchase order. This may also generate detailed costing distribution based on the cost code on the order lines.
