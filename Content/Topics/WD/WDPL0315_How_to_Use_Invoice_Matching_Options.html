If the invoiced quantity is less than the delivered quantity, update the matching line to enter the invoiced quantity. The line remains open for subsequent invoices.

If the invoiced quantity is greater than the delivered quantity:

  • treat it as a missing ticket, or
  • enter the invoiced quantity - applies a hold code to the line.

 Update the line to override the price and quantity.  

In all cases uses tolerances to allow or disallow a match:

    • allows the match if the invoiced amount is less than or equal to the expected amount plus the tolerance. 
    • disallows the match (does not allow you to accept the values) or warns you (asks if you want to accept the values) if the invoiced amount is greater than the expected amount plus the tolerance.
