Employee Forms


The Employee EOY form can be one of four types: P14, P38S, P38A, or NONE. By default, the form will be a P14. If this is inappropriate, you can change it using the F7 Form Type option.

The NONE option allows you to exclude employees who have transferred to a different pay frequency within the same Tax Scheme. Note: If the employee was transferred using the routine, this will not be required.

If you return on an employee, you will be allowed to see their form and, if appropriate, update the content.

  • F11 will take you to Employee Maintenance for the selected employee.
  • F12 will take you to NI Details Maintenance for the selected employee.
