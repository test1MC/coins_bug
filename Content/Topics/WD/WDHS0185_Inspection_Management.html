Inspection Management

The House Sales inspection management process allows you to forecast plot completion dates and manage internal QA inspections of plots.  The forecasts and inspection results can be made available to both after sales and construction department personnel.

Key scenarios in the inspection process include:

  • Initial entry of the first stage forecast data.
  • Booking of internal QA inspections.
  • Recording the results of plot inspections.
  • Site requesting inspection.
  • receiving notification of plots that have passed or failed.
  • Management reporting
