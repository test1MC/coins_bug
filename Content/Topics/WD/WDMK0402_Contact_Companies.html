Contact Companies

You can maintain details of the company a contact works at, and of other companies they have worked at. However, a contact does not necessarily have to belong to a company.

The contact detail shows the contact's current company, and a tab on the contact record shows all companies the contact is associated with.  If you enter a new company record on the Companies tab, and the Current box, that becomes the contact's current company.

If the contact's phone or fax numbers are blank, when you enter a company fills in the phone numbers from the company.
