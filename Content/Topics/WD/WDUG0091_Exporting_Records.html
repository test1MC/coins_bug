Exporting Records

Most browse screens allow you to export records to an Excel spreadsheet.

To export records from a browse:

  • To export all the records, make sure that no filters are applied, then click .
  • To export specific records only, either:
    • the records you want to export, move the mouse pointer over the icon, then in the browse menu click Show Selected, and then click .
    • Use the filters so that only the records you want to export are included, then click ; this will export only the records that match the filtering you applied.
