Selecting Additional Requisition Lines Onto a PO

From within Order Entry, you can select additional Requisition Items, which will then appear on the Order.

To Select Additional Requisition Lines onto a PO:

  1. From the Order Entry screen, select Tag Requisition Lines from the Options menu.
