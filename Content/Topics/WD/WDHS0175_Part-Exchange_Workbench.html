Part-Exchange Workbench

Part Exchange Workbench allows you to manage the buy-in and sale-on of part exchange properties, and to on detailed information relating to the process.

There are three groups of key scenarios in Part Exchange Workbench: those relating to buy-in of a purchaser's property in part exchange, those relating to sale-on of the property, and general tasks.

Buy-in

  • Recording a reservation with a PX (in Sales Workbench)
  • Inspecting the property
  • Recording valuations
  • Calculating an offer
  • Surveying the property
  • Tracking the progress of the buy-in
  • Exchanging contracts
  • Completing a sale
  • Recording meter readings at buy-in
  • Recording details of buy-in estate agents
  • Recording details of utility companies

Sale-on

  • Releasing a part-exchange property to market
  • Recording a reservation
  • Recording exchange of contracts
  • Recording conditional exchange
  • Completing a sale
  • Recording occuptation
  • Recording meter readings
  • Updating the purchaser's personal details
  • Updating sales chain details
  • Updating exchange conditions
  • Recording details of the selling agents
  • Updating discounts and incentives
  • Recording details of the purchaser's solicitor, financial advisor or mortgage lender
  • Entering details of your solicitor
  • Recording a cancellation
  • Recording a part-exchange against a part-exchange
  • Tracking the progress of sale-on
  • Dereleasing a part-exchange property

General tasks

  • Printing a letter
  • Printing a utility letter
  • Adding a note
  • Deleting an event
  • Recording questionnaire responses
