Changing the Subcontractor Record - Activity Rates

If the subcontractor is paid by timecard, you must use Activity Rates to enter the rates of pay for different activities for that subcontractor.

To enter the rates of pay for a subcontractor

  1. Go to Subcontractor Maintenance.
  2. Open the subcontractor record.
  3. Select the Activity Rates tab.
  4. Add records for the combinations of and activity this subcontractor can be paid for:
    • Enter the and (if applicable) phase for which you are setting the rates of pay, or leave blank if the rate applies to all . 
    • Enter the code for the activity for which you are setting the rates of pay, or leave blank if the rate applies to all activities. 
    • The same activity can be paid at different rates (for example, normal, overtime, and weekend). Enter the rate(s) of pay for this subcontractor. 
    • If a different unit (for example: hours, s) or costing analysis is appropriate for this subcontractor, enter it.
