Cancelling Contract Notification

If you have submitted a contract notification for a contract that you will not make payments on, you can cancel the notification electronically. 

To cancel a contract notification:

  1. Go to:
    • Subcontract Maintenance,
    • Subcontract RCT Details Review/Update

      or

    • Subcontractor RCT Details Review/Update (for an ongoing contract)
  2. Select the subcontract or subcontractor whose notification you want to cancel.
  3. Choose Create Contract Notification Cancellation from the action list.
  4. Go to Current.
  5. Select the notification cancellation record.
  6. Choose Submit to Revenue from the action list.
