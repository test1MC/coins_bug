Lieu Days

Days in lieu awarded to employees for working unpaid outside their normal working hours can be recorded in HR and taken into account when dealing with available . This information is visible in both the HR and Employee Self Service modules.

There is a 'Lieu Days' tab in Personnel Workbench where any lieu days awarded can be recorded, including the date awarded and an optional date by which the lieu days must be taken. The number of days must be a whole number of half days. To allow for days in lieu which are accrued gradually you are allowed to set the days initially as zero and use the Notes field to record the number of hours in lieu accrued until there is enough to properly fill in a lieu days value.

The lieu days will be used in precedence to the rest of the allowance for an employee for any between the date awarded and the date inclusive. Once the date has passed, any lieu days not used will be shown as expired and no longer add into the total available for the year. This does not prevent you from retrospectively adding a between the awarded and date which will then still use such expired days. Allocation of lieu days is automatic with no need to specify that a is using lieu days when it is entered.

The 'Other' tab in Personnel Workbench shows a summary of the lieu days for the current year.

Employee Self Service shows both a summary of lieu days, and a tab showing all lieu days records.

The Year End process and the  Report reports show columns for lieu days.
