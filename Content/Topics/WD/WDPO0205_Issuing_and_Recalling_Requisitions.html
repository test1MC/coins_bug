Issuing and Recalling Requisitions

Issuing a requisition makes it available for raising purchase orders.  Depending on how is configured, you may be able to make changes to the requisition after it has been issued; otherwise you will need to recall it.

You can issue either individual or multiple requisitions, or issue them as a batch.

To issue a requisition:

  1. Go to the requisition entry screen.
  2. the requisitions you want to issue.
  3. From the Choose Action list, select Issue Requisitions.

To issue a batch of requisitions:

  1. Go to Issue Requisitions (Batch).
  2. Fill in the selection fields to identify the requisitions you want to issue, and select the options for the report.
  3. Click [Forward].

    produces a report that lists the requisitions that have been issued.

To recall a requisition:

  1. Go to the requisition entry screen.
  2. the requisition you want to recall.
  3. From the Choose Action list, select Recall Requisitions.
