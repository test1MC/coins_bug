Global Alerts

 

If one of your regional offices identifies a serious concern (such as a health and safety issue) with a or subcontractor, you may want all regions to be aware of the situation.  can be set up to:

  • Globally change the status code of the or subcontractor.  Typically this would be an "inactive" code to prevent the or subcontractor being used.
  • Send an email alert to anyone who has a view of the or subcontractor.
