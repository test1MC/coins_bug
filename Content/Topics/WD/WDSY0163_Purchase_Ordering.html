Multi-Currency Purchase Ordering

Orders

When you raise an order, the currency on the office determines the currency of the order.

The Totals frame (click the ) displays the totals in the document currency, and also in the base currency.

Note that the base currency totals are calculated on the exchange rate that applies when the order is committed.

The Gains and Losses sheet (if used) must be completed in the base currency.

GRNs

When entering a GRN, if you add unordered goods to the GRN, the price must be in the foreign currency.

Invoice matching

The invoice matching screen shows the amount to be matched in the document currency.
