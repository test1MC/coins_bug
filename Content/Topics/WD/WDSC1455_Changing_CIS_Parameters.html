Changing Parameters

You need to fill in the relevant parameters.  These are all grouped under " 2007 Scheme" in the SC UK Tax Parameter list.  

The following parameters are those that are required for basic processing (most of the rest are concerned with electronic communication): CIS2DATE, CIS2TRAE, CIS2TRAS, CIS2TRAS, CONTXOFF, CONUTR, HPAYE%, VENDORID.
