Does This Affect Me?

This document will be of interest to all employers operating a UK payroll who will be doing their Payroll Year-end submissions electronically.

Starting with returns for the 2009/2010 tax year, all employers (subject to some minor exceptions which would not apply to clients) are compelled to complete their Payroll year-end submissions electronically.  Previously this only applied to employers with more than 50 employees in a single tax scheme.
