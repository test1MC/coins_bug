Scheduling Tasks

 

Scheduling involves assigning tasks to . Appointments Summary gives an overview of the tasks that need to be scheduled. The Scheduling screen allows you to drag and drop tasks into a daily schedule that shows the availability for all relevant .  

To schedule tasks:

  1. Either:
    1. Go to Single Day Scheduling.
    2. Use the selectors to choose the contract, trade, and date you want to schedule tasks for. 

      Selecting Appointed allows you to schedule that have specific appointments for that day; selecting Non-appointed allows you to schedule that do not have an appointment (for example, routine ). Generally, you should schedule appointed before scheduling any non-appointed .

or:

    1. Go to Appointments Summary.
    2. Use the selectors to choose the date range you want to schedule tasks for.
    3. Click a link to take you to Single Day Scheduling

      Clicking a link in a date column allows you to schedule that have specific appointments for that day; clicking a link in Unappointed allows you to schedule that do not have an appointment (for example, routine ). Generally, you should schedule appointed before scheduling any non-appointed .
