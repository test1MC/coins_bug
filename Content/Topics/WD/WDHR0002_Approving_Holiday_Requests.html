Approving Requests

When an employee for whom you are a ' authorer' enters a request, sends you an email, and creates an action in your Activity Workbench. Both of these contain links to the Request Approval screen where you can review the details and choose to approve or deny the request.

To approve a request:

    1. Either:

      • Follow the link in the email.

         

      • Follow the link on your Activity Workbench.

Both of these open the relevant Request Approval screen: 

  • To approve the request, click Approve; to deny the request, click Deny.

    sends notification emails to any other users who are set up to approve the employee's . 

To approve a change request:

If an employee makes a change to a request that has already been approved, sends you a similar email, and creates a " Amendment Request" action on your Activity Workbench.  On the Request Approval page the amendments to the booked are shown in red.

  • To approve or deny the request, click Approve or Deny.


To approve a cancellation request:

If an employee asks to cancel a request that has already been approved, sends you a similar email, and creates a " Cancellation Request" action on your Activity Workbench. 

  • To approve or deny the cancellation request, click Approve (the record is deleted) or Deny (the status returns to 'Booked').

Approving requests - configuration

The System User ID field on the Main tab in Personnel Workbench needs to be filled in for all users (requesters and approvers).

NOTE

If an employee is their own approver then any entered will be automatically approved and marked as "Booked" (no emails or actions are sent). They will also be free to make any deletions and amendments .

The Authorers need to set for each employee, on the Organation tab in Personnel Workbench.

In order to receive emails the users' email addresses must be entered on the Details tab for each user in Users.
