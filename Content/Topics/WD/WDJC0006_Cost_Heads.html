provide a link to the General Ledger accounts. Each can specify up to six full or partial General Ledger account masks (one for each of the six Cost Categories, although usually each will be restricted to a single category). These account masks combine with account masks on the to specify the General Ledger accounts against which different costs on each will be recorded (see Account Masking). A General Ledger account mask for revenue can also be specified. Several can use the same General Ledger account mask, so cost analysis and reporting in Status can be at a more detailed level than in General Ledger.
