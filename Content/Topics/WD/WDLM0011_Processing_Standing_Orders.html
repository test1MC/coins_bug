Processing Standing Orders

Auto Bank Reconciliation performs the matching of the standing orders against your bank statement items. The bank statement items can either be manually input into or loaded from a file. The match is based on the bank code, statement transaction type, statement description and amount.

If a match is found then a lettings cash transaction will be created using the analysis specified on the standing order. In addition if this cash transaction can then be matched to an outstanding lettings' rent charge/adjustment this will then auto allocate the cash to the charge.
