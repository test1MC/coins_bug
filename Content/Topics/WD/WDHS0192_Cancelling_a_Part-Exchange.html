Cancelling a Part-Exchange

If the sale of a part-exchange is cancelled, record this in Part Exchange Workbench. 

To record a cancellation:

  1. Open the part-exchange record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "cancel sale-on".
  3. Enter the amount of any refunded.
  4. On the Reason tab, the reason or reasons for the sale being cancelled.
  5. Click .

Cancellation - configuration
