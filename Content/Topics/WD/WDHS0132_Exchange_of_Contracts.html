Recording Exchange of Contracts

When the purchaser exchanges contracts, you should record the fact in the Sales Workbench. 

The plot must be reserved before you can record exchange of contracts.

To record the exchange of contracts:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "exchange of contracts".
  3. Enter the details of the exchange.
  4. Click .

Recording exchange of contracts - configuration

You need to set up an EXCHANGE event; see Sales Events.
