Updating Records

To update single records:

  1. Click next to the line you want to update.
  2. Change the information you want to update.
  3. Click . 

     

    For line update and detail update screens, this is at the beginning of the line you are adding.  Figure 1: Save Button on Line Update

     

    For screen update, it is at the bottom right of the screen.

    Figure 2: Save Button on Screen Update

    If you leave the screen without clicking , does not save the changes.
