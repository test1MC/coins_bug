Allocating After They Have Been Entered

If you have previously entered a that still remains partly or fully unallocated, you will at some stage need to allocate it.

There are different ways to apply unallocated payments to outstanding transactions:

  • Apply Unallocated Cash - (Manual) allows you to apply one payment for a at a time. displays a list of all the outstanding transactions that exist for the , allowing you to manually select which transactions to apply the payment to. See Allocating a Single Payment.
  • (Auto) Apply Unallocated Cash - (Automatic) displays a list of all existing unallocated payments on your system, and will automatically distribute the selected payment(s) to the oldest due invoices that are not on hold. See Allocating Multiple Payments.
