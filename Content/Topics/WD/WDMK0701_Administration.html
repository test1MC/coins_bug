Administration

The Administration menu includes the following functions:

  • Merge Companies allows you to merge two company records, for example if offices of the same company have been entered as two separate companies. 

    The copying process runs in the background.  copies all the offices, , contacts, invitations and so on from the secondary company to the primary company, and deletes the secondary company.

    You can merge companies as long as they have only been used in .  If any office of the company has been used anywhere else in , the merge will fail.

    The report shows the results of the merge, including any errors.

  • Date Conversion allows you to convert dates used on existing in GUI to the format used in .

  • MK Companies Conversion allows you to convert the format of companies from the format used in GUI to the format used in . 

    This only changes the way the data is stored, and is needed for performance reasons.  You only need to run this once.
