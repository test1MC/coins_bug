Rate Change - Overview

From time to time, rates change.  These pages explain what you need to do within to implement changes to the rates.

NOTE

Please note that these instructions are not specifically tailored to the requirements of a particular change in rate.  You should confirm the detailed actions required in a specific situation, before the change in rate becomes effective.

Changes to rates generally apply only to invoices dated on or after the rate changes. In the weeks immediately following a rate change, companies will be processing some invoices dated prior to the change at the old rate, together with other invoices dated on or after the rate change at the new rate. Therefore, it is necessary to use separate codes for each rate on -- it will not be possible to implement this change correctly by changing the percentage rate on existing codes in .

There are a number of different areas within the database where codes are stored against various types of record. Some of these may not apply to all clients, but several are very widely used. It is thus essential that the procedures in these pages are followed to ensure trouble-free implementation of the change.
