Resources

The amount of resource available for a given trade in a given timeslot will depend on the number of who engage in that trade. As are assigned to , the amount of available resource will decrease. When the resource allocated to a timeslot reaches a certain percentage of the resource available, the scheduling screen for that trade shows the timeslot as "busy" (so help desk users should avoid making appointments for that timeslot). When the resource allocated reaches a second percentage, the scheduling screen for that trade shows the timeslot as "unavailable" (so help desk users cannot make appointments for that timeslot). 

In order to allow for busy periods or specific working patterns for individual trades, you can reduce the amount of resource that is available for scheduling in a given timeslot. This does not affect the actual amount of resource, but means that the timeslot will show busy or unavailable at a lower percentage; this means, for example, that the remaining resource can be allocated to emergency .
