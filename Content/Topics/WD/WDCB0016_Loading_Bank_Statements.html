Loading Bank Statements

allows you to load bank statement files and reconcile them with transactions in .

Steps to load a bank statement:

  1. Retrieve/download the statement via your banking software.
  2. Check the current status of your statements.
  3. Load the Bank statement.

Click the links above for more information about the individual formats.

For types A - X, the medium for loading a statement is a file. It is probable that you will first load that file on to your PC via your bank's software. 

If you are using a manual statement, you can still use the Bank Reconciliation processes in but you will need to manually type in the statement details.

allows you to load up to one statement per statement date per bank account. Therefore, it is expected that you will not reconcile any more frequently than daily for any one bank account within a .
