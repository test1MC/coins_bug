Gains and Losses Sheet

If you are entering a head office , material or subcontract order, may prompt you to complete the Gains and Losses sheet.

The Gains and Losses sheet (also called "letting sheet") allows you to record and compare prices negotiated by buyers against those used in the by estimators. It is not designed to be a measure of profitability on a .

When you have completed the Gains & Losses sheet, click . calculates the saving or loss on an item-by-item basis, and for the whole order.
