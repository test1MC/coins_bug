Booking an Appointment

After a has been logged, you need to book appointments for the task (or tasks) on the .

To book an appointment:

  • Navigate to FM > Jobs
  • Click the link in the Job Column for the job to allocate

  • On the Tasks tab of the Workbench, click [Assign Appointment Date] to the right of the task you want to book.
  • Click the Assign Appointment Date tab.

  • This shows you which appointment slots you have available.

      • If the time-slot shows red, you cannot book an appointment for that time-slot. This may be because there is no available resource, or because the requirements of the will not allow you to book it at this time (for example, you cannot book an afternoon appointment if the task takes a whole day).
      • If the time-slot shows green, you can book an appointment.
    • To select a different range of dates, change the Date selector and click .
    • To change the response type of the (for example, if the has a high-priority response type, but the client is not available during the response period, you can change the response type to a lower priority), change the Response selector and click . recalculates the target start and end dates for the task.
    • Click the link on an available time-slot to book the appointment for that time.

The appointment is now booked. returns to the Workbench, and the task record shows the appointment date and time-slot.

Once you have made an appointment, you cannot change the date, but you can cancel and re-book it.
