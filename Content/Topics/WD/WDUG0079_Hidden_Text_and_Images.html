Hidden Text and Images

In order to save space on the screen, and make it easier to read the information, images of screens in the Online Documentation are often not shown at first, but are hidden.  Where the documentation contains a hidden screen shot, there is a link like the one below:

Screenshot

Click the link to display the screen shot; click it again to hide it.

Text is sometimes hidden too; for example, additional instructions that may not be relevant in most cases. In the same way, you can display this by clicking a link.

You can also use the button to display all the hidden images and text in the topic.
