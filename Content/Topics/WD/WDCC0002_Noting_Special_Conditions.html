Noting Special Conditions

If necessary, you can make a note of any special conditions relating to an individual plot. These notes will show on the plot summary.

To note special conditions for a plot:

  1. Open the plot record in Customer Care Workbench.

  1. Click the More > tab then the Special Conditions tab.

  1. Click to open the Conditions field.

  1. Enter the note and click .

    displays the note at the top of the plot summary tab.
