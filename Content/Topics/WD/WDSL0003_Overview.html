Repeat/Recurring Invoices - Overview

 

Intended Use

The repeat/recurring invoice feature enables the automatic generation of monthly and quarterly Sales Invoices based on entry of an annual 'invoice' value. For example, repeat invoices can be used for recurring monthly or quarterly service charges.

There is no limit to the frequency of invoicing. Typically we would expect it to be Monthly, Quarterly or Bi-annual, but there is no reason that it could not be used every week, fortnight or every day.

Availability

Repeat Invoices are available in V10.10 onwards of Open Architecture (OA).

Considerations

Users should be familiar with the standard operating facilities in OA. They should also be able to set up scheduled reports in OA or to request assistance.

Limitations

You must have OA to set up repeat invoices. But once that is done, editing the generated invoices can be conducted in COINSplus.
