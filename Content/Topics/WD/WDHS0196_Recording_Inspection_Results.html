Recording Inspection Results

Recording the results of a plot inspection involves changing the status of the inspection record, and entering any additional information about the inspection.

To record inspection results:

  1. Go to Inspection Workbench.
  2. Choose the , then the plot you want to record the inspection result for.

    displays the Plot Summary.

  3. Click the Inspections Booked tab.
  4. Click next to the inspection record you want to update.
  5. Select the status that corresponds to the result of the inspection (for example: Pass, Fail, Subject To).
  6. Fill in the other details and click .
