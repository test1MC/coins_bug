Changing the Default General Ledger Codes

As part of Maintenance, and depending on how it is set up, may display the G/L Accounts tab. Enter the General Ledger control accounts for this .

The accounts are the Control account (in the General Ledger chart of accounts); the Invoice Register account; and the General Ledger Discounts Taken account.
