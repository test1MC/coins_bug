Inviting People to an Event

lets you keep a record of who you have invited to an event, and whether they have accepted or declined the invitation.  adjusts the number of places available according to the number of people who have accepted or declined, so you can quickly see how many places are still available at an event. You can also record whether the people you invited actually attended the event.

To add invitees by interest:

  1. First you must set up the event.  When you have done this you can add invitees.
  2. Go to Contacts.
  3. Click the By Interest tab.
  4. Filter the list to show contacts with interests that are relevant to the event.

    You can use the advanced filter to include more than one interest in the filter.

To add individual invitees to an event:
