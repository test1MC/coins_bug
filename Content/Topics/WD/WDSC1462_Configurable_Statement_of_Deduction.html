Configurable Statement of Deduction

If you choose to issue the subcontractor with a monthly statement of deduction (as opposed to including the statutory details on a remittance advice or certificate), you will need to configure the statement form.

To configure the statement form

  1. Use Document Designer.
  2. Create a new document of type Statement of Deduction.
  3. the Statement Of Deductionbox.

    This marks the form as a statutory statement of deduction; when the statement is printed, stamps the payments with the date, time and user who printed the statement.
