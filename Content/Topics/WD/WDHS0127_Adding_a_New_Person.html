Adding a New Person in House Sales

There are various places where you may need to add a new person in House Sales; for example, adding someone to a prospect record.

To add a new person

  1. Open the Add Person screen. You might do this by:
    • Going to the People Workbench and clicking .
    • While adding a new prospect to the Prospect Workbench, clicking .
  2. Enter the person's name.
  3. To input the address using the lookup:
    1. Enter the delivery point (for example, the house number).
    2. Type the postcode in the Lookup field
    3. Click .

      displays a lookup showing the full address.

    4. Click to enter the address.
