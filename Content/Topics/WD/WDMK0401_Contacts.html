Contacts

The Contacts Workbench holds information on all external contacts, including:

Their personal details such as addresses, telephone numbers and social interests.    Use the Addresses, Telephones and Interests tabs to record these details.

The company they work for, and past companies they have worked for.    Use the Companies tab to record companies the contact has worked for. 
    You can also record the current company on the Contact Details tab.

in which they have been involved.    The tab shows companies they have been involved in.

Events that you have invited them to.    The Invitations tab shows events they have been invited to.

Other contacts that they know (either internal or external).    Use the Known To tab to record other contacts.
