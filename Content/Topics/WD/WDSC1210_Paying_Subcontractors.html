Paying Subcontractors

To pay subcontractors, you need to do the following:

  1. Select the certificates to be paid.
  2. Print a report of the certificates you have selected.
  3. Freeze the payment batch.
  4. Print the , and the remittances and certificates.
  5. Post the payment batch.

The following sections cover each of these steps. Steps 1, 3, 4 and 5 must be done in order. If you try to do something out of order, issues an error message and will not let you continue.

NOTE

Ensure that payment runs are started and completed on the same day. If you do not, and any subcontractors' tax certificates expire in the meantime, and postings may disagree. 

If you pay a subcontractor using a manual , you should enter the details using Manual Payment Entry. See Enter and Allocate Manual Payments
