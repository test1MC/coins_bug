Changing Personal Details

You may need to change the personal details of a purchaser or one of the people on a record; for example, if someone gets married or changes their correspondence address.

NOTE

If you change the address of the main contact on a record, this also changes the address on the record.

To change personal details:

  1. Find the record for the person.  You can do this:
    • From the Summary in the Workbench, click the Personal Details tab.
    • From the Plot Summary in the Sales Workbench, click the Reservation tab, then the Purchasers tab.
    • From the Part Exchange Summary in the Part Exchange Workbench, click the Purchasers tab.
    • Search for the person in the People Workbench.
  2. Open the record and make the relevant changes.
  3. Save the record.

NOTE

When you update details for a person whose information is stored in more than one workbench, their details in all other related workbenches are automatically updated.
