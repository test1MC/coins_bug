Dormant Subcontractor Processing

Dormant subcontractors are subcontractors you have not recently dealt with. may prevent transactions to dormant subcontractors.  You can choose whether to display dormant subcontractors on the Subcontractor lookup.

You can mark an individual subcontractor as dormant using Subcontractor Maintenance, or you can use Check for Dormant Subcontractors to mark several (or all) subcontractors as dormant, and "re-awaken" subcontractors.
