Recalling a Draft Certificate

Unless your security setting prevents you, you can "Recall" certificates once released for approval at the next level (to correct a mistake, or, if an approver approves the wrong payment cert by mistake). By default users will be able to recall any certificate.

If there are any later draft certificates for the same order, these will be recalled also.  asks whether you wish to proceed if this is the case.

To recall a draft certificate

  1. Set the Released or Authored selector to Include and click .
  2. Click the link in the Txn column for the certificate you want to recall.
  3. Select Recall from the Options menu.
  4. Enter comments about why you are recalling the certificate.
  5. Click .

The recalled certificate remains in view and the 'previous action' shows 'Recalled'.
