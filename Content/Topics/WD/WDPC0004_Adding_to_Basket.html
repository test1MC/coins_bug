– Adding Items to the Basket

When you have found the product you want to request (using Search or Groups), you need to add it to your basket.  Your basket is a list of the products you are going to include on the request. 

To add an item to the basket:

  1. From the results list, click the link or image on the product.

    opens the Add To Basket screen.

    If there are any documents associated with the product, they are shown as links, below all the other fields on the screen.  Click the link to display the document.

  2. Enter the Quantity you require.
  3. Enter the date that the item is Required From.  By default enters tomorrow's date.
  4. If the product is an asset you are (rather than an item you are buying), enter the Expected Date.  By default, enters a date one week from tomorrow.
  5. Enter any Comments - these allow you to communicate with the .
  6. If product has any associated items set up with it, you must choose whether or not to order these items: click Required or Not Required as appropriate.
  7. If you are requesting associated items, enters the quantities that have been set up as defaults against the associated item.  Enter the quantity you require.

    NOTES

    1. You cannot request associated items if you have entered a quantity of more than 1 for the main item.  If you want to request more than one of the main item, as well as requesting additional items, enter separate requests for each main item.
    2. You can request associated items as products in their own right.

    Some associated items have a Description field next to them; this is typically for consumables to go with rehired . 

  8. When you have filled in all the details, click OK to add the item to your basket.

    NOTE

    If you reale you do not want to request this item, just go to a different screen without clicking OK.  If you do add the wrong product to your basket, you can remove it: see Your Basket.
