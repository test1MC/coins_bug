Sales Chain

You can record details of the sales chain that affects a reserved part-exchange property, including details of the estate agents, the buyers, the sellers, and their financial advisors, mortgage lenders and solicitors.

To record sales chain details:

  1. Go to Part Exchange Workbench.
  2. Find the part exchange record, and open the Part Exchange Details screen.
  3. Click the More> tab.
  4. Click the Chain tab.
  5. To add a chain, click .
  6. Select which link you are entering the information about (for the first link, this will be the part-exchange property).
  7. Enter details of the property that forms the next link in the chain.  You can:
    • Copy the address details from the purchaser, or
    • Use the lookup.
  8. Click .

    9. displays the Link Detalis.

  9. You can also use the Notes, Buyer Details> and Seller Details> tabs to enter any additional information about the link.
  10. When you have finished, either:
    • To go back to the Chain tab, click .
    • To add another link, select Add Another Link from the Options menu.
