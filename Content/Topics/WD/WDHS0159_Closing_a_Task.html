Closing a Task

There are various ways that you can close a task.

To close a task:

  • Open the task details and enter a completion date.
  • Using Quick Complete:
    1. Open the call details and click the Tasks tab.
    2. the task record (or records).
    3. Choose Quick Complete from the actions list and click .
    4. Enter a completion date and time.
    5. Click .
  • Using Bulk Complete, you can select a task and will complete all tasks for the same subcontractor or on the same call.  Do this by following the steps above, but choose Bulk Complete instead of Quick Complete.
