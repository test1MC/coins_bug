Updating Progress from the Forecast

Progress from Forecast is one of three ways to update the construction progress in the Construction Workbench (the other two being the Progress  Sheet and Progress Update screens).

The construction stages are shown in the order in which they are forecast to be completed.

  • If the construction has gone according to the forecast, you can quickly update the actual progress by confirming the forecast dates. 
  • If some stages were completed behind (or ahead of) schedule, you can enter the actual completion date. 
  • If some stages have not yet been completed, you can revise the forecast for those stages.
