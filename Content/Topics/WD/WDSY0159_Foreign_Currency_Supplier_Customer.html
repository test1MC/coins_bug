Setting up a Foreign Currency or Customer

When you set up a new account from the Company Information Workbench, assigns the account currency according to the set up on the office of the company that you select.

To set up the Office:

  1. Go to Companies.
  2. Click the link for the company.
  3. Click the Offices tab and open the office record.
  4. Click the Accounts tab.
  5. In the Currency column, enter the code for the currency for the , Subcontract Ledger and rows as appropriate.
  6. Click .
  7. Then set up the account in the ledger in the usual way.

NOTE

 If the account already exists in the ledger of any , the currency code you set up on the office must match the currency of the account – will not let you enter a different one. This also means that on accounts with the same number in different , the currency code must be the same.
