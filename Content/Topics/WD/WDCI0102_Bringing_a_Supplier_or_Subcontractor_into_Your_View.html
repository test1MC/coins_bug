Bringing a Company into Your View

If you want to work with a company in the Company Information Workbench that is not in your view, you can bring it into base (lowest) view, from levels above your base view.

NOTE

Other users (for example, colleagues with similar responsibilities) may share your user view.  If you add a company, the other users who share your base view will also see the new company.  If you remove a company from your view, the other users will no longer have it in their view either.

To remove a record from your view:

  1. When in your base view, select the record you want to remove. (You can only remove records from your base view.)
