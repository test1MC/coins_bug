Tracking Progress of a Part-Exchange

You can check on the progress of the buy-in or sale-on of a part-exchange property using the Part Exchange Workbench - Buyin Progresssion and Part Exchange Workbench - Sale Out Progresssion tabs. 

  • Part Exchange Workbench - Buyin Progresssion lets you track the progress of the buy-in of a part-exchange.  It shows an overview of the stages the buy-in goes through, with the expected dates, and the actual dates of stages that have happened.
  • Part Exchange Workbench - Sale Out Progresssion lets you track the progress of the buy-in of a part-exchange.  It shows an overview of the stages the buy-in goes through, with the expected dates, and the actual dates of stages that have happened.

You can update some of the dates manually.
