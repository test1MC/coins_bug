Whereas for material order lines, calculates the accrual from the GRN or the due date on the order, for items the accrual is not created from the GRN, but from the Sheet.  provides different ways to generate sheets, depending on your business needs:

  • Maintenance (No Allocation) creates a single sheet per item.  This is used where your main concern is to record the date and do not need to record how and where is used on the .  GRNs may or may not be required, depending on the configuration.
    • If not using GRNs (accrual method = Order), assumes the was delivered on the due date, and calculates the accrual when you commit the order.
    • If using GRNs (accrual method = No Allocation), generates a sheet for each item that has been delivered, and calculates the accrual based on the delivery date.
  • Allocation (accrual method = GRN) creates weekly sheets, which allow you to record in detail what the item is used for and how much is charged.  This provides more information for invoice matching.  GRNs are required if you are using Allocation.  calculates the accrual when you confirm the sheet.
