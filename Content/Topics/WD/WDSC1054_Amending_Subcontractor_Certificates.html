Subcontractor Certificates - Amending Subcontractor Certificates

Until the batch has been posted, and as long as the certificate is the most recent one you have entered, you can amend any of the details.

If you have entered another certificate since the one you want to amend delete the later certificate, amend the previous certificate, then re-enter the later certificate.

If you have already posted the certificate, you can still amend some of the details (not the values) and change the hold status.
