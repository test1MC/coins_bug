Printing the Amount in Words

provides special fields that allow you to print the amount of the in words, either as individual words for each digit, or as a line of text that represents the amount.

To print the amount in words:

    • To print a line with the following format:

      ONE ZERO NINE SEVEN FIVE THREE 64

      add a line containing the field RO_scs_digitword__n where n is the position (1 = units, 2 = tens, 3 = hundreds, and so on). Repeat this field for each position. Use RO_scs_cents for the pence/cents amount in figures:

      The format ***,**9.99 prints the amount with leading asterisks; for example: **1,275.45.

    • To print the payee and the amount in "full text"; for example: 

      Anderson & Little ***************
      ONE HUNDRED AND TWENTY THREE $P$CO|CURUNIT$P$S 99$P$CO|CURDEC$P$ ONLY ****************
      *************************************************************

      add consecutive lines containing the fields RO_sbm_payeen*, RO_scs_amtWords__1 and RO_scs_amtWords__2.

    • To print the amount in words on one line, using the USA format, for example:

      ONE HUNDRED AND TWENTY THREE DOLLARS 99CENTS

      use the field RO_SpelledAmount.

    • To print the "Pay text" (the payee name and the amount in figures, with no spaces), for example:

      ANDERSON&LITTLE123.99ONLY******************

      use the field RO_scs_payText.
