eRCT - Paying Subcontractors

The process is:

  1. Enter certificates and post them (see eRCT - Certificate Entry).
  2. Select certificates for payment and freeze the payment batch (see Selecting Certificates for Payment).
  3. Process the payment notification (see eRCT - Payment Notification).
  4. Pay certificates (print /remittances, generate )  (see Printing and Remittance Advices).
