User Preferences

Using User Preferences, you can change various settings that relate to how you use , including:

  • Your password;
  • Your Desktop (or your user home page);
  • How buttons are displayed;
  • How remembers your settings on filters and lookups;
  • Which RSS feeds you want.

You can also manage your ites and see a list of recent alerts (messages that pop up in the bottom corner of the screen).

To change your preferences:

  1. Move the mouse pointer over your profile picture and click User Preferences.

    displays the Preferences screen in a new frame. 

  2. Click to edit your preferences.
  3. Make the changes and click to save them.
