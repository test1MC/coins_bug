Sales Workbench Overview

Sales Workbench covers all aspects of selling a property, and allows you to record and manage the events that take place during each stage of the sales process (plot reservation, legal completion and sale of the property).  It also covers areas such as sales incentives, part-exchanges and meter readings.

Key scenarios for Sales Workbench include:

  • Releasing a plot for sale
  • Reserving a plot
  • Exchanging a plot
  • Completing a sale
  • Cancelling a sale
  • Recording occupation
  • Recording meter readings
  • Printing a reservation letter
  • Searching for a plot
  • Searching for a purchaser
  • Updating the construction stage of a plot
  • Updating purchasers' details
  • Recording and options
  • Producing a quote for
  • Printing an statement
  • Record a part-exchange property
  • Recording questionnaire responses
  • Updating discounts and incentives
  • Tracking the sales chain
  • Tracking the progress of a sale
  • Noting special conditions of the sale
  • Updating the exchange conditions
  • Updating your solicitor's details
