You first load the Glenigan raw data from a file into the Glenigan Workbench.  You can then create new from any relevant leads. 

To load a Glenigan file:

This loads Glenigan into the Report Status workbench; it does not load them as into .

  1. Export the leads you are interested in from Glenigan, (using the CSV with titles format).
  2. Copy the Glenigan data file to a location that you can access from your computer.
  3. Go to Glenigan and click the New Load tab.
  4. Browse for the Glenigan data file.
  5. Click .

To link contacts, companies or offices to existing records:

  1. Look at the Details of the Glenigan .
  2. Click the Companies tab.
  3. Open the company record you want to link to an existing company in .
  4. Use the lookups to select the company and office in to link this company to, and the contact to link the contact to.
  5. Save the company record.

Configuration

You need to use Glenigan Company Types to map the numeric Glenigan codes to the equivalent company types you are using in .

Consider the setting of the parameters PRSTAT and PRTYPE.
