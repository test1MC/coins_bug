Events

To help you manage promotional events you organe, allows you to hold information such as dates, venue, who you have invited and feedback you receive. 

You can see which events you have invited a contact to, using the Invitations tab on the Contact workbench.

You can see which events you have invited (contacts at) a company to, using the Invitations tab on the Companies workbench.

To add a new event:

  1. Go to Events.
  2. Click .
  3. Enter the details of the event (for example, a description, the address and date, the number of places available).

  4. Click .

When you have saved the event record, you can add invitees and record their responses.  adjusts the number of places available according to the number of people who have accepted or declined.
