Part-Exchange

 

The Part-exchange is responsible for overseeing the life cycle of a part-exchange property. He/she manages the buy-in of the property such as arrangement of valuations, inspection of the property and preparing the offer value. In addition, the Part-exchange will also handle the sale on of a property. This includes the appointing of an estate agent, instructions made to solicitors and monitoring of the sales process.

House Sales will enable the Part-exchange to carry out administrative tasks such as organation of correspondence to buyers, vendors and utilities companies. House Sales can record valuations that have been carried out, and then calculate an offer value for the property; this will help the Part-exchange to prepare an offer value. Another key feature of House Sales is the Workflow; by using this functionality the Part-exchange will fulfil his/her daily tasks efficiently and professionally. It will also encourage him/her to be proactive in the management of part-exchange properties (for example notification of a new reservation with part-exchange, cancellations and missed deadlines).
