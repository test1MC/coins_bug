Responses

 

Responses are used to calculate target dates (start and completion) for a , and which days can be worked on the . Each major category on a can have several responses. When you create a , you select the appropriate response.

To set up responses:

  • Click the link for the major category you want to set up responses for.

  • On the Responses tab, click to set up a new response.

  • Fill in the response details. For example:

    • An EMERGENCY response might mean that must start within three hours and be complete within four hours, and be set up to allow working at weekends and on bank holidays.

    • A ROUTINE response might mean that must start within 10 days and be complete within 30 days.
