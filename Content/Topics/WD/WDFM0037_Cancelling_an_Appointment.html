Cancelling an Appointment

Once you have made an appointment, you cannot change the date or time-slot of the appointment. However, you can cancel the appointment.  When you cancel the appointment, automatically creates a duplicate task that is ready to be re-appointed.

To cancel an appointment:

  1. Open the in the Workbench.
  2. On the Tasks tab, click next to the task for which you want to cancel the appointment.
  3. On the Appointment Details tab, Cancel Appointment? and fill in the reasons for the cancellation.

  4. Click .

    5. creates a duplicate appointment, which you will need to make an appointment for.  The Workbench shows that the appointment for the original task has been cancelled, and shows the new task.
