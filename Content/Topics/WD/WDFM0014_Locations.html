Locations

 

Locations are the places where you will do work on a . In order to add a new , you must choose the location that the is for.

Each has its own list of locations. You may have a fixed list of locations that you set up at the start of the , or you may set up the locations when you add a new .

To set up a location on a contract:

  1. Open the contract in FM.
  2. On the Locations tab, add a new record.
  3. Fill in the address and other details of the location.
  4. Save the location record.
