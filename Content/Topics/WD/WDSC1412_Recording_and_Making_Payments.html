Recording and Making Payments

The procedure for processing subcontractor payments in is not significantly affected by the changes to the CIS scheme.  holds the two different rates of tax deduction in parameters, and applies them as appropriate dependent on the status of the subcontractor as recorded in subcontractor verification. 

You can make payments either through subcontractor certificates or through invoices with a element.  checks that the last payment date is within the past two tax years, and checks the verification status, to make sure that the subcontractor can be paid.  then calculates any necessary deduction according to the tax treatment of the subcontractor.
