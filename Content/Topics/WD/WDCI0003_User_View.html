User View

The Central Repository holds information about thousands of companies of different types (, subcontractors, customers, architects and so on). Normally you will only need to deal with a smaller number of companies, so the companies you see in the Companies can be restricted by your user view.  Your user view typically relates to the part of your organation in which you work.

You can change your view to see a higher level (for example, a wider range of companies). You cannot change to a level lower than your base view.

To change your user view:

  • Use the Level selector at the bottom of the workbench.

If there is information in the database that is not in your view, you can add it to your view.
