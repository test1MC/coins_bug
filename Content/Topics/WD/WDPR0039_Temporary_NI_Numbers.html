Temporary NI Numbers

As you may be aware the HMRC will no longer accept temporary NI numbers. still accepts them, but will not include the number on the employee's submission.

There has been discussion of introducing HMRC Personal References (IRPRs) to employees in the absence of a NI number. IRPRs have 2 digits, 1 alphabetic, 5 digits, such as 12A34567 format. These numbers are not valid for submission, though some tax offices still insist on issuing them as if they are a valid NI number. We expect the use of IRPRs will be clarified in the next year or so.
