Adding a Temporary Company

You may want to send to a company that is not recorded in the Company Information Workbench (for example if the client wants you to send to a subcontractor you have not dealt with). You can add the company as a temporary company; information about the company will be stored in the Company Information Workbench but will be specific to the current project and not visible elsewhere.

To add a temporary company:
