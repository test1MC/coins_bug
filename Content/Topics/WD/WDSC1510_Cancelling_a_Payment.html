eRCT - a Payment

When a is and not reissued, the Payment Notification(s) to Revenue need to be cancelled. 

To process payments:

    1. the payment in .

      This creates an entry in the Open Notified Certificates Workbench.

    2. If the payment was within the same Return period, either:
      • Cancel the notification manually on the ROS website, then record the cancellation of the Payment Notification using Open Notified Certificates Workbench.
      • Cancel the notification electronically in . See Unpaid Notified Certificates.
    3. If the payment was after the Return period, but before the Return due date, delete the line from the Return.

NOTE

  1. Any cancellation after the due date for the Return would constitute a late amendment to the Return and would incur a penalty.
  2. There are equivalent functions in for cancelling notifications for invoices: Open Notified Invoices Workbench and Cancel Notifications.
