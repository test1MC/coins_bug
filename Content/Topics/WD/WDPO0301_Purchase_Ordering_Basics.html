Purchase Ordering Basics

Purchase order processing includes:

  • Raising and printing purchase orders and orders.
  • Entering goods received notes and advice notes.
  • Regular allocations.
  • Matching invoices with purchase orders.
  • Reporting and at all stages of the process.
