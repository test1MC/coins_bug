Repeat Invoices - Editing Unposted Invoices

Generate Unposted Invoices

In order to generate unposted invoices so they can be edited before posting, use Generate Repeat Invoices as described in Generating and Posting Invoices except do not "Post Invoices".

The owner will receive email notification.

Edit Invoices

Once generated, the unposted invoices can be edited in either OA or COINSplus.

Choose Input | Invoice and Credit Note Entry from the menu.

Choose the Invoice Batch.

Choose lines.

Add a charge.

Post

Select the relevant batches and "Choose Action" to post.
