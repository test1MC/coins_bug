Adding New Records

Whenever you add a new company, or contact, checks the database for existing records that might be the same as the one you want to add. This avoids duplication and helps to ensure your database is consistent.

The process for adding a new record is similar, whether you are adding a company, or contact.

To add a new company, or contact record:

  1. Go to the relevant workbench.
  2. Click the New Company//Contact tab.
  3. Enter the basic details of the new record and click .

    If there are records that are similar to the one you want to enter, displays a list.

  4. Either:
    • If one of the records on the list is the same as the one you want to enter, click the link.

      opens the detailed record which you can then update.

      If the record you have opened is not the one you want, click the Internet Explorer Back button to return to the list.

    • If none of the records are the same as the one you want to enter, click .

      opens a new record with the details you entered already filled in.

      Fill in any additional details and click .
