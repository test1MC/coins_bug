Email Link

The Email Link is for emailing the link of a web page you are in to someone else (the receiver must have a username and password on the same system). This is useful for situations in which you want to show how something in works or if you just wanted to show someone how something looks.

To do this, move the mouse pointer over your profile picture, and in the User Menu click . This opens an email message containing the link, which you can send to the appropriate person. When the receiver clicks on the link and logs into when prompted, the exact web page you were in opens up.
