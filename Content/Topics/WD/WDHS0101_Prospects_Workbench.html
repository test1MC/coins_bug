Workbench

 

The Workbench allows you to record information about potential purchasers (that is, ); for example, the type of property they are interested in, their current property, and how they found out about you.

In addition, Workbench allows you to search for properties that match the 's requirements, and to keep a history of your contact with the .

The Workbench takes security into account, so you will only see the you have access to.   Navigate to House Sales > Prospects > Prospects Workbench

By Name Tab

Displays a list of people who are recorded as prospects.

By Development Tab

Shows a list of developments with a summary of the plots. You can dig deeper to display the prospects who have expressed an interest in a particular development.

My Prospects Tab

My Prospects displays a list of the prospects that are being actively monitored by the current logged-in user.

This allows for a small set of relevant records to be displayed on one screen so that users can more easily keep track of the records they are currently working with.

To Review Tab

The 'To Review' option allows you to review new prospect records that have come from external sources via the House Sales Connection interface.

For example, these details could have come from information submitted on your own website.

Add New Prospect Tab

Search for a Prospect allows you to add a new prospect to the House Sales > Prospects > Prospects Workbench. uses the initial information you enter to check whether the person (or people) has already been entered in the database.

Quick Add Tab

Prospect Quick Add allows you to quickly search for a person and add a new prospect record.
