Spend Analysis

The group spend analysis can inform your policy decisions by telling you who you have spent most with, and what you have spent most on, over the last three years.

The spend analysis is available within SRM Workbench:

  • To see who you spend with, use the Group Spend subtab on the Subcontractors and tabs.  These show the subcontractors and in your current view.
  • To see what you spend on, use the Spend subtab on the Trades and Materials tabs.

The show the spending with and subcontractors in your current view, across all your (that is, NOT just the amount you spend). The spend values are based on the , specifically, this is the value of goods (ex. ) on posted invoices (whether or not they have been costed) and the values from posted certificates.

You can sort the according to the value of spending in each of the three years shown, or the total spending over the three years.

Spend analysis configuration

To provide the data for these , you need to run Generate Data for Spend .  You can set this up to run as a scheduled function.  Since the is intended as a basis for setting policy rather than an up-to-the-minute , you may decide that you don't need to run it every day.

The Spend (Commodity) shows spending for commodities on the invoice.  Some invoices do not go through invoice matching, so they have no commodity information associated with them.  You can these under a "dummy" commodity code.  Set up the commodity code (using Materials) and set the SRM parameter COMMDIR to refer to this code.

Because Spend (Commodity) treats registered invoices according to the period in which they were registered, if an invoice was registered in the final period of one year and costed in the first period of the next, the invoice value will appear in the earlier year. You need to set up a commodity code for to show these values under, and set the SRM parameter COMMREG to refer to this code.
