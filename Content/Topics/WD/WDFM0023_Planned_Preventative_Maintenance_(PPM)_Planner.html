Planned Preventative Maintenance (PPM) Planner

FM will allow the PPM planner to:

  • Browse and maintain a location and asset register.
  • Group assets into categories and types.
  • Maintain a list of tasks and the time period that these tasks need to be repeated, against each asset sub category.
  • Automatically generate appointments for a period of time in advance. (These will be trade specific appointments but NOT specific appointments.)
  • It will be possible to review the prospective maintenance schedule before finally generating it.
  • Re-organe the appointments (using the Scheduler's tools).
  • Report to the client on the client's assets and the proposed maintenance schedule.
