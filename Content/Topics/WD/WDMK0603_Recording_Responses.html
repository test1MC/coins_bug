Recording Responses

You can see who has been invited to an event, and can see or record their response, in various ways:

  • The Invitations tab on a contact's details show which events you have invited the contact to.
  • The Invitations tab on a company's details show which events you have invited any contacts at that company to.
  • The Invitations tab on an event's details show anyone you have invited to the event.
