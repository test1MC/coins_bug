Logging Out of

 

When you have finished using , you should log out.  

NOTE

If you do not log out, will eventually log you out automatically.

To log out of

To log out of at any time: 

  1. Move the mouse pointer over your profile picture or name.
  2. On the user menu, click the Logout button .

This releases your user login record, and you are no longer counted as a user.

We recommend that you do not exit by clicking the in the top right corner of your browser. If you do this, you are deemed to be logged into until the automatic timeout feature takes effect (see Timed Logout).
