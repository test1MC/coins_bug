Union Reciprocity

  ***NOTE: THIS IS A US SPECIFIC TOPIC.***  

When clients are subject to collective bargaining agreements, they need to recognize Work Unions, or the union governing the , as well as the employee's Home Union. Union Reciprocity in Payroll helps to do this by allowing users to define which Union Calculations will apply when an employee's Home Union differs from his Work Union.

There are several factors to be considered if you want Union Reciprocity to work correctly in OA Payroll. These factors are discussed in some detail below.

Factors to consider when you want Union Reciprocity to work correctly:

Union Reciprocity Function

You can use the Union Reciprocity function to either add a new reciprocal agreement between two unions or to modify an existing agreement.

    1. In Payroll, go to Global Setup > US Specifics > Union Reciprocity.
    2. Enter the Home Union, Work Union, and Effective Date of the reciprocal agreement.

Profile tab in Employee Maintenance

The employee's Home Union is defined on the Profile tab of Employee Maintenance. You can also define the trade here and default it onto the Timecard. The trade can be overwritten during timecard entry if necessary.

Timesheets
