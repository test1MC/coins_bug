Performance Reviews

Performance reviews allow you to record assessments of how well subcontractors and have performed on individual orders. You enter the reviews against a or an individual office, and the list shows the average results of these reviews at a glance.

The Score shows your 's assessment of the or subcontractor. This is the average of the performance reviews for all offices of the company.  The Group Score shows the average assessment of all your .

From this you can dig deeper to see the scores for each year; you can display this as a table or as a graph. 

From the annual scores, you can dig deeper to display the detail of the reviews.

To record a performance review:

  1. Go to Companies.
  2. Go to the Company Summary Office Summary for the company or office for which you want to record the review.
  3. Click the Performance tab, then click the or Subcontractor tab, depending on whether the review relates to a purchase order or subcontract order.
  4. Set the Add Type drop-down to Normal Review and click .

    defaults in an order number of the last Material or Trade PO for the selected or Subcontractor.  To record a review for a different order, use the lookup to select it.

  5. Enter the details of the review: enter a score for each of the criteria that the company is being rated against.
  6. Click to save the review.

Performance review configuration

Use Performance Rating Codes to set up the scores and the descriptions for each score. You can determine whether scores are out of 5 or out of 10 - use CI/PERFBASE.

Use Subcontractor Performance Criteria and Performance Criteria to set up the performance criteria for subcontractors and for (that is, the things you want to score them against).

To configure the coding Performance Scores in the Company Workbench, use Data Display Ranges to set up the ranges for the code CIREVSCORE. Example

CIREVSCORE = 0,,1,perf30,39,perf60,59,perf100,100 If the score is 0, then there is no . If the score is between 1 and 39, the is Red (perf30). If the score is between 40 and 59, the is Amber (perf60). If the score is between 60 and 100, the is green (perf100).

You cannot separately configure the scores for and subcontractors, as in some places we combine the and subcontractor scores.
