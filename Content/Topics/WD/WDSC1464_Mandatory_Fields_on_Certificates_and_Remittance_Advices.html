Mandatory Fields on Certificates and Remittance Advices

The following list shows the statutory information you must provide to the subcontractor if you have deducted tax, and the fields to use.

Statutory Information Fields to Use
Contractor's own name coc_name
Contractor's employer's tax reference
(note this is NOT the contractor's UTR)		 coc_contref
The date of payment sbp_pdate
Subcontractor's name sbm_name
Subcontractor's unique taxpayer Reference number (UTR) sc7_certno  
  

Subcontractor's personal verification number
if the subcontractor could not be verified and a deduction at the higher rate has been made

sc7_ocertno

NOTE

You need to amend the format for sc7_ocertno from the default of 9 to 13, since 13 is the maximum needed to hold a verification number (matched or unmatched). 
The gross amount of the payments made to the subcontractor RO_grossPayment
The cost of any materials that has reduced the amount against which the deduction has been applied RO_materialsAmount
The amount of the deduction RO_taxDeducted
A clear indication if the statement is a duplicate You cannot currently indicate that a certificate or remittance advice in OA is a duplicate (this will be addressed in the February fix); however, you could produce a reprinted monthly Statement of Deduction, even if you are normally using per-payment statements.
