Sales Mail Shot

You can create mail merge letters to send to people on the Sales Workbench. You can select the people to send to according to a wide range of selection criteria.  You can produce the letters as Word documents, or as emails or SMS text messages.

To produce a mail shot:

  1. Go to New Sales Mail Shot.
  2. Select the Destination Media (whether to produce the mail shot as a Word document, email or SMS text message), and choose the template to use for the message.
  3. Set the selection criteria for the purchasers you want to include in the mail shot, and click .

    displays a list of purchasers that match the selection criteria you entered.

  4. You can manually remove purchasers from the list:
    • To remove a purchaser, click , un the Selected box, and click .
    • To remove several purchasers at the same time, select them, click and un the Selected box, then click .
  5. When you are happy with the list, click .
  6. Make any changes to the message (if you selected email or text).
  7. Enter a title for the mail shot history, and comments that will be included in the purchaser's history recrord.
  8. Click .

generates the mail shot.  For Word document mail shots, displays it in a new window, and you can save, edit or print the document.

Mailshot history

You can see details of the mail shots that have been sent, and the recipients, using View Sales Mail Shots.
