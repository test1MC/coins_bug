Printing the Body of the Certificate

In the body of the certificate, you may want detail lines showing this period values, previous values, and cumulative values for each of the following: gross certified, retention, discount, , and payment.

You do not need to define a separate line for each detail line you want to print.

To print the body of the certificate

  1. Add a body line containing the fields csd_desc (the line description), ctd_tp (this period value), ctd_prev (previous value) and ctd_cum (cumulative value):

    To enter the Rate on the line, use the field csd_rate. (The rate will not print if it is 0 or 100, or if the calculated value was over-ridden when the certificate was entered.)

  2. In the IDs line on the Document Designer Extras tab, enter the Item IDs for gross certified, retention, and so on (in other words, for each of the lines you want printed in the body).

    For example:

    IDs: GRC,CON,RET,DIS,VAT,PAY
