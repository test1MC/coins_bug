Adding a New Contact

Whenever you add a new contact, checks the database for existing contacts that might be the same as the one you want to add. This avoids duplication and helps to ensure your database is consistent.

To add a new contact record:

  1. Go to Contacts.
  2. Click the New Contact tab.
  3. Enter the basic details of the new contact and click .

    If there are contacts that are similar to the one you want to enter, displays a list.

  4. Either:
    • If one of the contacts on the list is the same as the one you want to enter, click the link.

      opens the contact details which you can then update.

      If the contact you have opened is not the one you want, click the Internet Explorer Back button to return to the list.

    • If none of the contacts are the same as the one you want to enter, click .

      opens a new record with the details you entered already filled in.

      Fill in any additional details and click .
