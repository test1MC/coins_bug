House Sales Overview

 

House Sales facilitates the management and maintenance of data relating to all stages of the sale of a house, from information, foot traffic data, and questionnaires, through to the management of monies and preparation of final accounts.

Key business processes supported:

  • Sales tracking.
  • Management of sales process from sales release through reservation to occupation.
  • Administration of cancellations.
  • Handling of receipt and processing of all client monies.
  • Tracking and processing of the buy-in and onward sale of part-exchange properties.
  • Monitoring of forecast to actual sales performance.
  • Processing of final accounts including all related financial transactions.
  • Handling of pre-release reservations, for example early birds.
