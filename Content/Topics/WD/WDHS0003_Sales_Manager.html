Sales

 

The Sales is primarily responsible for running a team of Site Sales Administrators, ensuring that target sales revenues are being achieved. In addition, he/she will make sure that a high standard of customer satisfaction is maintained at all times. Creating intelligent, understandable reports for the board is also one of the Sales 's key tasks, such as the Weekly Sales Activity and Forecast versus Actual Performance reports.

Within the House Sales module, the flexible reporting tool will enable the Sales to assess his/her team's performance by creating accurate reports with real-time information. The in-built authoration process will give him/her the ability to review and approve plot reservations. This can be done for all reservations or exclusively to a reservation that is outside of the Site Sales Administrator's limits. Furthermore, the Workflow functionality will help the Sales to fulfil his/her daily tasks efficiently and allow him/her to be proactive in the management of his/her department (for example, notification of reservations, cancellations and missed deadlines).
