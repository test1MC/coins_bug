Hazards

Against each , set up a list of standard hazards (actual or potential).

Against each location on a , record any actual hazards. These can be either hazards taken from the standard list, or hazards that are specific to that location.

Information about the hazards can be printed on the task sheets given to the .

To record standard hazards on a :

  1. Open the in .
  2. Select FM Setup tab.
  3. Select the Location Setup tab group then click click Hazards.
  4. Add details of the hazards.

To record location-specific hazards:

  1. Open the in .
  2. Click the Locations tab and click the link for the location.
  3. Click the Hazards tab.
  4. Add a new hazard record. Either:
    • Record a standard hazard at the location by entering the standard hazard code.
    • Enter a new hazard code if the hazard is not one from the standard list.
