Recording Occupation

When the purchaser occupies the property, you should record the fact in the Sales Workbench. 

To record occupation:

  1. Open the plot record.
  2. From the Options menu, click the option your system administrator has configured for "occupation".
  3. Enter the details of the occupation.
  4. Click .

Occupation - configuration

You need to set up an OCCUPY event; see Sales Events.
