Overview of CIS 2007

Changes to the Construction Industry Scheme come into force on 6th April 2007.  These topics explain what you need to do to use to operate the scheme.

For a summary of the scheme and for more details of configuring for the scheme, please refer to:

  • Overview of the Construction Industry Scheme 2007
  • How To Manage the Transition to CIS 2007

    These documents are available on the client area of the website.

  • Checklist: How to set up and configure to Operate CIS 2007

Operating the CIS 2007 scheme involves three main processes:

  1. Maintaining subcontractor CIS data (including verification)
  2. Processing subcontractor payments
  3. Month-end processing (including document printing)
