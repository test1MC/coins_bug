Creating an Expense Report

    Open Create New Expense Report.

    Enter a Report Title and Description.  (Note: depending on your company’s policies, CAS Expenses might fill in the report title automatically.)

    If your company’s polices allow it, you may be able to request that the expense claim is paid urgently (through ). If you want this and the option is available, Urgent payment required. Otherwise the claim will be paid through Payroll.

    There are three ways to add claim items to the report:

    Click Add Expense Item . This opens the Manage Expense Claim Item screen. The claim item will be added to the expense report.

    Right-click a line on the report and click Add. This opens the Manage Expense Claim Item screen. The claim item will be added to the expense report.

    Tick Show Unreported Items. This shows all your expense claim items that have not yet been added to a report. You can then the box in the Include Items column to add a claim item to the expense report. Alternatively you can click Include All to add all unreported claim items to the expense report.

    Note: You will only be able to add claim items that:

    include at least the minimum information: date, expense type and amount, plus any additional fields required for the Expense Type;

    have not broken any Error rules;

    have a Status of Draft or Prior Approval Given.

    Click Save Draft to save your changes.
