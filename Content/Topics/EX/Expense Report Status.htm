Expense Report Status

Expense reports can have one of the following statuses:

Are there any other statuses? I wonder if this section is necessary or whether they are obvious!

Draft

You are still working on the expense report and have not submitted it for approval.

Awaiting Approval

You have submitted the expense report but it has not yet been approved.

Rejected

The expense report has been rejected by an approver.

Awaiting Payment

The expense report has been approved and is awaiting payment.
