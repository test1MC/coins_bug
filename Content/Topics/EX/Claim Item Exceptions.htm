Claim Item Exceptions

Your company will have set up various rules about claim items, such as how much you can claim for different types of expense. If you save a claim item that breaks one of these rules, you will see a message at the top of the claim item screen. The message also appears on your Claim Items List, in the Policy Exception column. Note that a policy exception may be simply a notice for information, a warning or an error.

if a Claim Item breaks a Warning rule, information must be added in the Comments box before it can be added to an Expense Report.

If a Claim Item breaks an Error Rule, it can’t be added to an Expense Report.
