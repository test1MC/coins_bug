Withdrawing a Submitted Report

If you have submitted an expense report for approval, and then decide that you do not want the report to be processed (perhaps because you have made a mistake), you can withdraw the report, as long as it has not already been paid. You can also withdraw a report that has been rejected.

To withdraw an expense report:

    Open View My Expense Reports.

    Either:

    Right-click the report line and click Withdraw.

    View the report and click Withdraw.

    Confirm that you want to withdraw the report.

The report and all its claim items will be set back to draft status. You can then modify the report, and add, modify or delete claim items.
