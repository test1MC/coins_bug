Deadline Reminders

The deadlines for submitting expense reports are set by your company’s policies. CAS Expenses can email you automatically to remind you to submit your expense reports.

To set up automatic reminder emails:

    Click your user logo and click User Preferences .

    Click the My Settings tab.

    Click the Submission Reminder Email Notification Required box.

    Enter the number of days before the deadline that you want to be reminded.

    Click OK .
