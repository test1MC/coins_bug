OA Expenses - Introduction

Limited Expense processing functionality has been provided in OA, replacing functionality that was available in COINSplus. This covers:

  • Maintenance of bank account details (to cover those not paid through Payroll)
  • Input, validation and posting of claims.
  • Payment by or .
  • Payment posting.
  • Configuration and set up of claim items.

More extensive functionality is available in the new Construction Cloud  Expenses application, including rule-based validation of expense claims.
