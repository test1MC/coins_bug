Submitting an Expense Report

When your expense report contains all the necessary information and claim items, you can submit it for approval.

To submit an expense report:

    Open View My Expense Reports.

    Right-click the report and click Modify.

    Click Submit .

The report will move to the next stage: often this will be Awaiting Approval; however, depending on your company’s policies it may be approved automatically and go straight to Awaiting Payment.
