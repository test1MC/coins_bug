Expense Policies

Expense policies control how CAS Expenses treats claim items. Each claim item must have a policy associated with it.

Policies affect:

Who can enter claim items. Claim items can be available to all users in the company; users in specified locations or departments or with specified grades; or individual named users only.

How much claimants can claim for a specific expense type, and at what level warnings are issued.

How much approvers can approve, and the limits on automatic approval.

what validation is done.

What additional information is required when entering a claim for a given expense type.
