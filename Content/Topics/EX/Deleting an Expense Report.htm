Deleting an Expense Report

You can delete an expense report while it is still at Draft status. If you delete a report, you can choose to delete the claim items on the report, or delete only the report and keep the claim items as unreported items.

To delete an expense report:

    Open View My Expense Reports.

    Right-click the report and click Delete.

    If you want to delete the claim items as well, the box.

    Click Delete .

Once you have submitted an expense report for approval, you cannot delete it, but you can withdraw it (see "Withdrawing a Submitted Report" on page 1).
