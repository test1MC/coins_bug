Attaching Receipts to a Claim Item

In most cases you will need to provide scanned images of receipts to support your claim.

    On the Claim Item Detail tab, click Browse.

    Browse for the image of the receipt.

    Click Open

    Click Upload Receipt Picture .

You can add more than one image (for example, if you have a hotel receipt that goes over more than one page).

You can see the receipt image(s) on the Preview Receipts tab.

If the expense is subject to VAT, the VAT number on the receipt must be legible in order for your company to be able to reclaim the VAT. Tick “Can the tax information …” to confirm that the information can be read.
