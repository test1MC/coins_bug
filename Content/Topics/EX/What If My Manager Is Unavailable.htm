What If My Manager Is Unavailable?

If your manager is not available to review and approve your expense report, what happens will depend on your company’s policies and the specific circumstances, but in general there are a number of possibilities.

  • Your manager may have delegated the approval of expense reports to another staff member, in which case that person should deal with your report.
  • If your manager has not delegated approval and has not reviewed your report within a specified time, it will automatically be made available to other reviewers.
  • If your report has not been reviewed within a specified time, CAS Expenses may approve it automatically.
  • The CAS Expenses administrator may also be able to approve your expense report.
