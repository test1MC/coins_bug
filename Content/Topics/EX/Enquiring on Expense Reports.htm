Enquiring on Expense Reports

You can use the functions below to enquire about expense reports:

    My Employees Requests shows all active requests for your direct reports, whether you dealt with them or a delegate or pool member did. You can view the detail of a report, but not approve or reject it.

    My Employees Historical Reports shows any reports for which you were the approving manager that are Awaiting Payment or Paid.

    Find Historical Claim allows you to search all historical reports by entering part of the report name.
