Applications/Certificates

The Applications/Certificates is available on the > Applications/Certificates menu.

Adjusted Certificate Status

The Certificates was previously showing Part Paid as the status for Certificates that were adjusted (and therefore allocated to the zero-balance adjustment payment), but not allocated on any Cash Payment. It now shows an Adjusted instead of a Part Paid status if the Cash Payment does not exist, as shown in Figure 1: [%=Variables.Contract Sales%] Certificates [%=Variables.Enquiry caps%].

Adjusted Certificate Status

If a partial payment is entered (both an adjustment and a Cash Payment exist), the status changes to Part Paid.

Adjustment Certificate Status

The status of Adjusting Certificates with no actual payments processed was previously displayed as Paid MM/DD/YY. It now displays as Adjustment MM/DD/YY, as shown in Figure 2: Adjustment Certificate Status.
