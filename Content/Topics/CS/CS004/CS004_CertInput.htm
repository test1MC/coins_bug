Certificate Input

A Certificate summary screen is available when drilling down on the Certificate Reference link from either the or Certifications browse. If a payment is allocated to the Certificate, the Payments tab appears in the summary screen.

A new Adjustment column is available on the Payments tab, as shown in Figure 1: Certificate Payments Browse. This column appears if a Certificate has been adjusted and checked on payment line(s) coming from the adjustment Certificate(s).
