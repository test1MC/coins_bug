Entering and Allocating Cash

Cash Entry allows you to enter and post batches of transactions for cash received from customers.

Navigate to > Input > Cash Entry

Navigate to > Cash Reciepts > Cash Entry

Click to create a new batch.

 

Here you can enter or update details that relate to the cash entry batch.

Once the batch details have been filled out click

This will take you into the batch transaction tab, where you can add new transactions.

Click to add a new transaction into the batch.

You can now enter details of the individual payments you have received.

When you have cpmpleted the details, click the save icon.

Two allocation tabs will appear which allows you to allocate the payment to pay one or more of the customer's outstanding certificates.

The list shows outstanding certificates for the customer. The Unallocated field in the header shows the amount of the payment to be allocated.

Highlight the line that you wish to pay, select pay invoices from the dropdown and press the proceed arrow

This will update ‘Amount Paid’ and ‘Allocated’ amounts.

You may check the ‘Allocated’ Tab

 

Click to be taken back to the batch transaction entry tab.

Once the batch is complete and all transactions have been entered press the next icon.

Once you are satisfied the batch is complete post the batch.
