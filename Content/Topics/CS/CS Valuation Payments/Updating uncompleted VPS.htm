Updating uncompleted VPS (Certification)

The form below displays a VPS (Valuation 1) that has not been fully completed. We will add a new stage of Certify.

  • To add a certified stage, click
  • Navigate through the tabs and populate with the required information. You can enter expected value and the certified value.
  • You can look at Series to help you populate the forms correctly.

  • Once the certificate stage has been saved a ‘Distribution Tab’ will appear.

  • When the VPS stage has been completed it will be visible in the Valuation form.

  • Once you have the certified amount, this will enable the creation of a Certificate Batch and the printing of invoices where required.
