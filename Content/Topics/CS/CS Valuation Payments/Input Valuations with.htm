Input Valuations with Multiple Treatments

Navigate to > Input > Valuation Payment Stages

Navigate to > Valuations > Valuation Payment Stages

Enter the and Series that you wish to enter the valuation against. Use the next button to continue

Select the Valuation that you wish to enter and click to add the new stage (in this case Applied).

When updating the Main tab for the valuation stage select Multiple Lines from the Drop down

When you now click on the Details Tab you will be presented with a line for each code that has been configured.

 

Enter the Cumulative Gross amount in the top section of the page and then enter the breakdown of the Net Value this period against the various vat codes.   The total value in the Goods column must equal the Net This Period Figure.

 

Once you have made all of your entries click on the Comments Tab to add any comments and then click to save your entry and then click to  update the valuation and continue.
