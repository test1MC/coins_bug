Viewing and Entering VPS

This allows you to keep track of the different stages of a valuation cycle, from application to payment.

You can record the different values for each stage (for example, the amount you apply for, what you expect to get, the final certified amount). The stages are configurable.

It is a known behaviour that an additional application is made before the first application has been fully processed.  In addition the certified amount will vary from applied or expected amounts.  VPS does no financial posting until the penultimate stage is reached – usually certified/invoiced.

However data gathered prior to this stage can be used in various ways e.g. in and Cash-flow reporting.
