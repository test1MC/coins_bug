Updating Applied and Expected Amounts

Navigate to > Input > Valuation Payment Stages

Navigate to > Valuations > Valuation Payment Stages

Enter the contract and series numbers and click icon to continue

From the Valuation Summary page select the valuation you wish to amend by clicking on the underlined valuation number

Having selected the Valuation you wish to amend, click on the ‘Stages’ tab and click to update/amend either the Applied or Expected stages.  Once you have made your amendments, click to save your changes.
