Sundry Sales

  Sales that relate to non-contract activity and for contracts that require an invoice, these transactions are entered via the Sales Ledger.

 

To enter an invoice navigate to Sales Ledger > Input > Invoices & Credit Notes.

 

As with the other ledgers transactions are posted via batch so create your batch header.

Once complete click

 

Click

 

 

Selecting the Sales Ledger account from the dropdown will autocomplete the address

Change the transaction type as applicable.

On the “Analysis” dropdown select where you wish to allocate this sale to, the options are Contract if this sale relates to a contract and department if this is a non-contract invoice, and analysis will be to the GL .

Select the Contract/Sector as applicable.

Enter the customer reference, description, dates, and discount %.

The sales analysis is only used when analysing sales to plant items.

Leave “Multiple Lines” ticked, otherwise there will be no detail lines on your invoice.

You may enter any additional text in the “Text” tab

Once complete click

You will then be presented with the detail lines for completion.

The line number is displayed on the first detail line.

You only need to enter a product if you are selling a stock item.

Enter a description, quantity if applicable a value, the unit of measure and amend the VAT code as applicable.

Now click on the browse button alongside the Analysis box

Select the contract you wish to allocate the sale to, or if analysing to the GL the gl code, by clicking on the .

Once selected you will now get a list of costheads to allocate the sale to, sales are in section Z.

 

Filtered list showing only Z codes

Select the appropriate cost head and you will then be taken back to the line entry.

Save the line and enter all further lines as required.

 

Should you wish to allocate the revenue differently you may do so on the revenue distribution Tab, and any Credit Control notes can be added in the appropriate tab.

Once all lines are complete select Next at the bottom of the page, this will take you back to the batch input screen for you to add additional invoices

When all invoices have been entered, post the batch.
