Procurement Planning (24966)

The following enhancements have been made to the Project Summary screen within the Project Information Workbench:

  • Procurement Strategy Tab: Durations between events can be overridden on a project-by-project basis.
  • Event rules can be overridden on a package-by-package basis within a project.
  • Certain Package Planned and Forecast Dates are automatically calculated when an earlier event's date is entered.
  • A new selector at the foot of the Key Dates tabs allows you to show all Lead In days between events.
