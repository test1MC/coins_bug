MK GDPR in (25317)

New General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) come into effect in May 2018, for which companies involved in any direct marketing must be able to provide evidence that individuals have positively opted in to receive such marketing. The existing data protection fields in have therefore been enhanced to track such evidence. See OA-CE-MK022 for details.
