Recurring Invoices Ready to Post

PL Recurring Invoices Ready to Post allows you to flag recurring invoices as ready to post.

The browse shows all recurring invoices that are available to be posted.

Recurring invoices will not be available to post (and will not be shown) if:

  • The template of the recurring invoice is for a costed invoice and the cost distribution is missing, or there is an imbalance.
  • The template is a multi-lines invoice but no distribution has been entered.
  • The recurring invoice next period equals the last period and there exists a monthly invoice with a equal to the last/next period, then it can be assumed all monthly invoices have been posted.
  • An unposted monthly invoice for the same supplier exists.

To mark a recurring invoice type as ready to post, you can either select it and choose Ready to Post from the Choose Action list, or update it manually.
