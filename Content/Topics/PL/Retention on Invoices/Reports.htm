Retention on Invoices - Reports and

The A new Outstanding Retention Report shows details of material orders with retention which is outstanding.

The following reports also include a column to show retention:

  • Combined Reports (the existing Retention column is used)
  • PL Invoice Posting Report
  • PL Invoice Register Report
  • PL Withheld Invoices Report
  • PL Transaction History Report
  • PL Invoices Inside/Outside Tolerance Report

The GL Controls Accounts Report has been updated to show shows the PL Retention control account.

The following PL have been amended to show retention:

  • PL Invoices
  • PL Suppliers
  • PL Payments
