Retention on Invoices - Invoice Entry

New fields Fields on the Invoice Entry screen allow you to enter the retention for the invoice.

Fields

Field
Description

Retention %

The retention percentage to apply to this invoice. This defaults from the percentage on the order.

The field to the right of this shows the calculated retention amount.

Retention is only calculated and updated if a committed order that has been set up with a retention percentage is entered. Retention cannot be entered if an unposted invoice with retention already exists for the order.

Retention is calculated using the retention percentage and taking into account PL parameter CALCRET. For example on an invoice with the following amounts and a retention of 10%:

Merchandise $100.00
Freight (Additional Charge1) $20.00
Handling (Additional Charge 2) $10.00
Insurance (Additional Charge 3) $5.00
Subtotal $135.00
Sales Tax (7% on Merchandise and Freight) $8.40

If CALCRET is blank, the retention will be $10.00 (10% * 100).

If CALCRET = A1,A2,A3,T the retention will be $14.34 (10% * 100+20+10+5+8.40).

If CALCRET = A1 the retention will be $12.00 (10% * 100+20)

If the retention (calculated or entered) on the invoice, or the retention on the invoice combined with other invoices for the same order, is greater than the retention available, an error is displayed.

Discount calculation takes retention into account, based on merchandise + additional charges – retention. For example: ($100 + $20 + $10 + $5 - $14.34) * 0.1 = $12.06.

The invoice entry browse now shows a Retention column.
