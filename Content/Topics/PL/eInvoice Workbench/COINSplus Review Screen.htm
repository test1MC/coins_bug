It is possible to hold back or retain an invoice in the workbench. These held invoices cannot be moved to the .  Held invoices are flagged with H on the right-hand side of the Review screen and the label of the button changes according to the action that can be performed on the invoice from Hold to Off Hold and vice versa. Pressing CTRL-H takes all invoices off hold. There are two parameters that allow a whole selection of invoices to be put on hold based on the invoice value as they come into the workbench

The parameters EC/ LOVALINV and EC/ LOVALHLD can be set such that all invoices below or above a certain value can be put on hold. Set LOVALINV to the value to be used, typically 200; set LOVALHLD to LOW or HIGH to indicate whether the check should be greater than or less than.
