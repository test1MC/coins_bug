HOW TO Change PINV to PINN

This appendix describes how the eInvoice workbench processes PL transactions, invoices and credit notes that have a Purchase Order number in the XML message and would normally be treated as a “PINV” transaction but for business reasons should be treated as a “PINN” transaction. Typically it may be that the contractor uses two procurement systems, and another, so that some PO numbers, although valid, are unknown to . The relevant EC, PL and PO parameters listed are taken into account. Further configuration is required to enable this functionality.

The COINSetc Configuration Guide and COINSetc Invoice Workbench User Guide contain further relevant information.
