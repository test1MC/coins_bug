Part matching

The workbench will also attempt to part match Purchase Orders to invoices. That is to say, in general, a line on an invoice will part match the order the invoice refers to if there is an order line matching where the outstanding quantity is greater or equal to the invoice quantity. A part match might not be achieved if the ETOLAMT parameter is set.

The following examples show where part matches can be expected.

Scenario 1:

We have a PO with two lines and an Invoice with two identical lines in the same order. Each PL line price differs from the PO price by no more than ETOLPRC. We expect each line of the PL to match the PO. The PO will be marked as “complete”.
