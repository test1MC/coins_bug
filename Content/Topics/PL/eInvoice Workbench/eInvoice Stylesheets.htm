eInvoice Stylesheets

Every XML message can be seen on the workbench in a readable format by pressing CTRL-V or the View button. The XML is made readable by use of an XSLT Stylesheet. A standard stylesheet is used to render this image in a web browser. This stylesheet may be changed, just as a Configurable Report is changed to suit your needs.
