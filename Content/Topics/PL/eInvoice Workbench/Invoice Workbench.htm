Electronic Invoice Workbench - Load and Pending Screen

 

The screen shows the messages that have been sent by suppliers and routed to the relevant Company messages newly arrived that have not been reviewed are shown in a bold font. Selecting a batch, choosing the Load action checks each invoice in the batch for exceptions and opens the Review Screen, the second page of the workbench, where the batch of individual invoices that are carried by any one message can be seen and can be processed. The column labelled Txns shows how many invoices are contained in the message.

Messages can be rejected at any stage by selecting a batch and choosing the Reject action. Rejected messages will not be passed into the workbench.

The original batch of invoices in XML format can be seen by pressing the button in the View column.
