Assemblies

assemblies allow you to combine items as components that can be issued for on-site assembly or assembled to create a different item. The assemblies can be ordered as quantities of a single item, then issued and charged either as a whole or as individual components.

For example:

  • Simple issue of the components required for on-site assembly, so a length of single-sided 6.5 foot guardrail can be requested, and all the parts required calculated and issued automatically.
  • Assembly of components to create a different item. An item like a stop sign could be held in or assembled from other items (pole, supports, bolts, and stop sign panel).
  • Concrete or tarmac/asphalt, where the constituents are held separately and only combined when required.
  • Items that can be split; for example you may issue safety goggles in boxed quantity or individually.
