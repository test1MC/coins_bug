Overview

 

Performing a is usually done as a periodical process to update current levels and value in an organation. The process often precludes the financial year-end process. For example, where the financial year ends on December 31 - the would be done in late November. A full or part may also be necessary in order to resolve any stocking issues that arise throughout the year. It is worth noting that adjustments to amounts can also be done per stock item via the standard Transaction type - ‘A- Adjustment’.
