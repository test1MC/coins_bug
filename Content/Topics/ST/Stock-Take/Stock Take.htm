Stock Take Process

Navigate to Stock > Input > Stock Take Entry and click on the "Add" button to create a new batch

 

 

Enter which you will be accounting in, a Description, and which Stock Location you will be doing the Stock Take for, and click on the "Save" button

 

 

In the top right-hand corner click on "Options" and then click on "Generate Stock Take Transactions"

 

 

Enter if you want to do a "Random" count, or if you want to "Skip unused items";

 

 

Or if there is a particular Stock Group or Stock Item you want to account for enter it in the "Stock Selection" tab. Once completed click on the "Next" arrow button

 

 

This then starts to generate a list of Stock Items and their current counts, and the number will increase until it completes this process

 

 

 

Thereafter click on the Batch Number

 

 

 

You can now print the Stock Take Sheet to hand to the Yardsperson who will count all of the Stock Items in this list

 

 

You'd then need to decide on whether you want the Yardsperson to know how much has counted, or if you want them to do a count not knowing what is expecting as a count.

Tick the "Include On-Hand Stock?" box if you want them to know, or un it if you don't.

 

Then choose a "Sort by" option and click on the "Next" button

 

 

In Report Status, print/email the PDF/Excel output and provide it to the Yardsperson

 

 

 

 

Once the count has been completed and handed back to you, you can enter it in bulk using "Bulk" and "Open"

 

 

 

 

 

Amend the "Count" Column F with the figures provided by the Yardsperson

 

 

Copy all of the data and paste it into

 

 

Please note that if the count column is left empty for Stock Items and the batch is posted, understands this to mean zero and as such, will automatically do an adjustment (if required) to bring the count to zero

 

The Count has now been updated

 

 

Once completed it is good practise to run a "Posting/Listing Report" to review what transactions are going to occur as a result of the Stock Take

 

 

Finally post the batch and review the "GL Cost Posting Report" and the "Stock Take" Report.
