Entry- Bulk Adjustments

 

If a large amount of adjustments are required then it may be more efficient to do a bulk update.

To do this a Bulk export of the selection is required to amend and then upload back into .

 

Selecting the items for Export

Everything can be exported by simply clicking on the Bulk box and the Open icon (see next section). However it is likely that you may want to update smaller groups of items in separate batches.

It is important to note that the export function will dump whatever selection has been made in , for example if a group of items has been selected then only those items will be exported.

You can choose specific items by ticking the Select tick box adjacent. Holding down the ‘ctrl’ key, whilst clicking the items, allows for specific groups to be selected.
