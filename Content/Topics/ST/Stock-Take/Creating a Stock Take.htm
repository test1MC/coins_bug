Once the generation occurs a prompt will appear asking for selection parameters. There are 3 tabs.

 

Main Tab

 

Field

Description
Random 1 in 1 Random or full - The ‘Random 1 in 1’ field determines the ratio of items to perform on. This is useful where large inventories exist and a complete is not feasible. Enter a number ‘1’ to perform a complete stock take on all items. A higher number entered would create a random smaller selection, so for example, entering the number 20 performs a ‘one-in-twenty’ selection; effectively 5% of the items.
Skip Unused Items Whether to exclude items from the in cases where they have never been recorded at the selected location or to include any items, even if the balance has always been zero.

 

Selection Tab

 

The Selection tab allows you to specify which items to include. Several options are available. For example an inclusive range could be entered in the ‘From’ and ‘To’ selections, or a specific group of items could be targeted.
