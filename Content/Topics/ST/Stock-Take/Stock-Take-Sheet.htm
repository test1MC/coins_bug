Stock Take Sheet

 

The Stock Take Sheet is provided to check-off the stock items against.

The report is initially generated by the Stock Take Batch routine. It lists quantity on hand at each location, and then shows adjustments made to the stock quantities through the Stock > Input > Stock Take Entry routine.

When creating the sheet in options to format it are presented.

To create a Stock Take Sheet select it from the ‘Input’ menu folder.

 

 

Click the link of the batch number to access the Stock Sheet Option

 

 

Include Items On Hand? allows the selection/Deselection of the Items Quantity on-hand values (from the database) on the Stock Sheet.

In addition the Sort order can be decided. Bin Number (if used) can be selected or the Stock code.

Click to generate the Stock Take Sheet.

The Stock Take Sheet acts like any other report in in that it is output to the Report Status screen - allowing a PDF or spreadsheet version to be obtained.

The Excel version is more versatile as you can manipulate the output (adding columns etc).
