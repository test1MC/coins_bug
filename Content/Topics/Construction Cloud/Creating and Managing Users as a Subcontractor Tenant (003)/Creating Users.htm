Creating Users as a Subcontractor Tenant

Steps for the Administrator

In the Administration app, under User Administration, click Create User. This opens the Create User screen.

Enter the details of the new user. You must enter something for every field and the email must be a valid address.

Click to create the new user. You will receive a confirmation message at the bottom of the screen.

Steps for the New User

Once your administrator has completed the process to create your account, open your email. You should have received an email titled Welcome to COINS Construction Cloud, requesting you to activate your account.

Click . This will open a web browser and take you to the Activate User Account page.

 

Enter the password you wish to use. It must satisfy all four of the listed criteria.

Click to activate your account.

 

If more than twenty-four hours have passed since the email was sent, the activation link will have expired. If this is the case, there are two ways you can still activate your account:
1. Contact your administrator, who can send you a new activation email, then follow the steps above
2. Activate your account through the Reset Password process

To activate your account via Reset Password:

Open the login page for COINS Construction Cloud and click Forgotten Password? at the bottom of the window.

This opens the Reset Password window.

Enter the username you were given in the activation email then click . You should be shown a message confirming that an email has been sent to your address containing a reset password link.

Open your email and find the email titled COINS Construction Cloud Password Reset. This email will contain a link from which you can set your password and activate your account.

Click . This will open a web browser and take you to the Set Password screen. Note that the link in the email expires after fifteen minutes – if the link has already expired at this point you must repeat the above steps.

Enter the password you wish to use. It must satisfy all four of the listed criteria.

Click to save your password. Your account is now activated and you will be able to log in.
