Copying and Pasting

You can copy and paste values from one cell to another:

  1. Right-click the cell you want to copy and choose Copy from the menu.
  2. Right-click the cell where you want to paste the value, and choose Paste from the menu.

To copy the values from several cells at the same time:

  1. Click the first cell and drag the mouse pointer across or down to highlight the cells.
  2. Right-click and choose Copy.
  3. Right-click the first cell you want to paste into, and choose Paste.
