Totals

You can show totals for the columns and rows.

Click the Total button .

A totals column in the frozen columns area shows the totals for each row, and a totals row at the top of the matrix (below the column headers) shows the totals for each column.

Total Rows and Columns

The Total button turns yellow to show that totals are being displayed.

If you have filters applied (see 1 Filters), the totals only show the totals of the filtered cells.
