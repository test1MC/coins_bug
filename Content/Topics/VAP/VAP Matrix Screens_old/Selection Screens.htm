Selection Screens

Many of the new matrix screens are available from new workbench selection screens, rather than having separate menu items for each matrix screen.

Development Budget Workbench allows you to see all available budgets on all your developments, with -coding to identify the current status of each available (empty, unlocked or locked) . From here you can select an individual budget. The budget screen includes two tabs, one for the Development House Type Budget and one for the Development Plot Budget.
