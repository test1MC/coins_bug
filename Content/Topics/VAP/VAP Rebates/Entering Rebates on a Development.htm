Entering Rebates on a Development

 

Add rebates from the standard list of rebates, to the development:

Click into the rebate to see the plots, how the rebate is applied and to amend any values.

 

 

The Rebates Matrix Tab will show the Plots / Rebate types and amounts, along with the status of the rebate for that plot. Values can be edited here too.

 

Using the same Development Rebates menu option, you can view which rebates are ready to claim and prepare rebates for invoicing.

For any plot/rebate combination, will display if the appropriate construction stage has been reached and whether the rebate for that combination has been claimed.  Stages that are ready to claim but have not yet been, are highlighted in red.

    means that the required stage in Complete, but the rebate is Unclaimed

means that the required stage has is incomplete and the rebate is Unclaimed.

means that the stage is complete and the amount has been claimed.

 

Using the actions bar, you can add/delete and maintain rebates and rebate values.
