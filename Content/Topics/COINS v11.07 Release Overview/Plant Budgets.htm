Budgets (23487)

Budgets have been improved to make them easier to use:

  • Group budgets can be specified, which apply to all the group's assets without an override. Assets now use the group budget by default, but can instead have their own budget.
  • To make maintenance simpler values can be entered as an annual value by , and spread evenly over the periods.
  • The load routines for assets and groups now take budget data.
  • Copy Last Year's Asset has been extended to include budgets held on groups.
