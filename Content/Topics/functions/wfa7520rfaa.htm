Fixed Asset Costs Load loads asset costs (such as fuel card costs and phone bills), and automates the charging of the costs.

More...

The costs can be loaded from CSV files, and can be marked up to cover administration costs.

Costs are charged on to the of the employees who have been allocated the asset. If the asset was not allocated to a person, then the customer or the asset was dispatched to will be charged.
Confuguration

Use Cost Load File Configuration to specify the contents of load files and associate them with different file extensions.
