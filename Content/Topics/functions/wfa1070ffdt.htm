Post Asset Department Transfers allows you to create and post an asset department transfer batch.
More...

If you transfer an asset from one department to another that has different General Ledger accounts for any of the B/S Capital, B/S Depreciation, Revaluation or Revaluation Reserve accounts, creates a transfer transaction, which you need to post to update the General Ledger.

You should post all unposted transfers before you run the Calculate Depreciation procedure. You can use Unposted Transfers to on unposted transfers.
