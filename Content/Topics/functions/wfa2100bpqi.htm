Allocation shows the allocation of the asset to people in the HR system. HR allocation has no effect on the availability of the asset within . It can be used in automatic cost distribution linking the asset to an employee via the HR record.

More...

Pay Costed Charge Calculate allows you to charge based on payroll timesheet data. This can be used for assets like company cars and vans.

Fixed Asset Costs Load allows you to load costs like fuel card charges and mobile phone bills and charge based on payroll timesheet data of the allocated person.
