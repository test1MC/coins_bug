Profile Switching

 

Any subcontractor included in the iPortal database may have a third-party data provider feeding data automatically to update the subcontractor’s details in the iPortal database; the subcontractor may also update their details direct in the iPortal. Each such record is termed a Subcontractor Profile. A subcontractor may have a number of Profiles each linked with a different data-provider and/or a Direct Profile. Profile Switching applies only when a subcontractor has more than one Profile in the iPortal database.

 

Profile Links with OA

Only one OA Environment may be linked to the iPortal and for each subcontractor only one Profile may be linked to any one record in the Company Information Workbench. As data changes are received in the iPortal from data-providers, or data is changed for direct profiles, a corresponding data change is triggered in OA.

 

It may be that a subcontractor who had previously entered data direct to the iPortal signs up with a data-provider to share their details; or a subcontractor who had been registered with one data-provider decides to move to another data-provider. The Profile Switching option allows the contractor to change the iPortal record associated with their CIW record.
