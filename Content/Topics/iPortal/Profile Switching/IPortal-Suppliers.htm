IPortal (Suppliers)

 

The iPortal is designed to manage and maintain vendor (subcontractor and supplier) information.

This allows you to improve your relationship with existing and prospective Contractors by aligning to their new projects and performing objective assessments of both the contracted work and the relationship between you and the Contractor.

 

By maintaining your data and relationship through the iPortal, you reduce your administrative overheads while raising your profile

 

Benefits of registering:

Associate your iPortal account with an existing profile on a 3rd party data exchange such as Constructionline or Builder's Profile, saving time and effort by maintaining a single profile.

Create a dedicated profile in iPortal, where you will enter and keep all your company details up to date.

Pursue approved vendor status by completing targeted questionnaires and online PQQ assessments from participating contractors.

Check your current status with a chosen contractor at any time.
