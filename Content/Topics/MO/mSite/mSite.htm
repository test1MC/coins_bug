mSite

mSite allows site managers to quickly and easily update the construction stages of multiple plots, saving time and effort and providing real time information to managers back at head office.

By using a mobile application on a smartphone or tablet, you can link your construction site managers directly to the back-end System.

Make it easy for busy site managers to keep plot status information accurate and up-to-date. Its easy to use interface is ideal for use by field based construction staff without the need for in-depth IT training.

Easily upload relevant information in a variety of formats - site information including photos, images and notes can be attached to each construction stage of each plot.

Implementing mSite significantly improves efficiency which reduces overheads and can improve competitiveness. Positive results for your management team – and your customers.

mSite gives you complete visibility of your plot progress as well as more efficiency on-site and reduced administration overheads at head office. Site managers and office-based commercial managers can focus on their core role rather than dealing with paperwork or re-keying information.
