Repairs - On Route to Site

From the "On Route to Site" screen, if the has no access then they can log this on the mCare application by selecting "No Access".

Tapping on "No Access" will take the user to the No Access screen where they must enter notes as this is a mandatory field.

After the notes have been entered the will be prompted to confirm their action, Selecting "No" will show the "No Access" screen and selecting "Yes" will send the

this back to and the visit will be removed from the "Visit List" screen.

Tapping on the navigation icon for the visit, then the application will then launch the relevant default application on the device to complete this action with . When the is at their destination and selects "On Site"

application will launch the "Health And Safety" screen.

 

Repairs - Health And Safety

When the selects "On Site" from the "On Route to Site" screen they will be shown the "Health Ans Safety" form (if set up and configured to do so).

If the has not passed the Health And Safety form, then they will be alerted that they have failed the Heath and safety survey and will not be able to proceed.

The can either tap "Yes" which will close the task from the device or the operative can select "No" which will allow them to return to the survey to amend any

details that may have been entered incorrectly.

 

Once the has passed the survey, they will be able to proceed to the "Actions" screen.

Repairs - Actions - NOTES

Swiping left or tapping onto the "NOTES" tab will take the to the "NOTES" screen

Notes are not mandatory

Tapping onto "Visit Notes" the application will launch the onscreen keyboard and text field allowing the to manually enter their notes that will be saved against the visit.

 

Tapping "Cancel" the application will remove any notes that were entered, tapping "OK" will save the notes entered against the visit.

 

Repairs - Actions - SIGN

Swiping left from the "NOTES" screen will take the to the "SIGN" screen where the signature for the visit can be obtained.

Tapping on the green add (+) button will load the "Signature Capture" screen. The user can select a signatory from the drop down list which will display a list and obtain a signature

 

Tapping on the signature field, the will be able to capture a signature, After a signature has been captures the operative can also do the following:

  • Cancel - Will disregard the signature that was captures
  • Clear - Remove the signature allowing the to re-capture a signature
  • OK - Saves the signature that has been captured

 

Once the signature has been captured and the taps "SAVE", the application will prompt the operative to answer a few questions.

If the selects "No", the application will go to the "SIGN" screen where the signatures captured will be listed. However, if the operative selects "Yes" then the application will display the

"Survey" for the to complete.

 

Repairs - Actions - Survey

The questionnaire screen will display all questionnaire questions that have been set in .

 

If any mandatory questions have not been answered then the application will prompt the .

Once the user taps ‘OK’ they will be taken to the Visit Signatures screen.

 

Repairs - Actions - COMPLETE

Following the "SIGN" screen if the user swipes left the will be take to the "COMPLETE" screen. Similar to the Inspections, this is the final stage of the repairs task for the visit.

This screen will show the following:

  • On Site date and time (in blue)
  • Non mandatory warnings (in Orange)
  • Mandatory issue/actions that need to be complete

 

Tapping on the errors will take the to the screens where they can complete the mandatory sections. Once this has been done and the user returns to the "COMPLETE" tab,

they will now see the on site date and time along with the completed date and time

The will also be able to see a floating green submit button at the bottom right of the screen. Tapping on this will send the visit data back to and will be removed from the Visit List.
