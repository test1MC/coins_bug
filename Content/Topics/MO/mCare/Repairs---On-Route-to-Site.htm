Repairs - On Route to Site

From the "On Route to Site" screen, if the has no access then they can log this on the mCare application by selecting "No Access".

Tapping on "No Access" will take the user to the No Access screen where they must enter notes, as this is a mandatory field.

After the notes have been entered the will be prompted to confirm their action, Selecting "No" will show the "No Access" screen and selecting "Yes" will send the back to and the visit will be removed from the "Visit List" screen.

Tapping on the navigation icon for the visit, will prompt mCare to launch the default navigation app used by the device. When the is at their destination and selects "On Site", the application will launch the "Health And Safety" screen.
