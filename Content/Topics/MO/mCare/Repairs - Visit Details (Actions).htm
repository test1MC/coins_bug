Repairs - Visit Details Notes

Swiping left or tapping onto the "NOTES" tab will take the to the "NOTES" screen

Notes are not mandatory

Tapping onto "Visit Notes" the application will launch the onscreen keyboard and text field allowing the to manually enter their notes that will be saved against the visit.

 

Tapping "Cancel" the application will remove any notes that were entered, tapping "OK" will save the notes entered against the visit.
