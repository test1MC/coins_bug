No access (Repairs Task)

From the "On Route to Site" screen, if the has no access then they can log this on the mCare application by selecting "No Access".

Tapping on "No Access" will take the user to the No Access screen where they must enter notes as this is a mandatory field.

After the notes have been entered the will be prompted to confirm their action, Selecting "No" will show the "No Access" screen and selecting "Yes" will send the

this back to and the visit will be removed from the "Visit List" screen.
