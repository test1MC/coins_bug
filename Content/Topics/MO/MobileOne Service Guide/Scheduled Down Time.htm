Scheduled Down-Time

In order to improve the quality of service, is entitled to perform maintenance and upgrades of its platform.   will take reasonable steps to avoid unnecessary scheduled down-time and, wherever possible, schedule down-time when it is least disruptive to the Service e.g. overnight or weekends.   will provide written notice of scheduled down-time according to the following table:

 

Duration of down-time

Advance notice

> 60 minutes

7 days

> 15 – 60 minutes

2 days

0 -  15 minutes

1 day

 

For downtime in excess of 60 minutes, will attempt to reschedule the downtime in the event that a Customer has a specific and reasonable requirement to avoid the date specified by , provided the Customer notifies within 48 hours of issuing the written notice.

 

Where fail to provide sufficient notice, the down-time will count as unplanned down-time in the above calculations.
