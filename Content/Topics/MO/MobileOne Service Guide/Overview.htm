Overview

The M1x platform extends the functionality of customers’ back-end systems out to their mobile workers using a variety of mobile devices.  provide a managed service to support the operation of the platform and mobile-application:

As the overall application seen by the mobile worker crosses responsibility boundaries, the following definitions will be used in this document:

  • application – the functionality that the mobile worker accesses and that operates end-to-end through the mobile device, the contracted mobile network, the M1x platform, the integration to the customer environment and the functionality of a customer’s back-end systems.
  • Mobile Application – that part of the application that is developed-for and hosted-on the M1x platform.
  • managed service – the services to monitor, maintain and support the operation of the Mobile Application.

In using the managed service, the customer understands and recognises that the operation of the application involves elements that are not provided or managed by and that the overall service is dependant on factors outside of ' control e.g. the availability of GPRS/3G/4G/HSDPA network operator, functionality or limitations of the chosen mobile device, connectivity to and the availability/functionality of the customer’s systems.
