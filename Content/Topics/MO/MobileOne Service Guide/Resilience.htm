Resilience

The M1x platform is designed to have no single point of failure.  In the event of failure of a component, then will either have arrangements for next day delivery of a replacement or maintain at least one spare on site in its data-centre.

The Mobile Application running on the platform is typically designed with a loosely coupled architecture that provides resilience to network failures between the mobile device and the M1x platform and between the M1x platform and the customer’s back-end systems.  For more details refer to the functional specification for the mobile-application.  

The platform is hosted in a secure data-centre that provides environment monitoring, climate control, fire protection systems and 24 hour emergency power.

Any persistent data (e.g. user ids) necessary for the operation of the platform and mobile-application is backed-up daily and stored off-site.  Unless otherwise stated in the service agreement or functional specification, have no responsibility for maintaining, archiving or otherwise securing transient data for the mobile-application that is more than 5 days old.
