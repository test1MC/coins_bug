Problems may also be identified and raised by in which case the customer is informed of issues affecting their service.

In the event that monitoring identifies a P1, P2 issue with the M1x platform or the customer’s mobile-application, then an email alert will be sent within the A1 times listed above and then the issue will be treated as though reported by the customer.

Issues with the Customer Managed Environment

In the event that monitoring identifies an issue that is the customer’s responsibility, then will notify this to the customer using the same categorisation.   These problems will be logged but not count towards any of targets and expect the customer to update on the status of the issue through to resolution.  

require named points and methods of contact to raise these issues to the customer within the hours of service.   These names will be identified as part of service handover.

If the problem is not resolved within a reasonable time period and in opinion, the problem is jeopardising the overall M1x platform performance, then may suspend the customer’s Managed Service until the problem is resolved – will give written notice of this and make reasonable attempts to notify the customer in advance.
