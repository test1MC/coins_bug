Capacity Management

The M1x platform is multi-tenanted with shared capacity to provide efficient platform management and load management.

monitor the production M1x platform and maintain it to run within capacity targets for disk space, memory, cpu, network bandwidth to keep the platform operating with reasonable headroom for spikes in demand.   

If the platform is exceeds a capacity target for more than 2 hours in a day for at least 10 days (not necessarily consecutive) in a month, then will plan and implement a strategy to bring the platform back within its monitoring thresholds.  This may involve but is not be limited to:

  • Additional hardware.
  • Platform optimisation.
  • Application optimisation.
  • Increased Bandwidth.
