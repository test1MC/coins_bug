Standard

The standard option is inherent for all Customers and involves commissioning a new data centre with a target of 5 business days to restore service.

To this end, maintain a disaster recovery plan for reinstating a new data centre that is reviewed every 6 months and includes:

  • Review and update of kit list of all hardware required with verification it is available from at least two suppliers.
  • Audit that that latest versions of standard third party software is secured, available and licensed.
  • Identification of alternate hosting site and availability.
  • Audit that back-up/version repositories can re-instate the latest version of the M1x platform and all mobile-application.
  • Inventory of all connectivity that would need to be reinstated with customers.
  • Review of check-list to reinstate data-centre.
