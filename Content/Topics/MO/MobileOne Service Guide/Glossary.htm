Description

Application

The application that the mobile worker accesses that operates through the mobile device, the contracted mobile network, the M1x platform, the integration to the customer environment and the functionality of the customer back-end systems.

Authorised Representative

Nominated contacts in the customer who are authorised by the customer to raise issues and/or service requests. 

Business Day

Monday to Friday excluding English public holidays.

BST

British Standard Time

Customer

The customer (defined by the service agreement) that is contracted to use the managed service

Mobile Application

That part of the application which is developed for and hosted on the M1x platform and runs on the mobile worker’s Device.

Managed Service

The operation and monitoring of the Mobile Application as far as it directly under the control of .

Mobile Worker

The end-user who accesses the application through a Mobile device.

Service Agreement

The contract between and the Customer.

Transient Data

Data that transits through the M1x platform but that belongs to and is the responsibility of the customer.  This includes but is not limited to job details, images, signatures and time recording.
