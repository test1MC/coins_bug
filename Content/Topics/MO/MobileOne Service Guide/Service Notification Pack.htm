Service Notification Pack

At the point of hand-over, the Mobile Application is ready for long-term operation and support under the managed service, will issue a Service Notification pack to the customer to confirm:

  • The formal start of managed services and associated target reporting.
  • The scope of the service.
  • contact points for the service
  • Customer’s authorised representatives that can use the service and act as escalation points.
  • A copy of the latest version of this guide

The criteria used for hand-over of the application onto the platform and for issuing the service notification include but are not limited to:

  • Application has passed acceptance tests.
  • The Mobile Application documentation is up to date and available.
  • The application is in successful operation with a pilot group of mobile workers.
  • Appropriate contacts, escalation and provisions are in place for support of the application by the customer.
