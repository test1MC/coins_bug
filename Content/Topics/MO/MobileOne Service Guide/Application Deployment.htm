Application Deployment

Before the managed service can be delivered, the Mobile Application must be deployed on the production instance of M1x platform.  The services to define, develop, integrate and trial the Mobile Application are covered in other documents (e.g. Agreement, functional specification, project plan etc) and undertaken by professional services team or third-party developers and using non-production instances of the M1x platform.

Once accepted, the Mobile Application is ready for deployment on the production instance of M1x platform and the managed service will commence as defined in the Agreement.   However, it is normal for special arrangements to be in place during initial roll-out/pilot whereby the project team are actively involved for a period before the client is fully handed-over into the managed service.
