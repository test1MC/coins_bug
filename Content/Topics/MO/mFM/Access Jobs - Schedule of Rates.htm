Access Jobs - Schedule of Rates

When user acknowledges they are on site, they will then be displayed the list of SORs associated to the task initially.

They can edit them to increase the quantity by tapping the pen icon.

They can delete them by tapping the X icon.

Users can add new SORs that may not be on the list by tapping the Add SOR. Users will then be able to search for any specific type of SORs by tapping on the dropdown next to Select Trade. Alternatively they can search using a keyword in the search box. This will populate and display all the possible outcomes.

A camera icon can be seen at the top right. This can be used to take the photo of the task.
