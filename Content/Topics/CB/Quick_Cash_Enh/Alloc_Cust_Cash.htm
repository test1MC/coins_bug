Allocate Customer Cash

The following changes have been made to Allocate Customer Cash.

Analysis

It is now possible to write off small differences between the invoiced and received amounts by posting the difference to the General Ledger.

On the Analysis Lines entry screen, the Ledger drop-down has a new entry: GL. This allows you to enter a GL account to which the balance of customer cash is posted.

Preventing Unallocated Ledger Cash

A new feature (enabled by setting the new CB parameter LVCCPACB to Yes) makes it impossible to leave an amount fully or partially unallocated in either or . Instead the unallocated amount will post in as customer cash.

Further if or line is entered and the amount is not fully allocated, then during posting the or amount will be automatically updated to equal only the amount of the line that was actually allocated.

When you post the batch, displays a warning message to inform you that the customer cash has not been fully allocated; if you continue, automatically creates a customer cash analysis line for the unallocated amount.

For example:

  • You select customer cash of 1000 and enter line for 900 (leaving 100 unallocated). During posting a CC line of 100 will be created.

  • You select customer cash of 1000 and enter line for 1000, but only allocate 900. During posting a CC line of 100 will be created and the line amount will be updated from 1000 to 900.

New warning messages are displayed when you click Next to leave the allocation screen with an amount not fully allocated, and when posting the batch with or cash that is not fully allocated.
