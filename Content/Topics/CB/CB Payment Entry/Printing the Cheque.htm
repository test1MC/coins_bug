Printing the Cheque

Before you post the payment batch, you will need to print the cheque off ready to be sent to the supplier, follow the steps below to print the cheques/remittances:

Navigate to the Input menu in the module and enter the “Print Cheques/Remittances” screen

Navigate to > Cash Transactions > Print/Cheques Remittances

Select the relevant batch reference hyperlink

 

The information on the “main” and “cheque selection” tab will automatically populate from previous transaction screens. On the “Cheque Selection” tab, it is important to verify that the “Next Cheque Number” field corresponds with the next physical cheque number in your cheque printer

Once all of the information has been checked, click

Click “Ok” to confirm the task has been submitted successfully

The cheque and remittance advice will now be available in the Report Status Workbench and should be printed using the correct form.
