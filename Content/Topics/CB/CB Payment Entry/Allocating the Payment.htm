Allocating the Payment

Once the analysis line has been saved, will automatically take you to the payment allocation tabs

Select the To Allocate tab.

On this screen you will now see a list of invoices that the payment can be allocated against.

Select the invoice to allocate the payment against using the box on the far left

If the cheque is for the full amount of the invoice, Select the Pay Invoices option from the dropdown menu at the bottom of the screen and click apply action button.

If the cheque is for a partial payment of the invoice, Open the invoice line and enter the payment amount into the Amount Paid field.

 

  Click

When invoices are allocated against the payment – the allocated/unallocated amounts at the top of the screen will start to move. Once the unallocated amount reaches zero, the payment has been fully allocated.

Click to return to the Analysis Lines screen.

Click again to return to the Batch Page. The Status of the transaction will be “Cheque unprinted”.
