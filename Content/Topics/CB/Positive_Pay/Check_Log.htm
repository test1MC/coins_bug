Log

If the CB parameter USEPOSPY = Y, a new log table is populated with details of payments from bank accounts that have the new Export box ed (see Positive Pay – Bank Accounts). This is used by the Positive Pay process and reports.

Payments are added to the log as follows:

  • When payment batches in and Subcontract Ledger are posted.
  • When payment batches are posted (only and Subcontract Ledger payments are added).
  • When Payroll are posted in the Payroll module.
  • Depending on the setting of the Export Unposted Payroll field on the bank account record, a log record can be created when a Payroll is calculated or printed in the Payroll module.

The log also includes details of exceptions, such as duplicate numbers, negative , or that have been reported previously with different information.

posting and printing routines have been modified to update the log.
