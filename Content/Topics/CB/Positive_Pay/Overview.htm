Positive Pay Overview

Positive Pay is a process developed by the banks to mitigate the risk of physical fraud. Prior to a being presented for payment a data file is transmitted to the bank with information about the (s) released by the organation. If the information on the transmittal file matches the information on the then the transaction is cleared by the bank. If there is a mismatch of information then an exception is created and the company is contacted prior to clearing the . There is no standard format for the Positive Pay transmittal and each bank has its own standards.
