ALF

Enter ALF Values for the Products, with an option to have a schedule for up to 5 years.

The pricing will need to be calculated as a percentage of the ILF. This is a manual entry to provide a degree of flexibility.

AG19 contains the price for Progress Enterprise Bundle. For the ALF the price per user in cell AG56 would be entered as =22.5%*AG19

Complete this for each of the Products being sold. The MArgin and QP will then be reflected in the Financial Summary.
