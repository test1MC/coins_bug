Approving cross territory (cross Company time)

This approval process is applied when an employee enters timesheets to a different company than his or her home company. The home company is where that employee is set up under JC > Timesheets > Employees.

Only time which is chargeable should appear as Cross Company time.

The Admin Workbench is shown below:

Action button available

The first tab (example above) shows time booked by employees from other territories and needs to be approved in this screen by, in this example, a Company 10 Administrator. Until approved, the Company 55 create timesheet batch process will not post this time.

The second tab (example below) shows time entered by Company 10 employees across into other companies (shown in the analysis column as company 15, 45 etc). It allows the Company 10 administrator to see what timesheet entries will not be included in the next company 10 timesheet batch, because the time has not yet been approved by the other territories. 

Companies 55, 45 and 15 will each have their own view of the same data.

Currently there is one UK Administrator (Julie Clark) that makes sure all non-US cross company time is approved, so that the timesheets can be posted. The process will become more relevant when the US are live, as Julie Clark and the US administrator will need to work together to ensure time cross charged between US and other territories (both into and out of the US) gets approved.

The UK and other non US territories do not use the Project Manager Workbench – this workbench can be used to approve cross company time as well. The US need to choose which screens to use and by whom.
