Contract Timesheet Entry

Note:
The term "Contract" shown on pages within will appear as "Job" for US employees.

 

'My Contract Timesheets' allows you to enter timesheets for contracts only

In Contract Status, select Timesheets > My Contract Timesheets.

 

Click Add to create a new entry.

 

The Week Ending Date will default to the End Date of the current week, but can be amended if there is a need to enter hours in a previous week.

Click Save

On the Timesheet Entry Screen, set the Columns View to WBS View. This is the recommended view and ensure the correct operation of the Contract Lookups.

Note:

US employee: The combination of Contract/Job and SHO (WBS Section) replaces the TI concept of Client and Job

Non US employee: Contract/Job Activity is now Project Stage rather than location.

Enter the contract you wish to allocate time to. An inline lookup is provided.

For client based work, enter the appropriate contract as indicated on the SHO. The section and Activity fields should be completed in sequence as the lookups at each stage are dictated by the previous selection (i.e. If you don't select a section, you will not be able to select a Timesheet Activity).

 

NOTE:
Overhead contracts do not have Sections and activities, so for overheads these are left blank. For your overhead T contract, entering T into the contract field should show you a single contract (Your T Contract)

Once the Section and Contract/Job Activity (Project Stage) are completed, the Timesheet Activity can be selected.

Note:
The Contract/Job Activities and Timesheet Activities (Project Stage) available to enter time against will depend on the SHO Budget creation

 

Once the Timesheet activity is entered, you can start to analyse your time across the week.

A standard description appears in both the internal and invoice description fields. You can overtype either description.

The Invoice description will be printed on the sales invoice generated by Professional Billing, and may need to be different from the Internal description. If no specific instructions are received from the Project Manager, the invoice description will normally be left the same as the internal description. The Project Manager can amend the Invoice Description in Professional Billing.

Chargeable Time can be booked to a different territory by selecting the appropriate Company number

Current Budget hours will be loaded into JC by an administrator or a Project Manager, and will show in timesheet entry

Totalling on the screen will confirm the booked hours for that week
