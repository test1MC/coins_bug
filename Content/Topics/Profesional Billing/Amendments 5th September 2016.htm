Amendments 5th September 2016

NOTICE ON CHANGE IN TIMESHEET BOOKINGS

  • To make Support related Timesheet Activities more consistent, the activity SUPGEN has been added to work with SUP* contracts.
  • If you copy timesheets from the previous week you may need to amend the copied activity from CHGxSUP to SUPGEN

A reminder on use:

  • When performing chargeable client support work, book your time to the Chargeable SHO (currently relevant for some Australian SHO’s)
  • When performing non chargeable client support work, book your time to the relevant SUP* contract, and the SUPGEN activity.
  • When performing non chargeable non client work for the support department e.g. training, book to XSU1 contract, CHGxSUP activity.
