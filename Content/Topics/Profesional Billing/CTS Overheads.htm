Overhead Contract / Job

All overheads will now be allocated to overhead contracts and not to an individual P contract / job as was previously the case.

The available overhead contracts/jobs are listed in the table above.

Security will limit access to your own T contracts/jobs. (T stands for Timesheet Contract/job). The T contracts/jobs list is shown below – most employee’s will only have access to one of these.

Overhead Timesheet Activities

The available overhead Timesheet Activities are listed in the table below. You will select the appropriate OH…. Activity if you are booking time to your home department (T contract / job).

If you are using another of the Overhead Contracts / Jobs, the Timesheet Activity will normally default to just one option. The exception will be Developers, who will have DOH Timesheet Activities when booking direct to a DEV… Contract/Job (not via the PDR system).

PDR Time

Any system testing time on a PDR should be recorded via the PDR system not directly into the timesheet entry screen. After updating your time in the PDR system, the time record will be created, so your time entry screen will still total your all your hours for a working week.

Note:
DEL – Delivery, DEV – Development, SAL – Sales, PRO – Product Management, QAT – Regression Testing, TEC- Technical, SUP – Support DOH – Development Overhead, CHGx – Cross Charge.
