The next section of the SHO details the products being sold.

Pricing may be entered in two ways

Enter bundle values (brown cells) or Values by individual product (blue cells). You can use both methods as appropriate to the deal.

Discounts can also be recorded on the new SHO. Enter the actual discount amount, not the percentage.

The Product Fixed Element column allows the entry of a fixed price for the product where per user pricing is not being used and/or where a combination of a fixed price and per user pricing is being used e.g. Document Management Server plus document management users.

Hovering the cursor over the Yellow Info Icon will display a pop-up which will detail the items included in a particular bundle.
