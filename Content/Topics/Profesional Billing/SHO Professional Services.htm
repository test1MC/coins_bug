SHO Professional Services

Use this section to specify the types of professional services being sold. A drop down is provided on the left hand column to select from.

Once you have selected the services, the green columns to the right can be used to specify the number of hours/days and the price per hour/day being charged. The columns are titled to match the equivalent COMET categories as used by the Delivery Team Project Managers.

 

 

If the SHO is for services which do not fall within the standard COMET framework, the Initiation & Requirement column heading can be toggled by clicking on it and selecting No Comet Stage

A separate section is available for the sale of 3rd party consultancy.

A drop down is provided, or the name of the service provider may be entered as required.
