3rd Party Products Schedule

This section appears below the financial summary. For each product, specify the number of users and the price per user.

The SHO will calculate the Total Value, and the Margin, for accounting purposes. The SHO will also calculate the Qualifying Profit for sales commission purposes.

Where the cost of sale cells appear in white there is an automatic calculation based on a pre-existing agreement with a 3rd party provider. All other cells i.e. Green need to be completed based on the arrangements with that particular supplier.
