Training Delivery Checklist

Avoid mistakes, anticipate problems and get organised so you feel confident and your session runs smoothly.

Logistics

  • Check the room and layout possibilities beforehand
  • Early arrival - key, parking?
  • Bring own whiteboard pens

Refreshments

  • Pre-book lunch arrangements
  • Cookie Boxes for after the course

Technology

  • Set up and check equipment before attendees arrive
  • Be prepared to run session without technology
  • Simple is best

Training resources

  • Prepare list
  • Check off each item the day before

Advising attendees

  • Where and when
  • Objectives, expectations and what to bring

The Introduction

  • Prepare your own
  • Provide in advance
  • Take a copy on the day

Personal presentation

  • Dress up rather than down
  • Make time for mirror check

Positive visualisation

  • Important for confidence, overcoming anxiety
  • Imagine the session going well, pleased participants

Environmental check

  • Nearby construction, noise from other sessions
  • Preliminary scan for potential problems
  • On the day, think on your feet

Greeting participants

  • Finish preparations in time to greet people
  • Provides you with information
  • Helps reduce your own anxiety
  • Helps create rapport

Post-Course

  • Pass out certificates at end of course
  • Collect evaluations and post-tests
  • Return room to original set-up
