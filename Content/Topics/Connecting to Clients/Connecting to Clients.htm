Connecting to Clients

Customer connection information is held within remedy. To access this either search for the customer using the search facility or from within the call ticket click on the customer name.

Types of connections:

  • Direct Telnet/Hosted customers

For these types of customer you do not need to create a connection to access their coins system.

You will see the url’s for the different environments and you can click the link to open the OA page.

  • Windows VPN

Windows VPN details will be shown as below, the VPN Address, username and password will vary from customer to customer. Once you have setup the connection once you do not need to do it again for the same customer.

To setup a new Windows VPN connection:

Select Start and then Network:

The following window will open, select ‘Setup a new connection or network:

Select Connect to a workplace and then Next

Select ‘No,. create a new connection, next:

Use my internet connection (VPN):

Enter the details as per the VPN details on customer profile e.g:

Click next.

Enter the VPN username and password as per the VPN details on the customer profile:

Click Connect.

To see who you are connected to click on the network icon at the bottom of the screen:

Once you have finished with the connection click on the network icon and the customer name and click disconnect.

If you need to connect to the customer again all you need to do is click on the network icon and you should see the existing VPN connections that you have setup listed.

Once you have connected you can connect to the customer Coins OA/coinsplus system.

 

  • 3. Citrix connection:

With citix connections there will be a URL which you need to copy and paste into a new browser. The VPN Address/ Hostname will give details of the url. The url will take you to a page where you enter the Username and Password to connect.

Once you have connected you can connect to the customer Coins OA/coinsplus system.

Some connections do not allow multiple connections so before you connect check the Customer details under the Customer Description section for any notes that state this.

 

  • Cisco VPN:

Enter the connection details as per the customer VPN Details:

Select save.

Once the connection has been created you will be able to select it from the list and click connect.

You will then be asked to enter the password if required.

Once you have connected you can connect to the customer Coins OA/coinsplus system.
