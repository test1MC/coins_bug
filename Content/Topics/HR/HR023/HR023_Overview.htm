Overview

users running OA HR on Character COINS v7 needed the ability to:

  • track a temporary Leave of Absence (LOA) for company employees,
  • prevent those employees from being paid, and
  • not terminate their benefits.

To satisfy these requirements, changes were made for OA HR/Ti PR and are described in this document.

NOTE:
You must be on the .52 release of  Ti for these changes to apply. Also, the COINS OA changes described in this document are available in v11.07.
