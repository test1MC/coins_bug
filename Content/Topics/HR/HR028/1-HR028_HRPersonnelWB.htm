New Level of ESS Approvers: HR Personnel Workbench

The Authorers field in Human Resources > Personnel Workbench > Organation tab has been renamed Authorers/ESS TS Manager, as shown in Figure: ESS TS Manager. This now gives you the ability to select Managers, Supervisors, and Directors (Approvers) to authore ESS Timesheets.

After an Approver is changed in this field for an employee, all Pending records are updated with the new Approver.

ESS TS Manager

Before marking the HR record as an ESS TS Manager, some additional considerations may be needed:

  1. The Approver should be an OA user with a login to be able to approve time on the Approver Workbench in ESS.
  2. To designate an HR employee record to be a Manager, the box on the HR Organation tab on the Job Assignment layout needs to be ed, as shown in Figure: Mark HR Record to be Approver.
Mark HR Record to be Approver
  1. The Approver can now be selected under the Organation tab in the HR module. You can set the Approver on the HR Personnel Workbench record for whom the Approver will be approving time, as shown in Figure: Designate HR Records for Approver to See. Use the ellipses (...) to look up the available Approvers; if they do not appear in the list, they have not been set in the previous step.
Designate HR Records for Approver to See
  1. The ESS TS Manager should be set as shown in Figure: Set ESS TS Manager. The arrows can be used to bring the Manager, Director, or Supervisor over from the Available box to the Selected box. Once an Employee has entered time in the ESS Portal, it appears for approval on the Approvers Workbench in ESS.
NOTE: 
This setup can also be achieved with a Load file; to do this, please contact a  Consultant or COINS Support for assistance.
