Assigning a Competence to a Job Title

 

Navigate to: HR Module > Competence Menu > Job Groups

Job titles belong to Job Groups, so select the relevant Job Group first.

The Job Title Detail screen is displayed and immediately shows you the amount of people with a given job title and how many are ‘valid’ in terms of competencies associated with the Job Title

 

Clicking the against a Job Title will present three tabs.

 

‘Job details’ tab – Description details of the Job type are held here.

‘Personnel with Job Title’ tab – A list of all employees with that job title.

‘Competence Reqts’ tab – A list of competencies currently assigned to the Job title.
