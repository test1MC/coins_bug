Essential and Desirable Competencies

 

At present there is no natural method in to define a competency as being either essential or desirable for a job role. simply assumes the competency is either valid or not valid.

However duplicating competencies could be done to mimic this. For example cloning ‘Driver Skill level 1’ (keeping the same ‘elements’) and calling it ‘Driver Skill level 1 DESIRABLE’ and applying as normal to employees.
