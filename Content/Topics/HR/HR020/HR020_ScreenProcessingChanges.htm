  • When a new is added to the personnel record on the Personnel Workbench, the new Anniversary Month and Anniversary Day fields are available for use, as shown in Figure 2: Anniversary Month and Day Fields. These fields are not available for update on existing .
  • When running Create Benefit due to Age or Seniority Changes on the Plan Workbench, the Anniversary Month and Anniversary Day values are copied from the existing to the new .
  • When creating new using the Personnel Data Load, the Anniversary Month and Anniversary Day fields can be included on the Data file.
