Overview

Due to legislative changes in many states, OA users are beginning to offer more Anniversary Based Accrual Plans, and they are tracking these plans and balances in OA. Previously, the Anniversary Accrual Plans in OA always used the later of the Rehire Date or the Hire Date when determining a person’s anniversary date for accruals. Since this was becoming an issue for users with hire/rehire history implementing Anniversary Based Accruals, changes have been made in OA so that you can now use Anniversary Month and Anniversary Day fields to specify the anniversary date for each employee.

The changes described in this document apply to the US region only.
