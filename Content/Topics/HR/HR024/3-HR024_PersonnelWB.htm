HR Employees in Multiple Courses: Personnel Workbench

This section describes how the HR Personnel Workbench can be used to allow multiple course for employees.

Link To Training Course Group Action

A new Link to Training Course Group action has been added to the Personnel Workbench, as shown in Figure 1: Link to Training Course Group Action. You can use this action to select one or more employees for in a training course group. After selecting a Group (such as Daily Task Briefing), an employee is automatically assigned to any courses in that Group.

Link to Training Course Group Action

Link to Training Course Group Selection Screen

After you click the Link to Training Course Group action button, you are taken to a Link to Training Course Group selection screen that has the following two tabs:

  • Course/Assessment tab, as shown in Figure 2: Course/Assessment Tab Selection Screen. Use this tab to select a Group Code, then click Next at the bottom of the screen to complete the group selection. Any linked employee(s) are automatically assigned to courses in that Group.
  • Personnel tab, as shown in Figure 3: Personnel Tab Selection Screen. This tab automatically lists any employees who are already enrolled in the group.
