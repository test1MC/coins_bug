Cost Flow and WIP Reporting

Changes have been made to make the new monthly figures for each template section for Cost Flow and WIP available for reporting.

Cost Flow and WIP Standard Reports

Two new standard reports are on the appraisal options menu. One shows the new monthly figures for each template section for Cost Flow and the other for WIP. These have a similar format to the existing Appraisal Cash Flow Report.

ETL Cost Flow and WIP Figures

The new monthly figures for each template section for Cost Flow and WIP are available to the ETL for reporting in a similar way to the existing Cash Flow figures.
