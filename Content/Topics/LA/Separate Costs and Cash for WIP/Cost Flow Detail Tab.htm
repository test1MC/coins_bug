Appraisal

If the template for an appraisal has Separate Cost and Cash Flows ed, a new Cost Flow tab is available on the appraisal and the Detailed Cash Flow screen becomes only.

New Costflow Tab

Cost Flow Detail Tab

This is similar in format to the detailed cash flow tab and has the same functionality except as detailed below.

Costflow Detail Screen

The Cost Flow figures are calculated in the same way that Detailed Cash Flow figures were previously calculated and are updateable in the same ways (direct input or using a distribution method). The detailed cash flow figures are calculated from the cost flow figures (see 1.2, Detailed Cash Flow Tab ).

The Cost Flow tab includes Income so that all allocation to periods can be done on a single screen.

The Recalculate Finance and ROCE matrix option is not available on the Cost Flow tab. The Finance and ROCE and IRR figures all continue to be based on the Cash Flow figures.

Detailed Cash Flow Tab

The Detailed Cash Flow tab is for only. The figures in it are calculated from the Cost Flow Detail tab figures and the payment profile percentages defined against each detailed cash flow section.

The detailed cash flow will be updated from the Cost Flow Detail whenever the Recalculate Finance or Recalculate Finance and ROCE functions are run. It will also be updated during the Update Appraisal with Actuals process (see 1, Update Appraisal with Actuals).
