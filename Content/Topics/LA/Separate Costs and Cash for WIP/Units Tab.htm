Units Tab and Plots Tab

If the template for an appraisal has the Use Profit Contingency option selected, the recovery column for this section on the Units tab and the Plots tab shows, for each unit, the Profit Contingency figure calculated as follows:

  • Revenue for the unit.
  • Less Recoveries for the unit for those Cost Sections configured as being part of the calculation (see 3.1.4 above).
  • Multiplied by the profit percentage configured (see 3.1.4 above).
