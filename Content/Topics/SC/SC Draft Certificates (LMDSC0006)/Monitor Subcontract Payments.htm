Monitor Subcontract Payments

 

A Certificate will not appear as a cost until it has been posted by the Finance Department.  Authorising a Certificate will not automatically post it.  Although the Finance Department will monitor and post certificates regularly, it is good practise to monitor what Certificates are at what step in the process.

This section will demonstrate how to monitor a Subcontract Draft Certificate.

Select Enquiries > Draft Certificates

 

‘%’  Note - The Draft Certificate Enquiry workbench is accessible by all

‘%’  Note - From the Draft Certificate Enquiry workbench the only action available is to print certificates

    Locate the Draft Certificate and check its status. Statuses are:

•Attn reqd-The certificate can be amended, put on commercial hold, or released for authorisation

•To Auth 2-The certificate has been released and is awaiting level 2 authorisation

•Approved-The certificate is waiting to be batched

•Batched-The certificate has been included in a certificate batch

•Posted-The certificate has been posted
