Subcontractor Draft Certificates

Draft Certificates are raised against a Subcontract order and record how much a subcontractor has applied for. Once entered, they must be released for authorisation.

Certificates must be authorised before they can be paid. Certificates can be rejected or put on hold and can be passed backward and forward within the system until they are finally agreed and authorised.

There is no requirement to distribute Costs using cost codes or even to calculate retentions / discounts etc. This will be automatically carried out based on the order details.
