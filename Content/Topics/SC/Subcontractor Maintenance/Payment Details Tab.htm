Subcontractor Maintenance - Payment Details Tab

The Payment Details tab enables you to how this specific Subcontractor is paid.

Select the Payment Details tab and enter the payments terms in the relevant fields.

Fields

Description

Currency:

Shows which currency the figures are shown in. 

Alternative Currency:

If the Subcontractor can be paid in a different currencies than there base currency.

Parent Company:

You may wish to consolidate a group of subcontractors together under one parent record for the Turnover reports.

Factor Account:

The Factor account number is required. A tri-party agreement may be required.

Hold Payments:

If the payments are to be held for a reason. This will omit them from the automatic selection.

Labour Only:

Are they Labour Only.

Timecards:

Is the Subcontractor paid on a timecard basis, i.e. measured work times by rate.

Due Type:

The Due Type and the Due Date are used to determine when the payment is due. This is selecting the type, number of days, or specified months

Due Days:

The due days define when the certificates are and should be due for payment

Default Certificate:

This defines the method of arithmetic used to calculate payment. 

Dormant:

Is this subcontract not being used? In normal circumstances, when an subcontract account is no longer required, it should be flagged as dormant NOT deleted.

Factor:

Whether this account is for a factor. Other subcontractor accounts can then be factored to this one.

This field can only be maintained by the sysadmin user.  This is a security measure: since payments from other subcontractor accounts can be redirected to a factor account it is important that the setup of factor accounts is tightly controlled.

Once you have completed this screen, select the Payment Method Tab.
