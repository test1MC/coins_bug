Overview

Before the Subcontract Ledger can be used, you need to enter key details about your subcontractors.  You can add details of new subcontractors, or change the information about existing ones, at any time.

Before you start, you will need to gather as much information about the subcontractor as possible, for example, address, telephone number, VAT codes, and contacts.  You do not have to enter every field of information when you create the subcontractor file; some information can be added at a later date, but the more you have to start with the easier it will be to maintain the subcontractor file.

New subcontractors are initially set up as companies in the Central Repository – This is covered in the guide HOW TO…Add a Company to the Central Repository (LMDCD0001)
