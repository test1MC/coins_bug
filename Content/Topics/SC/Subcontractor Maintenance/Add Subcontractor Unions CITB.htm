Subcontractor Maintenance - Unions, CITB, H&S Tab

The Unions, CITB, H&S tab enables you to record information about Trade Union and Training Membership Board, along with Health and Safety representation. This is for information purposes only.

Fields

Description

Industrial Relations Officer:

The name of the Officer.

Operative’s Union:

The name of the trade union, if applicable

Trade Union Agreement:

Does the company have a trade union agreement

HPMS or JIB Registration No.:

The HPMS or JIB (Joint Industry Board) registration number.

EITB or CITB Registration No.:

The registered number for either the Electrical Industry Training Board (EITB) or the Construction Industry Training Board (CITB).

Health and Safety Officer:

The name of the Health and Safety Officer

Health and Safety Certificate:

The company’s Health and Safety Certificate Number.

Principle Trade:

The principle trade of the Subcontractor, i.e. bricklaying.

CITB Applicable?:

If CITB applies to this Subcontractor.

CITB Deduct Applicable?:

Is CITB Deduct applicable for this Subcontractor?

Enter all the details that apply in the relevant fields and select the Analysis tab.
