Subcontractor Maintenance - Tax & VAT Tab

The Tax & VAT tab enables you to enter details of the subcontractor’s VAT and Income Tax Papers.

Enter the details of the relevant VAT and Income Tax Information in the relevant fields.

Fields

Description

Country:

The Main Country where the Subcontractor is based.

VAT Registered:

Is the Subcontractor VAT registered? Some of them may not be. If they are then the VAT Number should be displayed in the description.

Default VAT Code :

Select the default VAT code for this Subcontractor. Note - This will automatically populate the VAT field when inputting the invoice or payment. 

Tax Scheme:

The Tax scheme for the Subcontractor. This will calculate the TAX. If you are entering the details of the TAX scheme then you should enter V = Valid.

Construction Industry Scheme - CIS

The CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) Tax Exception Certificates will be available in the table at the bottom of the page.

To enter details of a certificate,click .

A new line is displayed to enter the details of the certificate.

Enter the details of the certificate in the relevant fields defined in the table below.

Fields

Description

Type:

The Type of Tax Exemption Certificate held by the Subcontractor

Certificate:

The Certificate Number

NI / Co. Reg:

The Subcontractors National Insurance Number or Company Registrations Number.

From:

To:

The Dates the Certificate is valid

Holder:

The “Issued To” name exactly as it appears on the Certificate.

Txns?:

Are they any transaction, orders or payments against the certificate?

Complete the details and select the Verification Tab.
