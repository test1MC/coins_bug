Subcontractor Maintenance

Navigate to Subcontract Ledger > Subcontractors > Subcontractor Maintenance

The Subcontract Maintenance browse screen is displayed.

This screen lists all the Supplier Accounts in that are within your view. If the subcontractor does not have a subcontractor ledger account, it will not be shown on the list.

 

Field

Description

Subcontractor Account

The account number of the subcontractor in the Subcontract Ledger.

Name

The subcontractor's name.

VAT Reg. No.

The subcontractor's VAT registration number.

Address/Postcode/Telephone

The subcontractors address and contact telephone number

Tax Scheme

The tax scheme under which the subcontractor's tax is calculated.

T = taxable
U = unknown (will not allow payment until changed)
V = valid
X = exempt (outside the scope of the subcontractor tax scheme)

Notes (1st unlabelled column)

Whether notes have been entered against this company.

If notes have been entered against the company, this field shows a flag icon.

If at least one note is marked by a Red Flag, the flag will appear here as a Red Flag.

To see the notes, click the flag icon.

Warnings (2nd unlabelled column)

A in this column indicates that warnings have been entered for the subcontractor – for example; the account is dormant, or the account has not been verified.

Click the image to show the warnings.
