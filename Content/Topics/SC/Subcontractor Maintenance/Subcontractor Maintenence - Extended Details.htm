Subcontractor Maintenance - Extended Details

Once a Subcontractor account has been set up, additional information may be recorded against the account.

From the Subcontractor Maintenance screen, click on the hyperlink for the account to be maintained.

On the Subcontractor Details screen a drop down is displayed allowing the user to switch between Main and Extended details.

The Subcontractor Maintenance Tabs that were completed during the account setup are shown by selecting Main.

Additional Tabs are shown by Selecting Extended Details

To maintain the fields for either Main or Extended Details, click at the bottom of the screen.
