Creating the Subcontract Ledger Account

To create an account for a Subcontractor, click

The Subcontract Maintenance - Add screen is displayed. Use the Lookup button to locate the Subcontractor record that you require.

A list of all Subcontractors will be shown. There may be more than one shown as the list shows each office

A column is displayed to show if an account already exists for this company.

If the subcontractor that you require is not shown, check the subcontractor record in the Company Information Workbench.  It may be that your record has not been flagged as a subcontractor on the Account Types tab.

Locate the record that you require. Click the Select button adjacent to the record. This will populate the Company/Office field.

If you have selected the wrong account by mistake, you must use the Clear button to remove the current information and repeat the process from step 3 to select the correct details.

 

Click The full details of the Subcontractor record will be displayed to enable you to add the details of the Subcontract Ledger account.

The record and information supplied is split into various tabs.
