Notes Tab

The Notes tab provides a simple free-format text box to record any relevant notes concerning this account.

If any notes are entered for the account, the icon in the top right of the screen will turn from black (no notes) to orange (notes present).

This icon is always visible regardless of the Tab being displayed. Clicking on the icon will present a Notes pop-up from which the Notes may be viewed or maintained without the need to visit the Notes Tab.
