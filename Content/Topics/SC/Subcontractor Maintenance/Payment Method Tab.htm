Subcontractor Maintenance - Payment Method Tab

The Payment Method tab enables you to enter the details of your preferred payment method for the Subcontractor. You may have a company standard that all Subcontractors comply with.

There is only one field available for you to select as the Payment Method.  Select the Dropdown button to display the list of options available.

However upon selecting the options from the dropdown list for BACS, additional fields will be available.

BACS Option

When selecting the option for BACS additional fields are available to enter the relevant details of the bank account.

When all the information has been entered select the Tax & VAT tab.
