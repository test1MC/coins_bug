Cancel Certificates

 

To cancel a certificate, Navigate to Subcontract Ledger > Certificates > Cancel Certificates.

  • Click

  • Create a batch header in the normal way and click

  • Click to add transactions to the batch.

 

The lookup shows any unpaid certificates that you may want to cancel.

  • Select the require certificate to cancel.

  • Click
  • Continue selecting certificates to cancel as required.
  • When happy with the selections made – post the batch in the normal way.

Upon posting a Certificate batch is created and posted to reverse the original certificate entries.

A zero value Manual Payment Batch is also created to ‘close’ the otherwise open ledger balances.

Cancelled certificates have a status of ‘Cancelled’ and can be filtered separately within certificate enquiries.
