Contra Workbench - Overview

A new Contra Workbench caters for all regions and allows recovery of costs linked to filtered cost transactions. The filtered transactions will be marked as unallocated with the ability to match these to other transactions or link to a contra (on which you can adjust the recovery amount and optionally add an administration charge) and then recover via a sales invoice. and Subcontract Ledger payment runs now allow you to automatically offset these sales invoices against the payment.

The approach of using a sales invoice has been adopted for two reasons:

  1. To ensure the correct handling of on contra charges.
  2. To ensure that contra charges can be recovered against and Subcontract Ledger payments, while avoiding problems with the sequence in which subcontractor certificates are posted, approved and selected for payment.
