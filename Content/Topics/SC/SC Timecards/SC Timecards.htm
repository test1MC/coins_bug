Maintenance of Subcontractors and Subcontracts

Subcontractors

When setting up Subcontractors you can use the Company Information Workbench to setup them up as normal. It is when you get to Subcontract Maintenance you have to select Timecards option and the New Timecard certificate on Payment Details.

 

Once you have saved the Subcontractor you can add the Timecard Activity Rates, if you leave the contract blank then this rate will affect every contract not the one specified.

 

 

 

Subcontracts

For each Subcontractor that is going to be using Timecards a Subcontract needs to be created in Subcontract Maintenance.

When creating the Subcontract you do not put in a contract number.
