Working with Browse Screens

Browse screens show a list of records you can work with. Some of the typical things you can do on a browse screen include:

  • Finding particular records.
  • Looking at the detail of a particular record.
  • Adding new records.
  • Updating existing records.
  • Deleting records.

Sorting Records

You can change the order in which records are displayed:

  • Click the column heading - the records are then sorted by the contents of that column.

  • To reverse the sort order, click the column again.

Row Menu

The Row Menu provides actions you can apply to an individual record, such as viewing it, updating it or viewing related records. The actions available will be different depending on the browse screen and the individual record involved.

  1. Either:
    • Right-click the row.

    • Click the row, then click the menu icon.

  2. Click the menu item for the action you want.
