Making an Application for Payment

  1. On the SC Controller tab, go to SC Orders.
  2. Right-click the line for the order for which you want to make an application, and choose Create Application for Order.

    The Add New Application screen opens.

    New Application Screen

  3. Complete the details of the application:
    • You must complete the Measured Works and Materials on Site fields.
    • Values are cumulative.
    • The Discount, Retention and Liable for Tax fields are optional.
    • The Anticipated Final Accounts fields are memorandum fields for your own use.
    • The Final Payment Date is calculated based on your relationship with the main contractor.
    • You must enter Notes, and upload a file (for example, a spreadsheet) to back up your application.
  4. If you want to save your application without submitting it, click Save.
  5. To submit the application, click Submit.

    You will receive a notification that your application has been submitted (based on your notification preferences, this may be an email, or an in-app notification).
