Registering

As a new User you will receive an email inviting you to join the Construction Cloud:

Invitation Email

If you already have a Construction Cloud account, click Connect to link your account with this company.

If you are new to Construction Cloud,

  1. Click Register to register your company and register yourself as a user.

    The Company Registration screen opens.

    Registration Screen

  2. Enter your name and .
  3. If you want to use a user name to log in, instead of using your email address, enter a User Name. This is the name that will appear on the desktop header.
  4. Create a Password.
  5. Read the terms and conditions for the company, and I Agree.
  6. Click Continue.
  7. Read the terms and conditions that apply to you as a user, and I Agree.
  8. Click Continue.

    Your Construction Cloud desktop is displayed.
