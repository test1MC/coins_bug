Payment Notices

When your application has been paid, you will receive a notification (based on your notification preferences, this may be an email, or an in-app notification).

To view your Payment Notice

  1. Do one of the following:
    1. On the SC Controller tab, go to SC Orders.

      The Notices column shows how many payment notices there are for each order.

    2. Right-click the order line and choose View Related Notices.

    Or

    1. On the SC Controller tab, go to Notices.
    2. You can filter the list to show Paid or Unpaid notices.

  2. Right-click the notice you want to view and choose View.

  3. To email the QS, click the email link in the notice.
  4. To download an attachment, click one of the links in the bottom-left corner. Attachments SC are those you (the subcontractor) have attached; attachments MC are those the main contractor has attached.
