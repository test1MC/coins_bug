Post Standing Journals

Post Standing Journals allows you to post a range of standing journals. Only journals marked as "ready to post" will be posted.

Standing journals will be posted if:

  • they are not template journals, and
  • they are marked "Ready to Post", and
  • the "Next Post" period on the journal is the same as the selected posting period

Fixed standing journals will not have their ready to post flag reset to No after posting, whereas non-fixed ones do.

From the Standing sub-menu, select the Post Standard Journals option.

Enter the details for the standing journals to be posted in the relevant fields.

Field

Description
From The start range for the standing journals. Leave blank to include all standing journals
To The end range of the standing journals. Leave blank to include all standing journals
Period The financial period to make the payments, for example this month.

Click and the journals will be posted

The journals can be found in GL> Journals > Journal Batch.
