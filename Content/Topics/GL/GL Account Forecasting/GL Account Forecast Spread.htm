GL Account Forecast Workbench - Forecast Spread

The Forecast Spread screen is shown if you follow a link from the account number field on the Account Forecast Workbench. Forecast Spread allows you to enter forecast amounts against General Ledger periods.

This has the following tabs:

  • Rolling Forecast.
  • Future Year Forecast (next year).
  • Future Year Forecasts (additional years as specified by GL/FCNUMYRS).
  • Notes.

The same actions are available as on the GL Account Forecast workbench (see 1 - GL Account Forecast Browse Actions).
