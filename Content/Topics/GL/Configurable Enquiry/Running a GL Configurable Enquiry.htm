Running a GL Configurable Enquiry

To run a GL Configurable Enquiry navigate to General Ledger > Enquiries > Configurable GL Enquiry

Choose the enquiry you wish to run by typing in the name of the enquiry (which is configured separately) or click on the “look up” icon and select.

Select the appropriate period and currency for the enquiry and click

Once the enquiry has run you will be presented with various rows and columns indicative of that particular enquiry. Use the arrows to move up, down, left and right to view the enquiry.

Any figures in a light have a link to further information available. This allows you to “dig deeper” into the values and understand the source data.

By clicking the link you will open a new window (alternatively, hold down CTRL and click the link to open a new tab).

The screens which are triggered by the links are typical OA enquiry screens with the usual filtering options
