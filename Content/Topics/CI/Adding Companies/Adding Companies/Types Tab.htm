Types Tab

The Company Types is available to enable you to search on types of companies. Select the company types that are associated with the Supplier, as you can allocate more than one. For example the company could be a supplier (materials), subcontractors or plant hire.

Select the Tick Box adjacent to all the required company types. A company must have at least one type, i.e. supplier.

If you select the Save button without selecting a company type, a message is displayed stating “You must select a Company Type”.  Select the OK button. You will return to the Company Add screen. Select the Types Tabs to allocate the company types.
