Adding Offices to a Company

Module: Central Repository

Companies can have many offices: one record for the Company and many office records.

This process will be used to add a new office/location to an existing company. This should only be done if they have the same Tax Identification NumberCompany Registration Number. If the TINsCRNs are different, refer to Adding a Company.

You should not have the same company entered in the system more than once and therefore each office becomes an additional record against the main company record. It is on the office that the order is placed rather than the head office, so we recommend that you have all the offices entered into the system.

  1. Go to Central Repository > Companies. Companies

  2. Click the hyperlink on the company you wish to edit.Edit Company

  3. Select the Offices tab.Offices Tab

  4. Click at the bottom of the screen.Add Office

  5. Select the appropriate Office Type from the drop-down menu.Select Office Type

  6. Complete the Office and Trading As fields.Office and Trading As

  7. Complete all appropriate Address and Tax information in the remaining fields, then click .Address and Tax Information

    The newly created office now displays in the Offices tab.
