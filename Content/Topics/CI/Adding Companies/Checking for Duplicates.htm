Checking for Duplicates

It is always recommended you search for the company first before you add a new record at all levels. It might be the supplier is already in the system however you have details of a new office that you are using. This should be added as an office or additional branch, rather than a new supplier record.

Navigate to the Companies Workbench in Central Repository.

Click

In the top panel, enter the details of the company to be added. You may search on the following criteria:

Field

Description
Postcode Enter a postcode to check whether the company you want to add has already been recorded in .
Name Enter a Name to check whether the company you want to add has already been recorded in . Entered Name will be compared with the Company Name, Short Name and Legal Name of the companies in the database.
Co. Reg. No. Enter a registration number to check whether the company you want to add has already been recorded in .
Phone Number Enter a Phone Number to check whether the company you want to add has already been recorded in .
VAT Registration No Enter a VAT registration number to check whether the company you want to add has already been recorded in .

Once you have entered the details you wish to search on, click CHECK DUPLICATES.

If you one or more of the company types that correspond to , or subcontractor, you are given the option to also create the corresponding ledger account.

If you one of the Create ... Account boxes, after you add and save the company record you will be taken to the Add , Add or Add Subcontractor screen (as appropriate), with the company details completed.

The Potential Duplicates section shows a list of companies whose name or short name begins with the company name you entered, or that have the same , company registration number, Registration Number, address, phone number, email or website address as you entered. 

The possible duplicates are sorted by their score. The score depends on how many of the company fields on the records found match what you entered.

If you want to search for companies with names that contain the name you entered, the Use Matches Search box.

If the company you want to add is shown here, click the hyper-link to update the details. 

If the company is not shown, click to add a new one.
