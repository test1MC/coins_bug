CIW Offices - General Tab

The General tab enables more general information to be entered about the company. It relates to how the purchase orders are going to be processed, are GRN’s required and other additional information. The information that is entered at the office level will override the information entered on the Company head office.

Field

Description
   
Status (Group) Enter the status given to this group.
Status (This Company) The status your company has given to this company.
DX Number If appropriate, the DX (Document Exchange) number of the office.
PO Required Is a Purchase Order number required when invoices from this office are entered?
The default value (Y or N) for the PO Required field when setting up a new company.
Material Accrual Method Whether you want to process GRNs for this office. Leave this field blank unless you want to override the contract-specific setting (for example, you would not expect to process GRNs for British Telecom).
Plant Accrual Method How calculates accruals on plant orders for this office.

Full Allocation - Plant GRNs are required and weekly plant allocation is used. creates the accrual from the confirmed plant sheet.

No Allocation (GRN) - Plant GRNs and Plant Maintenance are used to record the on hire and off hire dates; calculates the accrual based on the time between the on hire date (from the GRN) and the off hire date or today's date.

No Allocation (no GRN) - Plant GRNs are not used; calculates the accrual based on the time between the due date from outstanding orders and the off hire date or today's date.
Use contract accrual method - uses the default accrual method specified for the contract that the order is raised for.
Match Whether runs detailed Purchase Order Matching on invoices from this office. Typically you would only NOT run Purchase Order matching for "internal" plant suppliers.
Charge Centres Office, Contact, Plant, Charge Centres
