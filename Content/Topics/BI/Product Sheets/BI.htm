Business Intelligence is a comprehensive and ever growing suite of tools, applications and technologies for gathering, storing, analysing, and providing access to data to help you make better business decisions.

 

 

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

Howard Dresner, a Gartner analyst, popularised Business Intelligence (BI) as an umbrella term to describe a set of concepts and methods to improve business decision-making by using fact-based decision support systems.

Business Intelligence allows querying and reporting, online analytical processing (OLAP), statistical analysis, data mining, and data warehousing. Information can bepresented in textual or graphical reports, screen inquiries, and dashboards, all of which are fully integrated to .
