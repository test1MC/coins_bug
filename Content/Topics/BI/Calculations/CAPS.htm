CAPS$

Purpose:

 

Converts any characters in a character string to uppercase or lowercase characters, and returns the resulting string.

Syntax:

 

CAPS$(string,flag)

 

string:

A character expression.  The string may be a constant, field name, variable, or expression that results in a character value.

 

flag:

1 – String will be converted to uppercase

2 – String will be converted to lowercase
