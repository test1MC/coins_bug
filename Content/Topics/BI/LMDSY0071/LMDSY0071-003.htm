Workflow Roles

In our design, we identified a role of “Level 1 Auth” – a business role for the approval of our purchase orders.  It is important not to confuse a role with an actual person.  Workflow roles can be defined and used across multiple workflows, so it is important to ensure they mirror an actual business role in your organisation such as Financial Manager, Buying Team etc.

In Workflow Folder, navigate to Workflow Roles.

Create New Role

Click

Add a Role with name TR?LEVEL1  where ? equates to the Training Login details.  (e.g.  TRAIN1 will create TR1LEVEL1)

Add a description (e.g.  Level 1 Auth)

Assign the Group ‘Workflow Projects’ to the Role

Leave Responsibility Type and Key fields blank.  This will leave the Role open to be used with any order released.
