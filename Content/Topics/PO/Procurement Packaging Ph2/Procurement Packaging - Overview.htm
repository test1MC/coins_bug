Procurement Packaging (24167)

Various changes have been made to improve the usability of the process of raising requisitions and orders from resources. See OA-CE-PO016 for details.

  • New indicators and tooltips show if unissued requisitions or uncommitted orders exist for a resource.
  • Requisitions can be raised from the package resources list.
  • Resources can be added to unissued requisitions.
  • Resource lines can be consolidated by associating them with a single budget line.
