Configuring Order Documents to Print Terms and Conditions

You can configure purchase order documents to print the details of the terms and condition set associated with the order.

  1. Go to Document Designer.
  2. Update an existing purchase order document, or create a new document using the Purchase Order document type. See Configurable Documents in the online documentation for information on how to define configurable documents.
  3. On the Extras tab, enter IDs for the header, body and footer forms for terms and conditions.

  4. If you want the terms and conditions to be printed on , the Print Terms & Conditions On Variation? box.

  5. On the Fields tab, add lines for the fields and text you want to include in the terms and conditions section of the order.

    The Terms & Conditions Body Form can include fields from the po_hdrterm table; for example:

    • pht_title to display the title of the terms and conditions line.

    • RO_pht_text_populated to display the content of the line.
