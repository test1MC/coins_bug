Multi-Analysis Type Purchase Orders (24209) - Overview

Multi-Analysis Type Purchase Orders (24209)

v11.05 introduced the Single Form style of PO entry which included the ability to raise multi-analysis orders (PDR 24091). The standard order workbenches have now been enhanced to provide similar functionality to support multi-analysis orders.

Multi-analysis ordering applies to the following major types of order only:

  • Material Orders
  • Workshop Orders
  • Asset Orders
  • Orders
