PO Notifications - Email Templates

New email Email templates need to be configured for emails that are sent when orders are committed. Three new Types have been added in standard Email Templates maintenance (see Email Templates in the online documentation).

  • PONOTIFY-INT - Template for internal emails.

  • PONOTIFY-EXT - Template for external emails.

  • PONOTIFY-ADM - Template for emails to administrators.

The following special tokens can be used in the body of the Internal or Admin notification templates.

{CommittedFrom} and {CommittedTo} - display the affected time period (for example, in the template for emails to administrators).
