National Purchasing Agreements - Company Information Workbench

Attaching an Agreement Document to a CIW Record

Any relevant agreement documentation should be uploaded against the company in the Central Repository. These documents should be uploaded into the Document Group defined in the new CI parameter NPAGROUP.

Company Summary

If the CI parameter USENPA is set to Y then two new fields appear on the “General” tab.

Company Summary Screen General Tab

Field
Description

National Purchasing Agreement

A box to indicate that a National Purchasing Agreement exists for this company.

Scope

If the National Purchasing Agreement box has been ed, this allows you to indicate whether the agreement applies to all products, or only to some products.

Both of these fields are for information only.

Company Browse

If the National Purchasing Agreement box has been ed, the Group T/A field on the Company Information Workbench browse is shown as ed.
