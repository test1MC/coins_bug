National Purchase Agreements - Invoice Matching

If the CI parameter USENPA is set to Y, the invoice matching lines show the NPA and Eligible Manufacturer details that were entered on the order lines.

You can update these fields: click to open the line. When the invoice is posted, all versions of the related purchase order line are updated.

Additional Charges

Additional charges, which do not relate to a purchase order line, can be entered during the Invoice Matching process. Both the NPA and the Eligible Manufacturer fields are available for updating if the supplier account on the invoice relates to a company in the Central Repository marked as National Purchasing Agreement (as described in 1, National Purchasing Agreements - Company Information Workbench).
