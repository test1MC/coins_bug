National Purchasing Agreements - Overview

National Purchasing Agreements allow you to track information on rebate agreements. When entering or updating a purchase order or invoice, the user can indicate which items qualify for a rebate under a purchasing agreement.

Rebates can be direct or indirect:

  • Direct rebates are given by the supplier from whom you bought the goods.
  • Indirect rebates are given by the manufacturer of the goods.

You can report on the goods value of transactions that qualify for a rebate.

This functionality applies to all non-subcontract orders (for example: material orders, overhead orders, or orders).

NOTE

This is different from existing functionality related to Luckins, which identifies and calculates rebates automatically.
