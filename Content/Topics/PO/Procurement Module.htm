Procurement Module

Procurement is based on a comprehensive understanding of the core business processes of winning and managing construction contracts. It supports activities throughout the procurement process: planning; defining procurement packages; selecting vendors; creating and issuing requisitions, tender appraisals, order approvals, and purchase orders; and reviewing performance. Procurement also logs deliveries and matches material and plant invoices to receipts and orders.

 

Like other modules, Procurement accesses Central Repository for up-to-date information on suppliers, subcontractors, plant , and more so that you can perform procurement activities efficiently and conveniently
