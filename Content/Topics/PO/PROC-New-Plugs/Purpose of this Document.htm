Plugs

Purpose of this Document

This document explains how to set up clauses that contain plugs, and how to use these clauses in purchase orders.

Overview of Functionality

Plugs provide a way of reserving space in a standard clause for users to enter dates, numbers or text, or of inserting fields into subcontract agreement reminder text.

The old style plugs are discontinued as of v10.14.

Some of the new plugs are only available in OA.
