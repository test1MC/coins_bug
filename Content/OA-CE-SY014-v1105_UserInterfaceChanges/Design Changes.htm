Design Changes

The redesign of OA includes the following:

  • New, cleaner icons.
  • A lighter, more modern scheme.
  • The introduction of a “theme ” which can be changed on a company-by-company basis.
  • Clearer font.
  • Data is shown in bold, with labels in a lighter font weight.
  • Rows and fields are highlighted as you move the mouse pointer over them or tab through them.
  • The heights of field layout boxes on detail and update pages are adjusted so they automatically line up.
