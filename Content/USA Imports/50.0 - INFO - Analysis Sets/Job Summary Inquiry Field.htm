Job Summary Inquiry Field Calculations:

 

  • Initial EMAC $ = Contract Amount (Initial Revenue Budget) – Total Initial Cost Budget
  • Initial EMAC % = Initial Revenue Budget – Total Initial Cost Budget    X 100

     Initial Revenue Budget

 

  • Current EMAC $ = Current Revenue Forecast – ECAC to Date

 

  • Current EMAC % = Current Revenue Forecast – ECAC to Date   X 100

  Current Revenue Forecast

 

  • Current % = (Costs to Date/Total Current Cost Budget) X 100

 

  • Current % Any Cost Category – same calculation as shown below. Labor used as an example:

 

  • Current % Labor= (Labor Costs to Date/Total Current Labor Cost Budget) X 100

 

  • Forecast % = (Costs to Date/Total Forecast Cost Budget) X 100

 

  • Forecast % Any Cost Category – same calculation as shown below. Material used as an example:

 

  • Forecast % Material= (Material Costs to Date/Total Forecast Material Cost Budget) X 100

 

  • % Complete = (Costs to Date/ECAC to Date) X 100

 

  • ECAC % = (Costs to Date/ECAC to Date) X 100

 

  • ECAC % Any Cost Category – same calculation as shown below. Labor used as an example:

 

  • ECAC % Labor = (Labor Costs to Date/Labor ECAC to Date) X 100

 

  • PTCV (Projected Total Cost Variance) = ECAC to Date - Total Current Cost Budget

 

  • PTCV (Projected Total Cost Variance) Hours = Forecast Hour Budget - Current Hour Budget

 

  • PTCV (Projected Total Cost Variance) Any Cost Category = same calculation as shown below. Equipment used as an example:

 

  • PTCV (Projected Total Cost Variance) Equipment = Equipment ECAC to Date - Total Current Equipment Cost Budget

 

  • Total Comm (Committed) = Committed Costs to Date + Accrued Costs to Date + Costs to Date

 

  • Total Comm (Committed) Any Cost Category - same calculation as shown below. Labor used as an example:

 

  • Total Comm (Committed) Labor = Committed Labor Costs to Date + Accrued Labor Costs to Date + Labor Costs to Date

 

  • To Complete = ECAC to Date – Total Costs to Date

 

  • To Complete Hours = Forecast Hour Budget – Total Hours to Date

 

  • To Complete Any Cost Category = same calculation as shown below. Other used as an example:

 

  • To Complete Other = Other ECAC to Date – Other Costs to Date

 

  • PCT (Percentage of) Billed = (Billings to Date/Forecast Revenue Budget) X 100

 

  • Bill TD – Inc. TD = Billings to Date – Total Costs to Date

 

  • Future Recognized Revenue = Recognized Revenue at Completion – Recognized Revenue to Date

 

  • Under Billed = Recognized Revenue to Date – Billings to Date

 

  • Over Billed = Billings to Date – Recognized Revenue to Date

 

  • Investment = Recognized Revenue to Date – Cash Receipts

 

  • PCT (Percentage) of Extras = Forecast Contract Amount – Initial Contract Amount    X 100

    Forecast Contract Amount

 

  • EMAC (Estimated Margin at Completion) = Recognized Revenue at Completion – ECAC to Date

 

  • EMAC % = (EMAC/Recognized Revenue at Completion) X 100

 

  • EMAC – IBM (Estimated Margin at Completion – Initial Budget Margin) = (Forecast Revenue Budget – ECAC to Date) – (Initial Revenue Budget – Initial Cost Budget)

 

  • Margin $ to Date = Recognized Revenue to Date – Costs to Date

 

  • Future Margin = (Forecast Revenue Amount – Recognized Revenue to Date) – (ECAC to Date – Costs to Date)

 

  • Margin % to Date = Recognized Revenue to Date – Costs to Date    X 100

     Recognized Revenue to Date

 

  • Any Budget Type Margin % = same calculation as shown below. Initial used as an example:

 

  • Initial Margin % = Initial Revenue Budget – Initial Costs Budget    X 100

    Initial Revenue Budget

 

  • Contract Backlog = (Forecast Revenue Amount – Recognized Revenue to Date)

 

  • Future Margin % = (Future Margin/Contract Backlog) X 100

 

  • Cash Flow to Date = Cash Receipts – Cash Disbursements

 

  • Change Orders to Date (Revenue) = Current Revenue Budget – Initial Revenue Budget
