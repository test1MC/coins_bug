1. The Documents Tab has a drop-down menu that appears when you hover over with the mouse:

 

  1. Browse Documents will simply show all of the documents attached to this RFI. Click the hyperlink to view the document.
  2. Linked Documents will show ALL documents that are attached to any item in this project. You can hyperlink into the item via its reference number, if applicable, or the document itself, via the description hyperlink.

  1. Upload Documents will bring you to the standard COINS document upload screen, and allow you to attach new documents to this RFI.

 

  1. The Linked Tab also has a drop down, which appears with a mouse hover:

  1. All Linked Items will show all of the other communication items that link to the Project that this RFI is attached to.
  2. Additionally the specific types of communications are listed, and any linked items can be viewed on a type-by-type basis.
