Add Shift Pay on Crew Daily Reports

Module: Job Status/mCDR [11.08]

This document demonstrates how to enable and use Shift Pay functionality for Labor on Crew Daily Reports, to allow different pay rates for individuals on different shifts, which will carry through to Payroll.

The functionality is demonstrated in both OA Crew Daily Reports and Mobile Crew Daily Reports (mCDR).

 

Setup

Note: Using the Shift Pay functionality in CDR/mCDR requires prior setup in the Payroll module. If Shift Pay has not already been implemented, contact your COINS Payroll Consultant. Major setup is covered below, but not all setup/testing required is explained.

 

  1. No new parameters or configuration options are required to implement the Shift Pay functionality in CDR/mCDR.
  1. Pay Shift Codes are set up in Payroll > Global Setup > US Specifics > Pay Shift Codes (Figure 1).
  1. Enter a Pay Shift Code, a Description, and a Display Sequence (Figure 1).
  • Display Sequence determines the order in which each option shows up when adding on a Crew Daily Report.

Figure 1: Payroll > Pay Shift Codes

 

 

 

  1. Pay Shift Codes are used in conjunction with Earning Categories.
  1. Earning Categories are set up in Payroll > Global Setup > US Specifics > Earning Categories (Figure 2).

Figure 2: Payroll > Earning Categories

 

  1. Pay Shift Codes and Earning Categories tie together on Employee files in Payroll.
  1. Go to Payroll > Employee Maintenance (Figure 3).
  1. Click on the hyperlink of the appropriate employee(s) (Figure 3).

Figure 3: Click Employee Hyperlink in Employee Maintenance

 

  1. Go to the Earning Categories tab (Figure 4).
  1. A combination of an Earning Category and Pay Shift needs to exist here with an Effective Date and the appropriate Rates (Figure 4).
  1. The row marked as Primary will default for the employee during Crew Daily Report entry (Figure 4).

Figure 4: Earning Categories on Employee File in Payroll

OA Crew Daily Report Entry

  1. In OA Crew Daily Reports (Job Status > Daily Reports > Daily Reports), click Add at the bottom of the screen (Figure 5).

Figure 5: Add a Crew Daily Report in OA

 

  1. Select the appropriate option from the Shift Pay column, and enter in the other appropriate options (Figure 6).
  1. It is also necessary to select the checkbox for Earning Category (Figure 6).
  1. Click the floppy disk to Save (Figure 6).

Figure 6: Specify Pay Shift, Click Save

 

 

  1. The Pay Shift selected will be displayed at the top of the screen when filling out the Crew Daily Report (Figure 7).

Figure 7: Pay Shift Displayed

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Mobile Crew Daily Report (mCDR) Entry

  1. Once logged into the mCDR app, and a Job and Crew is selected, select the appropriate job card.
  1. Once inside of a job card (Job/Crew combination), tap the + symbol in the bottom, right corner of the screen to add a new Daily Report (Figures 8 and 9).

Figure 8: Add a Crew Daily Report in mCDR

 

Figure 9: Add Report

  1. At the Report Settings screen, select the appropriate Pay Shift from the available options and then tap Save (Figure 10).

Figure 10: Select Pay Shift in Report Settings

 

  1. Then, tap on the newly created daily report (Figure 11).

Figure 11: Select New Crew Daily Report

  1. The Pay Shift can also be referenced in Report Settings, while inside of the Crew Daily Report (Figure 12).
  1. Tap the triangle icon in the upper, right corner of the screen (Figure 12).

Figure 12: Select Triangle Icon

 

  1. Then, select Report Settings (Figure 13).

Figure 13: Select Report Settings

 

  1. The Pay Shift for this daily report is shown on this screen (Figure 14).

Figure 14: Pay Shift in Report Settings

Review and Approval

  1. Back in OA, after the crew daily report has been submitted, go to Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval or Review and Approval Vertical (Figure 15).
  1. The Pay Shift of the daily report will be shown in the standard columns of the Review and Approval workbench (Figure 15).

Figure 15: Pay Shift Specified on Review and Approval Workbench

 

  1. The Pay Shift is also shown in the Daily Report Detail if the Date hyperlink is clicked on in the Review and Approval Workbench (Figure 16).

Figure 16: Pay Shift Specified in Daily Report Detail
