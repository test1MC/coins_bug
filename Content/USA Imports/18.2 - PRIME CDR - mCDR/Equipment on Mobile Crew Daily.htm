Equipment on Mobile Crew Daily Reports (mCDR)

Module: Equipment Manager/mCDR (Mobile)

This document demonstrates how to set up and charge equipment using mobile Crew Daily Reports (mCDR). This how-to guide is broken into three sections: Setup/Pre-Requisites (page 1), mCDR Entry (page 7), and Review and Approval/Posting (page 16).

 

Setup/Pre-Requisites

  1. First, the foreman (person entering the crew daily report) needs to be set up as an Equipment User with Depot(s) specified.
  1. Go to Equipment Manager > Setup > Equipment Users (Figure 1).
  1. The mCDR User (the foreman who will be entering the mCDR) needs to be entered in as Equipment User with appropriate Depot(s) specified.

Figure 1: Equipment Manger > Equipment Users

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Also, hourly rates need to be specified on either the Asset or Asset Group of equipment items for them to show up as options in crew daily reports.
  1. Hourly rates can be specified in the Rates tab inside of an Asset (Figure 2).
  1. Open an existing rate or Add a new rate (Figure 2).

Figure 2: Fixed Assets – Rental Rates

 

  1. Enter the appropriate hourly rates (Figure 3).
  1. Click Save in the bottom, right corner of the screen (Figure 3).

Figure 3: Hourly Rates on Asset

  1. Alternately, instead of specifying rates on individual Assets, rates can be specified on the Asset Group level.
  1. Inside of the appropriate Asset Group(s), go to the Rates tab (Figure 4).
  1. Open or Add a new Rate (Figure 4).
  1. H (for hourly) needs to be in the list of Default Hire Method(s) (Figure 4).

Figure 4: Rates Tab on Asset Group

 

  1. Inside of the rate, rates for each hourly rate type are set in the Hourly Rates tab (Figure 5).
  1. Click Save in the bottom, right corner of the screen when finished editing (Figure 5).

Figure 5: Hourly Rates Tab inside Asset Group Rate

  1. Next, the equipment items need to be added to the crew daily report.
  1. Go to Job Status > Daily Reports > Maintenance > Crew Maintenance (Figure 6).
  1. Equipment can be added in two ways.
  1. It can be added in the Equipment tab of a Crew (Figure 6).
  1. Click Add at the bottom of the screen (Figure 6).
  1. Select/enter the appropriate Equipment Code (Figure 6).
  1. Optionally, enter a Start and/or End Date (Figure 6).
  1. Click the floppy disk to Save (Figure 6).

Figure 6: Adding Equipment to Crew via Equipment Tab in Crew Maintenance

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. The other method involves going to the Equipment Quick Add tab inside of a Crew (Figure 7).
  1. Select the appropriate equipment item row (Figure 7).
  1. Select Make Available in Crew in the drop-down on the bottom action bar and click the arrow to “Apply Action” (Figure 7).
  1. Then, the Crew will display in the Equipment in Crews column of the equipment item (Figure 7).

Figure 7: Adding Equipment to Crew via Equipment Quick Add in Crew Maintenance

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Next, go to Job Status > Setup > Configuration > JC Parameters (Figure 8).
  1. Search for the parameter CDREQAP (Auto-Post Plant Charges) (Figure 8).
  • If set to Y, Equipment charges will post automatically when the daily report is approved.
  • If set to N, an unposted batch will be created. A user then must go to Equipment Manager > Charges > Charges, go to the Batches tab, select the Batch Number specified on the daily report approval report, and Post it manually.

Figure 8: JC Parameter CDREQAP

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

mCDR Entry

  1. Once logged into the app, tap the + icon in the bottom, right corner of the screen (Figure 9).

Figure 9: Tap the + Icon

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Select the appropriate Crew and Job (Figures 10 and 11).

Figure 10: Select Crew

 

Figure 11: Select Job

 

  1. Then, select the newly created job card (Figure 12).

Figure 12: Select Job Card

  1. Then, tap the + icon at the bottom, right corner of the screen and add a new daily report (Figure 13).

Figure 13: Add New Daily Report

 

  1. Specify the appropriate Report Settings and select Save (Figure 14).

Figure 14: Report Settings, Save

  1. Tap on the newly created daily report (Figure 15).

Figure 15: Select Newly Created Daily Report

 

  1. Add the appropriate Labor for the foreman and other crew members.
  1. Then, tap on the hamburger bun in the upper, left corner of the screen (Figure 16).

Figure 16: Tap the Hamburger Bun

  1. Select Equipment from the side menu that emerges (Figure 17).

Figure 17: Select Equipment from Side Menu

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. The Equipment item(s) added to the crew during setup should pre-populate here (Figure 18).
  1. Tap the + icon and select Add Multiple Cost Codes (Figure 18).

Figure 18: Equipment Pre-Populates, Add Multiple Cost Codes

 

  1. Select the appropriate Cost Code(s) and select Save (Figure 19).

Figure 19: Add Multiple Cost Code(s), Save

  1. Enter in the appropriate hours for Working (W), Standing (S), and Breakdown (B) (Figure 20).

Figure 20: Enter Hours for Equipment

 

  1. When finished adding equipment and hours, tap the triangle icon in the top, right corner of the screen and select Review and Submit (Figure 21).

Figure 21: Select Review and Submit

  1. The Equipment items and Cost Codes used in the daily report can be reviewed at the Review and Submit screen (Figure 22).
  1. Then, select Next in the top, right corner of the screen (Figure 22).

Figure 22: Review and Submit Screen

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Optionally, enter notes for the day (Figure 23).
  1. The foreman needs to enter his or her signature (Figure 23).
  1. Then, tap the paper plane symbol in the bottom, right corner of the screen (Figure 23).

Figure 23: Enter Notes, Add Signature

 

  1. The crew daily report will now show in the app as Submitted (Figure 24).

Figure 24: Crew Daily Report is Submitted

Review and Approval/Posting

  1. Go to Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval (Figure 25).
  1. The status of the CDR should be “Submitted” (Figure 25).
  1. The daily report can be approved from the Review and Approval workbench for from inside of the daily report.
  1. Optionally, click the Date hyperlink to review the daily report in more detail (Figure 25).
  1. Alternately, select the appropriate daily report row, select Approve Current Daily Reports, and click the arrow to “Apply Action” (Figure 25).

Figure 25: Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. At the next screen, move the daily report from the Available box to the Selected box (Figure 26).
  1. Make sure the Action is APPROVE (Figure 26).
  1. Click Next (Figure 26).

Figure 26: Approve Current Daily Reports, Next

 

  1. The next steps depend on the setting of the JC parameter CDREQAP, as detailed earlier in step 28.
  • If set to Y, skip to step #65.
  • If set to N, continue onto step #58.
  1. Hover over the printer icon and click into Approve Current Daily Reports (Figure 27).

Figure 27: Click into Approve Current Daily Reports

 

  1. The report will direct you to an unposted equipment batch that was created during the approval of the crew daily report (Figure 28).

Figure 28: Report references Equipment batch

 

  1. Next, go to Equipment Manager > Charges > Charges (Figure 29).
  1. Find the batch number specified previously in Figure 28 (Figure 29).
  1. Optionally, click the hyperlink to review the batch in more detail (Figure 29).
  1. Select the row.
  1. Select Post from the drop-down on the bottom action bar, and click the arrow to “Apply Action” (Figure 29).

Figure 29: Equipment Manager > Charges > Charges > Post

 

  1. Hover over the printer icon and click into either the GL Cost Posting Report or PC Invoice Posting Report (Figure 30).

Figure 30: Click into Posting Report

 

  1. Review that the appropriate GL accounts were hit and everything processed correctly/as intended (Figure 31).

Figure 31: Review Posting Report
