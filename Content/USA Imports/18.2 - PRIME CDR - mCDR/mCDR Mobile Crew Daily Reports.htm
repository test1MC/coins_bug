mCDR – Mobile Crew Daily Reports

Module: Module Name (Version Number) [Subtitle]

mCDR and Daily Reports (see Daily Reports OA) are used to record time entered from both labor and equipment. Track quantities installed and other miscellaneous

 

Open the app on you i-Pad and this will bring you to the login screen

Login with you username and password – defined by your role based user

Figure 1: Login Screen

 

  1. Once you have logged in you will be here on the main home screen.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Figure 2: Jobs Screen

  1. We first need to associate your crew to a job. Select the plus button in the bottom right hand corner.

Figure 3: Crew Selection

  1. You will on get the below screen, if you (as the foreman) are in charge of more than one crew
  1. Select your crew and choose “Next”

 

 

 

 

Figure 4: Job Selection

  1. Choose the job that you will be working with and select add

Figure 5: Job/Crew Link on Home Screen

  1. You have just create the “job card”. This card highlights the number of reports associated to that job/crew combination and their status. The red squares indicate rejected reports, the green = draft, and the blue = submitted.
  1. Select the Job Card

Figure 6: Reports Screen

  1. These are the reports that were previously created for this Job and crew combination
  1. Select the add button and choose between the two options

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Figure 7: Add Reports Screen

  1. Choose add new report or copy from (which allows you to copy the people [including any deviations from the standard crew set up a head of time as that always carries through] and cost codes from a previous day)
  1. Selected add new report which brings up report settings.

Figure 8: Add Reports Setting

8.Defaults to today’s date. Description is also required. Choose save.

Figure 9: Opens to Labor Add

 

  1. Once in the report we see the predefined crew list. Choose the plus button above that and it provides three options.

 

  1. Will provide a list of crew members to add to your crew list.

i. They will not be on the list the next day unless you use the copy from function.

  1. Add predefined Cost Code to choose from a list of available cost codes on the job
  2. Add previously used codes is a list of recently used codes

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Figure 10: Opens to Labor Add

  1. Choose predefined cost code.
  1. Choose a code and select save.
  2. R = Regular time and O= Overtime

 

To navigate to other sections select the 3 lines in the top left

Figure 11: Misc. Payroll

  1. Misc Payroll allows the foreman to record reimbursements and various extraneous pay items such as cell phone allowances and per diem.
