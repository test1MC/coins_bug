Prevailing Wage Schemes on Crew Daily Reports

Module: Job Status/mCDR [11.08]

This document demonstrates how to enable and use the PW (prevailing wage) schemes functionality for Crew Daily Reports, which will carry through to Payroll. The functionality is demonstrated in both OA Crew Daily Reports and Mobile Crew Daily Reports (mCDR).

 

Setup

  1. First, go to Job Status > Setup > Configuration > JC Parameters (Figure 1).
  1. Open the parameter CDRLTFLD and add PWTRADE and PWSKILL, separated by commas, to the Value (Figure 1).
  1. Click the floppy disk to Save (Figure 1).

Figure 1: Necessary Job Status Parameter CDRLTFLD

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. On the job to enter a crew daily report for, in the Payroll Info section, a Prevailing Wage Schedule must be set and Certified must be checked (Figure 2). If the Prevailing Wage Schedule is blank, the mCDR app will NOT display the PW fields.

Figure 2: Payroll Info Section in Job File

 

  • The file maintenance for Prevailing Wage Schedule is located at Payroll > Company Setup > Prevailing Wage Schedules (Figure 3).

Figure 3: Payroll > Company Setup > Prevailing Wage Schedules

  1. The file maintenance for Prevailing Wage Trades is located at Payroll > Global Setup > Prevailing Wage Trades (Figure 4).

Figure 4: Payroll > Global Setup > Prevailing Wage Trades

 

  1. The file maintenance for Prevailing Wage Skill Levels is located at Payroll > Global Setup > Prevailing Wage Skill Levels (Figure 5).

Figure 5: Payroll > Global Setup > Prevailing Wage Skill Levels

 

  1. Prevailing Wage Trade and Prevailing Wage Skill Level can be specified beforehand on employees in Payroll > Employee Maintenance, so they don’t have to be specified during Crew Daily Report entry (Figure 6).
  1. Open each appropriate employee.
  1. Once inside the employee, go to the Profile tab (Figure 6).
  1. Use the Lookup (…) to set the values for Prevailing Wage Trade and Prevailing Wage Skill Level (Figure 6).
  1. When finished editing, click Save in the bottom, right corner of the page (Figure 6).

Figure 6: Payroll > Employee Maintenance

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

OA Crew Daily Report Entry

  1. In OA Crew Daily Reports, PW Skill Level and PW Trade will show up as optional Additional Columns to select when adding a Crew Daily Report from Job Status > Daily Reports > Daily Reports (Figure 7).

Figure 7: PW Checkboxes When Adding Daily Report

 

  1. At the next screen, the Labor Quick Add tab, PW Trade and PW Skill Level show up as columns. The values will default for the employee if they were set in step #10 (Figure 8). Otherwise, they can also be added/changed here.

Figure 8: PW Fields in Labor Quick Add Tab

  1. PW Trade and PW Skill Level also show up as fields when adding a Misc Payroll line in the Payroll tab (Figure 9).

Figure 9: PW Fields in Payroll Tab

 

Mobile Crew Daily Report (mCDR) Entry

  1. When entering a new daily report, click the + symbol next to LABOR, and select Add Crew Members (Figure 10).

Figure 10: Add Crew Members

 

 

 

  1. Select the appropriate crew members, then select Continue (Figure 11).

Figure 11: Select Crew Member(s), Continue

 

  1. At the next screen, tap the name of each Crew Member, which will expand and show the PW Skill and PW Trade fields (Figure 12).
  1. PW Skill and PW Trade can be seen and edited here (Figure 12).
  1. Select Save to add the crew member (Figure 12).

Figure 12: Expand Crew Member, PW Fields, Save

 

 

 

  1. Also, at the Labor Quick Add screen, the PW Skill and PW Trade can also be seen and edited in the Details of a crew member (Figures 13 and 14).

Figure 13: Tap Crew Member, then Details

 

Figure 14: PW Fields on Crew Member Details Screen

  1. At the Labor Quick Add screen, tap on the name of each appropriate crew member, then select Review and Sign (Figure 15).

Figure 15: Tap Crew Member, then Review and Sign

 

  1. The Review and Sign screen will also show the PW Skill and PW Trade fields, but as read only (Figure 16). They cannot be edited or changed here.

Figure 16: PW Fields on Review and Sign Screen

Review and Approval

  1. Next, back in OA, go to Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval or Review and Approval Vertical (Figure 17).
  1. Click on the Date hyperlink of the appropriate Crew Daily Report (Figure 17).

Figure 17: Review and Approval, Click Date Hyperlink

 

  1. PW Trade and PW Skill are seen in the Labor Detail tab (Figure 18).

Figure 18: PW Fields in Labor Detail Tab
