Approve Crew Daily Reports

Module: Job Status

This document demonstrates the review and approval process regarding Crew Daily Reports.

 

  1. Go to Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval (Figure 1).
  1. Optionally, use the filter at the bottom of the screen to filter for PM, which is specified on the job file (Figure 1).
  1. Also, the hamburger bun in the upper, left corner can be clicked on to adjust the column sets and add more fields to see on the workbench screen, if desired (Figure 1).

Figure 1: Job Status > Daily Reports > Review and Approval

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Review and Approve from Workbench

  1. Crew Daily Reports can be approved on the workbench screen by selecting the row(s), then selecting the appropriate option from the drop-down on the bottom action bar (Figure 2):
  • Reject Daily Reports
  • Approve Current Daily Reports (this will be selected for this example)
  • Unapprove Current Daily Reports (to unapproved daily reports that were previously approved)
  1. Then, click the arrow to “Apply Action” (Figure 2).

Figure 2: Approve via Review and Approval Workbench

 

  1. Skip to step #11 to see the next screen.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Review Daily Report in Detail and Approve Individually

  1. At the Review and Approval screen, to review an individual crew daily report in more detail before approving, click the date hyperlink in the appropriate row (Figure 3).

Figure 3: Click Date Hyperlink

 

  1. Once inside, review the necessary/appropriate information and tabs (Figure 4).
  1. Then, hover over Options and click Approve Current Daily Reports (Figure 4).
  1. Continue onto step #11.

Figure 4: Options > Approve Current Daily Reports

  1. At the next screen, click either of the green + symbols to move the daily reports to the Selected area (Figure 5).
  1. Then, click Next in the bottom, right corner of the screen (Figure 5).

Figure 5: Approve Current Daily Reports Screen, Click Next

 

  1. Back at the Review and Approval screen, the crew daily report will now show a Status of Approved, along with the User who approved it and the Date it was approved (Figure 6).

Figure 6: Daily Report is Approved
