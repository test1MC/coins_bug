Setting Up a New Supplier

Module: Accounts Payable

The Supplier must be previously established in Central Repository > Companies Workbench and designated as a Supplier.

  1. Go to Accounts Payable > Suppliers > Suppliers and click ADD.
Supplier

 

  1. Click on the Company/Office Lookup button.
Company/Office
  1. Click on the hand icon to select the desired Company.
Company/Office Selection

 

  1. Click NEXT
Next
  1. Fill in known data on the Main tab then click on the Terms tab.
Main Tab

 

  1. Fill in data on the Terms tab then click on the Configuration tab.
Terms Tab

 

  1. Fill in data on the Configuration tab then click on the Payment Method tab.
Configuration Tab

 

  1. Fill in data on the Payment Method tab then click on the Analysis Sets tab.
Payment Method Tab

 

  1. Fill in data on the Analysis Sets tab then click on the Notes tab.
  • The fields on this screen will vary depending on which Analysis Sets are already established.
Analysis Sets Tab

 

  1. Enter any notes and click SAVE.
Notes Tab

 

  1. The Supplier now appears on the Supplier Workbench.
Supplier Workbench
