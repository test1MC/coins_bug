Email Notification for Payment

Module:

Use this process if you want to email the Electronic Funds Transfer remittance to the .

  1. To set up a for email notification, first go to Central Repository > Companies, and click the link of the appropriate company.Figure 1: Select Company

  2. In Office tab, Open the MAIN office record.Figure 2: Select Main Office

  3. Add the email address into the Email field and click .Figure 3: Enter Email Address

  4. Next, go to > Suppliers > Supplier Maintenance. Click next to the Supplier Account that corresponds to the Company you have just edited. Figure 4: Select Company in

  5. Change the Default Print Method to E-mail and click .Figure 5: Change Default Print Method

  6. If you get the error message below, go back to the Company record in Central Repository to make sure an e-mail has been set up for that company.Figure 6: Save, Check for error

  7. The Payment should be set up under > Suppliers > Supplier Maintenance.Open the Supplier. Figure 7: Select Company

  8. On the Payment Method Tab, set Payment Method to and fill in required bank information.Figure 8: EFT Payment Method Setup

  9. Any system-generated email will show as being from the address set in the parameter MAILFROM, for example: sender@email.com.Figure 9: Email Configuration
