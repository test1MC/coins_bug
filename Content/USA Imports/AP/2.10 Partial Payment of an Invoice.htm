Partial Payment of an Invoice

Module:

This process is used for selecting specific invoices for payment or partially paying an invoice.

  1. This step will allow for a partial payment of an Invoice, when necessary. Go to > Payments > Select/Deselect Invoices (Manual) to select individual invoices for payment. Find the invoice here. It will appear with a status of Approved. Click to the left of the line you wish to partially pay.Invoice Selection

  2. Type in the amount you wish to pay in the Amount Paid box. Enter a note for the Reason for Partial Payment (for example, down payment). Click to the left of the line.Adjust Payment Amount

  3. After the line has been saved, the status will change from Approved to Selected for Payment and under the Amount Paid column it will show the partial amount of what has been entered and selected to pay this invoice with.Partial Payment
