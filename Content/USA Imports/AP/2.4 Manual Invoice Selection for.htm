Manual Invoice Selection for Payment

Module:

The manual function is used for selecting specific invoices for payment or partially paying an invoice. For mass selection of invoices, refer to Select Invoices for Payment.

Note: Before starting this module, you should check to see if there is an unfrozen batch in the Payment Batches. To get an overview and check all available invoices, you may also go to > > Invoices . Displayed here will be all Invoices that have been previously created and have a balance.

Select the Invoice(s) for Payment

  1. This step will select the full amount in the Invoice to be paid. Go to > Payments > Select/Deselect Invoices (Manual) to select individual invoices for payment. Displayed here will be all Invoices that have been previously created and have a balance. Find the Invoice here. It will appear with a status of Approved. Invoice Selection

  2. To select invoice to be paid, select the box to the left of the vendor, select Pay Invoices in the bottom drop down menu and click the action arrow to proceed. Select and Pay Invoice

  3. The status will change from Approved to Selected for Payment. Paid Invoice

    Freeze the Payment Batch

  4. Go to > Payments > Payments Batches and find your batch. Click on the box to the left of the batch number and select Freeze Payment Batches from the bottom function list, then click on the action arrow. Batch Selection

  5. The status of batch will change to Frozen (hit refresh if status does not change immediately)
    • Note: The of payment batch must be the same month as the payment date. If not, unfreeze the batch and click on the hyperlink batch number to change the fiscal month of the batch.
