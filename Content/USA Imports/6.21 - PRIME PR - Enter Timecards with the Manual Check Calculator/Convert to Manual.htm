Convert to Manual

Module: Payroll

Verify that there is an open Regular or Adjusting Period.

  1. On the Timesheet Input Page, select Convert to Manual on the action bar, and apply the Action.Convert to a Manual

  2. This will bring up the Output Options screen. Enter the open Period in which the manual will be added.
    • Here is it possible to Terminate the Employee by clicking the kbox (this feature is only available on 11.07 and above) and selecting a reason for terminating the employee from the drop down that appears. If you are using a version prior to 11.07 you will click the button, enter the convert to a Future/Regular/Adjusting Period, and the employee will need to be terminated in their employee file manually. Layoff an Employee (Optional)

    • A warning message will appear that the employee will be terminated. Confirm Layoff (Optional)

    • View the report by clicking the icon in the top right of the screen. It will display a message that the employee is now terminated.Verify Termination

  3. Once this process is complete, the timecard will appear in the Period you converted to and you will process that payroll as usual. This will include the information/calculations in all of the reports, but it will NOT produce another .
