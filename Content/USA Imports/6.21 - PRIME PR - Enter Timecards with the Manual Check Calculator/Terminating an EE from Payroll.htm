Terminating an Employee from Payroll and HR

Module: Payroll/HR (11.07)

Terminating and deleting an employee, without pay history, from Payroll and Human Resources.   

  1. In Payroll, the employee that needs to be terminated, should have a Date entered within their Employee Record.  Terminate Employee in Payroll


  2. Once this has been entered and saved, delete the employee from Payroll by ing their flag and using :Delete Employee from Payroll


  3. Then go into Human Resources > Personnel Workbench and filter on ALL types of employees (this will include Terminated) and now delete the employee from HR:Delete Employee from HR


    This will purge all Payroll and HR records for any employee without Payroll history.  If the employee has Payroll history, you will have to use the Re-Number utility and then perform the above steps. 

  4. To change an employee’s Original Hire Date you can make the edit within the Personnel Workbench.  Simply open the record, make your edits, and save the line.  You can also change their Term or Rehire date from here as well and it will flow through to HR & Payroll --  it must be last record in the list.Employment History in HR
