Create Portal Users

Module: Employee Self Service [ES]

After the ESS configuration is complete, portal users need to be created.

 

  1. To create a Portal User. Go to Employee Self Service > ESS Portal > Portal Users > Create New Portal User.

Figure 1: Create New Portal User

 

  1. Select the Employee. Hit Next. This will bring up the Personnel and Employee Info.

Figure 2: Enter Employee

 

  • Personnel and Employee Info will be displayed. New user Id will be suggested. Please Enter the User Name and a unique User Email.

Figure 3: Personal Email for Employee

 

 

  1. Click Next. The new user is now in the list of employees on the Portal.

Figure 4: View Employee Record

 

  1. The new user (to the email address) will receive an automatically generated email message from the COINS system, created on the email template.

Figure 5: Email

 

 

 

  • NOTES**
  • For timesheets to be entered in ESS, a link needs to be established on the PR employee file. Go to Payroll > Employee Maintenance > Employee Details Tab > General Tab > Timesheet Input Group. To allow the “My Timesheets Input” tile appear on the ESS Portal, select the “Timesheet Input Group” seen below. Created on the ESS group setup.

Figure 6: ESS Link to Payroll

 

  • To have Benefits show on the Portal, an employee needs to be enrolled in the benefit plan on the HR personnel workbench > drill on employee > Benefits tabs. Add any benefits here. Also check the PR > employee maintenance > drill on employee> Pay calculations. To make sure benefits are being calculated on the employee paycheck.

 

  • To enter time a timesheet on the ESS Portal – A new Payroll period must be added for each Payroll frequency prior to employees submitting their timecards
  •  
  • Enter time on the ESS Portal under - My Timesheet Input. Submit.

 

  • Approver / Reviewer Workbench. Just show that the reviewer is there. Approve the timesheet on the approver workbench. You must be logged in as the approver to see the timecards!

Figure 1: Caption Title

 

 

  • The Timesheet Approval tab allows you to keep track of timesheet records awaiting approval. You can use the following actions on this screen:

l Approve Reporting - Approves a timesheet record for reporting.

l Close Reporting - Closes a timesheet record.

l Reject Reporting - Rejects a timesheet record for reporting.

l Unapprove Reporting - Unapproves a timesheet record that was already approved for reporting.

l Re-Open Reporting - Reopens a timesheet record that was closed.

 

  • After approving the timecards they must be pulled into an open payroll period. Make sure a period is open (future or regular).
  • Payroll > Pay Frequency > Input > Transfer Employee Self Service TS. Validate then transfer to the correct open period.
