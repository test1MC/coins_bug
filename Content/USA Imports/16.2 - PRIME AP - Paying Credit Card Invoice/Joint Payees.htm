Joint Payees

Module: Accounts Payable

This document will demonstrate how to set up joint payees.

    Go to Accounts Payable>Configuration>Parameters. Search for Payjoint and open the line.

 

Figure 1: Parameters


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Figure 2: Payjoint Parameter


 

 

 

 

 

This parameter determines whether the joint payee options will be shown in the PL maintenance screens, and how many will be offered for entry. 0 or blank means the joint payment options will not be shown. Enter a value and save the line.

  1. Then, search for Paytype and open the line.

 

 

Figure 3: Paytype Parameter


 

 

 

 

 

This parameter will allow the option to choose the payment type when entering an AP invoice. Set the parameter to YES and save the line.

  1. Begin the AP invoice entry process.

Figure 4: Payment Type


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

In the Invoice Tab, select the payment type as separate. Go to the Joint Payees Tab.

  1. Fill in the Payee names and amounts, then Click Save.

 

 

 

Figure 5: Joint Payees Tab


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

**Note** Make sure the Payee name entered is a company  already in the system.

Continue the AP invoice process.

  1. Once the batch gets frozen, COINS splits the payment records. Then, when the checks get printed they are split.
