Validate Batch (Costing Workbench)

Module: Payroll

Validate Batches performs a validation on the selected batch(es) to ensure they are ready to be posted.

  1. Go to the Costing Workbench. Payroll > [Payroll Frequency] > Costing Workbench.  the flag for your Period (Period 4 in this example) and use the action bar to Validate Batches.Validate Batch

  2. This will prompt you to another screen. Click located in the bottom right.Continue

  3. After running the validation report, fix ALL errors before moving forward.
  4. Timesheets must be “reset” for the period (period 4 in this example) before making any adjustments to will fix the timecard error.
  5. Once all changes have been made, the calculation needs to be re-run.
  6. Repeat the above steps to re-validate.
