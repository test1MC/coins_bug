Check for Negative

Module: Payroll

Enter the Net Detail screen, which allows you to view net amount details for an employee. Filter for negative , then find and correct any negative that before processing, as no negative should ever be “paid” out to an employee by payroll.

  1. Go to Payroll > [Payroll Frequency] > Period Maintenance. The Period will appear as a hyperlink, Click this to review the data.Period Maintenance Screen

  2. Navigate to the Net Detail tab.Review Net

  3. In the bottom right of the screen, choose the filter category “Negative ”, this will search for any negative in the payroll period. Apply the filter by clicking in the far bottom right.Apply Negative Filter

  4. If any appear here, they will have a negative value and must be corrected before proceeding. It would then be necessary to reset, fix the errors, recalculate the employee and then check for negative again to make sure the changes have been fixed.
