Rename GL Elements and Accounts

Module: General Ledger (11.06) [Chart of Accounts]

  1. Go to General Ledger à Chart of Accounts à GL Elements
  1. Select the correct Element Type and hit “Apply Filter” on the right.

Figure 1: GL Elements

 

  1. Open the Element to be renamed.

Figure 2: GL Elements – Select Element

 

  1. Change the name and click on Save.

Figure 3: GL Elements – Rename Element

 

  1. Go to General Ledger à Administration à Chart of Accounts Administration à Rebuild Account Descriptions

Figure 4: Rebuild Account Details

 

  1. Select Run Type as Update and click on Next.

Figure 5: Rebuild Account Details

 

  1. Go to General Ledgerà Chart of Accounts à Account Maintenance.

Figure 5: Rebuild Account Details

 

  1. The Element and Account are renamed.
