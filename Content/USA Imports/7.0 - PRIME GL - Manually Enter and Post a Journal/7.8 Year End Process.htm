Year End Process

Module: General Ledger (11.06) [Period Processing]

  1. COINS OA allows two years to be available in the General Ledger module at any given time; these two years are identified in the system as Current Year and Next Year.

 

  1. This two-year window is usually rolled forward in August, if the company’s fiscals are based on a calendar year.

 

  1. To determine which two years are available in GL, go to Global Periods Maintenance by using the appropriate path below:

General Ledger > Period Processing > Periods ‐ All Modules ‐ This Company.

Figure 1: Global Periods Maintenance

 

  1. In Global Periods Maintenance, filter to Next Fiscal Year, as shown in the screen below.

Figure 2: Global Periods Maintenance – Next Fiscal Period

 

  1. If your fiscals are not based on a calendar year, you do not need to roll your year forward in December; instead, you will probably do it a month before your Next Fiscal Year ends.

 

  1. You should also filter to All Years (as shown in the screen below) and add the following years periods if they are not already on file.
  • Note:  The periods must be created in each separate internal company (kco).
  1. To setup the individual periods do the following:
  • Click +ADD
  • Enter the Period and F/Date
  • Click the Save icon

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Figure 3: Global Periods Maintenance - Add

 

  1. Prior to running the Year End process, you can identify any open items by running the following reports:
  • General Ledger > Period Processing > Period Processing Reports > PL/SC Open Item Report
  • General Ledger > Period Processing > Period Processing Reports > SL/CS Open Item Report

Verify all statuses say posted.

Figure 4: GL Status


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Click NEXT in the lower right corner of the window to generate the report.

 

  1. After you have set up the next 12 fiscal periods and posted any open items, you can roll your year forward by going to: General Ledger > Period Processing > Year End ‐ This Company

Figure 5: Year End – This Company


 

  1. Complete the checklist and then click NEXT.
  • Backup Complete- was a backup done already?
  • Copy Budget- The GL budgets will get copied to the next period.

 

  1. Note: It is recommended that you perform the Year End process at least one month before the end of Next Year.
  1. Once end of year is completed, a report will generate saying year end has successfully processed and the new This Year and Next Year periods show up in

General Ledger > Period Processing > Periods ‐ All Modules ‐ This Company.
