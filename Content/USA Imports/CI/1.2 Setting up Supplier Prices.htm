Setting up Supplier Prices

Module: Company Information

This process is used to create a unique pricing structure for each supplier.

  1. Access the Company Workbench via Central Repository, , , or, as in this example, Procurement. So, in this example, go to Procurement > Companies.Enter Company to Edit

  2. Go to the Other tab, and click Commodity Prices.
  3. Click .Add Commodity Prices

  4. Click to pull up a list of the available commodities that you can set prices for. If a commodity that you want to add a price for isn’t showing up in the commodity prices workbench, it is because you haven’t created a materials PO with that commodity for that specific supplier. Once you do it will show up in this workbench and allow you to put a price in for it.
    1. PR: The unit of measure.
    2. Disc %: The percentage of any discount.
