Add New Office to Company

Module: Central Repository

This process will be used to add a new office/location to an existing company. This should only be done if they have the same Tax Identification Number. If the TINs are different, refer to document A (Creating a Company).

 

  1. Go to Central Repository > Companies.
Companies

 

  1. Click the hyperlink on the company you wish to edit.
Edit Company

 

 

  1. Select the Offices tab.
Offices Tab

 

  1. Click the Add button at the bottom of the screen.
Add Office

 

  1. Select the appropriate Office Type from the drop-down menu.
Select Office Type

 

  1. Complete the Office and Trading As fields.
Office and Trading As

 

  1. Complete all appropriate Address and Tax information in the remaining fields, then Click SAVE.
Address and Tax Information

 

  1. The newly created office now displays in the Offices tab.
Newly Created Office

 

Making a Newly Created Office Location into an AR Customer

  1. Go to Accounts Receivable > Customers > Customer Maintenance. Click the ADD button.
Figure 9: Add Customer

 

  1. Click the drop-down menu button.
Company/Office Drop Down

 

  1. Click on the finger icon of the desired location.
Select desired location

 

  1. Click the NEXT button.
Next

 

  1. This will populate the Customer Maintenance page. The remainder of the process will involve making your way through the various fields and populating the appropriate information in each field.
Customer Maintenance

 

  1. Click SAVE when finished populating the Customer Maintenance fields.
Save Customer Maintenance

 

  1. The previously created branch will now appear on the AR Customer Maintenance Workbench.
Updated Customer Maintenance Workbench
