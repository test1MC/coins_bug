Asset Sale

Module: Fixed Assets

The purpose of this document is to show how the correctly dispose of an asset. It can be scrapped, sold or reported lost/stolen.

  1. Go to Fixed Assets > Input > Enter Asset Sale.

Figure 1: Enter Asset Sale


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Select the asset that is going to be disposed and then Click NEXT.
  1. Under the Main tab, fill in the appropriate fields:

Figure 2: Disposal Main Tab


 

 

  • Customer- the company that the asset is going to
  • Reason- the reason the asset is being disposed
  • Status- the status of the asset being disposed
  • Disposal Date- the date the asset is being disposed
  • Proceeds- the amount received for the disposal of the asset
  • Sales/Use Tax- the tax that must be paid on the disposal of the asset

 

  1. Verify the GL accounts are correct.
  1. Under the Payment Details tab, fill in the appropriate fields:


Figure 3: Payment Details

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  • Check- if the asset was paid for by check, enter the check number
  • Bank Code- make sure the appropriate bank is selected
  1. Then click NEXT.
  1. A report will generate.

 

Figure 4: Asset Sale Report-1


Figure 5: Asset Sale Report-2


 

  1. Go to Fixed Assets > Input > Posting > Post Disposal Batch.


Figure 6: Post Disposal Batch

 

  1. The batch is now posted.

 

  1. Multiple Assets can be disposed at once as well. Go to Fixed Assets> Administration> Dispose Multiple Assets. Select the assets that are to be disposed of, then click on the action bar.


Figure 7: Dispose Multiple Assets

 

 


  1. Fill in the appropriate information.

Figure 8: Disposal Information

 

 

  • Sold To: The company that received the asset.
  • Reference: A reference number to keep track of the transaction.
  • Disp Date: The date the asset was disposed.
  • Proceeds: The amount received for disposing the assets.
  • Reason: The reason for disposing the assets.
  • Status: The current status of the asset.
  1. Then click Next.
  1. The disposal details can now be seen from the Dispose Multiple Assets table.


Figure 9: Disposal Details
