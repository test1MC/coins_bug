Adding Asset Departments

Module: Fixed Assets

The purpose of setting up asset departments is to identify financial information about assets and for costing.

  1. Got to Fixed Assets> Setup> Maintenance>Asset Departments.


Figure 1: Adding an Asset Department

 

  1. Click Add.
  1. Fill in the following fields.

                                Figure 2: Asset Department Entry


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  • Department- Enter the name of the department that is being added
  • Description- Provide a description for the department
  • The various GL accounts will be selected as well

 

  1. Click SAVE.
