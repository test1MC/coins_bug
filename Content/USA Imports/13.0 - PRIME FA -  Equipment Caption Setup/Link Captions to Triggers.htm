Link Captions to Triggers

Module: Fixed Assets

  1. To associate a new Trigger to the appropriate Caption, navigate to Fixed Assets > Setup > Maintenance > Caption Maintenance, and click the hyperlink.

 

Figure 1: Clicking the Hyperlink of the Caption

 

  1. While within this Caption, on the “Triggers” tab, click Add.
  1. Use the Lookup to find and select the appropriate Trigger.

Figure 2: Selecting the Trigger on the Caption Trigger Tab

 

  1. Click Save.
