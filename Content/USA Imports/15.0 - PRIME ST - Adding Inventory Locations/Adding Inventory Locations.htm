Adding Inventory Locations

Module: Inventory (ST)

The purpose of an inventory location it to set storage locations for given assets

  1. Go to Inventory>Setup>Maintenance>Inventory Locations

Figure 1: Inventory Locations


 

 

 

  1. Click ADD

Figure 2: Inventory Location Setup


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Fill in the appropriate fields
  • Location- where the inventory will be stored
  • F/A Department- the asset department that corresponds with the inventory location
  • Sales/Use Code- the appropriate tax code used in the region the inventory location is
  • In Addition:
  • Workshop- whether there is a workshop at the inventory location
  • Accrual Method- if there is a workshop, what accrual method is used
  • GL Accounts- the GL accounts that are associated with the inventory location

 

  1. Enter in the appropriate GL accounts for each field.


Figure 3: GL Account Configuration

 

  • Inventory Account- Corporate Inventory
  • Receipt Control – Asset Purchase Control
  • Adjustment Control – Unbilled Revenue Adjustment
  • Cost of Sale – AP Control Account
  • External Revenue- Construction Revenue
  • Internal Revenue – Corporate Inventory
  • Variance Account – Corporate Inventory
  1. Click SAVE

**Note** To map the inventory location to multiple GL accounts, the account can be entered like the following:   __.000._____    or   10.___._____ or __.___.33000
