Adding Inventory Groups

Module: Inventory

After having added an Inventory Location the next step is to add Inventory Groups. Inventory Groups categorize Items. The step after creating Inventory Groups is adding Inventory Items.

  1. Go to Inventory > Inventory Groups using the tree menu

Figure 1: Inventory Groups

  1. Click on the green “ADD” button at the bottom of the page

Figure 2: Add Button

  1. This will bring up a screen for adding Inventory Groups fill out all of the pertinent information

Figure 3: Inventory Group Information

  1. Click on the save button on the bottom right side of the screen

Figure 4: Save Button

  1. Going back to the Inventory Groups the newly added Inventory Group will be on the page

Figure 5: New Inventory Group

  1. If any edits need to be made click either the folder open button to the left of the record that needs updating, or highlight the record and click the open folder

Figure 6: Multiple Open Buttons
