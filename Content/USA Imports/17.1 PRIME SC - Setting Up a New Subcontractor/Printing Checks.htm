Printing Checks

Module: Subcontract Ledger(11.06) [Payments]

  1. Go to Subcontract ledger > Payments > Payment Batches. Select the Payment Batch.

Figure 1: Payment Batch

 

  1. Freeze Payment Batches.

Figure 2: Freeze Payment Batches

 

  1. The Status of the batch changes to ‘Frozen’ after the refresh button is clicked.

Figure 3: Payment Batches - Frozen

 

 

  1. Go to Subcontract ledger>Payments>Print Checks/Remittances

Figure 4: Print Checks/Remittances

 

  1. Click on the Batch Reference number hyperlink.

Figure 7: Print Payments/Remittances – Subcontractor Selection
