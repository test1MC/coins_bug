Adding Rental Rates

Module: Fixed Assets (PC)

These will be the default rental rates to charge for any asset that is added to this group. If rental rates are not added to the group, then every asset added will need to have rates added. Adding rental rates can save time in the future.

  1. Go to Fixed Assets > Setup > Maintenance > Asset Groups.

Figure 1: Selecting the Asset Group

 

  1. Click on the appropriate hyperlink for the group.

 

  1. Go to the Rates Tab and click the folder to open the automatically generated line.

  • Default Rental Method(s): the following can be entered in the Default Rental Field. More than one method can be entered into the field.

**Note: For every item you enter into the Default Rental Field a new tab will appear alongside the Main Tab

 

D = Daily, H = Hourly L = Lump (Single Charge), M = Monthly,

P = Pattern, R = Rated Sale, S = Sale, T = Track (no change), W = Weekly, V = Variable

  1. Then click on the Daily Tab
  1. Under the Daily Tab, fill in the appropriate data:

Figure 4: Daily Tab

  • Internal Rate – the rate for which it gets rented out with in the company
  • External Rate- the rate for which it gets rented out outside the company
  • Dur- the period of the primary rate
  1. Then click SAVE.
