Recharging EQ Service Tickets

Module: Equipment Manager

This document walks through the Recharging of Costs that hit EQ Service Tickets. Recharging is invoicing a job or customer for those costs.

 

  1. Now that all of the costs have hit this EQ Service Ticket, the Recharges need to be processed.
  1. Begin on the Workshop Recharge Details Tab on the EQ Service Ticket record.
  1. Here all of the costs incurred by the Service Ticket are displayed, with the Recharge column showing identical amounts to the Costs column. These amounts have been created by default from the actual costs, but can be changed by opening each line and updating the amount.

Figure 1: Updating Recharge Amount in Workshop Recharge Details Tab

 

  1. These cost lines could also be reduced, or zeroed, if needed.
  1. Additional lines can be manually added here by clicking the Add button. Fill out the appropriate details, including Cost Type, Description, and Recharge Value. There will be no cost directly associated with this entry.

Figure 2: Adding Additional Recharge Line

 

  1. Once all changes to the recharge lines have been made, the Service Ticket can be completed. Continue on to the Printing and Completing EQ Service Ticket Document.
