Setup Training/Assessments, Qualifications and Competencies

Module: Human Resources

The purpose of this document is to describe the setup required for COINS to track Training/Assessments, Qualifications and Required Competencies. 

 

  1. File Maintenance Requirements
  1. Go to Human Resources > HR Maintenance > Lookup Codes > HR Requirement.
  1. Add a list of Requirements that employees can work toward, such as becoming a Core Apprentice, earning an Electrical Certification, HR Administrator, etc.

Click on the hyperlink of the Job Group.

 

 


Figure 10: Job Groups

Figure 11: Job Group Details
