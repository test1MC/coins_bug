Manual Budget Entry

Module: Status

This document demonstrates how to enter a budget manually, without using an upload/import file.

To enter in a budget via import using an Excel spreadsheet, reference the how-to document for “Import Cost Codes and Budget.”

  1. Go to Status > Budget Set Up > Memo Data and Budgets Entry.
  2. Enter the appropriate number.
  3. Select 10-Initial Budget from the drop-down for Type.
  4. Enter the appropriate .
  5. Click at the bottom, right corner of the screen.Memo Data and Budgets Entry, Click Next

  6. Click in each appropriate row.Click Open Folder in Appropriate Row

  7. In the Costs tab, all costs go into the first month in which the will incur costs. Typically, this will be the same month as the contract date. Costs Tab

  8. In the Quantity/Hours tab. If applicable, enter hours for any codes and/or material quantity amounts, if quantities installed are being tracked.
    • Certain clients have used a method to track percent complete by entering a quantity of 100 to mean “100%” – this way, the user will be able to enter the percent complete on each code.
  9. When finished entering in values, click .Quantity/Hours Tab, Click Save

  10. Repeat steps #6-9 for all cost codes needing budget information.
  11. Next, click in the row of the revenue cost code. For this example, the revenue cost code is 00 – Billings.Click to Open Revenue Cost Code

  12. The Revenue tab should only be available on the revenue cost code.
  13. Enter the appropriate budget information in the Revenue tab. The contract amount should equal the revenue budget on the revenue cost code.
  14. Don’t enter any numbers in the Costs or Quantity/Hours tabs for the revenue cost code.
  15. When finished, click at the bottom, right corner of the screen.Revenue Tab, Click Save

  16. Once the user confirms that the numbers are correct and the budget is finaled, go to Status > Budget Set Up > Freeze Initial Budget.
  17. Enter the appropriate number.
  18. Click at the bottom, right corner of the screen.Click Next to Freeze Initial Budget

  19. Move the mouse pointer over the Printer icon at the top of the screen to view Reports, then click on the Freeze Initial Budget report.Click on “Freeze Initial Budget” Report

  20. The Freeze Initial Budget report will show the budgets, cost codes, and amounts, as well as the cost, revenue, quantity, and hour totals within the .Freeze Initial Budget Report
