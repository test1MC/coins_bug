WIP Adjustments

Module: Status

This document demonstrates how to enter WIP (Work in Progress) adjustments for .

  1. Go to Status > Input > WIP Adjustments.
  2. Optionally, set up for filters for the to adjust.
  3. Click in the bottom, right corner of the screen.Click Next

  4. Click in the row of the appropriate .
  5. Alternatively, click Concurrent to adjust many at the same time.Open Appropriate or Click Concurrent

  6. Update any updatable fields. Other fields may re-calculate based on changes made. The most common fields to be changed are the Estimated Revenue at Completed and Estimated Cost at Completion.Updatable Fields

  7. Click in the far left to save the line.Click Save to Save

  8. When finished making adjustments, highlight the row(s), select Finale Monthly WIP from the drop-down on the bottom taskbar, and click .Finale Monthly WIP
