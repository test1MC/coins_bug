Module: Status

  1. Go to Status > Daily Reports > Daily Reports and click on the bottom of the screenDaily Reports Screen

  2. The date will automatically enter in today’s date. In the Crew member section put in the crew using the item, and in the Foreman section put in the Foreman in charge of that crew. Enter in the that the was sent to in the Name section, finally your user ID will automatically fill inEntering New Report

  3. Click on to the left of the line when all information is entered inSave New Report Line

  4. This will bring up the “ Quick Add” screen. Click on the “Miscellaneous Tab” to add items Quick Add Screen

  5. Click on the bottom of the screenMiscellaneous Tab

  6. Leave the Company as company 1 if that is what has been worked in, for the Depot put the location the came from. For the column Type put in Items, for the Product choose the item that was used. For quantity choose the amount that was used, Per, Cost Type, and Cost Category will default in. Finally choose the PersonnelAdding Line

  7. Click to the left of the line addedSaving Line

  8. Go to Options and Submit for ApprovalSubmitting for Approval

  9. Go back to Status > Daily Reports > Daily Reports and look at the line of the recently added report, It should say submitted as the example doesSubmitted Daily Report
