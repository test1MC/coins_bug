Setting Up a New Company

Module: Central Repository

This will be the starting point for creating a company. A new company should be created if it has a Tax Identification Number different from any other company in the system. If it has the same TIN, refer to Document Q (Adding New Office to Company).

 

  1. Go to Central Repository > Companies and click ADD.
Figure 1: Company Workbench - Add

 

  1. Enter the Name, Phone Number and/or any other known company information and click Check Duplicates.
Figure 2: Check Duplicates
  1. Any companies that exist in the database that are close matches to the information provided will be shown. If no duplicates are found, click NEXT.
Figure 3: Check Duplicates Listing
  1. Enter all known information on the Main tab, making sure to enter a label for the Main Office. When finished entering data on this screen, click on the Types tab.
Figure 4: Main Tab

 

  1. On the Types tab, to make this company available as an Accounts Receivable Customer, check the Customer box. To make this company available as a Subcontract Ledger Subcontractor, check the Subcontractor box. To make this company available as an Accounts Payable Supplier, check the Supplier box.
  • The checkboxes other than Customer, Subcontractor and Supplier are user-defined options so the below image may vary.
  1. Once all relevant types are checked, click on the General tab.
Figure 5: Types Tab
  1. Once the information on the General tab is complete, click SAVE.
Figure 6: General Tab

 

  1. The new Company now appears on the Company Workbench.
Figure 7: Company Workbench
