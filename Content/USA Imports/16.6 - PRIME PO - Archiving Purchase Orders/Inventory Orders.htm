Inventory Orders

Module: Procurement

Inventory Purchase Orders are used to purchase inventory items directly to an inventory location. In order to purchase items from an external vendor directly to a job, you must use a Material Order with line items by inventory item code.

Before placing inventory orders make sure inventory items, inventory groups, inventory locations, and overhead departments have been previously set up.

  1. Go to Procurement > More Orders > Inventory Orders and click the green “ADD” button
