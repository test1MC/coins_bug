How To Generate Agreement Renewals

Module: Service Management

Every 12 months we recommend renewing an agreement. This is the process to renew these agreements.

  1. First go to Service Management > Agreements

Figure 1: Agreements Workbench

  1. This will pull up the main agreements workbench. This will allow you to see the agreements and their end dates. You can also run the “Agreement Renewal Report” to get this information.

Figure 2: Checking Agreement Renewal End Dates

  1. Now go to Service Management > Renew Agreements > Select Agreements to Renew. Then pick the folder open button of the agreement you wish to renew.

Figure 3: Select Agreement to Renew

  1. Here you can update the renewal. This includes if there will be an increase in the cost of the agreement, or an increase/decrease in the cost budget or schedule of the renewal. You will also see the new start and end date.

Figure 4: Main Renewal Screen

  1. Under the areas to copy tab, you will see all the areas that will copy over from the new agreement. Every area will be automatically checked, you can make changes if necessary. Hit the save button.

Figure 5: Areas to Copy Tab

  1. You will now see the new renewal end date under the agreement.

Figure 6: Renewal Shown on Screen

  1. Open up Service Management > Renew Agreement > Process Agreement Renewals > Process Agreement Renewals

Figure 7: Navigate to Process Agreement Renewal

  1. Select the agreement renewal you wish to process, then select process agreement renewals on the action bar.

Figure 8: Process Agreement Screen

  1. You will now see the process screen. Select process to complete.

Figure 9: Process Agreement Renewal

  1. If you navigate to the agreement and hyperlink into it you will now see the new renewal.

Figure 10: Agreement Renewal Screen
