Creating Schedule Bill Invoice

Module: Service Management

Once an agreement is in every month you will run a scheduled bill to create all of the invoices that need to go out for these types of agreements.

  1. Go to Service Management > Generate Scheduled Billing Service Orders

Figure 1: Navigate to Generate Scheduled Billing Service Orders

  1. Under the Main Tab, Enter the date range of the PM Scheduled Service orders you wish to create. Enter the Problem Code to Assign which will be PMI (or whatever your pm invoice code is). Also you will need to enter the TRADE which in this case will be HVAC. These last two fields are not parameters for the report, but they will appear on the service order when it is created for filtering. The other fields, are not mandatory.

Figure 2: Main Screen

  1. Check the Generate Service Orders Box (this is if you want to actually create the service orders, for the first few times it may be a good idea to not check this box and verify you are pulling the correct service orders before going back and checking this box and running the function again).

Figure 3: Generate Service Orders Box

  1. Under the Agreement Selection Tab, you can further filter which PM Scheduled Service Orders you are generating. The Termination Status will automatically filter to non-terminated. You can filter for agreement, customer, agreement group, agreement type, service manager or multi-year agreement. None of these fields are mandatory.

Figure 4: Agreement Selection

  1. Under the Location Selection tab, you can filter your results by location. These fields are also not mandatory. Now hit the “PROCESS” button in the bottom right of the screen.

Figure 5: Location Selection

  1. Open up the reports workbench in the top right hand corner of the screen. You will see the report of Generated PM Service Orders, along with the corresponding cost codes creation. Hyperlink into the Generate Scheduled Billing Service Orders Report.

Figure 6: Report Viewer

  1. When you open up this report you will see the PM Scheduled Service Order Calls that were generated along with the Service Order Number and the information for the order, such as Estimated Hours, Asset and Inspection type.

Figure 7: Generate Scheduled Billing Service Orders Report

  1. Go to Service Management > Service Orders. You will now see the service order(s) created.

Figure 8: Create Service Orders
