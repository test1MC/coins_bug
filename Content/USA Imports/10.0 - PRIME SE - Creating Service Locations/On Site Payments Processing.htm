On-Site Payments Processing

Module: Service Management

If there is a payment taken by the technician On-Site this is how it would be entered onto the Service Order for record keeping.

  1. Open up Service Order or while creating a new Service Order, select require On-Site payment

Figure 1: Opened Service Order

 

  1. You will now see a tab of On Site Payment

Figure 2: On-Site Payment Tab

 

 

 

 

 

  1. From the On-Site Payments tab, Select Add

Figure 3: Adding an On-Site Payment Line

 

  1. Enter the date of the payment. Type will be how they are paying, cash, check or credit

Figure 4: Date and Type of Payment

 

  1. Select Amount tendered, which operating account the payment will be credited to, if there is a check or credit card, you can enter that information next

Figure 5: Amount Paid and Bank of Payment

 

  1. Check the approve box, if applicable

Figure 6: Approved Payment Check Box

 

  1. Select save by hitting the floppy disc to the left of the line

Figure 7: Save Line

 

  1. You will see the detail line

Figure 8: Finished On-Site Payment Line
