Approve Visit Labor

Module: Service Management

When a Service Technician has finished their mobile visit and put their labor on costs onto the Service Order this is how it gets approved.

 

  1. Open up Service Management Module, then under visits, open up “Visit Labor Review and Approval”. Choose the week ending date. It will default to the current week ending date, but this can be changed if necessary, then hit next

Figure 1: Visit Labor Review and Approval

 

  1. This will bring up the technician list. Any visits that need to be approved along with a breakdown of hours worked will appear here

Figure 2: Technician List

 

  1. Highlight and click on the technician needing approval

Figure 3: Highlighting Technician

 

 

 

  1. Here the weekly visit history along with payroll for the technician selected will appear

Figure 4: Visits for Technician

 

  1. Select what visits needing approval. Then process the approval on the bottom of the screen

Figure 5: Highlighting Visit for Approval

 

  1. Now both visits have been approved

Figure 6: Approved Visit
