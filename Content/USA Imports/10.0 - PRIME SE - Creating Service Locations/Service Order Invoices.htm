Service Order Invoices

Module: Service Management

After Service Orders have been checked for accuracy they need to be sent out for billing. This document shows how to create an invoice from a completed service order. 

 

  1. Ensure Service Order Visits are completed and ready for invoicing.

Figure 1: Completed Service Order

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Open up the Generate Invoices Tab from the tree menu Service Management > Invoices > Generate Invoices

Figure 2: Generate Invoices Tree Menu

 

  1. Enter the invoice date at the top, under Service Order type select “TM”  and Hit Process on the bottom right

Figure 3: Generate Invoices

  1. Open up the invoices tab on the left menu

Figure 4: Invoices on Tree Menu

 

  1. Notice the invoice batch automatically created. Select the batch number blue hyperlink

Figure 5: Finding Invoice Batch

 

  1. Highlight the line ( check the box to the left of the invoice) then select freeze batches on the bottom of the page

Figure 6: Freezing Invoice

 

  1. Once the batch is frozen the invoice can be printed by selecting Service Management > Invoices > Print Invoices. Click the hyperlink for the invoice that was frozen

Figure 7: Select Batch to Print

 

  1. Select the form to use to print the invoice and the delivery method (print or email) that is on the customer file

Figure 8: Select Form for Invoice

  1. Select next on the bottom right

Figure 9: Next button

 

  1. The invoices will print, click the printer in the top right corner to see them

Figure 10: Checking Printed Invoice

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. View PDF to check that the invoice is correct

Figure 11: Example Invoice

 

  1. Go back to the invoices tab on the tree menu, highlight the batch line by checking the box, then hit select post batches towards the bottom of the page.

Figure 12: Post Invoice Batch

 

  1. Then run the posting Mode: Run a validate first then post, Period: Fiscal that invoices are posted to, Billing complete date: Enter the date to be used when setting the billing as complete. Then hit process

Figure 13: Options for Posting Batch

 

 

  1. Status of invoice will now say posted

Figure 14: Posted Batch
