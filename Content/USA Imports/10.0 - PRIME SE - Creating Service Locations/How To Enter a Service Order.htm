How-To Enter a Service Order from S&D Board

Module: Service Management

After creating the Location and Customer Account at the Location the dispatcher can create a Service Order. This is one of the ways to create a Service Order. Once this step is finished the Service Order will be ready for assigning and dispatching.

  1. On the tree menu click on Service Management > Scheduling > Schedule & Dispatch

Figure 1: Service Management Schedule and Dispatch Board

 

  1. Select new service order button from the top of the screen.

Figure 2: Schedule and Dispatch Board New Service Order Button

  1. Below is a blank service order.

Figure 3: Blank New Service Order Form

 

  1. Select the service location first, either begin to type the name or use the “…” lookup field.

Figure 4: Partially Filled Service Order

 

 

 

  1. Due to the previous system setup a lot of information will prefill. Any information in blue will be required to be filled up, all other fields are optional.

Figure 5: Service Order showing Blue Forms

 

  1. Fill out the customer’s contact information, including who called and the point of contact.

Figure 6: Subsection 1 of Service Order

 

  1. Fill out any blank fields in: Type of work, Cost Type Classes, Service Department, Service Order Group, Response type and Service Order Type.

Figure 7: Subsection 2 of Service Order

 

  1. Fill our Problem Description, Problem Code and Service Order Description. Remember Problem Description can only be 55 characters to start then hit the tab button. You can then go back and fill in as many characters as you want.

Figure 8: Subsection 3 of Service Order

 

  1. Agreement will default if one is present for location.

Figure 9: Subsection 4 of Service Order

  1. Although not mandatory if known fill out Customer Information. If estimated hours are entered they will appear on technician’s day view on the Service and Dispatch Board.

Figure 10: Subsection 5 of Service Order

 

  1. Click save on the bottom right of the page.

Figure 11: Save Button on Service Order

 

 

  1. Once saved a message appears giving you the Service Order Number and asking how you would like to proceed.

Figure 12: Message After Completing Service Order

 

Click, Service Order Details to view the order, Schedule a Visit to schedule a tech or OK to return to the Service and Dispatch Board.

  1. If Ok, back on the Schedule and Dispatch board the Service Order’s tab will have the created service order listed.

Figure 13: Highlighting Finished Service Order

 

  1. Select schedule a visit, then select the technician it is going to, and the date and time the call is scheduled, then hit process.

Figure 14: Screen if Step 12 Schedule Visit is Chosen
