How to Schedule a Service Visit from S&D Board

Module: Service Management

Once a Service Location and Service Location Customer have been created a Service Order can be created. This step is creating the Service Order so it is ready to be assigned and dispatched to a service technician.

  1. Go to Service Management > Scheduling > Schedule & Dispatch

Figure 1: Schedule and Dispatch Board

 

  1. From Service Orders tab, highlight the service order that is to be scheduled

Figure 2: Schedule & Dispatch Board Highlighted Service Order

 

  1. Click and Hold the Service Order and drag it over to the desired technician on the desired date

Figure 3: Moving a Service Order to a Technician

 

 

  1. A screen will pop up asking if a technician should be dispatched to the visit

Figure 4: Pop Up After Scheduling

  • Selecting Yes will dispatch them to the visit, which will be shown on the S&D Board as the order filled in black

Figure 5: Service Order Dispatched from OA

  • Selecting No will assign it in their call queue on their mobile device, they will need to dispatch themselves when they arrive to the call
  • You will see a white fill until the tech sees the call

Figure 6: Service Order Not Dispatched

 

  • Once the tech opens the IPAD and views the call on their current list an “A” will appear next to the location name

Figure 7: Service Order Noticed by Technician

 

  • When the tech starts the call the dispatch color of black designated the call has started. The S next to the location name is the flag that the call has started on mobile

Figure 8: Service Order Dispatched by Technician

 

  • If the tech rejects the visit it will remain white, but an E will appear next to the location

Figure 9: Service Order Rejected by Technician

 

  • Once the call is completed, the call turns gray

Figure 10: Completed Service Orders

  • Yellow dot – visit has not been marked ready for billing
  • C – Call Completed
  • 1 Check mark – tech considers all work done
  • 2 check marks – office and tech consider work done
