Making Visits Ready to Bill

Module: Service Management

Once a Service Order has been acted upon, completed, and Labor and materials have been confirmed it is time to mark the visit ready for billing. This is the last step before sending out the invoices for customer to pay, make sure to verify everything on the bill.

 

  1. Select the Visit Billing Review and Approval tab, and select a Service Order that has multiple visits.

Figure 1: Visit Billing Review and Approval Tab

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

  1. Select the Service Order link, and then click on the Visits tab. You will see the multiple visits listed here. Also notice that “Ready for Billing” to the right is unchecked.

Figure 2: Visits Tab on Service Order

 

  1. Check the visits you want to approve for billing (Ctrl + Click to select multiple) and “Set Ready for Billing.”

Figure 3: Set Ready for Billing

 

  1. You can also Set Billing Complete here.

Figure 4: Set Billing Complete

 

  1. The statuses of the visits are now “Ready for Billing” and “Billing Complete.”

Figure 5: Checking Billing Complete
