Bank Reconciliation Opening Balance

Module:

This document will demonstrate how to set the opening balance prior to the first bank reconciliation done in OA (see Bank Reconciliation).

  1. Go to > Bank Reconciliation. If importing a bank statement, click Import Bank Statement. If manually entering a bank statement, click Bank Statements. Bank Statements


    If importing a bank statement, a batch will auto-create (click the Date hyperlink). If manually entering a bank statement click .

  2. Since this is the first bank reconciliation for the bank there is no opening or closing balance. Bank Statement Batch


    It will not save if the balances are entered because the statement type is set to Generic. It needs to be changed to Manual.

  3. Go to > Setup Maintenance> Bank Accounts and select the bank that is going to be reconciled. Bank Accounts


  4. Change the statement type from Generic to Manual. Click . Statement Type


  5. Go back to > Bank Reconciliation> Bank Statements and add/select the batch again.Setting Balances


  6. Enter the appropriate information again including the opening and closing balances found on the bank statement and click .
  7. Proceed with Bank Reconciliation Process.
