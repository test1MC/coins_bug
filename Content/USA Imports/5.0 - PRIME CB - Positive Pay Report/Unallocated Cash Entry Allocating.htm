Unallocated Cash Entry, Allocating to Invoice

Module:

This document demonstrates how to locate unallocated customer cash in COINS, and how to use this cash at a future date to pay an invoice for a customer.

How to Find Unallocated Customer Cash

There are multiple reports you can run to see unallocated cash for a customer:

  • Combined Report by Customer – Current
    1. Go to > Reports > Customer Reports > Combined by Customer – Current.
    2. Select the box for Include Unallocated Customer Cash.
    3. For the field, select Unallocated. This option determines what column of the report to show this cash.Combined by Customer - Current

    4. Enter in the appropriate information in the other tabs.
    5. Click in the bottom, right corner of the screen to generate the report.

      The report will show the unallocated customer cash in the column on the far right.

      Combined by Customer – Current (Report)

  • Unallocated Cash Transactions Report
    1. Go to > Reports > Cash Transaction Reports > Unallocated Cash Transactions Report.
    2. Enter in the appropriate information and click in the bottom, right corner of the screen to generate the report.Enter in Report Information, Click Next

      Unallocated Cash Transactions (Report)

Allocating Customer Cash to Invoices

Unallocated cash can later be allocated to an invoice, or invoices, belonging to a customer.

  1. To begin, go to > Cash Transactions > Allocate Customer Cash or use the Menu Search to find CB Allocate Customer Cash – Batches.
  2. Click at the bottom of the screen to add a new batch.Add a New Batch to Allocate Customer Cash

  3. Enter in the appropriate and Bank Code for this transaction, and optionally, a Description. When Inter-company is ed, it allows customer cash to be allocated to a different company from the current company.
  4. Click in the bottom, right corner of the screen.Enter in Appropriate Information, Click Save

    This will bring you to the Allocate Cash – Update Batch screen.

  5. Click at the bottom of the screen to add a new transaction.Click to Add a New Transaction

  6. Click the Customer, then click on the appropriate customer.Click Lookup, Select Appropriate Customer

  7. Click the Cash Ref to pop up that customer’s list of unallocated cash. Then, click on the appropriate .Click Lookup, Select Appropriate

    The corresponding data from the will populate into the transaction line.

  8. Click to save the line.Data Populates into Line, Click Save

  9. Click on the hyperlink under Cash Ref in the line that was just created.Click Hyperlink Under Cash Ref

    This will bring you to the “Allocate Cash – Check Details” screen.

  10. In the Analysis Lines tab, click to add a new allocation line.Click Add in Analysis Lines Tab

    For the next steps, it’s important to know where the invoice to allocate the cash to is in COINS (either or ).

    1. To find out what module the invoice is in, go to > > Customers.
    2. Click the hyperlink of the appropriate Customer Account.Click the Customer Account Hyperlink

    3. From the Customer Details , there will be a drop-down in the top, left corner of the screen.
    4. Miscellaneous Sales invoices will be in the Invoices tab. These invoices would be marked as SL Ledger analysis in step 11.Miscellaneous Sales Invoices (SL Ledger analysis)

    5. invoices will be in the Certificates tab. These invoices would be marked as CS Ledger analysis in step 11.Job Receivables Invoices (CS Ledger analysis)

  11. Back at the “Allocate Cash – Check Details” screen in the Analysis Lines tab, where the user is adding a transaction, the code in the drop-down under Ledger, and the subsequent Analysis, will indicate what type of transaction it will be.
    • CS Ledger – and a number analysis, indicates a allocation.
    • SL Ledger – and a customer number analysis, indicates an allocation, for Service and miscellaneous non- invoices.
    • CC Ledger – and a customer number analysis, is used for the remainder of the allocated balance, when the CS or component doesn’t account for the total amount.
  12. Click to save the line after the appropriate Ledger and Analysis are selected.Select Appropriate Ledger and Analysis, Click Save

    There can be multiple Analysis Lines, if entering invoices from multiple modules.

    This will bring you to a Page, CS or SL, depending on the previous choices taken in the process. This page will show any unpaid invoices tied to this and customer, or any unpaid invoices tied to this customer.

  13. In the To Allocate tab, click in the line of the invoice that is being paid using the unallocated cash.Open Appropriate Line in To Allocate Tab

  14. Enter the Amount Paid, then click to save the line.Enter Amount Paid, Click Save

    The section at the top of the page will show the cash has been fully allocated.

  15. Click in the bottom, right corner of the screen.Cash is Fully Allocated, Click Next

  16. Using the action bar at the bottom of the screen, select Post from the drop-down and click to post the batch.Click Apply Action to Post
