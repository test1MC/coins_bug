Discounts on Quick Cash Receipt Entry for Invoices

Module:

This document demonstrates the process of entering a discount taken by the customer, when cash is received, on invoice.

Note: This process will only work with invoices, and will NOT work for invoices billed through or . To enter a cash receipt with a discount on a bill, see the document Enter a Cash Receipt with a Discount for a Job Invoice.

Discount is set up for the GL account specified in Configuration:

Configuration Discounts Taken GL Account

  1. Start at > Cash Transactions > Quick Cash Receipt Entry.

    Quick Cash Receipt Entry

  2. Click to create a batch:Add Batch

  3. Under Bank Code, select the bank which will be used for the deposit and click .Create Receipt Entry Batch

  4. Click to enter the receipt.Add Transaction

  5. Enter Number (optional), Customer account, Number, Amount, and Deposit Reference. Click .Enter Transaction Information

  6. You will be brought to the Allocation Screen, where you can allocate your receipt to the invoices you are paying.Enter Allocations

  7. To allocate this receipt and enter a discount on the invoice, you will either need to use the button (if only allocating to one invoice) or the button (if allocating to multiple invoices).
  8. Enter the receipt and discount amounts, and click . Verify the receipt is now fully allocated.Allocations

  9. Click .
  10. Using the action menu, click Post to post the receiptPosting Receipt Batch

  11. GL Cost Posting Report will print to your report printer.GL Cost Posting Report
