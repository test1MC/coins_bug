Applying Credit to an Invoice

Module: (11.06) [Cash Transactions]

  1. After you have entered and posted your credit, go to > Cash Transactions > Quick Cash Receipt Entry and click .Quick Cash Receipt Entry

  2. Set your , Transaction Date, Bank Code and optional Description. Then click .CB Quick Cash Receipt Entry – Add Batch

  3. Click .CB Quick Cash Receipt Entry – Update Batch

  4. Enter the Customer Account, Deposit ref, receipt amount, and optional number. Click .Transactions


  5. Select your credit and click on Pay Invoices/Certificates. The unallocated amount should be zero.Pay Invoices/Certificates


  6. Click to go back to the batch. The transaction can now be posted.Posting Transaction
