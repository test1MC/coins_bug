Entering a credit note

  1. Go to: > Invoices> Invoices & Credit Note Entry。Invoice & Credit Note Entry

  2. Click to add a batch.Invoice Entry – Add Batch

  3. Enter the , transaction date, transaction type as ARCJ, and optional description. Then click .Invoice Entry – Add Batch

  4. Click .Invoice Entry – Add Invoice


  5. Enter the customer account, optional analysis, invoice date, and tax code. Then click .
  6. On the Revenue Distribution tab, click .Revenue Distribution


  7. Enter Product, optional description, quantity, price, and analysis. Click on .Distribution Lines

  8. On the Credit Control Notes tab, click .Credit Control Notes Tab

  9. Enter Priority, Next Action Date, Promised Date of the , Promised Amount, and any Credit Notes. Click on .Credit Control Notes Lines

  10. This Credit Control Note will be stored in the customer file under the credit control notes tab.Credit Control Notes Tab

  11. After all Invoice Lines and Credit Control Notes are entered, post the batch.Post Batch
