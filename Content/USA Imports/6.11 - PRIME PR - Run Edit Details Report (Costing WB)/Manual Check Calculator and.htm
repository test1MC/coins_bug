Manual Calculator and Future Periods

The Manual Calculator screen allows the user to enter (in this example weekly) manual calculator periods, which are future periods that can be processed through the printing of . These periods are hidden from the main payroll runs. They allow calculation of employee to determine gross to net and gross and burden values. These values can then be supplied to off-site locations.

  1. Create a Future Period to begin the process of paying employees during the off-cycle or terminating employees through the layoff process. Go to Payroll > [Frequency Period] > Input > Manual Calculator. Add a future period. Add a Future Period

  2. Enter the data. When creating layoff , verify that the date is correct. Most commonly this will be the current date, due to the concept of “layoff is payoff” for (union) employees. Click on the line. A message will appear, displaying a warning that the date is before the period ending date, click OK. Enter data

  3. View the newly created record. The future period is now visible from the Manual Calculator screen. Future Period Created
