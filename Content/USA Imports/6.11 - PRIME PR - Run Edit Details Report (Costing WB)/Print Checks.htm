Print

Module: Payroll

Print allows you to print the physical for employees in your payroll frequency.

  1. Go to Payroll > [Payroll Frequency] > Print . Most of the information will default in.  Select the correct Bank and then click to run the report.  Print

  2. Click Ok on the pop-up message. This shows the number of that will be printed (along with their total value).Warning Message

  3. Click the icon in the top right corner to check the payslips and and make sure all fringes, deductions and benefits look okay for all employees. The PDF will show how the Employee's pay has been broken down.View ReportEmployee Paycheck
