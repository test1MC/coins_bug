Accessing Portal via COINS

 

This new and improved help portal brings together our online help, how to guides and videos into one single searchable site.

Given the volume of information available, we appreciate that some of the resources may be in need of updating to the latest software release and this is an ongoing programme of development.

A client facing version is in development with plans to release it early 2018.

Please use the portal when seeking resources and please use the email icon at the top of each topic to let us know how we can improve it.
