Enquiring and Reporting on Triggers

Trigger Enquiry shows the assets on which the selected trigger has been specified. You can filter on those assets on which the trigger has been set off.

 

Fixed Assets > Reports > Operational Reports > Asset Triggers allows you to produce a report that shows which events are due by reporting on expired triggers. You can report on events that will become due in the future by entering a different date to use. You can produce a word-processing merge file containing the data.
